Summary

Roxy wakes up from her dream of being teacher and going home to the man of his dreams and considers the dream to be bliss as she once again faces the reality of surviving the Teleport Labyrinth while recalling how she got separated from her party for accidentally stepping into a Teleportation Circle. Roxy continues to explore the Labyrinth as she fights her way through near endless Monsters but she eventually runs out of Mana and is cornered while still longing for her dream to come true of having a family like Paul and
Zenith
 until...
