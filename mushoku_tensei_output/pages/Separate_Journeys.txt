Separate Journeys
 (それぞれの旅
Sorezore no tabi
) is the eighteenth episode of the
Mushoku Tensei
 anime series originally broadcasted on November 14, 2021.

Synopsis

After reconciling with Paul and parting ways to find the other's family member, Rudeus, Eris and Ruijerd take a ship to the central continent. Meanwhile, Roxie, Elinalise, and Talhand search for the Greyrat family, including Zenith. At the urging of Nokopara, whom she meets again in the city of Rikarisu, Roxy visits the village of the Migurds, her hometown. Roxy is not at peace with the fact that she ran away from home, but will she be reunited with her parents?
