Fangs of the Black Wolf
 (黒狼の牙
Kokurō no Kiba, lit. Kuro Ōkami no Kiba
) is a Rank S Adventurer Party led by
Paul Greyrat
 in his youth. The party had varying personalities and was one of the most famous parties in the
Central Continent
 before it was disbanded after Paul and Zenith quit to settle down.

Members

Paul Greyrat
: Leader of search group; vanguard swordsman; frontline tank

Geese Nukadia
: Leader of preparation group; thief

Tallhand
: Middle guard; magician

Ghislaine Dedoldia
: Vanguard swordswoman

Elinalise Dragonroad
: Warrior; frontline tank

Zenith Latreia
: Healer

Achievements

Attains Rank S

Trivia

All members of the party were outcasts in their own house, family, or village.

Except for
Geese
, who is not a true outcast but rather is the last living member of his tribe.
