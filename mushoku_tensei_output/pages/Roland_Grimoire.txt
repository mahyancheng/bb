Roland Grimoire
 is the son of
Clive Grimoire
 and
Lucy Greyrat
.

Background

After his parents travelled happily and in love to the holy kingdom of Millis, they had him as their first and only child.

Trivia

He is the first descendant of
Rudeus
 who does not have the surname
Greyrat
.

Thanks to his elven lineage on both his mother's and father's side, it is possible that he could live up to 100-200 years.

Because he is descended from Rudeus, he is immune to the Curse of
Orsted
.

This could mean that he is one of Rudeus' descendants who help Orsted defeat
Hitogami
.

But it is also possible that he will become the Pope in the Kingdom of Millis, similar to his paternal ancestors.

He is the only known character who has 4 different races as ancestors.

Human, maternal and paternal,

Elve, paternal and maternal,

Hobbit, paternal

and beast race, maternal.
