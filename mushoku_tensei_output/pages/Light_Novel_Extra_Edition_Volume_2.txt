Synopsis

A deluxe special edition that includes a special acrylic stand with the second volume of the book “Extra Edition”!!

Rudeus Greyrat
 was victorious after a decisive battle in the Kingdom of Biheiril.

A collection of stories depicting the aftermath of Rudeus and the people surrounding him, "The Extra Edition".

The second volume of the series includes "Let's Make an Automaton!" the story about the production of an
automaton
 and the troubles that come with it, and "
Millis Diary
" which describes the journey that Rudeus planned to make memories with his family and additional perspectives of
Eris
,
Kalman III
, and
Orsted
. In addition to "The God Who Lives in the
Holy Land of Swords
", which tells the story of Rudeus' visit to the current
Sword God

Jino Britts
, it also includes four newly written short stories, "Children of the
Greyrat
", which depict the daily lives of the children of Rudeus.

Life continues even after the decisive battle.

What are Rudeus' thoughts and what will he do after that...?

A life-over-reincarnation fantasy, a story that continues after the main story, the second in the series!

And, you will also receive an acrylic stand with an illustration of
Sylphiette
,
Roxy
,
Eris
, and others!
