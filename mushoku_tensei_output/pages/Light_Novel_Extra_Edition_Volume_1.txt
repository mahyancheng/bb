Synopsis

The story after the decisive battle...

After the decisive battle in the Kingdom of Biheiril,
Rudeus Greyrat
 won.
A collection of stories depicting the aftermath of the people surrounding him!

The first volume of the series covers
Norn Greyrat
's marriage story - "Wedding of Norn", "Lucy and Papa" depicting
Lucy Greyrat
's first
school
 attendance,
Doga

Woman
 Who Was Once Called a "Mad Dog".
