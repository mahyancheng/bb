Summary

Light Novel Extra Edition Volume 3
 also known as
Mushoku Tensei: Redundant Reincarnation Volume 3
 is the upcoming third volume of the
Mushoku Tensei
 Light Novel Extra Edition.

On April 4, 2025, the official X of the series @MFBooks_Edit tweeted the release date of Volume 3 is on June 25, 2025

Synopsis

A special edition with an acrylic stand featuring an original illustration by Shirotaka-sensei!

After the decisive battle in the Kingdom of Beheril, Rudeus Greyrat was victorious. The "Extra Edition" is a collection of stories that depict what happened to him and the people around him afterwards.

The third volume of the series includes the long story which revolves around the elopement of Rudeus' son Ars. This special edition depicts the family's greatest crisis from both Rudeus and Ars' perspectives!

Life continues even after the decisive battle. What will Rudeus think and do after that...?

The third volume of the series of life-restarting reincarnation fantasy stories that weave together the aftermath of the main story!

And you can get an acrylic stand with an original illustration by Shirotaka-sensei of Rudeus and Eris in your hands!
A deluxe special edition that includes the third volume of the "Stolen Amount" book with a special acrylic stand!!
