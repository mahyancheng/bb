Tsubasa
 ( ツバサ, lit. "Wings") is the seventh ending theme song of the
Mushoku Tensei anime adaptation
 performed by Shion Wakayama,
Nanahoshi Shizuka
's voice actor. It is a cover from the song "Tsubasa", sung by
Under Graph
 in 2004. It is only used in
Episode 39.

In April 21, 2024, the Mushoku Tensei official X handle: @mushokutensei_A also announced the use of the song in the
Afar
 episode with the screenshot of Nanahoshi singing.
