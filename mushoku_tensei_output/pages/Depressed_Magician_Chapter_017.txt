Summary

After parting ways with Sara, Rudeus agrees to accompany Soldat who introduces him to the Stepped Leader members who are waiting for Soldat before they depart. The members were surprised that Rudeus is with Soldat who they are aware that he dislikes but welcomes him nonetheless. During the trip, Rudeus shows his ability to
Silent Spell Cast
 and when Rudeus tries to explain his other abilities, Soldat stops him and challenges him to a fight.
