Summary

Rudeus meets Sara at the Citadel of Roa who is slowly recovering after the
Teleport Incident
. They exchange stories of their lives where Rudeus learns that Sara is already married and has started opened restaurant and treats him for a meal. After seeing how Sara has changed and enjoying his new life, Rudeus tells her that he will come back when he is in town.
