Synopsis

THE TRUE FACE OF SILENT FITZ

Rudeus enrolled at the Ranoa University of Magic to unravel the secrets of a magical calamity, but he keeps getting caught up in new kinds of trouble. Now he’s in the position to dish out some trouble of his own, with two animal-eared bullies at his mercy. Between being the new top dog on campus and experiencing some very confusing feelings for the charismatic Silent Fitz, Rudy’s school days aren’t easy!
