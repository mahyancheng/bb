Synopsis

Elementary, My Dear Roxy!

Now that she’s all settled in at the Ranoa University of Magic, Roxy dives head-first into her studies. When one of the library’s tomes goes missing, Roxy takes it upon herself to track down a replacement copy. Unfortunately, the replacement book winds up being stolen merchandise, and Roxy gets thrown into jail under suspicion of grand theft spell-tome! With a false accusation hanging over her head, can Roxy and friends clear her good name and track down the real book thief?
