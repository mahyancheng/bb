God
.

Appearance

A man with eight arms, six horns, a height of over 3 meters, and magic eyes.

Plot

The battle between the
First Dragon God
 and First Demon God is unusually long because because of the Demon God's power to regenerate his body parts and his other ability to alter his size. On the 100th day of battle, First Dragon God activates the Magic Circle beneath the feet the the gigantic Demon God which causes the body of the Demon God to rapidly collapse. After, the First Dragon God collapses and the Dragon Generals carried him back to the
Dragon World
.
