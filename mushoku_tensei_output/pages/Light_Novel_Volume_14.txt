Synopsis

CASTLE IN THE SKY

Nanahoshi
,
Rudeus
, and the rest of their group take a trip to
Perugius Dola
’s mysterious floating fortress, seeking adventure and a once-in-a-lifetime chance to learn new magic. What they find instead is disaster as one of their members is struck down by a deadly
illness
. How far will Rudeus go to save a dear friend’s life?
