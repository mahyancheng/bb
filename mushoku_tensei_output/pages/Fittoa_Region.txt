Fittoa Region
 (フィットア領
Fittoa-ryō
) is a fiefdom in the Asura Kingdom ruled by the Boreas Family.

Location

It is located in the northeastern part of the
Asura Kingdom
.
 It lies on the
Central Continent
.

Landmarks

It is a vast agricultural area on the borders of the Kingdom. The lands in the Fittoan Region are largely rural with many forests

Notable Places

Citadel of Roa
: The largest settlement in the Fittoa Region where the liege lord resides.

Buena Village
: A peaceful hamlet.

Wieden: A small town where Rudeus Greyrat and
Eris Boreas Greyrat
 once got kidnapped and held at. It is two settlements away from Roa.

Feudal Lord

Under the crown of the Kingdom of Asura, the Fittoa Region is reigned by the
Boreas Greyrat
 family, on of the great families of Asura Kingdom.
