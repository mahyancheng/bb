Summary

Despite defeating the
Manatite Hydra
, Rudeus and the group are at a loss due to Paul's death. they cremate his remains and took his ashes and remaining equipment with them while Rudeus recalls his previous life on how he did not event attend his parent's funeral when they died. They return to Rapan to report what has happened and rest while waiting for Zenith to wake up. Rudeus imagined that Zenith will wake up that everything will be alright but to his surprise Zenith is unresponsive causing Rudeus to fall in despair.
