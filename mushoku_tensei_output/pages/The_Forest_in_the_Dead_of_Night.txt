The Forest in the Dead of Night
 (真夜中の森
Mayonaka no Mori
) is the second episode of the
second season

Mushoku Tensei
 anime series originally broadcasted on July 16, 2023.

Synopsis

In order to search for his mother,
Zenith
, Rudeus spreads his name under the nickname of "
Quagmire
" due to his achievements as an adventurer and his proficiency for the spell. One day, he joins
Counter Arrow
 on their mission in Gargau ruins, but a large number of
Snow Drakes
 attacks them.The party is saved by
Stepped Leader
 party lead by
Soldat Heckler
 who were also in the area but after the battle, Soldat angrily approaches Rudeus and his party...

Plot

Several months pass as Rudeus builds his fame and reputation in the Basherant Kingdom, hoping that word of his exploits will reach Zenith or that someone will know her whereabouts. After completing a snow gathering mission, He decides to accompany Counter Arrow on another quest to collect
Snow Drake
 scales, but end up being ambushed by the Snow drakes and accidentally running into another party,
Stepped Leader
, who are out to hunt the snow drakes. Despite this setback, Counter Arrow successfully complete the quest and return to Rosenburg, where Sara convinces Rudeus to join their victory celebration. However, during the celebration, the leader of Stepped Leader, Soldat, goes on a drunken rant over how he is put off over Rudeus because he can tell he's holding back his true power.

The next day, Rudeus learns that Mimir was killed and Sara had gone missing during a quest, with the rest of Counter Arrow being forced to retreat due to a blizzard. He heads to Trier Forest, kills the
Snow Buffaloes
, recovers Mimir's remains, and manages to rescue Sara from a
Icefall Treant
 whom he thought is also dead after finding her
earring
. After being thanked by Sara and Counter Arrow, Rudeus feels like he is the one saved and forgiven, as he looks forward to a good sleep in a long time.

Adaptation Notes

This episode adapts the following material:

Light Novel Volume 7

Chapter 3: Quagmire Rudeus

Chapter 4: The Forest at Night

Differences with the Light Novel:

The event where Rudeus completes a snow gathering mission occurs first before the gathering of Snow Drake Scales mission

Sara's monologue after the Soldat confrontation is cut

Mimir's remains were burned together with the rest of the Snow Buffalo's victims in the Anime while in the Light Novel he brings it with him which Sara carries back to Rosenburg after being rescued
