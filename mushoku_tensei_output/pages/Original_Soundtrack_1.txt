Mushoku Tensei Original Soundtrack 1
 (『無職転生 ～異世界行ったら本気だす～』 オリジナル・サウンドトラック1) is the official soundtrack album for the first cour of the
Mushoku Tensei
 TV-anime composed by
Yoshiaki Fujisawa
 (with the exception of track #21 and #37, which was composed by
Yuiko Ohara
). The album was released enclosed with
Blu-ray Chapter 1
 special edition on April 21, 2021. The album comprises of a total of 30 tracks in the regular digital edition and 37 in the special physical edition.
