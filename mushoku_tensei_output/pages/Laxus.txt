Laxus is the Death God that precedes
Randolph Marianne
. He was the creator of the 『The Bone Ring of Laxus』.

Personality

Laxus was very loyal to the demon lord he served. After
Vita
 controlled his master and caused his death, Laxus was hellbent on killing Vita, developing various tools and strategies to counter him. He was very persistent, as he continued with his quest until his last breath.

Background

Laxus was the loyal subordinate and best friend of the demon lord he served. After Vita controlled his master, Laxus defeated him as he discovered the truth. As a revenge for his friend, Laxus was hellbent on killing Vita, developing various magic tools and strategies to counter him, including the Bone Ring made from the demon lord's bones. He exterminated the entire slime tribe during the process, but was unable to reach Vita as he escaped to the labyrinth "Netherworld" and infested the guardian.

It's presumed that Laxus was defeated by
Randolph Marianne
 at some point of time, who inherited his title as the Death God and took his bone ring.
