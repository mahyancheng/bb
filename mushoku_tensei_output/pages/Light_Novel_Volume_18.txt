Summary

This is the eighteenth volume of the
Mushoku Tensei
 light novel, which covers the Subordinates arc. Its contents correspond to volume 19 of the web novel.

Synopsis

RUDEUS TRAVELS AROUND THE WORLD

Rudeus
 completes his first mission to make
Ariel
 the next King of
Asura Kingdom
. Since then, a year and a half has passed, receiving numerous missions from
Orsted
, and when he returned to his
house
, he purchased her old senpai
Linia
 from a slave merchant.

One day, a letter arrives from the Doldia village to Rinia ["Urgent, the
Holy Beast
 is missing! search!]  Rudeus decides to go to the Great Forest to talk about the situation of Leo. Visiting again the prison of the village, he finds an unexpected person.....
