Synopsis

DEMON DAYS IN THE HOLY CITY

The humans of Millishion fear and distrust demonkin like Roxy–bad news for an adventurer hard up for work! Thankfully, after a chance meeting with another of her own kind, Roxy’s made some new connections. One of them is a mysterious, handsome magician… Could this be the beginnings of true love?!.
