Summary

At the Ranoa Magic Academy Girl's Dormitory, Rudeus and Norn are finally able to reconcile and are now in good terms. Rudeus also begins tutoring Norn at the Academy and they eventually started working together on making a book about the heroic deeds of Ruijerd.

Meanwhile Rudeus asks Aisha if there is anything she wants which she asks to be given a salary to which Rudeus and Sylphiette agrees to and Rudeus is curious on what she buys from her money.
