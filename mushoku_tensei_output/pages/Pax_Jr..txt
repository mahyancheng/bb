Pax Jr.
 is the son of the late King
Pax Shirone
 and best friend of
Sieghart Saladin Greyrat
.

His storyline is one of the major plot threads left unresolved by the end of Mushoku Tensei, making it likely he will once again appear in the planned sequel series.

Appearance

When he grew up, Pax Jr. has light blue hair inherited from
Benedict Kingdragon
 and stout figure like
Pax Shirone
.

Personality

Pax Jr is intelligent, diligent and ambitious. Despite being discriminated due to his hair colour, he never gave up to fate and poured his effort into excelling academically. As a leader of a duchy, he is willing to claim and develop a piece land deemed impossible to develop, and wait for years until the ideal chance for him to realize his dream of forming his own independent country.

Background

Pax Jr was born to Pax Shirone and Benedict Kingdragon.

Plot

Jobless Oblige

Pax Jr. is enrolled into Asura Royal Academy, where he meets
Sieghart Saladin Greyrat
 and befriends him. They spend time teaching each other things, with Pax Jr. tutoring Sieghart in School. After the graduation ceremony Pax Jr. reveals to Sieghart he is planned to govern a dangerous wasteland and take control of an important point for the King Dragon Kingdom and asks him to help him in his dream to establish an independent country. After he determines that Sieghart refused, he returns back to the King Dragon Kingdom and transforms the wasteland through smart tactics and good leadership, though he is then taken by a tribe of Indigenous people living in a nearby jungle for chopping down their Sacred Trees, where he is saved right before execution by his old friend
Sieghart Saladin Greyrat
 who defeats the tribe and makes them become allies. In the final chapter, Pax Jr. has transformed the land and it is thriving with him becoming part of a newly formed Knights Order. With Pax Jr. sitting at a table with 6 other people. These people are "Death God" Sieghart Saladin [
Seven Great Powers
], North Saint "Loyal Hound Knight" Jeda, Tribal Knight Herpel, Iron Knight Atmos Roland, "Dwarf Knight" Pyi Ku, his granddaughter "Screaming Princess"
Lauri Shirone
. With tensions rising around them between Asura, the King Dragon Kingdom, and Ogre God Empire. He then announces it is their time to become independent and sets out his forces for his plan to become independent from the King Dragon Kingdom.

Power and Abilities

Intellectual Abilities

Pax Jr. is a clever man and during his time at the Asura Royal Academy, he is able to quickly grasp the concept of Mathematics,
Magical Arts
 even entirely theoretical concepts but he holds a strong interest in the fields of territory management and economics for his ambition of governing a land that catches the attention of the teachers but ignored by the aristocrat students. By his second year in the academy, he is top of the class when it comes to academics while Sieghart is top in combat but is able to catch up in his academics with Pax Jr's help.

Pax Jr. is also able to speak the languages of
Human Tongue
,
Fighting God Tongue
 and
Beast Race
 Language that he plans to utilize when it comes to fulfilling his ambitions and negotiating with various races to achieve his goal.
