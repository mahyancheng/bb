Summary

Several months have passed, Rudeus steadily grows his reputation as an adventurer and the citizens of Rosenburg calls him "Quagmire". Due to the winter season, most adventurer are barely doing any missions so Rudeus takes a rankless mission to gather snow. After completing the mission, Rudeus spots Counter Arrow party Timothy, Suzanne and Patris but they look in despair.
