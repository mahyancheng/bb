 established by

 and
Linia Dedoldia
.

Background

In
K427
, the Rude Mercenary Company was founded by Aisha and Linia in order to find a way for her to pay off Linia's debt to
Rudeus
 when he bought her from slave dealers. Since she was useless at house work, causing discord within the
Greyrat Family
, they needed to find another way for her to pay off her debt. Monopolizing on her charisma to attract people and using the start up money given to her by Rudeus, with Aisha managing her, the two were able to create a company.

The Rude Mercenary Company provides mercenary jobs such as hiring bodyguards, it is also a recruiting ground for able people to run
Zanoba
's doll stores, and the
ORSTED Corporation
.

Uniform

The Rude Mercenary Company Uniform is a gloomy black coat with a yellow rat embroidered on the back. It was designed and implemented by Aisha, after getting advice from
Nanahoshi Shizuka
, to help build solidarity within the group.
