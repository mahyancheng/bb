Summary

This is the twenty-second volume of the
Mushoku Tensei
 light novel, which covers the Organization arc. Its contents corresponds to the earlier half of volume 22 of the web novel.

Synopsis

REUNION WITH PAST ENEMIES

An apostle of Hitogami, who had been hiding until now, was finally discovered. Rudeus, who is now gathering his strength to fight, decides to persuade Atofe, the immortal demon king who reigns in the Demon Continent to make her a powerful ally.

However, negotiations are difficult for the group of Rudeus. "Atofe-sama. Could you please listen to me before a fight?" Rudeus's party fights against a demon king that can't understand words.
