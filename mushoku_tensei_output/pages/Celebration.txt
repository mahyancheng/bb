Celebration
 is the 86th chapter of the
Mushoku Tensei

manga adaptation
. This chapter also announces that the Manga series will be on hiatus until February 2023.

Cover Characters

Rudeus Greyrat

Nanahoshi Shizuka

Zanoba Shirone

Elinalise Dragonroad

Cliff Grimoire

Summary

After receiving advise from the other Special Students, Nanahoshi completes her new
Magic Circle
 is able to summon a plastic bottle from her home world. With the summoning successful, Elinalise teases Nanahoshi being so close to Rudeus but then dismisses it as a joke and suggests they celebrate. Nanahoshi accepts the suggestion and as they head out, the rest of their friends join them.
