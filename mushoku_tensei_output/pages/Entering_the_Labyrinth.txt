Summary

Rudeus, Paul, Elinalise, Geese and Talhand enters the Teleportation Labyrinth where Geese reminds Rudeus on the
Roles
 of each party member while Talhand reminds him not to use Fire Magic to avoid consuming the oxygen. With the Geese leading through the labyrinth with the "An Exploratory Account of the Teleportation Labyrinth" Book to guide them, they manage to easily go beyond the 1st and 2nd floor. But when they reach the 3rd floor, Rudeus can sense Roxy...
