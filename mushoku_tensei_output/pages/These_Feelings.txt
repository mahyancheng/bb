These Feelings
 (この気持ち
Kono Kimochi
) is the tenth episode of the
second season
 (34th overall)
Mushoku Tensei
 anime series originally broadcasted on September 10, 2023.

Synopsis

Rudeus contiues his research on Teleport and
Summoning Magic
 and begins to cooperate with Nanahoshi, who came from the same world as him, even though she was not reincarnated. Fitz feels a strange bond between them, even though he does not know why and begins to feel depressed. However, Rudeus also begins to have feelings for Fitz, even though he is a man.

Plot

Fitz
 continues to feel jealous how
Rudeus
 is spending most of his time assisting in
Nanahoshi
's experiments now, but remains too afraid to reveal her identity to him. One year after enrolling in the university, Rudeus notices that the other students are in awe of him thanks to the reputation he had built up defeating Fitz,
Linia
,
Pursena
, and
Badigadi
, but turns down suggestions to take a leadership role.

Later, he receives an invitation from
Soldat
 to attend a clan reunion. After the reunion, he parts with
Elinalise
 and
Cliff
, who plan to do some adventuring to give Cliff experience. After meeting Fitz again in town and in the library, Rudeus realizes that he has romantic feelings for Fitz, and begins to have doubts that Fitz is male.

During another meeting in the library, Fitz accidentally falls on top of Rudeus, and he is able to confirm that she is in fact female. Fitz denies this and flees in embarrassment. Rudeus realizes that for a brief moment, he felt an erection. Now knowing Fitz's true sex and that she holds the key to curing his impotence, Rudeus decides to start a relationship with her.

Adaptation Notes

This episode adapts the following material:

Light Novel Volume 9

Side Story: Sylphiette (Part 4)

Chapter 7: A Day at the University of Magic

Chapter 8: Clueless, but Perceptive

Differences with the Light Novel:

The event where Fitz also joins Rudeus and Nanahoshi on their research is cut.

The event where Badigadi and Rudeus talks about
Battle Aura
 and
Laplace
 is cut.

The event that Goliade is terrified of Rudeus is changed to Goliade simply avoiding him.
