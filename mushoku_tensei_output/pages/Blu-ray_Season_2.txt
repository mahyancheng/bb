The Blu Ray for the
Anime Season 2
 adaptation will be compiled into four Blu-ray volumes similar to the Season 1
Blu-ray
 impilation. The first two volumes include episodes from the first cour (including
Episode 0
 while the next two volumes will include episodes from the second cour. Each volume has a  limited special edition that comes with special benefits along with additional store benefits from respective sellers.

Blu-ray Specifications:

Layer: Dual-layer (BD50G)

Resolution: 1080p High-resolution

Pulse-code Modulation: Linear PCM 2.0ch stereo

Aspect Ratio: 16:9 Widescreen

Discs: 2 (+1 OST CD for Ch. 1 & 3)
