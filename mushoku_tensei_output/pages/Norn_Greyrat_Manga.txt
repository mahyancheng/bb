Summary

Norn recalls his encounters with Rudeus and how she came to hate him. Then she recalls her life in Millishion where she is always compared to Aisha which causes her to develop an inferiority complex. This remains until shereunites with Rudeus in Sharia and she feels like she is on her own even though Rudeus permits her to live in the academy dorms. Going back to the present she sees Rudeus in front of her, sitting down watching her...
