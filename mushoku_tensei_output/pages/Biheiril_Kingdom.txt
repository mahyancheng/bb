The
Biheiril Kingdom
 is a Human/Orc kingdom located in the northenmost part of the
Central Continent
 and besides Onigashima, the orcs' homeland. Orcs are highly respected by humans and vice-versa, this is because a party of human adventurers had saved the orcs from the ocean-race in the past, causing the two to become closer.

The
Superd
