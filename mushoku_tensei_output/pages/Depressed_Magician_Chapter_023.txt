Summary

Soldat and Big Bro are attacked by a group of former adventurers where the old man tries to fend them off and tells them to run away but his wooden legs were cut off as Soldat drags Big Bro away whose hand was cut off during the attack. As Soldat decides to press on, Big Bro tells Soldat to live on before bleeding to death.
