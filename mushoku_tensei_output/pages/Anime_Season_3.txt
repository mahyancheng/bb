Announcements

At the ending of the
Anime Season 2

Succession
 episode, a teaser is previewed with the logo of the Mushoku Tensei is changing from II to III and Rudeus' speaking of the foreshadowing events for the season.

On June 30, 2024, the official Mushoku Tensei for x announces that Season 3 is in production with the teaser from the Succession episode.

On July 1, 2024, the official Mushoku Tensei youtube channel TOHO animation チャンネル also releases the teaser.(see Promotional Videos)

On July 6, 2025, the official Mushoku Tensei X account announces that Season  is scheduled for Broadcast in 2026 along with the release of the 1st teaser that highlights the events of Rudeus' life in
Sharia
, foreshadowing his encounter with Perugius Dola, Atoferatofe Rybak,
battle
 against Orsted and reunion with Eris.
