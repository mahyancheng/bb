Drama CD Booklet 『Mushoku Tensei ~Isekai Ittara Honki Dasu~ Metastatic Labyrinth』

This Drama CD is divided in 2 parts and take place in the events of
Volume 12
, and includes a booklet with a short novel.

Synopsis

Rudeus joins to his father Paul to explore a dangerous labyrinth to rescue his mother. Along the way, she helps Roxy, a woman who used to be his tutor. The exploration of the labyrinth progressed better than expected because she joined the party, but for Rudeus it was too big to overcome the thing that was waiting at the deepest part !? A battle against the Hydra that shocked many readers into a drama CD. At that time it becomes clear what my father was thinking!
