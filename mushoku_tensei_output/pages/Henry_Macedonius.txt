Henry Macedonius
 is the grandson of
Lucy Greyrat
 and the partner of
Luicelia Superdia
 who currently works under as an agent of the Ogre God Empire.

Personality

Henry is described as a man whose presence was so faint it was almost imperceptible, this is thanks to his curse making him impersetible to others that makes him a perfect spy. Despite this, Henry is still a kind and sociable person, and a capable warrior making him a good partner to Lucelia.

Background

Henry is born with a curse that makes him invisible to other people, causing no one to recognize him and no one to remember him, including his own father who struggled to recognize him, and his own grandmother
Lucy Greyrat
, who couldn't even remember that Henry was her grandson. The only exception was the Superd tribe who, thanks to their third eye, can recognize Henry. Henry becomes an ally of the ogre kingdom with Lucelia Superdia, becoming Lucelia's partner.

Plot

Lucelia is tasked with hunting down a spy from the kingdom of Asura, after killing the spy in one of Rudeus' laboratories, Henry finds a copy of volume 29 of Rudeus' diary and takes it to
Ars Greyrat
, who now, an old man, begins to tell them the story of his youth and his life with
Aisha Greyrat
.

Curses
