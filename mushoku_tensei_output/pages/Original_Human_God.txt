Plot

A featureless man with a mosaic covered face and while he was the weakest, he was also the most respected by all the other gods and was considered their leader. he was the one who suggested that all the gods come together and share their knowledge and technology with each other for the benefit of all worlds. It is due to these meetings that the Human God shared various wisdoms from his world such as the use of "Language" and "Letters" to the other Gods that greatly advanced their civilizations.
