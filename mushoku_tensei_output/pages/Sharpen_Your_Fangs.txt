Summary

Eris finds a peninsula where she spends most of her day practice her swings everyday, eat when she's hungry and returns to the Sword God Style Main Dojo afterwards as memories of her battle against Orsted continue to linger. On her return to the main dojo, Eris encounters Auber which turned to a battle.
