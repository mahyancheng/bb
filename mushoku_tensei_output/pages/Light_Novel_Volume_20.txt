Summary

This is the twentieth volume of the
Mushoku Tensei
 light novel, which covers the Cliff arc. Its contents corresponds to volume 20.5 and the earlier portion of volume 21 of the web novel.

Synopsis

CLIFF HOMECOMING

Rudeus
 returns with
Zanoba
 from Shirone Kingdom to Ranoa Kingdom. One day, a letter arrives to
Cliff
, who is graduating from the Magic University of Sharia, from his grandfather in
Millis
. As with Zanoba, he suspected that may be a trap of
Hitogami
.

Rudeus also receives a letter from the Latreia family,
Zenith's
 parents' house. What is waiting for Rudeus and the others who came to
Millishion
?
