Summary

Gal Farion is reading a letter and gets angry then cramples and throws the paper near Eris who tells him off while she throws the letter to the bin. Gal in response also tells her off that she still hasn't mastered the Longsword of Light despite having figured something out. Gal then tells her to go kill the sender of the letter with a warning that if she doesn't master the Longsword of Light, she will die.
