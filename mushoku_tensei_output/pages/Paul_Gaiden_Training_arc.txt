Summary

Paul Gaiden: Runaway arc
 is the second story released for the mobile game and supervised by the author.

Synopsis

Armored Dragon King Year 399, Paul is 12 years old.

He was born as the eldest son of a high class nobleman, but he abandoned the name of his family and the privileges of nobility, and became just Paul.

He decides to travel out of his domain, and looking for a place to stay, he breaks into a swordsmanship dojo in the town and decides to use it as an lodging.

During the search for a place to stay, a mysterious man appears and Paul fights him, but is easily defeated and loses his consciousness.

When he wakes up, he finds a girl who calls herself Lilia in front of him. ...
