The
Mushoku Tensei
 anime adaptation will be compiled into four Blu-ray volumes. The first two volumes include episodes from the first cour while the next two volumes will include episodes from the second cour. Each volume has a  limited special edition that comes with special benefits along with additional store benefits from respective sellers.

Blu-ray Specifications:

Layer: Dual-layer (BD50G)

Resolution: 1080p High-resolution

Pulse-code Modulation: Linear PCM 2.0ch stereo

Aspect Ratio: 16:9 Widescreen

Discs: 1 (+1 OST CD for Ch. 1 & 3)
