Magic Tools and Magic Items in the Manga



Magic Tools
 (
魔道具
,

Ma Dōgu
)
 are specially crafted equipment that feature a carved magic circle. When a user channels their mana through the tool and recites an incantation, the magic circle activates, unleashing the desired magical effect. As long as the user has remaining
Mana
, the magic tool can be used repeatedly
.
Magic Items
 (
魔力付与品
,

Maryoku Fuyo-hin
)
, on the other hand, are objects infused with mana that possess unique and specific abilities. By performing a specific action or trigger, the user can activate the item's magical effect. Magic Items have a limited number of uses per day, but their mana reserves naturally replenish over time
.

Aside from the direct use of
Magic
, Magic Tools and Magic Items are developed and manufactured by the Magician Guild in
Sharia
 to aid people in adventures or even their everyday life.

List of Magic Potions

Mana Recovery Therapy (Magic Potion)

Five years after the final battle with Geese. Rudeus has been researching mana recovery to improve Orsted’s mana recovery rate. Eventually, he produced a potion that restored mana, but for some reason, Orsted’s mana didn’t recover.

Possibly because of the difference in the quality of mana between the human race and dragon race or an entirely different reason.

The potion itself was quite popular among the adventurers so their true intentions weren't completely in vain.

The mana potion became such a great success that the era of swordsmen being the strongest ended. Not only that, but it was helpful in improving the magician’s social status as well.
