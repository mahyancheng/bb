Asura Kingdom
 and opposes the
First Prince

king of Asura Kingdom
 and establishes the group to support her ascent. But due to Ariel experiencing numerous assassination attempts since childhood that traumatizes her, Ariel refuses and would spend her days having tea at the
White Lilies Garden
 until the Teleport Incident occurs.
