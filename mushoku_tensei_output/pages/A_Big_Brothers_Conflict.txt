Summary

Rudeus is at a loss when he realizes it is his fault that Norn locked herself in her room. Sylphiette, Linia and Pursena encourages him and also tries to give him advice on what to do but Rudeus still does not know how to talk to Norn. Rudeus enters the Girl's Dormitory with the help of Linia and Pursena while Sylphiette arranges a distraction. After entering Norn's room and a brief exchange, Rudeus tries to apologize on what has happened but still unable to find the words to tell Norn as he recalls his similar experience from his previous life.
