Summary

A few days after Norn and Aisha begins living at the Greyrat House, Rudeus receive Norn and Aisha's test results from Vice Principal Jinas that they took the other day. Back at the Greyrat House, Rudeus delivers the results and asks them what are their plans. Aisha still wants to continue working as a maid while Norn makes a decision that surprises Rudeus and Sylphiette.
