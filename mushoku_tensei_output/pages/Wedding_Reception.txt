Wedding Reception
 (披露宴
Hirōen
) is the fourteenth episode of the
second season
 (38th overall)
Mushoku Tensei
 anime series originally broadcasted on April 14, 2024.

Synopsis

In this
world
, when people get married, they hold a party at their home, so Rudeus prepares for it with Sylphy. And on the day of the party, while Ariel, Zanoba, and other members of Ranoa Magic Academy were in attendance, Elinalise expression seems different than usual. Meanwhile, Rudeus is summoned by Ariel...

Plot

Rudeus and Sylphie decide to organize the customary wedding reception feast, and invite all of their friends and classmates to attend where Sylphy personally wants Elinalise and Nanahoshi to be invited.

Rudeus and Sylphy then goes to the
Commerce District
 to shop the materials for the wedding where Sylphy wants to buy Rudeus some new clothes to which Rudeus agrees although has fond memories of his current robe that he has been wearing since his time at the
Demon Continent
.

At the clothes store, the shopkeeper offered various clothes that matches Rudeus' preference for water and earth magic but Sylphy chooses a gray robe that is made of
Lucky Rat
 pelts from the Demon Continent which also makes it expensive. But thanks to the patronage of Luke, the shopkeeper gives a discount after learning that Rudeus is also a Greyrat.

Rudeus and Sylphy continues with their shopping where they buy kitchen wares, dining wares, furniture and various foods. That night they went to the bath together where Sylphy tells Rudeus that he will do the cooking and after a few more exchanges, Rudeus carries Sylphy to the bedroom.

A few days later, the day of the wedding reception arrives and most of their friends have arrived except
Soldat Heckler
,
Jinas Halfas
 while Badigadi made a surprise entrance during Rudeus' speech. At course of the reception, the guests each gave their well wishes but Sylphy asks Nanahoshi what she meant when she said that Rudeus came from the same place to which Nanahoshi responds that she just assumed it since they both can speak Japanese.

When Cliff and Elinalise gave their congratulations, Elinalise suddenly broke down in tears to which Sylphy confirms that Elinalise is indeed her grandmother. Rudeus suggests that Cliff take Elinalise to a room upstairs to calm her down while jokingly whispering to Elinalise that she did became related to
Paul
 that she never wanted.

The guests eventually leaves for the night and upon seeing Elinalise feeling better, Sylphy have a talk with Elinalise while Rudeus and Cliff talks to Ariel who wishes for Rudeus to duel Luke with swords.

At Ariel's signal, the duel to begin so Rudeus and Luke both charge at each other. Rudeus strikes Luke with
Sword God Style Arm Chop
 causing Luke to drop his sword but insists to continue fighting so Rudeus knocks him down before he can pick up his sword and Ariel declares Rudeus to be the winner. Ariel then tells Rudeus how Sylphy helped them throughout their journey and her plans that she will someday return to
Asura Kingdom
 where she will either win the throne or die trying and asks Rudeus to stop Sylphy from joining her and leaves after hearing Rudeu's answer.

Cliff informs Sylphy and Elinalise about the duel that just concluded and they all settle down at the living room once again. While Sylphy and Elinalise talks by the fireplace, Cliff informs Rudeus that he wants to cure Elinalise's
curse
 and needs his help to do it to which he accepts. After Elinalise and Cliff leaves for the night, Sylphy plans to grow her hair out and they also retire for the night.
