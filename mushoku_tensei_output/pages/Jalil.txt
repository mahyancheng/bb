Jalil was a member of Lugonia Race, an adventurer and temporary member of
Dead End
.

Appearance

Jalil looked like an anthropomorphic lizard with green skin.

Personality

Jalil was somewhat cowardly and very easily intimidated. He was prone to committing petty crime for easy money, but he was actually a decent person as he upheld his end of the bargain pretty well when they had an agreement with Dead End.

Background

He was the owner of a Pet shop and member of P-hunter, a party that kidnap pets and when the owners publish a petition in the adventure guild, they returned the pets for the reward.

After he was caught by
Roman
, he and
Vizquel
 were blackmailed and started to work for him.

Plot

Volume 3 - Juvenile Period - Adventurer Chapter

Jalil is a petty criminal that specialize in lost pets with Roman and Vizquel. They were originally running a scam where they were stealing pets and claiming the lost reward later on. After that
Dead End
 catch them and kill Roman. They are forced to collaborate with
Dead End
.

Volume 14 - Young Man Period - Summoning Chapter

It was mentioned that he got killed 2 years ago while taming a magic beast.
