Summary

Light Novel Volume 9
 is the ninth volume of the
Mushoku Tensei
 light novel.

Synopsis

BLAST FROM THE PAST!

Life at the
Ranoa University of Magic
 suddenly gets more interesting when Rudeus discovers the mysterious Fitz’s true identity. What will this unforeseen reunion bring? And what if it is too late for him and his long-lost friend to repair the bond they once had?
