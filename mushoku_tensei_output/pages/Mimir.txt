Plot

Mimir and the party traveled to Rosenburg in the
Northern Regions of the Central continent

Rudeus

In a mission in Trier Forest, a blizzard greatly lowers the visibility of the party and and they were ambushed by a group of
Snow Buffaloes
 that killed him and forced the party to leave Sara behind.

Legacy

Timothy confirms to Rudeus that Mimir is dead and was regretful of his decision to retreat and leaving Mimir and Sara behind.

Suzanne consoles Timothy that the party followed his command and they would all have been wiped out if they stayed behind.

Rudeus searches for Sara in the forest alone where he kills the Snow buffaloes and was able to find the remains of Mimir's head and places it on his bag.

After rescuing Sara, Rudeus informs her what happened to Mimir and the latter carries Mimir's remains back to Rosenburg to be buried.
