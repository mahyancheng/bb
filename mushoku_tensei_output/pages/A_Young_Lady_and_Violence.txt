A Young Lady and Violence
 (お嬢様と暴力
Ojōsama to Bōryoku
) is the fifth episode of the
Mushoku Tensei
 anime series originally broadcasted on February 7, 2021.

Synopsis

Rudy arrives in the city of Roa, where he's supposed to spend the next five years tutoring the daughter of the local lord. His student, however, is more than he bargained for, and he'll have to fight to start his first ever job, let alone keep it.

Plot

As Ghislaine takes Rudeus away, he reads a letter from Paul explaining that he found a tutoring job for him to pay for his and Sylphiette's studies, but certain that his attachment to Sylphiette is hindering his development, Paul forbids him from returning home or meeting her for the next five years. In the city of Roa, Rudeus meets his employer, Philip Boreas Greyrat, who is also the mayor and his father's cousin, and is tasked to teach Philip's daughter Eris. However, once Rudeus is introduced to Eris, she acts hostile towards him. Certain that Eris will not listen to him at all, Rudeus comes with a plan to earn her trust by faking their kidnapping with her father's consent. Rudeus and Eris are captured and after earning her cooperation, he uses his powers to escape their captors with her. Back in Roa, Rudeus discovers that their kidnapping was not fake and one of Philip's servants is working with the kidnappers. Rudeus fights the bandits to protect Eris until Ghislaine comes to their rescue, killing them and arresting the servant. Once they return home, Eris accepts Rudeus as her tutor and he begins living in her house.

Differences with the light novel:

A short PoV of Paul after his fight with Rudeus was cut.

In the light novel, when Rudeus arrives to the mansion, he meets
Sauros Boreas Greyrat
 and his son Philip meanwhile in the anime only meets Philip.

Sauros is introduced in the next episode.

In the light novel, Eris was kicked in two different occasions by the kidnappers, meanwhile in the anime only one time.

In the light novel, Rudeus and Eris sleep and return to Roa in the next day, meanwhile in the anime they return in the same night

In the light novel, after Eris is kidnapped again, they cover her mouth with  clothes, in the anime no

In the light novel, after the fireworks, Rudeus use the wind magic True Sonic Boom to attack the kidnapper that have Eris , Meanwhile in the anime Rudeus use a unnamed earth magic.

In the light novel, the technique that use Ghislaine to kill the kidnappers was the Sword of Light, a technique that launch a long distance cut without moving of her position, in the anime she do a quick run and cut the kidnappers.

Is unknown if is the same technique (need verification).

In the light novel, the butler Thomas was caught off screen, meanwhile in the Anime he appears after the second kidnapping and confess his plan. After the battle, he was caught by Ghislaine.

In the light novel, after Eris return to the mansion, she is received by the maids, meanwhile in the anime by Philip.
