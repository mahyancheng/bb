Summary

This is the nineteenth volume of the
Mushoku Tensei
 light novel, which covers the Zanoba arc. Its contents corresponds to volume 20 of the web novel.

Synopsis

ZANOBA'S HOMECOMING

A letter has arrived from the Kingdom of Shirone to
Zanoba
, asking him to return home. It seems that they need defense forces to make up for the troops that have fallen due to a coup d'etat in the kingdom.

Rudeus
 realizes that the content of the letter is a trap by
Hitogami
. He can't prevent that Zanoba return to his home, so decides to accompanies him with
Roxy
. However, in the Kingdom, the one who awaited them was the Death God,
Randolph Marianne
, the number five of the
Seven Great Powers
.

Is possible for Rudeus to destroy Hitogami's plot in a situation where he doesn't know who is an enemy or an ally?
