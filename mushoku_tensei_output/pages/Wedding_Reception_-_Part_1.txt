Summary

With the house purchased and the renovations completed, Rudeus shows Sylphy around the house and proposes to her. Days later, the wedding reception begins and all their friends attend the party congratulating the newly wed with Badigadi arriving late in a flashy display. While the party is ongoing, Elinalise has been gazing at Sylphy with something in mind.
