Synopsis

SKELETONS IN THE CLOSET

Having recently become tutor to the hot-headed young Eris, Rudy is elated to be deemed worthy by the eccentric side of his family. But the Boreas household seems to have more than a few dark secrets, and while the opportunities for knowledge and romance are tempting, there are some mysteries better left unsolved.
