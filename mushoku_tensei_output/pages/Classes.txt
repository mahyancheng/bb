Classes
 refer to titles or professions bestowed upon a person or a group that gives them their role or function in a party or even a country.

Although some Classes are granted by teachers or masters of a certain school, people has their personal freedom to call themselves whatever they wish and can also hold more than one title or class.

Lower Class Knight

Lower Class Knights are knights that are recognized "bodyguards" of the kingdom who are assigned by a lord of the kingdom to defend villages far from the capital from Monsters and serve as a mediator between villagers or farmers like
Paul Greyrat
 who is assigned in
Buena Village
.

Guardian Knight

Guardian Knights are knights from spare children of powerful nobilities who protects the Royal family. When an assassin is sent they die bravely to protect their the royal family, in a sense a sacrificial pawn while the child's parents grieve exaggeratedly on how brave their child was. The royal family acknowledges their grief by sending a medal of honor as recompense and to deepen their bonds between the nobility and the royal family.

Magician

Magicians
 (
魔術師
,

Majutsu-shi
)
 are individuals who possess the ability to use
Magic
 and cast
Magic Spells
. It has been stated that only 1 in 20 people are born with the  capacity to using
Mana
. Moreover, out of those individuals, only 1 in 20 can learn to master it and become a Magician. However, only 1 in 100 of those is actually capable of becoming advanced Magicians in only one element. The magician's ranking title is determined by the most advanced spell that a magician could cast.

Guardian Magician

Guardian Magicians are magicians who are also spare children of powerful nobilities to protect the Royal Family. Guardian Magician are generally paired with Guardian Knights. Although the Guardian Magician are given to those of the upper nobilities, Ariel insists that Sylphiette who saved her life replace his Derrick who gave his life to protect her.

Adventurer

Adventurers
 (
冒険者
,

Bōken-sha
)
 are individuals who accept quests categorized by the level of threat within the
Adventure Guild
. They receive rewards for completing these quests and can also sell materials obtained from
Monsters
 or treasures found in dungeons. By registering with the Adventure Guild, which is always present in large towns, individuals can become adventurers, accept quests, and form parties with other adventurers.

Scout
 (
スカウト
,

Sukauto
)
 is a role generally fulfilled by an Archer
 or Thief and when it comes to exploring an uncharted labyrinths, they position ahead of the party to look out traps or provide intel of incoming enemies like their numbers and also uses various tools to ensure their party does not fall to any traps or be overwhelmed by enemies like having a marker on their boots to confirm its a safe place to step in for the party.
 They are also generally in charge of navigation and map charting.
 When a retreat is called by the party, Scouts would also assist in the retreat such as using Smoke Grenades to obscure their retreat and also ensure everyone is falling back.

 (

,

)
 are roles fulfilled by Swordsmen or Warriors where they take the frontline to engage the enemy first while the rest move to position or draw their fire to prevent them from attacking their healers and attackers in the back line as they have the means to handle a head on attack to provide an opening for the Attackers to finish off their enemy.

When a retreat is called, Attackers also provide support like the Scout as the tanks disengage their enemy or provide distractions or other hindrance to prevent their enemy from pursuing them to ensure a successful retreat.

Mercenary
 (

,

Yōhei

 are groups or people that completes a request of a "client" regardless of factions such as acting as their bodyguards for a certain period of time or a condition of the contract is met which is in a sense similar to Adventurers but more focused on combat. Slave






Julie
 during the time she was a slave in the Anime



Slaves are people who are generally sold to slave market form various reasons such as: those orphaned during a war, sold by their parents when crop harvests failed to save the family or voluntarily done to save the family or those who are unable to pay their debts. Unlike
Millis Continent
 and Southern
Central Continent
, most countries in the north has legalized slave trade and is a key part of their economy while there also rumors that slave trade is also done by the Thieves' Guild that smuggle these slaves to various government to serve as cannon fodder during war.

Slaves are also marked with a brand or magic seal to ensure they won't escape nor attack their master but this is at the choice of their owners.

Bard






Big Boys Orchestra - a party of Bards in the Anime



Bards, also known as Troubadours is a profession where they perform on a pub's stage to sing, play music for tips. In bigger cities, bards sign exclusive contracts with theaters. Adventurers can also become bards by turning their experiences into music or from fellow adventurers. Bards who share similar interests also tends to form parties and perform around the world especially if they specialize in different instruments.
