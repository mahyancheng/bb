Summary

This is the twenty-fourth volume of the
Mushoku Tensei
 light novel, which covers the first half of the Final Battle arc. This volume corresponds to the earlier half of volume 23 of the web novel.

Synopsis

SEEKING SUPPORT

Rudeus arrives at Irel City in Biheiril Kingdom to request Ruijerd’s support in his battle against Geese. The road to Ruijerd is a bumpy one, but after ten long years apart they finally reunite. Will Rudeus’s impassioned pleas for aid in the Laplace War reach Ruijerd’s heart? The answer might surprise you!

Adaptation Notes

Differences from the original web novels.

This volume covers the Web Novel Vol. 23 (Chapter 1-10)

Chapter 1: A Strategy Meeting: some minor change in the dialogue because some events in LN 22-23.

Chapter 5: Abyssal King Vita: include two new illusions of Vita, one about Sara, and one about Zenith.

Interlude: Somebody to Someone: New story about Norn before she travel with Cliff to the Superd Village.

Interlude: Vita and Raxos: New story about the past of Vita and his relation with Raxos and Hitogami.
