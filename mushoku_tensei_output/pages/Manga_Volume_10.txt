Synopsis

HOMECOMING

After a disastrous meeting with the deadly, enigmatic Orsted, Rudeus just barely manages to escape with his life. But there is light at the end of the tunnel, because he and his companions have at last reached their destination: Rudeus’s home, Fittoa. After a magical calamity flung its denizens around the world, what has become of Fittoa? And what fate awaits those who have finally returned?.
