Summary

This is the twenty-fifth volume of the
Mushoku Tensei
 light novel, which covers the Decisive Battle arc Part 2. This volume corresponds to the latter half of volume 23 of the web novel.

Synopsis

DEAD END AGAIN!　WHO WILL BE THE WINNER OF THE DECISIVE BATTLE?

While the communication tablet and the transfer magic circle in the various locations stop functioning, Rudeus and his friends gather at the village of the Superd tribe.
Just as Geese had hoped, a strike force from the Kingdom of Biheiril was approaching the village of the Superds. Along with the strike force, the Northern God Kalman III, the former Sword God Gal Farion, and the Ogre God Malta appear.
With the world's strongest fighting force against them, the battle for the fate of Rudeus and his friends is about to begin!
"...... I am Rudeus Greyat "Quagmire", a subordinate of the Dragon God.
I'm Alexander Karlman Ryback, the North God!
In the meantime, what will happen to Geese who don't show up for the final battle? ......
