Summary

Rudeus and Eris try to board a ship to take them back to the Asura Kingdom, but the ship fare is inflated due to discrimination against Rujeird, they head back to a tavern and come up with ways to earn money but they just end at a roadblock. Meanwhile, Roxy heads out into town after being intrigued about the group,
Dead End
, after hearing rumours about them. As Rudeus comes back from purchasing food, she stumbles upon a
little girl
 who was been harassed by a strange man, he rescues only to find that she didn't want to be rescued. She introduces herself as Kishirika Kishirisu, the great demon lord empress. At first, Rudeus assumed that she was just an imposter who was a big fan and played along, then to her delight she asked him make a wish. After three silly wishes, she then stabs his eye to grant him a magic eye.
