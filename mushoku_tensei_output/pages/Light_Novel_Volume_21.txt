Summary

This is the twenty-first volume of the
Mushoku Tensei
 light novel, which covers the Zenith arc. Its contents corresponds to the latter portion of volume 21 of the web novel.

Synopsis

ZENITH IS KIDNAPPED

Zenith was abducted by someone and disappeared. Rudeus scrambles the city of Millishion to find her. "I am the son of Zenith. My dad told me to protect my mother even if I die". Is it possible to recover Zenith safely?
