Summary

For half a year, Eris has been training at a peninsula as her routine for her sword training as the memories of Rudeus and Orsted are still in her mind. On her return to Gal's dojo, a man tries to ask questions but she cuts him off then tells him to move. The man still tries to converse with her so Eris decides to kill him.
