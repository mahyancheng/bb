Missed Connections
 (すれ違い
Surechigai
) is the thirteenth episode of the
Mushoku Tensei
 anime series originally broadcasted on October 10, 2021.

Synopsis

Roxy is looking for Rudeus and the others who have disappeared. While searching for clues, Roxy hears rumors of the infamous Dead End, and comes to Wind port with Elinalise and Talhand, acquaintances of Paul. In the meantime, Rudeus and his friends make a deal with the smuggling organization of Gallus, and cross over to the continent of Millis, which they have been longing for on the condition that they free the slaves in the hideout......

Adaptation Notes

This episode adapts the following material:

Light Novel Volume 4

Side Story: Missed Connections, Extra Story

Chapter 4: The Sage on Board

Chapter 5: The Demon in the Warehouse

Chapter 6: The Beastfolk Children

Differences with the light novel:

Roxy's feelings about leaving and returning to the Demon Continent were cut.

The description of Elinalise and Tallhand's equipment was cut.

The details about howRoxy's group formed and made their way to the Demon Continent as well as the Asura Kingdom's actions were cut.

A short scene where Rudeus sells the Lizard that was used as transport was cut.

A short scene where the boat that was traveling to Zant port was attacked by a octopus and defended by a S-rank party was cut.

In the Light Novel, the smuggler hideout was a warehouse near the wharf.

In the light novel, there were seven children - some were beast race and some were elves. The anime has only six children and they are all beast race.

The anime kills one of the beast children.

A short scene where Ruijerd and Rudeus gathered and burned the corpses of the smugglers was cut.

In the light novel, the trap that maintained captive the holy beast was a magic barrier that came from a tool of the upper floor and a chain in his body.

A short thought of how the two beast man looks similar to Ghislaine was cut.

In the light novel, the voice magic used by Gyes is with both hands near his mouth.

In the light novel, there is a short scene where Gustav meets Ruijerd which was cut.
