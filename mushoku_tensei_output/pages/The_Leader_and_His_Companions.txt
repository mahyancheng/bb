Summary

Aisha sleeps with Rudeus when Sylphiette is on Magician Guardian duty for the night to which Rudeus does not mind, after realizing the situation of the two sisters while in Millishion. One month has already passed and Norn still stays at the Greyrat House every 10 days but their relationship as siblings are not yet that close. At the Ranoa Magic Academy, even Linia and Pursenia notices Rudeus is feeling down and after a conversation, they give Rudeus a bag but also tells him to open the bag when nobody is watching.
