Summary

This is the first volume of the
Mushoku Tensei
 light novel.

Synopsis

English (Seven Seas)
DEATH IS ONLY THE BEGINNING!

Kicked out by his family and wandering the streets, an unemployed 34-year-old shut-in thinks he’s hit rock-bottom—just as he’s hit and killed by a speeding truck! Awakening to find himself reborn as an infant in a world of swords and sorcery, but with the memories of his first life intact,
Rudeus Greyrat
 is determined not to repeat his past mistakes. He’s going to make the most of this reincarnation as he sets off on the adventure of a second lifetime!.

Synopsis from Seven Seas.

Anime Special Edition

In commemoration of the anime, Volume 1 was re-released with a new cover. This version includes a special 24 pages booklet with a new short-story Named "Sylphiette and etiquette" and a showcasing the anime and light novel versions of the character designs of Rudeus, Sylphiette and Lilia.
