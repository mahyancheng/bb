Summary

After leaving her village, Roxy decides to travel to the southwest to the Rikarisu Town, because for some words of her teacher, she knows that is one of the biggest town near the Migurd village, also have a adventurer guild and roxy heard some incredibles adventurers about them. After some days and without knowing if she takes the right path, she was attacked by a group of Pax coyotes, she was able to defeat some monster, but in the end she was saved by a group of adventurers and indicates to her the localization of the town, but this cost her all her money.

Roxy was able to arrive in the Town and register as adventurer, when she tried to pick a E- Rank quest to collect some flowers, but some adventurer tried to advise her to give up, because it was dangerous if she went alone but she ignored his advice and decided to take it anyway. When she arrived at the place of the flowers, Roxy was attacked by a group of gillray rats and was unable to defend herself because their attacks interrupted her chant. Suddenly the adventurer that wanted to stop her to take the quest and his party arrived and started to help Roxy, and she was able to chant Firewall and defeat the rats. After the battle, he presents himself as Dale and the party asks Roxy compensation because they saved her, but because she is penniless, they decide that she needs to work as a party member with them for some time. After returning to the town, Roxy's money for the quest went to the pool of the party and also for the first time since she left her home, she was able to eat a proper meal.
