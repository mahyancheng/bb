Rudeus

Gyes

Appearance

Gustav is an old man with dark skin and white fur. He has white hair and brown eyes, and his tan is similar to that of his son and daughter. He also has beast ears and a tiger tail. His fur is very thick. His tail is the same color as his hair, and he usually wears common tribal clothing.
