Appearance

Tersena has short gray hair and brown eyes, with a pair of floppy dog ears on top of her head and a fluffy tail behind, indicating her Adoldia heritage. She has a slim hourglass figure and a well-developed chest. She's very well endowed, even by animal breed standards, more so than Minitona.

Personality

Tersena is shy and often hides behind Minitona. She is often forced to resound out Minitona when she loses her temper, and like Minitona, Tersena has become friends with Eris and Rudus.
