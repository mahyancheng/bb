This is the fourth volume of the
Mushoku Tensei
 manga adaptation.

Synopsis

“ALL YOU HAVE TO DO.... IS TRUST ME”

Rudeus Greyrat’s new life takes a shocking turn when the country of Roa is overcome by a blinding light, leaving the fate of his family and friends unknown. He and his student Eris are transported to the Demon Continent, a foreign land which is strange even to Rudy. When a mysterious figure, claiming to be a god and offering advice, Rudy must decide whether or not this stranger can be trusted. Can Rudy and Eria make it back home, or will this be the last new world in which Rudy ever awakens?
