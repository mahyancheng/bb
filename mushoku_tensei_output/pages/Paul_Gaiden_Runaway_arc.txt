Summary

Paul Gaiden: Runaway arc
 is the first story released for the mobile game and supervised by the author.

Synopsis

Born as the long-awaited eldest son of a high-ranking aristocrat,
Paul
 was already demonstrating a liberal attitude toward sexuality at the age of five.

His father, Amaranth, instructed him to go to school in order to develop his education and awareness as a nobleman and Paul was sent to a school in the Milbots Region. However, he doesn't find appealing the school's lessons and ends up skipping all classes except for the sword fighting class.

There is no place for him here. ....

As a nobleman, he is torn between his family's disparity, the love of his family, and the various relationships at school, which eventually lead to a huge rift.
