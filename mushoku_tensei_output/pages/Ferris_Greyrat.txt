Ferris Greyrat
 (フェリス・グレイラット Ferisu Gureiratto) Is the granddaughter of
Ars
 and great-granddaughter of
Rudeus
, at whose death she was present and she is associated with
Orsted
.

Appearance

Ferris Greyrat is identical to her Great-grandmother
Eris
; She has long, straight, red hair, red eyes, a single-fold eyelids and a determined line to her chin.

Personality

Not much is known about Ferris' personality but Rudeus sees her as slightly spoiled because she took his bracelet without asking. However, she also showed guilt and remorse (after taking the bracelet) and respect for Rudeus.

Background

Ferris Greyrat Is the daughter of
Leroy Greyrat
 and was born approximately in the year K476.

Plot

Light Novel Volume 26

Story 1: The Final Dream

When she tried to take the bracelet off Rudeus while he was sleeping, he woke up again and Ferris ran to tell her family. Later, Rudeus asked her if she took off his bracelet and in tears she confessed and Rudeus gave her the bracelet.

Many years later, she helped her relatives and Orsted to seal the
Man God
 and they noticed Rudeus in the Void and everyone said their goodbyes before they went through a teleport circle again.

Equipment

Sword:
 Ferris is shown to own a sword.

Dragon God's Bracelet :
 Its crest was engraved by Dragon God with the ability to block Hitogami's future foresight from seeing the future of the wearer. Feris inherited it from Rudeus.

Achievements

Sealing the Man God
