Synopsis

THE ADVENTURING LIFE

Roxy and the rest of her party set out from Rikarisu in search of their grandest adventure yet. Dale dreams of unearthing the secrets within an uncharted labyrinth, but in a life of sword and sorcery, things rarely go as planned. Instead, Roxy's path takes an unexpected turn...and soon, the Guild hires her for a secret, undercover mission!
