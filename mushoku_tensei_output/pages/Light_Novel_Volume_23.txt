Summary

This is the twenty-third volume of the
Mushoku Tensei
 light novel, which covers the Fourth Child arc. Its contents corresponds to the latter half of volume 22 of the web novel.

Synopsis

TO THE HEAVENS: THE TRIAL OF PERUGIUS

The fourth child Sieghard was born, with the same green hair as Laplace. However, one of the Perugius familiars who witnessed the scene, told to Rudeus and the others to come to the sky citadel with the baby.

"If this kid grows up and really causes a war with the human race then I will deal with it ... "Rudeus tries to prepare himself for his family.

Adaptation Notes

Differences from the original web novels.

This volume covers the last 4 chapter of Web Novel Vol 22

In the web novel, in a short event, Sieg was baptized in Perugius' castle, while in the light novel Perugius gave Rudeus a trial, and he travelled with his wives and Sieg to an old Ruins of the Laplace in the Divine Continent, and Sieg was baptized there.

New chapters of Rudeus and Eris traveling to Sword Sanctum and the Conflict Zone.

New Chapter about Geese and Badigadi.
