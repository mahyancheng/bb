Mushoku Tensei ~ Isekai Ittara Honki Dasu ~ Recollection
 (無職転生 ～異世界行ったら本気だす～ リコレクション) is a digital-only anthology of short stories written by
Rifujin na Magonote
 featuring illustrations by
Shirotaka
 which was published on March 25, 2022 in Japan and May 30, 2024 in English. The book is a compilation of short stories which play out between Volume 1 - 10 of the
Mushoku Tensei: Jobless Reincarnation
 light novel series, paired with an interview of the author
Rifujin na Magonote
.

Synopsis

DIGITAL EXCLUSIVE!

Experience the past adventures of the Jobless Reincarnation crew once again in this special collection! Enjoy more stories with some of your favorite characters during their early adventures—and tons more!

This Mushoku Tensei Recollection includes:

32 short stories set during the Light Novels 1-10.

A brand new short story exclusive to this collection.

Cover and color illustration gallery.

Character profiles.

Interview with author Rifujin na Magonote.
