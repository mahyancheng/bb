Summary

Rudeus is in shock upon learning that Norn has locked herself in her dormitory room while Sylphiette decides to go ahead and check on Norn. Recalling what happened to him in his past life, Rudeus concludes that someone could be bullying her and is willing to fight is willing to confront them regardless of their social standing so he tells Linia and Pursena to accompany him to Norn's classroom. After questioning Norn's classmates on what happened, Rudeus is in belief on whose at fault.
