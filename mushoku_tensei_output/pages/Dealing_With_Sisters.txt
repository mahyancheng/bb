Summary

Rudeus finishes his morning jogging and upon returning home, he sees Badigadi standing at the front door of their but he is actually staring at Ruijerd guarding the door. Badigadi realizes that Rudeus is there and departs after realizing the situation. After breakfast, Ruijerd is heading out since he has completed the request to bring Norn and Aisha to Rudeus but Norn wants to got with him. Back at home, Aisha has decided to become Rudeus' maid while Norn still is still struggling with his new life with her brother.
