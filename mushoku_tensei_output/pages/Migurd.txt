Traits





Male and Female Migurds in the Anime



Migurds have blue hair, small childish body and long lifespan of 200 years. Their physical growth stops when they reach middle school age and finally can grow older again after they reach 150 years.





Migurd's telepathic ability in the Anime



They have a special ability that allows them to communicate telepathically to each other depicted as lights on their forehead at a close distance without seeing each other, with the exception of
Roxy
.

Culture





Migurd Village in the Anime



Migurds live in the desert region of the Demon Continent and uses the shells of
Giant Stone Turtles
 for rooves of their homes.

The Migurd are closely related to the Superd and used to get treated much the same as they were. But like most races in the
Human World
, they also use the infamous tales of Superd to scare their children to go to bed.
