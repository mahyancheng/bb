Pilemon Notos Greyrat
 is the brother of
Paul Greyrat
 and the father of
Luke
. He was the head of Notos house until Luke took over the position under
Ariel
's order.

Appearance

He has a pointy nose and a short mustache with drawn back hair and a cleft chin. He is often seen sporting a suit as is common for nobles.

Personality

Pilemon is unconfident and is a person that cling to power and wealth. Unlike Paul, he is a cowardly opportunist. This can be seen with his unwillingness to stand up to
Darius Silva Ganius
, and his attempt to act as if he never betrayed Ariel after Darius' downfall.

Background

Pilemon is an Asuran noble that was supporting
Ariel
, but after a surprise attack against her in the Red Dragon's Upper Jaw, he received Intel that she had perished, resulting in him joining to the
First Prince
's Faction.
