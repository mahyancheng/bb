Detoxification Magic
 is one of the four systems of
Healing Magic
 (magic category), focused on curing poison or sickness. At higher stages, Detoxification Magic even be used to create poison or medicine to cure poisons. Status-affliction related Detoxification Magic is said to be
Saint-ranked
 and above, and very difficult to learn.

With Detoxification Magic, the large majority of conditions can be cured using just Elementary-level. However, particular illnesses, poisons produced by powerful monsters, or diseases that have reached an advanced stage will need a pinpoint incantation and a massive amount of magic power which required higher level Detoxification Magic.

The God-class Detoxification Magic is a single magic that cures『Magic Stone Disease』. However, its incantation is carefully guarded in the storage of the
Great Church of Milishion
.

Trivia

Detoxification magic has also been shown to enable sobriety caused from drinking alcohol, but it can't remove the intoxication or fix hangovers from overdrinking.
