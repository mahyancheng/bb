Summary

Rudeus, Cliff and Zanoba take turns on watch duty as night approaches in the "Haunted" House. Cliff hears movement of someone in the house and wakes up Rudeus and Zanoba to engage the "Ghost" but to Zanoba stops them from attacking the Ghost after grabbing with his arms. The 3 bring their findings back to Ranoa Magic Academy and Zanoba volunteers to conduct research.
