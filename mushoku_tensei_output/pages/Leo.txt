Leo
 is a Sacred Beast that the Doldia Tribe was serving. It was said that he was born from the Sacred Tree and that together with his partner will be the savior that the Doldia Tribe were waiting.

Appearance

Leo's size is about 2 meters, he has short limbs, but he will grow larger since he is still just a puppy.

His fur is silver.

Personality

Despite being a holy symbol, Leo is playful and mischievous, often sneaking away from its sacred house to meet Rudeus, who saved him from smugglers.

Background

He is the Sacred Beast, a type of magical beast born once every few hundred years that don’t have a proper name. It only appeared when the world faced a crisis, and when it became an adult it would set out alongside with a hero, using its great power to save the world.
