Summary

After a 34 year old NEET was kick out of his house by his surviving siblings, he finds himself hopeless and unfit for the world. He soon dies trying to save a group of students from a runaway truck in the rain. But to his surprise, he finds himself no longer on Earth, but reincarnated as a baby named
Rudeus Greyrat
, the first son of
Zenith
 and
Paul
. With a new life, Rudy decides to not make the same mistakes he did in his previous life, and secretly trains in magic in order to learn what he can. When he was 3 years old and was secretly training, he misused a spell and his parents discover his potential as a mage. While Zenith is overjoyed and Paul wanted to commit to instructing Rudy, they ultimately decide to have him study both magic and swordsmanship in the morning and afternoon respectively on
Lillia
's suggestion. Proceeding to hire a magic tutor, they are met by
Roxy Migurdia
.
