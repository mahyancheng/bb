Summary

Rudeus is asleep while Leo is watching over him at the Greyrat House Living Room. Sylphiette enters the room and sees him asleep then gives him a kiss and leaves. Roxy then enters the room also seeing this and also gives him a kiss as he sleeps then leaves. Eris enters the room and also gives him a kiss then carries him to his room.
