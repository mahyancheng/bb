Summary

This is the seventeenth volume of the
Mushoku Tensei
 light novel, which covers the Asura Kingdom Arc Part 2. Its contents corresponds to volume 18 of the web novel.

Synopsis

MISSION AGAINST HITOGAMI AND HIS APOSTLES

It's been a few weeks since they returned from the
library labyrinth
.
Rudeus
 accompanies
Ariel
 to the
Asura Kingdom
 to complete the Mission in making Ariel queen. In preparation for the decisive battle, the group prepares the stage at the royal castle of Ars.

On the day of the party that happens after overcoming many obstacles. Ariel take down the enemy power of the
First Prince
. However, the enemy also has a trump card hidden, ending up in a predicament...?. Can Rudeus protect Ariel in ascending to the throne?
