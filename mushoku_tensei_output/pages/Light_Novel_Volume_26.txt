Summary

This is the twenty-sixth volume and the final installment of the
Mushoku Tensei
 light novel series, which covers the Conclusion Arc. This volume corresponds to volume 24 of the web novel.

Synopsis

The last of Hitogami's apostles, Badigadi, appears with Geese. Rudeus and his team are confronted with the strongest opponent of all, who possesses the demonic power of being able to regenerate his body over and over again and is equipped with the legendary "Fighting God Armor" created by Laplace.
"The duel is on. I accept the duel. All of us here will fight with you."
What will happen in the inevitable, all-out battle to the death?　And what will be the outcome of the long battle with Hitogami? The story of Rudeus is now complete!
