Plot

Somar was one of the three children who bullied Sylphiette as a child. They pelted her with balls of mud and mocked her because of her hair color, telling her to return to the Demon Continent. Rudeus interferes and attacks him with his magic, after which they target him instead. This ends up being a fruitless endeavour as Rudeus easily evades their attacks.

He ends up in a few more confrontations with Rudeus, and every time is forced to go with his mother to Paul Greyrat where she complains about what his son did to Somal. Rudeus mentions that he seemed uncomfortable and later stops.

After the
Teleport Incident
, Somar ended up in
Millis Continent
. He was enslaved and forced to work as a male prostitute of some kind until Paul's companions kidnapped and rescued him, bringing him to their base in
Millishion
.

Trivia

•  In the Roxy Gets Serious manga, Teo is the one with black hair and Arlo is the one with blonde hair.

• In the Mushoku Tensei English light novel and the Roxy Gets Serious English manga. His name is written as “Somal” and not “Somar.” Although the name could be translated differently, depending on the language.
