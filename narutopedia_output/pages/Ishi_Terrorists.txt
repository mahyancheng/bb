The
Ishi Terrorists
 were a group of
missing-nin
 terrorists from
Ishigakure
 that possessed a collection of
kekkei genkai
. They held no allegiance to any other hidden villages and instead worked for the highest bidder.

Because of the Konjiki twins' abilities, this terrorist cell specialised in destroying buildings and merchant ships.
