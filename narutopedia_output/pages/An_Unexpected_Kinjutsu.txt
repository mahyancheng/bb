"

Naruto
 manga.

Summary

Tobi
 falls into the chasm, and
Konan
 continuously detonates exploding tags for ten minutes. Tobi survives, and states that he used
Izanagi
 to escape from her attack. After Tobi explains how Izanagi works, Konan tries to kill him. Her attack is interrupted by Tobi, and he uses a
genjutsu
 to force her to reveal the location of
Nagato
's corpse. Tobi then arrives at the shrine where Nagato is buried, and states that Nagato is the "third of the six paths" and a member of the
Uzumaki clan
.
