Kunijirō
 (
クニジロウ
) is a rich jeweller who started off poor and is an old acquaintance of
Tsunade
.

Background

Kunijirō started from nothing but later became rich from selling rare gems. At some point he became acquainted with Tsunade and they became friends.

Appearance

Kunijirō is a tan-skinned man with short greyish-blue hair, which slightly curls at the end. He also bears black-coloured eyes, a beauty mark, which is located on the left side of his face, and pink stud earrings. His mouth is also noticeably large.
