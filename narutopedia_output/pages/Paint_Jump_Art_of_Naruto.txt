Paint Jump: Art of Naruto
 is an instructional colouring book. Using
Naruto Uzumaki
 and
Sasuke Uchiha
 as examples,
Masashi Kishimoto
 offers tips on what tools and techniques to use to imitate his style, providing exact shades in both coloured pencil and
Copic marker
 and advice in the colouring process. The book contains seven pages of full-colour illustration samples and nineteen single-sided pages of line art that can be coloured in, as well as one final page with character illustrations and a paint palette.
