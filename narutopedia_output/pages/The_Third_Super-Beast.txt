"
The Third Super-Beast!
" (
第三の超獣　最大のライバル
,
Daisan no Chōjū, Saidai no Raibaru
) is episode 195 of the original
Naruto
 anime.

Synopsis

Tsunade
, fearing that
Rock Lee
 will overexert himself and once again end his career as a ninja, warns him not to accept missions or train too hard. At the same time, a student arrives in town and is able to beat Lee at
taijutsu
, dodging all his moves and using one that nearly breaks his ankle. The student goes out with
Might Guy
 on a
C-ranked
 mission to the
Land of Mountain Streams
 while Lee recovers, causing Lee to believe that Guy has abandoned him. It is then revealed that the student is an impostor, and he lures Guy into a trap as
Naruto
,
Neji
, and
Tenten
 arrive to support him.
