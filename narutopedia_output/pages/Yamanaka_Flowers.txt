Yamanaka Flowers
 (
やまなか花
,
Yamanaka Hana

Konohagakure
 that is owned by the
Yamanaka clan
, which has been in business since the
First Hokage
's era.

Inoichi
 usually ran the shop, until it was destroyed during
Pain's assault
. More than a decade after, a modern version of the shop is run by
Ino
 and
Sai Yamanaka
.
