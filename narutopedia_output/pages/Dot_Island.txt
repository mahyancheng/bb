Dotō Island.



Dotō Island
 (
ドトウ島
,
Dotō-jima
) is an island located in the
Land of Water
. Traditionally a fishing village, recent discovery of gold deposits have turned it into its main source of income and attracted people from all over, but its complex and soft ground make mining it dangerous.

Trivia

Written as 怒涛, "Dotō" means "surging waves".
