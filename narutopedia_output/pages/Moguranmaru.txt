Moguranmaru
 (
もぐらん丸
) was a giant
mole
, and the personal summon of
Muyami
.

Personality

Moguranmaru was of a silent disposition. It had also been described as a "clean-freak" by Muyami.

Appearance






Moguranmaru's full appearance.



It was a large mole with long white claws, brown fur and a spiked tail. It also wore shades, blue underwear, and a green scarf around its neck with an orange pouch bearing the
Kusagakure
 symbol on it which can be used to carry people in.

Abilities

Moguranmaru had a tail that can drill holes underground. Its skin was clean, sensitive and could detect
insects
 attached to it. Its claws were helpful for digging and moving underground rapidly.

Interlude

In Naruto's Footsteps: The Friends' Paths






Moguranmaru's death.



During the
Chūnin Exams
, Moguranmaru was summoned to aid Muyami and his teammates escape from
Team Kurenai
 after stealing their scroll. It was later summoned to battle Team Kurenai, but was
killed
 by
Shino
's kidaichū infestation.
