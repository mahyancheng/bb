Iyashi
 (
イヤシ
) is a
medical-nin
 from
Konohagakure
's
Medic Corps
.

Appearance

He wears the standard high-collared, full body uniform of the Konoha medical ninja. His uniform has also the Konoha Medic Corps emblem on the left side of his chest.

Abilities

As a medical-nin, Iyashi presumably has a high level of intellect, is well-versed in
medical ninjutsu
, and has excellent
chakra control
 to perform medical procedures. In the anime, he is able to perform the
Mystical Palm Technique
.
