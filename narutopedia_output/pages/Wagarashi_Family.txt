The
Wagarashi Family
 (
ワガラシ一家
,
Wagarashi Ikka
) is a clan in the
Land of Tea
. They are one of two clans who wants ownership over the
Degarashi Port
.

Overview

For many years, The Wagarashi family have had a rivalry with the
Wasabi Family
, in which they have fought for the ownership of the Degarashi Port. It is led by its founder, Kyūroku Wagarashi. The two families held a race called,
Todoroki Shrine Race
, to determine the ownership of the port. Another member of this clan is Fukusuke Hikyakuya, a runner who participated in the race on the clans behalf. Kyūroku had a long time deal with the
 Daimyō's minister
, bribing him on their behalf. This was ultimately ended when the
Tea Daimyō
 found out about it, where he dismissed his minister and made the Wagarashi family break up for their crimes.

Trivia

Some members of this clan appear as random enemy encounters in
Naruto: Path of the Ninja 2
.

"Wagarashi" (
和辛子
) can refer to "Japanese hot mustard".
