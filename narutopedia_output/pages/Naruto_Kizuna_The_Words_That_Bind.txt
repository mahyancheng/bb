Naruto Kizuna: The Words That Bind
 (
NARUTO -ナルト- 名言集　絆-KIZUNA-
,
Naruto: Meigenshū Kizuna
) is a two volume reflection on the first 63 volumes of the manga, exploring its global success and impact. Key scenes are selected to illustrate character arcs and/or analyse broader topics of the series. An interview with
Masashi Kishimoto
 is split across both volumes, and each volume concludes with critical readings of select subjects.
