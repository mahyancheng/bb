Kirisaki
 (
キリサキ
) is a
missing-nin
 from
Kirigakure
.

Background

At some point in the past, Kirisaki's medical misdeeds were discovered by Kirigakure, forcing him to leave his village and become a missing-nin.

Personality

Kirisaki is lecherous, selfish, opportunistic, and greedy. He also has a merciless streak as seen when he beat up and tried to murder a
young boy
 who stole his briefcase.

Appearance

Kirisaki has long white hair and hazel eyes. He wears a white shirt, an orange tie, a purple vest, black pants and a purple overcoat.

Abilities

Kirisaki is a skilled
medical-nin
, considered skilled enough to be able to work with
Hashirama
's cell. He uses scalpels in battle.

At some point Kirisaki was anonymously contacted for his services. He was offered a large sum of money to work with the famous cells of the First Hokage. He was told to meet at the Hinoda Bridge in the
Land of Silence
 at 6 a.m. the following day. During the night, he decided to enjoy himself, drinking heartily and charming women with his impending money. Soon, he was approached by a
breathtakingly beautiful woman
 who quickly got his full favour and attention. As they left the bar together, the woman was revealed to be
Boruto Uzumaki
, who quickly subdued the missing-nin. After he came to, Kirisaki was questioned by
Team 7
. After learning all of the vague information Kirisaki had regarding his assignment, it was decided to keep him detained while they
impersonated
 him for the meeting at the bridge.

He was later discovered by citizens of the Land of Silence, ruining the Konoha-nin's plan.
