The
Shimura Clan
 (
志村一族
,
Shimura Ichizoku
) is a
shinobi
 clan from
Konohagakure
.

History

After Konohagakure's formation following the
Warring States Period
, the Shimura were amongst the first set of
clans
 to ally themselves with the
Senju
 and
Uchiha
-founded village, alongside the
Sarutobi clan
.
 Not much else is known of the clan, except that
Danzō Shimura
 noted that both his father and grandfather died on the battlefield.
 The clan was also well renowned, as
Madara Uchiha
 was shocked upon hearing that the clan was joining the newly formed village.
