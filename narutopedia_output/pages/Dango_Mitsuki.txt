Personality

While much is not know about Dango, it was stated that he has good observational skills. In his spare time, he likes to look at the moon.

Appearance

Dango is a young man with dark hair and small, dark-coloured eyes. Because of his hobby of looking at the moon, he squints. He wears the typical Konoha-nin attire complete with a bandanna-styled
forehead protector

flak jacket
