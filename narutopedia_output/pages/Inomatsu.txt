Inomatsu
 (
イノマツ
) is a
genin
 from
Konohagakure
.

Background

Inomatsu once studied under the guidance of
Asuma Sarutobi
.

Personality

Like the members of
Team Asuma
, he was deeply saddened by his former teacher's death.

Appearance

Inomatsu has long black hair and black eyes. During Asuma's funeral, he wore the typical Konoha funeral clothing. He also has small black markings underneath his eyes.

Part II

Akatsuki Suppression Mission

Inomatsu was present at Asuma's funeral, mourning his loss.
