Mokume Arai
 (
荒井モクメ
) is a construction worker of
Konohagakure
.

New Era

The day before the
Konoha Barrier Team
 ran a test of their newly updated sensing system, Mokume was dropped at a
Thunder Train
 station by a co-worker. He took a train to attend a friend's wedding outside Konoha. The day of the test, Mokume's
chakra signature
 was detected in the village, having been impersonated by
Hyōi Yorishiro
, who had the ability to copy others' chakra.
Inojin Yamanaka
 was able to detect something felt off with his chakra, and further investigation resulted in Hyoi's arrest. The discovery of this vulnerability led the barrier team to take action mitigate it.
