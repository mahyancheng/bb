The
Kinkaku Force
 (
金角部隊
,
Kinkaku Butai
) was a special team of at least twenty highly skilled shinobi from
Kumogakure
.

History

During the
First Shinobi World War
, the team tracked down
Konohagakure
's
Escort Unit
, and surrounded them on the battlefield.
 The
Second Hokage
 acted as a decoy in order for his comrades to escape, and ultimately was killed by
Kinkaku
.

Trivia

Kinkaku
 (
金角
) means "Golden Horn". It is the name of a character from the famous story
Journey to the West
.
