He was a shinobi from an unknown village and the older brother of
Inori
.

Personality

He cared very much for his sister, being able to locate her when she disappeared to
Boro
's Cult. He did not believe Boro's tales of the
Infinite Tsukuyomi
's grace, attempting to convince his sister of its fallacies. He showed some degree of despair when he could not kill Boro, but immediately expressed courage, choosing to die rather than join the cult.

Appearance

He was a young man of medium height with short purple hair that sticks out in different directions. He also had large light-coloured eyes. He wore a dark turtleneck, pants, a pair of sandals, and a flak jacket not unlike
Konohagakure
's, with multiple chest pockets.

Abilities

He was a fairly experienced shinobi, exceling in
Water Release
 techniques. With it, he can create a
stream of water
 that bursts out of the ground in his chosen-direction, slicing anything it comes into contact with, even rock. He also utilised a long-distance technique, being able to fire off powerful water projectiles capable of knocking out large men with a single blow.
