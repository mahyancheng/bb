Trivia

In the third version, this opening changes with colour palettes on each character, Boruto and Naruto (post-
Time Slip Arc
), and Sumire has been excluded (due to her transfer to
Scientific Ninja Weapons Team
 from
Team 15
 as of
episode 137
).

Episode 171
 reuses the animation from this opening of Boruto fighting
Shojoji
.

Before this song released, it was thought it would be called
Cherish Straightforward Emotions
 (
まっすぐな感情を大切に
,
Massugu na Kanjō o Taisetsu ni
), because of what miwa said about the song.
