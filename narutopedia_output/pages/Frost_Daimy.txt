He is the
daimyō
 of the
Land of Frost
.

Background

In the anime, while chatting up a
geisha
, the
Lightning Group
 went after him for taking money from the less fortunate. They said they wouldn't hurt him if he paid up, however, before they could come to an agreement,
shinobi
 from
Kirigakure
 appeared and attacked the group, saving the daimyō in the process.

Personality

In the anime, it was shown that he is a cowardly man who has no problem begging for his life and taking money from the less fortunate.

Appearance

He is an elderly man with short, pale purple hair that spikes up at the back, a bristling moustache and a long beard. He wears a hat of sorts which has the symbol of his village on it, similar to a
forehead protector
.
 He also squints and wears what appears to be a purple kimono.
