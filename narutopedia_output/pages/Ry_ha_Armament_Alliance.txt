The
Ryūha Armament Alliance
 (
龍波武装同盟
,
Ryūha Busō Dōmei
,
Viz:
 Ryuha Armed Alliance) is an organisation formed in the
Land of Waves
 after the
Fourth Shinobi World War
. Following the example set by
Madara Uchiha
, the Armament Alliance seeks to free the world from the corruptive influence of money. Its members are primarily
missing-nin
.
