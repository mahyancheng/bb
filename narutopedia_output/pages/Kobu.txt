Personality

As the chief advisor of the Fire Daimyō, Kobu is a trustworthy, intelligent, and efficient man. During the meeting with Konoha, he showed himself to be a no-nonsense individual.

Appearance

Kobu is a tall, middle-aged man. He has a round face with pronounced cheekbones. He has trimmed, dark hair with bangs swiped to the left and streaks of white on the temples and the front. He wears light pants with dark robes that have a high collar. He also has a belt with the Land of Fire emblem on the front.
