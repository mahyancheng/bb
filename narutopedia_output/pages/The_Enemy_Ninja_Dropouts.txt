"
The Enemy: Ninja Dropouts
" (
敵は『不忍』
,
Teki wa "Shinobazu"
) is episode 209 of the original
Naruto
 anime.

Synopsis

Naruto
,
Sakura
 and
Lee
 are hired to escort a "
Ninja Dropout
" — a criminal ninja — named
Gantetsu
 to prison, and guard him against his comrades, who may be trying to free him. They learn that
Todoroki
, the captain of the guard, has a personal vendetta against Gantetsu for what happened to his parents and
younger brother
. During an attack by one of the Ninja Dropouts, Naruto, Todoroki and Gantetsu are separated from Sakura, Lee and the guards.

Trivia

This was the last episode of the English dub aired on
Cartoon Network
, due to closure of
Toonami Jetstream
, and
YTV
's
Bionix
 continued airing until December 6, 2009.
