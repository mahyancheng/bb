Dangō
 (
ダンゴウ
) is a citizen of the
Land of Fire
.

Appearance

Dangō has a short crop of brown hair and black eyes. He wears a pair of circular black glasses.

Part II

Five Kage Summit

Dangō advises the
Fire Daimyō
, sitting nearest to the daimyō out of all his advisers. During an emergency meeting following
Pain's assault
 on
Konoha
, he vows to provide whatever resources will be needed to rebuild the destroyed village.

Trivia

Dangō's name, when written as 談合, means "consultation".
