Komori
 is a member of the
Enlightened Ones
.

Personality

Komori is confident in his team-mates' abilities. He also held an interest in
Hinoko
.

Appearance

Komori has a short crop of black hair that is spiked towards the front and dark-coloured eyes. He wears a black trench coat common of the Enlightened Ones.

Blank Period

Shikamaru Hiden: A Cloud Drifting in Silent Darkness

Komori is first seen cleaning in the inn where
Shikamaru Nara
, Hinoko and
Rō
 enter. When the inn gets raided by
shinobi
, Komori, claiming to hate Tobari officials, helps them escape. Later, When Hinoko asks to join the
Land of Silence
, Komori reveals himself as an Enlightened One and tells his captain she is serious about joining their cause. The captain insists she proves her loyalty to them by erasing Shikamaru and Rō. Satisfied with the outcome of the "battle", Komori takes Hinoko into the village only to be knocked out by her.
