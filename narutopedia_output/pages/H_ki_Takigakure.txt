 from

Appearance

Hoki has brown curly hair (spiky in the manga) and black eyes. He wears a grey shirt over a blue jacket. He also wears a blue Takigakure
forehead protector
.

Part I

Chūnin Exams

Main article:
Chūnin Exams

Hoki was a genin who took part in the
Chūnin Exams
. After listening to
Naruto
's speech, he passed with flying colors. He would later run out of strength and fail in the
Forest of Death
.

Trivia

"Hōki" has various meanings, for example "abandonment" (
放棄
), "laws and regulations" (
法規
), "uprising"/"revolting" (
蜂起
) or "broom" (
箒
).
