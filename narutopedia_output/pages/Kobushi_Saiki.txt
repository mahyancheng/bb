Kobushi Saiki
 (
砕気コブシ
,
Saiki Kobushi
) is a shinobi from
Kumogakure
.

Appearance

Kobushi has light-brown skin, greyish-brown hair, greyish-brown eyebrows, and green eyes, with one of them sporting a long horizontal scar right through his left eye and left his eyebrow. His outfit consists of a single long-sleeved white kimono with dark-blue trimmings, with the left sleeve already missing, and a dark-blue obi belt. He wears a light orange forehead protector as a collar. Kobushi also dons a wrist protector and
tekkō
 when in combat.

Abilities

Not much is known of Kobushi's abilities other than that his fighting style revolves around
taijutsu
. He wields wide metal brass knuckles, with which he apparently fights.
