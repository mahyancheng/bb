Boruto: Two Blue Vortex
 manga.

Summary

Sarada
 thinks back to her conversations with her comrades and friends about her dream of becoming
Hokage
 and her repressed feelings for
Boruto
. In a flashback, after leaving
Konohagakure
, Boruto asks
Sasuke
 what the
Mangekyō Sharingan
 is. Sasuke explains it to Boruto but he knows that though Sarada had awakened her Mangekyo Sharingan, she is unaware of the awakening but in due time, she will learn how to use it in the future which will benefit them. Sarada coldly combats
Ryū
 with her new ability while
Mitsuki
 watches where she becomes victorious.

Mitsuki catches Sarada when she falls where he realizes that Sarada using her new technique causes her to be exhausted. Elsewhere,
Koji Kashin
 and
Ada
 communicates with one another. They are glad Sarada awakened her Mangekyo Sharingan's new ability but saddened that it had to come to
Yodo
's sacrifice. When Boruto asks Koji whether all their plans succeeding had to involve people dying, Koji tries to change the subject by ordering Boruto not to leave as
Jura
's target is Boruto. Boruto does not heed his commands and he goes to save
Konohamaru
 from
Matsuri
 which he succeeds in drawing Jura's attention to him.
