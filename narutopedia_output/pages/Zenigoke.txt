Zenigoke
 (
銭苔
) is a
mercenary ninja
.

Appearance

Zenigoke has fair skin, black irises, short black spikey hair and thin eyebrows. He wears a jumpsuit, beige shin-guards, and black ninja sandals.

Abilites

Zenigoke is a stealthy individual, able to sneak into
Konohagakure
 undected and plant explosive tag traps all over unnoticed.

Plot Overview

Zenigoke snuck into Konohagakure with the intent to destroy it by placing numerous traps, but was discovered and stopped by
Neji Hyūga
. He escaped into the woods, but encountered Neji and
Naruto Uzumaki
. He was able to set off a trap, destroying one of Naruto's
shadow clones
 and fought the two. He attempted to attack Neji but was knocked out by Naruto.

Trivia

The kanji used to write his name mean "liverwort".

Zenigoke's plan of planting explosives all over Konohagakure is similar to
Gennō
's.
