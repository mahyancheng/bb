This attack has two variations:

The first, shown in
Naruto Shippūden: Ultimate Ninja Storm 2
, is of Hidan slashing the opponent with his
Triple-Bladed Scythe
 in a combo.

The second, shown in
Naruto Shippūden: Ultimate Ninja Impact
, is of Hidan throwing out his scythe into the ground in front of him while in the air.
