Sekka
 (
セッカ
) is a talented
medical-nin
 of
Sunagakure
.

Appearance

Sekka has dark eyes and thick eyebrows. He wears what is seemingly the standard uniform for medical ninja in Sunagakure which entails a red tunic with a white poncho-like collar which he wears along with a ghutrah and white
forehead protector
.

Abilities

Sekka is a talented medical-nin who is said to have saved numerous lives in the past.

Part II

Kazekage Rescue Mission

When Kankurō was retrieved from the desert after having been poisoned by Sasori, he along with his pupil,
Ryōkan
 attempted to treat him, but to no avail. He later aided
Sakura Haruno
, who was able to successfully extract the poison and create an antidote as well, in treating Kankurō.
