Water Ball
 (
水球弾

Water Release
 ninjutsu, forming an orb that shoots bullets of water.

Lightning Gun
 (
雷砲
,
Raideppō
): The user releases a
Lightning Release
 ninjutsu, shooting a beam of electricity.

Trivia

In
