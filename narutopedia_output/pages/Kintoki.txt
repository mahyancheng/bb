Appearance

Kintoki is a bear with white fur on his arms, legs and head. The fur on his paws and torso is black, as is a mark that runs over his right eye that resembles an eye patch.

Abilities

Kintoki is undoubtedly very strong considering he's a giant bear. He is also able to Sumo Wrestle according to Killer B.

Trivia

Mount Kintoki is a mountain in Japan said to be home of
Kintarō
, a folklore character in Japan. Kintarō often sumō wrestled with his four animal friends, the most powerful of which was a bear like Kintoki.
