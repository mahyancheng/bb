The Last
 (

omake
 set after the events of
The Last: Naruto the Movie

Retsu no Sho

The Last

Summary

By checking
Sai
's books about proper human interaction,
Naruto
 learns that men are supposed to pay for everything on a date. When he takes
Hinata
 out to a restaurant, however, he is intimidated by how expensive the food is. Hinata uses her
Byakugan
 to look inside his
wallet
 and sees he doesn't have very much money on him. Not letting on that she knows this, Hinata asks if they can eat at
Ramen Ichiraku
 instead, which a relieved Naruto agrees to.
