Chishima
 (
チシマ
) is the assistant of the Chief Advisor to the
Daimyō
 of the
Land of Birds
 and a childhood friend of
Sagi
 and
Toki
.

Personality

He has a very ravenous appetite. He shows very much concern for Sagi and appears to be in love with Toki. He is very kind and would do anything for Sagi as shown by him giving his own money to help pay for the mission, leaving him with no money for food.

Appearance






Chishima as a child.



He has short brown hair, black eyes, and wears circular glasses. He is often seen in a purple and white kimono and hakama.

Part I

Cursed Warrior Extermination Mission






Chishima protects Naruto.



He is devoted to the siblings, and was willing to ask
Konohagakure
 for help dealing with the
Cursed Warrior
, despite Toki's (as Sagi) refusal for help. He uncovered
Hōki
's deception, and, while he was nearly killed in the process, reported his findings to Toki and
Naruto
. He was almost killed by one of Hōki's attacks, but survived.

Trivia

"Chishima" is the name of one of Japan's
