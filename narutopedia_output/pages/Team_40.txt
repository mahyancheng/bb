Team 40
 is a shinobi team from
Konohagakure
, consisting of
Enko Onikuma
,
Dōshu Goetsu
 and
Tsuru Itoi
, as well as an unknown team leader. The team participated in the
Chūnin Exams
, but failed the first task, resulting in their elimination. After their original
jōnin
 leader was incapacitated due to Enko's
uncontrolled technique
, the team leader was replaced by
Ibiki Morino
.

With the continued thefts performed by the
Byakuya Gang
 throughout Konohagakure, Team 40 was assigned alongside the other genin teams to help stop the self-proclaimed noble thieves. However, the genin were not permitted to engage the Byakuya Gang without aid from a chūnin or higher-ranked shinobi.
