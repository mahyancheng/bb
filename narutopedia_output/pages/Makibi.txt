Makibi
 (
マキビ
) is a wealthy businessman and the president of the Makibi Company. He was a patron at the
Coliseum
.

Personality

Makibi indulges in a wealthy lifestyle, such as drinking expensive wine and having courtesans who accompany him.

Appearance

Makibi is a middle-aged, fair-skinned man with neck-length brown hair that parts in the middle and frames his face, with the back slicked down. He has small black eyes with bags underneath them. He wears a navy blue suit with a green scarf.
