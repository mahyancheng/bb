"
Hidden Jutsu! The Price of Ninja Art: Kujaku
" (
秘術　孔雀妙法の代償
,
Hijutsu Kujaku Myōhō no Daishō
) is episode 180 of the original
Naruto
 anime.

Synopsis

It is revealed that the masked ninja who stole the star is
Sumaru
's mother,
Natsuhi
, and that
Akahoshi
 is the one who kidnapped Sumaru. The group learns that the star training is potentially fatal to the ninja who take part in it, but resolve to find the star.
Tenten
 and
Lee
 infiltrate Akahoshi's residence and rescue Sumaru. Meanwhile, Akahoshi approaches Natsuhi and demands the star in exchange for Sumaru, and the two fight. While Natsuhi gains the upper hand, Naruto and Neji intervene for Akahoshi, and Naruto manages to get close enough to punch Natsuhi. After he does, Akahoshi launches an attack at the two of them, dropping them into the Valley of Death.
