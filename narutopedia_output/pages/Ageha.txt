Ageha
 (
アゲハ
) is a poor girl living in the
Land of Waves
.

Background

Ageha became an orphan following the murder of her parents by
Gatō
's
men
.

Personality

Ageha is a sweet girl who, despite her poverty, lives a bright and kind life.

Appearance

Ageha has cheek-length messy brown hair and big blue eyes. She wore a grape coloured, short-sleeved shirt with a green blue collar.
