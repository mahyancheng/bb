This article is about the technique used by the Kedōin Clan. For the technique used by Orochimaru, see
Vanishing Facial Copy Technique
.

The user becomes a near-perfect copy of another. In addition to appearance, it also makes them copy their target's scent, chakra signature, and general personality. The technique is considered to be a flawless
Transformation Technique
 as even the copied person's allies won't be able to tell the difference. Despite the extensive aesthetical recreation of the technique, the user can't use the techniques or unique abilities of the target, and they are also very weak to attack, both striking and enduring. In order to copy an appearance, the user needs to wear a special mask and stay close to the target for a long time. When the technique commences, the user's eyes begin to glow. The transformation lasts only a few days and is unique to the
Kedōin clan
.
