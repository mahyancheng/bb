The five elemental Genryū.



The
Genryū
 (
源龍
) are five legendary dragons that appear in
Naruto Shippūden: Dragon Blade Chronicles
. Each Genryū represents the five
elemental natures
:
earth
,
water
,
wind
,
fire
, and
lightning
.

History





The Light Genryū.



Before the
First Shinobi World War
, the Genryū fertilised the land belonging to the
Ryū clan
. If all five of the Genryū are merged together, they form the
Light Genryū
 (
光源龍
,
Kōgenryū
), a very powerful dragon capable of destroying the world.
Kuroma Tatsushiro
 sought to become the Light Genryū to seek revenge on those who hate him and his sister
Akari Tatsushiro
 for being descendants of the Ryū clan and destroy the world.
