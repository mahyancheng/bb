Iō
 (
イオウ
,
Iō
) is a
medical-nin
 from
Konohagakure
.

Personality

Iō is a calm individual, often seen with a composed look, even when he is facing strong opponents.

Appearance

Iō has black eyes and brown hair covered by his
forehead protector
 which he wears in a bandanna-style. He wears the standard attire of the Konoha-nin complete with
flak jacket
. While working on the
Logistical Support and Medical Division
 compound he wears medical overalls to cover his attire.

Abilities

Ninjutsu






Iō using his Earth Release: Flying Thrown Stones.



As a medical-nin, Iō has excellent
chakra control
 with which he can perform
medical ninjutsu
. Battle-wise, Iō is a competent shinobi and is capable of using
Earth Release
, making use of techniques such as the
Earth Release: Flying Thrown Stones
 to bombard targets with, delivering devastating blows with each shard of stone. He also has a great understanding for the use of
sealing tags
, as he was able to combine them with his Earth Release to help seal away
reincarnated
 shinobi.

Physical Prowess

Iō is also physically strong, capable of lifting a giant boulder several times his size without difficulty. In case of close-range combat, he is skilled enough to fight a gifted
kenjutsu
 user with just a kunai, although only for a short period. He is also very durable, as he could perform his sealing technique flawlessly even when injured.

Part II

Fourth Shinobi World War: Confrontation

During the
Fourth Shinobi World War
, Iō was assigned to the Logistic Support and medical Division. At a point in the war, the compound was attacked by three reincarnated shinobi, one of which was
Hayate Gekkō
, they stole the KIA
scroll
.
Sukui
 made a team composed of Iō,
Sakura Haruno
,
Yokaze
, and
Yūgao Uzuki
 to retrieve this scroll. When the team caught up to the opposition, Iō fought alongside his comrades and was successful in sealing away both the
Sunagakure
 and
Kumogakure
-nin. However, his confrontation with Hayate ended with him being heavily wounded. Even though he was
healed
 by Sakura, Iō was not able to continue on the mission and left the sealing of Hayate to his remaining comrades. Their mission ended in a success with all three shinobi being sealed away successfully.
