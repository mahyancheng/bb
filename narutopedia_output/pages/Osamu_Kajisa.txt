Osamu Kajisa
 (
加治佐修
,
Kajisa Osamu
), born on December 30, 1973, is a Japanese
mangaka
 and former assistant to
Masashi Kishimoto
. Following his work on
Naruto
, he went on to moderate success and is best known for the manga series
Tattoo Hearts
, which was serialised in
Weekly Shōnen Jump
 in 2003.
