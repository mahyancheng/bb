Agari
 (
アガリ
) is the wife of
Giichi
.

Background

At some point in the past, she met Giichi and married him. Unable to have children, she ends up treating
Inari
 as if he were her own.

Personality

Agari cares about and wants to save the
Land of Waves
 along with her husband, Inari and others.

Appearance

Agari is an older woman with chin-length, shaggy grey hair. She wears a grey shirt with an orange and brown cardigan jacket around her shoulders and a tanish-coloured hat.

Part I

Prologue — Land of Waves

Prologue — Land of Waves

When she hears Inari's resolution to save the Land of Waves, she remarks that he has become a fine young man. Her words encouraged her husband to help Inari.

Trivia

"Agari" (
上がり
) has various meanings like "rise/increase", "income" or "end result".

Her hobby is knitting.
