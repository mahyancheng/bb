Personality

Tōfu was very self-confident and would call himself an ex-bandit to try and strike fear into others.

Appearance

He has brown hair that normally spiky above the forehead, long sideburns, a uni-brow, and big ears. He is also seen to always be squinting. Tōfu's appearance strongly resembles a monkey.
