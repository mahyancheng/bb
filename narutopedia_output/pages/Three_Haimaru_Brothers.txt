Personality

Normally, they are friendly and calm, but their combative spirits arise when a battle breaks out.

Appearance

Being triplets, all three dogs have the same grey fur with white undersides and short pointed ears and mostly resemble
Huskies

Abilities

As Inuzuka ninken, they use
collaborative attacks
 with Hana. The four could use a
technique
