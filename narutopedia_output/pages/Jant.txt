Jantō
 (
ジャントウ
) is a gambler from the
Tanzaku Quarters
.

Personality

Jantō is an experienced and calculating gambler who likes games with good odds.

Appearance

Jantō has short spiky light brown hair and black-coloured eyes. He also wears a pair of glasses. He wears a black kimono with white rimmings.

Part I

Search for Tsunade

He is behind the times, as he had never heard of
Tsunade
's notoriously bad luck when
Jiraiya
 and
Naruto
 come to ask him about her.

In Other Media

Video Games

Jantō appears in
Naruto Mobile
 as a constant rival for the player in the Tanzaku location. He fights with cards, throwing them at the opponent.

Trivia

Written as "雀頭", Jantō is a mahjong term for a pair as part of a winning hand together with four melds.
