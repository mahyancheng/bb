The Memorial Park.



Memorial Park
 is an area on the edge of a cliff in
Kirigakure
, which contains the village's Memorial Stone alongside another construct. During the
New Seven Ninja Swordsmen
's revolution, the park was damaged.

Shizuma Hoshigaki
 claims that
his family
's name no longer shows up on Memorial stones due to them being troublesome during peaceful times.
