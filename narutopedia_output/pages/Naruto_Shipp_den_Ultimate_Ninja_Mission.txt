Naruto Shippūden: Ultimate Ninja Mission
 is the second Naruto game designed for the popular Data Carddass arcade system released in 2007.

Gameplay

Like other Data
Carddass
,
Narutimate Mission
 is an interactive card game, using real cards and 3D graphics to animate battles on screen. Also, like other Data Card games, which are often derived from PlayStation 2 games, much of
Narutimate Mission
 originates from
Naruto Shippūden: Ultimate Ninja 4
.
