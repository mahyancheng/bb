Tessen Togeito
 (
棘糸テッセン
,
Togeito Tessen
) is a
tokubetsu jōnin
 of
Konohagakure
.

Appearance

Tessen has a short crop of brown hair and dark eyes, under which he sports red triangular markings. He also wears the standard Konohagakure attire complete with
flak jacket
,
forehead protector
 and bandages around his lower arms and wrists. Additionally, he wields a special
shuriken
 launcher, which is stored in a
scroll
.

Abilities

Tessen uses ninja tools as his main
fighting style
.
 He makes use of a special
shuriken
 launcher, bombarding the enemy with a volley of shuriken in an instant.

Trivia

"Tessen" (
鉄線
) is the Japanese name of the "
Clematis florida
", a plant originating from Japan. When written as 鉄扇, it describes a type of a
Japanese war fan
. "Togeito" is written with the kanji "棘", which can be read as "thorn", and "糸", which means "string".
