The
Nokizaru Group
 is a
mercenary ninja
 organisation comprised of
shinobi
 who are hired to hunt and assassinate other
shinobi
. They hold no allegiance to any country or village and were proficient in the use of the
Exploding Insect

technique
, and also showed skill in concealment, mostly through
camouflage
, hiding in
shadows
,
water
 and even
fire
, and spying techniques such as linking themselves to
bugs
. All members of this group were depicted as wearing sashes that resembled the tails of monkeys. They also had painted faces and wore outfits that had stripes on one side, while the other was a solid colour.

Members

There are five known members of the Nokizaru Group.

Himushi

Himushi is the leader of the group.

Hiding in Shadows member

He has shoulder length black hair and dark-coloured eyes. He wears the same type of clothing as the other members though his is yellow.

Hiding in Fire member

He was the one that stole the scroll from Kumogakure. He wears the same clothing as the other members but his are green and he has short blond hair.

Trivia

The Nokizaru Group appear to be named after the
Nokizaru
 ninja clan who historically served warlord
Uesugi Kenshin
.
