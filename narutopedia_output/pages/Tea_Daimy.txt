He is the daimyō of the
Land of Tea
.

Personality

He is a good man who doesn't hesitate to punish corrupted people. This is proven when he caught Kyūroku and the minister red-handed for cheating.

Appearance

He has a short crop of black hair and black eyes. He also has a black moustache, goatee, and large ears.
