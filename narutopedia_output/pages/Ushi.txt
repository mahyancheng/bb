Ushi
 (
ウシ
) was an
Anbu
 member of
Konohagakure
 who served under the
Second Hokage
.

Background

He was one of the Anbu ordered to watch the
Uchiha clan
 to keep them from rebelling again and he did so vigilantly.

Personality

Ushi was vigilant in his duties.

Appearance

Ushi wore a black Anbu cloak and a white Anbu mask over his face with a big triangular purple mark on the mask's forehead area.

Abilities

As an Anbu, he was one of Konohagakure's strongest
shinobi
. The fact that he was tasked with the very important and difficult mission to observe the Uchiha clan furthermore implies that he was very powerful.

Trivia

"Ushi" means "cow". It is likely not his real name, but a codename.
