Gamamichi
 is a toad instructor in
Naruto x Boruto: Ninja Voltage
.

Appearance

Gamamichi is a light orange toad with blue eyes, a yellow belly, three yellow stripes on its head, and a mustache pattern on its cheeks in the manner of
Naruto Uzumaki
. He wears a red vest with a fur collar and headphones. In addition, Gamamichi's front legs are wrapped in bandages, and there is a green medallion around his neck.

In Other Media

Naruto x Boruto: Ninja Voltage

Gamamichi instructs new players, explaining the rules of the game and its features.
