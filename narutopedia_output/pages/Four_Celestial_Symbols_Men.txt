The
Four Celestial Symbols Men
 were a group of shinobi from the
Takumi Village
.

History

The Four Celestial Symbols Men were amongst those resentful of the ninja villages for being ungrateful towards their village's years of supplying their shinobi with weapons. In revenge, and to save their village from extinction, the four members trained harder than others in the ninja arts and sought to resurrect their village's founder,
Seimei
. To accomplish this objective, they needed the power of
Shukaku
.

Abilities

Each member specialised in wielding a particular ninja weapon, which responded to their chakra to activate special abilities. They could also inject their chakra into a water vein via their bladed weapons to use the
Ninja Tools Barrier: Reverse Fish Scales Formation
.

Plot Overview

In order to revive Seimei, the Four Celestial Symbols Men plotted to steal the power of
Shukaku
, who resided inside
Gaara
. To accomplish this objective, they kidnapped Gaara's student,
Matsuri
, and lured him to a trap to weaken him. Gaara and
siblings
 were picked off one by one of each member, and would have been killed if not for the backup sent by
Konoha
 arriving on time.

Suiko was killed by Gaara,
Rock Lee
 and
Naruto Uzumaki
; Kujaku was killed by
Temari
,
Shikamaru Nara
 and
Ino Yamanaka
; Ryūgan was killed by
Kankurō
,
Kiba Inuzuka
, and
Chōji Akimichi
.
Hōki
 died when he offered all of his life force and body to revive
Seimei
. Ultimately, however, the Four Celestial Symbols Men's goal was thwarted when Gaara killed Seimei with the
Sand Waterfall Imperial Funeral
.
