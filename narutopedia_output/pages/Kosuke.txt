Kosuke
 (
小助
) was an
Anbu
 of
Kirigakure
.

Background

During the
Third Shinobi World War
, Kosuke was part of a team of
hunter-nin
 sent after
Rin Nohara
. When Rin was killed by
Kakashi Hatake
,
Obito Uchiha
 who had witnessed the killing and who had been in love with Rin responded by slaughtering Kosuke and his team in a fit of grief, with Kosuke being one of the first victims of Obito's rampage.

Personality

Kosuke was a loyal shinobi from the
Village of the Bloody Mist
, willing to execute any mission for its sake.

Appearance

Kosuke wore Kirigakure's
flak jacket
,  alongside the mask of the hunter-nin, decorated with a dark rhombus-pattern at the bottom. He had black hair, which was tied in a ponytail. On his left shoulder, he also wore a
sword
.

Abilities

Being a member of the
Anbu
,
 Kosuke was presumably a very skilled
shinobi
. He was also seen wielding a
sword
, implying skill in
kenjutsu
.

Trivia

The kanji in Kosuke's name mean "little" (
小
) and "help" (
助
).

"Kosuke" could also be a reference to Anayama Kosuke (
穴山 小助
) of
the Sanada Ten Braves
, a group of legendary ninja that served under daimyō Yukimura Sanada during Japan's Warring States period.
