"

Naruto
 manga.

Summary

After
Team 7
 completes an uneventful,
D-rank
 mission of finding a
stray cat
,
Naruto
 demands that the
Third Hokage
 give them a more interesting assignment. The Third agrees to give them the
C-rank
 task of escorting a man named
Tazuna
 to his home in the
Land of Waves
. After leaving
Konoha
, they are attacked by the
Demon Brothers
, who quickly seem to kill
Kakashi
 before turning their attention to Naruto.
