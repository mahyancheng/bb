"
Kurenai's Decision: Team 8 Left Behind
" (
紅の決断　とり残された第8班
,
Kurenai no Ketsudan, Torinokosareta Daihappan
) is episode 203 of the original
Naruto
 anime.

Synopsis

Naruto
 encounters a girl named
Yakumo
, who, by painting a picture of
Konoha
, causes lightning to strike the
Academy
. Shortly thereafter,
Kurenai Yūhi
 resigns as leader of
Team 8
, and Naruto is determined to find out why. Later that night, Naruto — disguised as Kurenai — goes into Yakumo's mansion and discovers a room full of Yakumo's paintings. He escapes after one of the paintings nearly kills him, and is confronted by
Anbu
 security, who tranquillise him.
