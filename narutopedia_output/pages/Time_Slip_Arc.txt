Time Slip Arc
 (
タイムスリップ編
,
Taimu Surippu-hen
,
Viz:
 Back in Time Arc) is the fifteenth
arc
 of the
Boruto: Naruto Next Generations
 anime. It covers episodes 128 to 136. It is preceded by
One-Tail Escort Arc
 and is followed by the
Mujina Bandits Arc
. This arc is part of the 20th anniversary project for
Masashi Kishimoto
's original
Naruto
 manga.
