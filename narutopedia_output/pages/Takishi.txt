Takishi
 (
タキシ
) was a prisoner at
Orochimaru
's
Northern Hideout
.

Personality

Takishi was very arrogant and hated to lose. Even when fatally injured, he would continue to mock his enemies.

Appearance

Takishi wore the typical attire of all Orochimaru's prisoners which consisted of a white T-shirt and a matching pair of pants. He also had short messy black hair and black eyes.

Part II

Itachi Pursuit Mission

After being defeated by
Hebi
, he mockingly warned
Suigetsu Hōzuki
 of the dangers involved in releasing
Jūgo
. Even though Suigetsu claimed that they could handle him, Takishi continued to warn him of their impending doom, prompting the latter to kill him.

Trivia

In the manga, Takishi had light-coloured hair instead of black hair.
