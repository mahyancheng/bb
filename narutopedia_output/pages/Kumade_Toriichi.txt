Kumade Toriichi
 (
酉市クマデ
,
Toriichi Kumade
) was a
tokubetsu jōnin
 of
Konohagakure
.

Background






Zabuza kills Kumade.



Not much is known about Kumade's life. The anime revealed that he entered the
Academy
 at the same time as
Kakashi Hatake
 and others.
 During a mission to
Kirigakure
, Kumade clashed with
Zabuza Momochi
. He was killed by the latter with a combination of the
Hiding in Mist Technique
 and
Silent Killing
.

Appearance

Kumade had a short, spiky grey hair. He wore the standard attire of the Konoha-nin complete with a
forehead protector
 and
flak jacket
 along with black gloves with metal plates on the backhand. He also wore a mask that covered the lower half of his face making him look a lot like
Kakashi
.

Trivia

"Kumade" (
熊手
) can mean "rake/bamboo rake" and "fork". "Toriichi" is written with the kanji "酉", which can be read as "west", and "市", which means "city".

In the manga, Zabuza used a
tantō
 to kill Kumade. In the anime, he used a
kunai
.
