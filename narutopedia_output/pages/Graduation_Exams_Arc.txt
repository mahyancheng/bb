The
Graduation Exams Arc
 (
卒業試験編
,
Sotsugyō Shiken-hen
), also known as the
Academy Graduation Arc
 (
忍者学校卒業編
,
Akademī Sotsugyō-hen
), is the fourth
arc
 of the
Boruto: Naruto Next Generations
 anime. It covers episodes 34 to 37 and is preceded by the
School Trip Arc
 and followed by the
Genin Mission Arc
. This arc focuses on
Shino Aburame
's class participating in the
Genin Exams
.
