Sand Characters
 is a technique that utilises
sand
 as a method of communication.

Usage

Sajin used this technique during the written test of the joint
Chūnin Exams
, where he altered the terrain by using sand to leave a message to
his

teammates
.

During the events of
Naruto Shippūden the Movie: The Will of Fire
, Gaara gave
Jiraiya
 a bag of sand, which, when scattered, took the shape of a sign that signalled a temporary cease of fire for
Sunagakure
 troops.
