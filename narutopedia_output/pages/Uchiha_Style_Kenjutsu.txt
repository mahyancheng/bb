The
Uchiha Style Kenjutsu
 (
うちは流剣術
,
Uchiha Ryū Kenjutsu
) is a unique style of
kenjutsu
 developed and mastered by
Sasuke Uchiha
, who passed on the skill to his student
Boruto
.

This style of kenjutsu utilises moves similar to Japanese
iai
, where the enemy is slashed at the same moment the user draws their sword from its sheath.

Lightning Release
 chakra can also be used to coat the blade and increase its sharpness.
