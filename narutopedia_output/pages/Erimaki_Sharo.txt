Erimaki Sharo
 (
斜路エリマキ
,
Sharo Erimaki
) is a kunoichi from
Iwagakure
.

Appearance

Erimaki is a young girl with short, olive green hair and black eyes. Her outfit consists of a yellow shirt over mesh, red shoes and short-shorts over mesh, and a blue quilt that she wears as a dress. She also dons a light-green hair hoop, a red
Iwagakure
 forehead protector on her neck, and has a pink scarf tied around her wrist.

Abilities

Not much is known of Erimaki's abilities other than that her fighting style involves her
Frilled-Neck Lizard
 companion.
