The
Sasuke Retsuden Arc
 (
サスケ烈伝帖編
,
Sasuke Retsuden-hen
) the twenty-fifth
arc
 of the
Boruto: Naruto Next Generations
 anime. It covered episode 282 to 286. It is an adaptation of the
Sasuke Retsuden: The Uchiha Descendants and the Heavenly Stardust
 novel. It is preceded by the
Labyrinth Game Arc
 and is succeeded by the
Code's Assault Arc
.
