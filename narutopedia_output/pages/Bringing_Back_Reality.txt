Synopsis

Naruto
 and
Menma
 are sent on a mission with
Neji
 and
Tenten
.
Tsunade
 begins believing that Menma is an
Otogakure
 ninja due to his skills, and Neji begins suspecting the same thing when Menma moves even faster than he does. The group enters a town that has been largely destroyed by bandits, and Menma convinces Naruto's unit to help rebuild it to guard against another potential attack.
