This orphanage is seemingly located on the outskirts of
Konohagakure
. It was run by a number of wardens, one of which was
Nonō Yakushi
 — one of the captains of the
Medic Corps
 and former member of
Root

During wartime, the orphanage also helped out the Konoha shinobi acting as a medical centre where the shinobi would come for treatment. It was also revealed that once old enough, the children of this orphanage could train as Konoha shinobi.
