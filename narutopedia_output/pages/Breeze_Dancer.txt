Breeze Dancer
 (
疾风舞者
) is a ninja of
Konohagakure
, and one of the playable characters in
Naruto: Ultimate Ninja Online
.

Personality

She currently does not belong to any squad and is temporarily a student of the ninja academy. For a long time, she has travelled vastly to different countries to train and has a unique perspective on the society. She enjoys gazing into the distance.

Appearance

Breeze Dancer has blonde hair and blue eyes. She wears a red kimono jacket with a black obi and a red cord, black armguards with red cords on her wrists, fishnet stockings, and long shinobi sandals. She also has bandages wrapped around her cleavage and a red Konoha forehead protector around her neck.

Abilities

Her style lies in
Wind Release
 techniques and continuous attacks. Her movements in battle denotes splendour and can be a boost to motivate team morale.
