Ryōkan
 (
リョウカン
) is a
medical-nin
 of
Sunagakure
.

Appearance

Ryōkan has dark eyes and thin eyebrows. He wears what is seemingly the standard uniform for medical ninja in Sunagakure which entails a red tunic with a white poncho-like collar which he wears along with a ghutrah and a white
forehead protector
.

Personality

Ryōkan seems to get frustrated a lot when he is unable to help his fellow comrades. He was filled with regret at not being able to help
Kankurō
.

Abilities

Ryōkan is a talented medical-nin, but he along with his mentor
Sekka
 were unable to help Kankurō, something he deeply regretted.
 As a medical-nin, he has a good chakra control in general.

Part II

Kazekage Rescue Mission

When Kankurō was retrieved from the desert after having been poisoned by
Sasori
, he along with Sekka attempted to treat him, but to no avail. He later aided
Sakura Haruno
, who was able to successfully extract the poison and create an antidote as well, in treating Kankurō.
