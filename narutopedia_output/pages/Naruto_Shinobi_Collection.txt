Naruto: Shinobi Collection
 is a social RPG released by GREE for mobile phones. The game is no longer available for download. It was succeeded by
Naruto: Shinobi Collection Shippū Ranbu
.

Gameplay

Naruto: Shinobi Collection
 is a role playing game where players use collected cards as playable characters.
