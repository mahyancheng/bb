Naruto: Shinobi Masters
 is a roleplaying card game released for the
Android
 and
iOS
 by Japanese social networking giant GREE alongside Namco Bandai Games. The game is no longer available for download.

Gameplay

The game was formally titled
Naruto x GREE
,
Naruto: Shinobi Masters
 is a social, mobile roleplaying card game.

Players can collect ninja cards featuring illustrations of characters from the series, taking on missions and other forms of challenges to raise their skill levels. Players can also join together with friends to form ninja teams.

Trivia

The game had a small crossover with
Bleach: Soul Masters
.
