Unmo Samidare
 (
五月雨ウンモ

Samidare Unmo
) was a
jōnin
 from
Kirigakure
.

Background

Unmo was known for his animosity against
Konohagakure
, and therefore,
Root
 deemed him a potential threat to the village. As such,
Sai
 was dispatched to assassinate Unmo.

Personality

Unmo was known for his strong feelings against Konohagakure.

Appearance

Unmo was a man with round eyes and dark markings around his eyes (in the anime, they were more like wide, snake-like eyes). He wears the standard attire of the Kirigakure shinobi inclusive of the village's standard
flak jacket
 and
forehead protector
 which he wore like a bandanna.

Abilities

Although his abilities remain unknown,
Yamato
 claimed that Unmo wasn't one to be defeated easily.

Trivia

Unmo was introduced in Sai's
Bingo Book
 when Yamato was looking at his belongings, with his name on the top-left corner of the page. In the manga, the name was unclear, but was shown in the anime.

五月雨
