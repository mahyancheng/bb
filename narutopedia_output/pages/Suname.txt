 (

) was a
chūnin
 of
Sunagakure

Appearance

He had short brown hair that was mostly concealed under a ghutrah. He wore the standard attire of the Suna-nin, which included its
flak jacket
 and its
forehead protector
. He also had small dark-coloured eyes.

Part II

Kazekage Rescue Mission

Suname served as a scout who watched the skies above Sunagakure. He was killed by one of
Deidara
's
explosive clay
 spiders during the course of his watch.
