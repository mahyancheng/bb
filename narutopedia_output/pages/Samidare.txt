Samidare
 (
五月雨
) is a
genin
 from
Amegakure
.

Personality

Samidare seemed to be an opportunistic and curious person, tailing other shinobi in order to discover their objective, as well as subsequently steal it if possible.

Appearance

Samidare has spiky brown hair with a ponytail and short spiky bangs hanging over his
forehead protector
. He wears a green scarf around his neck, a light grey jacket and an attire reminiscent of
Team Shigure
.

Abilities

Little is known about Samidare's overall abilities, but he was shown to be holding a place of authority amongst his fellow Amegakure genin. He also showed some knowledge about foreign techniques, recognising the
Shadow Clone Technique
.

Ninjutsu

Nature Transformation

Samidare showed proficiency in
Water Release
, being capable of, in cooperation with his comrades, using the
Grudge Rain
 technique against many foes at once.

In the anime, Samidare was amongst those who failed to get past the first test of the
Chūnin Exam
. He along with
Suiu
 and thirteen other Amegakure genin followed
Team 7
 and
Team 10
 to find out what it is they are after and if possible take it with them back to Amegakure. After failing in their first attempt the Amegakure genin attack using their technique to obtain the legacy of the
Fourth Hokage
, they are stopped by the intervention of
Sasuke Uchiha
, who proceeds to attack and defeat them all using
Chidori
.

Trivia

Samidare translates as "early summer rain", and is also one of the
Naruto: Shippūden
 soundtracks.
