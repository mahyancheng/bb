A mask resembling the
Shinigami
's face, it resided on a wall inside the
Uzumaki Clan's Mask Storage Temple
 located on the outskirts of
Konohagakure
, along with twenty-six other similar-looking masks. It allows the wearer to summon and control the Shinigami by allowing it to possess the user. This enables the user to perform
Dead Demon Consuming Seal: Release
.

Trivia

This mask resembles the
Hannya mask
 used in
Japanese Noh theatre
.
