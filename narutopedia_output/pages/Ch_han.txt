Chōhan
 (
チョウハン
) is a gambler from the
Land of Fire
.

Personality

Chōhan seems to enjoy gambling a great deal going as far as to wager information in exchange for money should his opponent lose to him.

Appearance

He has brown hair styled into an afro and dark-coloured eyes. He wears a simple, black, loose-fitting kimono with white rimmings.

Part I

Search for Tsunade

While gambling with
Jiraiya
, he makes a bet in return for information about
Tsunade
. He almost won, which would have cost Jiraiya 1000
ryō
, but
Naruto Uzumaki
's
Rasengan
 practice allows Jiraiya to get the info for free by interfering with their game.

Trivia

"Chōhan" has several meanings. When written in
katakana
, it names the
raccoon butterflyfish
 while "重犯" describes a "felony/felon". However, most importantly for this character, when written as "丁半", "Chōhan" stands for "odd and even numbers", a "dice game" or "gambling" in general.
