Sagi
 (
サギ
) was the son of
Ōwashi
, the older twin brother of
Toki
, and the real heir of the
Land of Birds
.

Background






Sagi, Toki, and Chishima when they were children.



As a child, Sagi and Toki were very close, and spent much time playing together with their mutual friend
Chishima
. After his death, his spirit appeared and saved his sister from committing suicide. The spirit gave his sister a pocket watch that had stopped. She swore to avenge her brother's suspicious death and make his frozen time move again,
 by disguising herself and becoming
daimyō
 in his place to find the one responsible.

Personality

He was very caring to his sister as shown by his spirit to
Naruto
 as well as to the Land of Birds. He seems to value life very much as indicated by the couple of times he used his spirit to save the lives of Naruto and Toki. According to Chishima, he was a cheerful and gentle person.

Appearance

Sagi had jaw length blackish purple hair and black eyes. He had thin eyebrows and a heart-shaped face. He wore a style of daimyō clothing seen in the Land of Birds.

Trivia

Like his father and sister, Sagi's name is an allusion to a bird. "Sagi" (
鷺
) is the Japanese name for
heron
.
