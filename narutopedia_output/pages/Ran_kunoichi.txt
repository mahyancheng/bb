Appearance

Ran has a short crop of brown hair and blue eyes. She wore a midriff blouse and a skirt along with a customised midriff Kumogakure
flak jacket

forehead protector

Allied Shinobi Forces

Abilities

Not much is known of her abilities but she was presumably skilled in
kenjutsu

tantō
