After surrounding the opponent with flowers, the user will ignite the flowers. These flames will then shoot towards and explode on the target.

Trivia

The phrase "Hyakkaryōran" (
百花繚乱
,
literally meaning:
 Riot of Flowers) is a Japanese idiom that describes situations in which a number of vivid flowers blooming wildly, but is used for human beings as well to describe many skilled, smart, talented and/or beautiful people gathering up in one place at once.
