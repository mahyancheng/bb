"
Open for Business! The Leaf Moving Service
" (
開業!!木ノ葉引越センター
,
Kaigyō!! Konoha Hikkoshi Sentā
) is episode 187 of the original
Naruto
 anime.

Synopsis

Naruto
,
Chōji
, and
Hinata
 are sent to help some peddlers move out of the
Land of Vegetables
. Because of a package from
Tsunade
, the peddlers mistake them for "The
Konoha
 Moving Service". Unbeknownst to them, a group called the
Janin
, have taken control of the land and are looking for
one of the peddlers
.
