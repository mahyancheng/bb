Jump Ultimate Stars
 was released in Japan on November 23, 2006.

Gameplay

Jump Ultimate Stars is a fighting game which is based on the characters from various anime and manga series including
Naruto
,
One Piece
 and
Dragon Ball Z
. It was developed by Ganbarion and published by Nintendo for the Nintendo DS. The game has over 300 characters from 41 different manga series. It is the second game in the series.
