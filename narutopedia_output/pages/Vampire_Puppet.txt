The
Vampire Puppet
 is a foreign puppet of unknown origins. Bloodthirsty by nature, the puppet drinks the blood of its targets whilst killing them in sewers. The
Fifth Hokage
 assigns a mission to destroy it, giving it a
B-Rank
 in difficulty.

Appearance

The puppet has a demonic appearance, and speaks in a foreign accent.

Abilities

The puppet itself is capable of using both
Fire Release
 in the form of
Fire Release: Great Fireball Technique
 and
Lightning Release
.
