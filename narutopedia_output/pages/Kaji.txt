Kaji
 (
カジ
) is a citizen of the
Land of Waves
.

Personality

Kaji is a very cautious man considering all the precautions he took to not be caught by
Gatō
's men.

Appearance

Kaji is a middle-aged man with short brown hair and usually wears a pointed straw hat along with a long-sleeved grey and black-coloured outfit with the collar upturned. Along with this he wears a pair of pants and a simple sandals. He also has dark-coloured eyes.

Abilities

Having 20 years of experience as a ferryman across the entirety of the Land of Waves, Kaji is privy to the intricacies of the waterways, even being able to tell when one is no good and rerouting in the process.

Kaji transported
Team 7
 and
Tazuna
 to the Land of Waves. Whilst travelling, he left the boat's motor off so that they could slip in without being heard. With the same reason in mind, he scolded
Naruto
 for shouting whilst near the bridge. He was able to take them to the edge of the Land of Waves, though he was reluctant to go that far, at which point he turned the motor on and left, wishing the group luck on their journey.

Trivia

When written as "舵", "Kaji" means "rudder", "wheel" or "helm". When written as "舵", it is the Japanese name for the
paper mulberry
.
