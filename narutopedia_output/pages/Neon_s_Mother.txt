Background

Neon's mother has been friends with
Hibari Kamakura
 for a long time, and
their

daughters
 grew up together as childhood friends.

Personality

While not much is known about her, Neon's mother is a kindhearted individual, offering to walk Osuka home as she was aware of Hibari's busy schedule. When she learned of Osuka's kidnapping, Neon's mother and Hibari worked together tirelessly to develop a
drone
 with a sensory ability to locate Osuka.

Appearance

She possesses a fair skin with black eyes and brown hair. Her hair is chin-length and bob-cut. She wears purple glasses with circular lenses that are half-rimmed on the bottom, an orange buttoned shirt underneath a lab coat. She also wears baggy orange pants.
