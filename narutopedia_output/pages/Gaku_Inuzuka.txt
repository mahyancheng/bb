Background






Gaku, his ninken and their team confront the Kumo-nin.



Gaku, his

 In one of the battles, his team clashed with Kumogakure's

A

Killer B

jinchūriki transformation

Personality

As the eldest of his team, he thought of Minato as a younger brother.

Appearance

Gaku is dressed in the usual
Konohagakure

flak jacket
 and has the distinctive Inuzuka's fang markings on his cheeks. He has swept-back brown hair with a beard of the same colour and black eyes.

Abilities

Gaku served as the short-range combat shinobi of his platoon.
 As an Inuzuka, it is very likely that he uses his clan's assorted techniques alongside his large-breed ninken while in battle.
