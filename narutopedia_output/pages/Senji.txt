Senji
 (
戦時
) is the former village head of
Takigakure
 and the aid of
Hisen
. He is also the aid of
Shibuki
 and has only appeared in the light novel adaption,
Naruto: Mission: Protect The Waterfall Village!
.

Personality

Senji has a serious personality, telling Shibuki to take up the village as his own and defending it with life after the death of his father. Senji is also protective towards Shibuki such as Suien tried to attack the latter.

Appearance

Senji is an old man with a craggy face, and long white hair.

Abilities

Senji is very proficient with
Water Release
, as shown when he used the
Water Release: Great Waterfall Technique
 against
Suien
.

Trivia

"Senji" (戦時) means "Wartime"
