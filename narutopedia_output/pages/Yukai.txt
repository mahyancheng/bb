Yukai
 (
ユカイ
) is a young
shinobi
 from
Kumogakure
 and the leader of the
Thundercloud Unit
.

Background

Yukai was a young prodigy from Kumogakure, well versed in
kenjutsu
 and assigned to be the
Thundercloud Unit
's leader in the
Fourth Shinobi World War
.

Personality

Despite his young age, Yukai showed great courage and leadership skills, managing to keep calm and focused while facing certain death when he and his unit were cornered by nearly two thousand
White Zetsu clones
. He also greatly respects Omoi, referring to the older shinobi as "onii-san" (
お兄さん
,
literally meaning:
 elder brother).

Appearance

Yukai has dark red hair, with two bangs, and golden eyes. He wears a white
Shinobi Alliance
 forehead protector and a green vest, with white cloth around the waist. Like the rest of his unit, he also carries a large scroll on his back attached to arm straps. Additionally, he wears a yellow armband with the
Kumogakure
 insignia, which was given to him by Omoi.

Abilities

Being hailed as a prodigy, Yukai, alongside his teammates, was deemed combat ready and was allowed to participate in the Fourth Shinobi World War, albeit in the role of a Supply Unit, rather than direct combat.

Kenjutsu

Like many of his
teammates
, he is considered to be considerably skilled in
kenjutsu
, despite his young age.

Nature Transformation

Yukai has been shown to be proficient in
Lightning Release
; being able to use a
powerful combination technique
, with the assistance of his unit and Omoi, which neutralised a large number of White Zetsu.

Fourth Shinobi World War: Confrontation






Yukai with Omoi and the Thundercloud Unit attack the White Zetsu Army.



During the
Fourth Shinobi World War
, Yukai and the rest of the Thundercloud Unit, was sent to the
Seventh Front
 in order to back up the
Water Attack Raid Unit
, who had been overrun by two thousand White Zetsu clones. Sometime during this, they became trapped within a large statue on the battlefield after the White Zetsu
used the surrounding, poisonous red soil
 to create a
vast smokescreen
 of poisonous gas. After speaking with his teammates within the statue, Yukai and the others emerge to fight the Zetsu and face their fate. In the ensuing fight, Yukai is injured, however,
Omoi
 appears and fights alongside them. At this point, using the
Lightning Release Co-Operation: Thunder Bomb
 technique, the group is able to neutralise and finish off the remaining White Zetsu.

Trivia

When written as 愉快, "Yukai" means "pleasant, happy".
