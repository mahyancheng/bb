Maruten Akimichi
 (
秋道マルテン
,
Akimichi Maruten
) is a
tokubetsu jōnin
 of
Konohagakure
's
Akimichi clan
.

Background

In the anime, it was shown that Maruten entered the
Academy
 at the same time as
Itachi Uchiha
. The two also attended the same class.

Personality

Maruten adores
Shitō Akimichi
 like a brother and even attempts to copy his hairstyle and face paint.

Appearance

Like other members of the Akimichi clan he is very robust, also, like most members of his clan he wears a samurai-like outfit with the kanji for "food" (
食
,
shoku
) on the front and has distinctive circular markings on his cheeks and chin. He wears his short light coloured hair in a topknot ponytail style. He also carries a
bō
.

As a child, he wore a brown hat, an orange-white shirt and overalls with the kanji for "food" (
食
,
shoku
) on them, akin to the outfit he wears in his adulthood.

Abilities

Although the full extent of his abilities are unknown, as an Akimichi, Maruten can convert calories into
chakra
 in order to perform his clan's secret techniques. He was also seemingly proficient with wielding a bō.
