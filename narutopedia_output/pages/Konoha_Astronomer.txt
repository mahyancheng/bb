Appearance






The astronomer.



She has orange eyes with black pupils, blonde hair tied up in a big heart-shaped bun, and red-coloured glasses. She wears a long white shirt, with a light blue collar, a short black skirt, dark stockings and open-toed boots.

Abilities

Given her status as an astronomer, she is highly knowledgeable in stars and other heavenly bodies, like the
moon
. Being Kakashi's trusted assistant, she can also place a
countdown clock
 on an ally's hand, allowing them to see when a major disaster will occur.
