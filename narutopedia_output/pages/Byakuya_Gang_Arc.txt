The
Byakuya Gang Arc
 (
白夜団編
,
Byakuyadan-hen
) is the sixth
arc
 of the
Boruto: Naruto Next Generations
 anime. It covers episodes 43 to 47 and is preceded by the
Genin Mission Arc
 and followed by the
Versus Momoshiki Arc
. This arc focuses on
Boruto
 and
Shikadai
 dealing with the noble thieves called the
Byakuya Gang
.
