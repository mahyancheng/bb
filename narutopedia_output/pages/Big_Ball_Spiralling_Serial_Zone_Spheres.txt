After performing the
Multiple Shadow Clone Technique
, Naruto creates several
Big Ball Rasengan
. The clones then charge towards the target in horizontal line formation and slam their Rasengan into the target, causing a massive amount of damage.

Trivia

In
Weekly Shōnen Jump
 publication, this technique was called
Big Ball Spiralling
 (
大玉螺旋
,
Ōdama Rasen
). This was later changed to
Big Ball Spiralling Serial Zone Spheres
 (
大玉螺旋帯連丸
,
Ōdama Rasen Tairengan
) in the tankōbon version.
