Personality

Matsuri seems to be a curious person, as she asked the Third Hokage who he wants to protect the most.

Appearance

She has locks of thick, black hair, in which she wears a red headband and large, dark eyes. Attire-wise, she wears a simple purple blouse with a pink tank top, capri pants and sandals.
