The user can merge their Truth-Seeking Balls to form a ball larger in size. They can also merge a pair of two to have a larger sized set. In the games, merging all the balls into one have a multitude of uses.

Trivia

While the amount of regular sized Truth-Seeking Balls that Asura carried without the use of his
avatar
 in the manga was unknown, he was shown to possess eight in the anime, which enabled him to make a set of five larger balls.
