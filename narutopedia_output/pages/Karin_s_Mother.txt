Personality

From what little was seen of her, she cared tremendously for her daughter, Karin. She was willing to risk her health and life simply to provide a stable home for the two.

Appearance

She was a fairly tall and light-skinned woman with grey eyes. Common among those of Uzumaki descent, she had bright red hair, which was straight, chin-length and parted to frame both sides of her face. Like her daughter, after her passing, the woman possessed countless bite-marks about her body from repeatedly letting people feed on her chakra to heal.
