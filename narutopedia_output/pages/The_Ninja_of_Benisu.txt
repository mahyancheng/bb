"
The Ninja of Benisu
" (
紅州の商忍
,
Benisu no Shōnin
) is episode 224 of the
Naruto: Shippūden
 anime.

Synopsis

During their voyage,
Naruto
,
Yamato
,
Guy
, and
Aoba
 stop at an
island
 to recover from severe seasickness. There, they unexpectedly meet up with
Sakura
,
Ino
, and
Chōji
 who are on an assignment to gather medical herbs under instruction of
Tsunade
 for the upcoming war. Ninja from
Benisu Island
 are also collecting medical herbs and have taken almost all of the herbs on the island, which they intend to sell on for money to fund their village. After an encounter in a cave on the island where Naruto saves the Benisu ninja and offers them the service for free, they decide to give
Konoha
 half of the herbs they collected in gratitude.

Trivia

The Japanese title of this episode, "Benisu no Shōnin" (
literally meaning:
 Merchant of Benisu), may be a reference to
William Shakespeare
's
Merchant of Venice
.
