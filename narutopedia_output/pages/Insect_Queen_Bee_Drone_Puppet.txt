Insect Queen Bee Drone Puppets
 are a special type of
puppet
 that are unique to Menō's arsenal and were created by the
Shirogane clan
.

Appearance

Its appearance heavily resembles that of a real-life
queen bee
, but with a dark golden head with dark golden antennae that have yellow-golden tips, purple stripes on the middle side of its body, a dark blue stainless steel carapace, two long insect-like wings that have the Japanese kanji "雷" (
literally meaning:
 thunder) on both of them, a stainless steel bronze bottom at the near end, and a light silver stinger at the end.

Variations

Smaller Queen Bee Drone Puppets

They are smaller versions of this puppet, which can be fired out or created from this puppet's carapace.
