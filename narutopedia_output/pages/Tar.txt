Tarō
 (
タロウ
) is a
Konohagakure

genin
 and a student failed by
Kakashi Hatake
.

Background

Put in a team with his brothers led by Kakashi, Tarō is assured by his restrained older brother to eat the lunches their teacher left out. Having exactly followed his orders, Kakashi fails Tarō and his brothers. Sometime later, Tarō and his brothers thank Kakashi for sending them back to the
Academy
, as they realise what he was trying to teach them, much to his surprise.

Personality

Tarō did not like any of Kakashi's training methods and hated the fact that Kakashi had failed him and his brother. Later though he realised why Kakashi had failed him and came to respect him for it.

Appearance

Tarō has a short crop of brown hair that is smooth on one side but spiky on the other. He also has big black eyes.
