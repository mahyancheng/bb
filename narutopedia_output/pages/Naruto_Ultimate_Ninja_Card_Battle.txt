Naruto: Ultimate Ninja Card Battle
 is the first Naruto game designed for the Data Card arcade system.

Gameplay

Like other Data Carddass games,
Narutimate Card Battle
 is an interactive card game, using real cards and 3D graphics to animate battles on screen. Also like other Data Card games, which are often derived from Playstation 2 games, much of
Narutimate Card Battle
 originates from
Naruto: Ultimate Ninja 3
.
