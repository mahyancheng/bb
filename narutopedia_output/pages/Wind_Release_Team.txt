The
Wind Release Team
 is a subdivision of the
Fourth Division
 of the
Allied Shinobi Forces
. Lead by
Temari
, it consists of skilled
Wind Release
 users, mainly equipped with
Giant Folding Fans
. The team has several battle formations, one of which (Formation C) is the collective use of the
Wind Release: Cast Net
 technique. This team fought the
Third Raikage
, whose tough body was able to withstand all of their attacks.
