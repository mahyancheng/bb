"
When Time Stands Still
" (
止まったままの時間
,
Tomatta Mama no Jikan
) is episode 166 of the original
Naruto
 anime.

Synopsis

A flashback reveals that
Sagi
, not
Toki
, died, and that Toki took on her brother's identity to avenge his death. Toki assumes the identity of the
Cursed Warrior
, and attempts to attack
Mōsō
, but is subdued by Mōsō's imitation ninjutsu. Mōsō's
Watari ninja
 and
Naruto
's group join the battle. While his companions fight the Watari ninja, Naruto pursues Mōsō and is caught in a
genjutsu
. Mōsō reveals himself to be Hōki the migrating ninja, who plans to travel and collect as many techniques as possible.
