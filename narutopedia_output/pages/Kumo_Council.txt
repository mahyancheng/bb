This council is an assembly presided over by the
Raikage
, consisting of Kumogakure's highest ranking and most prominent
shinobi
 which gather to discuss strategy and tactics regarding the village.

The Council convened prior to the start of the
Fourth Shinobi World War
 in order to discuss further tactics and the general cooperation within the
Allied Shinobi Forces
. Unbeknown to the council, this meeting was spied upon by
Kisame Hoshigaki
, who proceeded to leak vital information regarding Kumogakure's military strength back to
Tobi
.
