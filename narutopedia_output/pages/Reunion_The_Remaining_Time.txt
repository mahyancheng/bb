"
Reunion: The Remaining Time
" (
再会　残された時間
,
Saikai Nokosareta Jikan
) is episode 182 of the original
Naruto
 anime.

Synopsis

As
Sumaru
, the rest of the Star trainees and
Naruto
's teammates arrive,
Akahoshi
 takes Sumaru hostage and forces
Natsuhi
 to hand over the star.
Neji
 examines Natsuhi and reveals that she could die if she uses her
star chakra technique
 again. After Sumaru awakens, he speaks with his mother, who reveals that while she knew that she had to leave him behind to spare him the pain of becoming an exile, she regrets leaving him. While Naruto and
Lee
 insist on going after Akahoshi, Neji says the mission is over and they, as they cannot betray a client. That night, Natsuhi goes to the star temple to steal the star, but is killed by Akahoshi and
his

men
. Sumaru, following with Naruto,
Team Guy
, and Hokuto, senses her death and flies to the temple. Akahoshi declares himself Fourth
Hoshikage
 and orders his ninja to kill the Konoha shinobi, Sumaru and
Hokuto
 if they set foot in the village.
