The
Konoha Special Mission Platoon
 (
木ノ葉特別任務小隊
,
Konoha Tokubetsu Ninmu Shōtai
,
literally meaning:
 Tree Leaf Special Mission Platoon)
 is one of the many teams specially formed to carry out missions after the loss in manpower following the
Konoha Crush
. While returning to
Konoha
 after a tiring mission, the team senses the
Sound Four
 leaving the village.
Genma
 and
Raidō
 confront them, but are defeated.
Shizune
 later finds them and provides emergency
medical
 attention.
