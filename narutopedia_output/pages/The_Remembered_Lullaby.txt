Synopsis

With the star being stolen, the group has no leads on the mysterious thief who can use chakra to fly.
Naruto
 learns that
Sumaru
's parents died defending the star from a foreign ninja.
Tenten
 and
Lee
 attempt to track two of
Akahoshi
's men, but they are discovered and find no answers. Sumaru and some of his friends came across a masked ninja and restrained him, but he renders them unconscious and kidnaps Sumaru.
