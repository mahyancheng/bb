Crimson Fist
 (
赤色岩拳
) is a ninja of
Konohagakure
, and one of the playable characters in
Naruto: Ultimate Ninja Online
.

Personality

He is a steady, simple, honest and trustworthy person. His mature appearance contrasts his young age. He enjoys gardening.

Abilities

A reliable team-mate who possesses a steady and burly physique. His forte lies in
Earth Release
 techniques and
taijutsu
.

In Other Media

Naruto
 and
Naruto: Shippūden
 alongside the main characters.
