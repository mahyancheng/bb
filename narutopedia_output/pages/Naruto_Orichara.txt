Naruto Orichara
 (
NARUTO -ナルト- オリキャラ
,
Naruto Orikyara
), short for
Naruto Original Characters
, is a recurring feature in the tankōbon editions of
Naruto
 in which fans submit drawings of their own original characters. The best of these characters are then displayed across several single-page Excellent Work (
優秀作
,
Yūshūsaku
) galleries between chapters. The feature culminates with
Masashi Kishimoto
 selecting his favourite submission and providing an illustration of the character in his own style. The feature first appeared in volume 22 and continued through all subsequent volumes of the manga, with the exception of volumes 61 and 72. The character designs for Watage Mimikaki (#27), Tsurara (#41), and Azari (#42) served as the basis for the
Naruto: Ultimate Ninja Online
 playable character classes Azure Fang, Breeze Dancer, and Scarlet Blaze, respectively.

After the original manga ended,
Boruto: Naruto Next Generations
 reintroduced the Orichara section as part of the Borukura (
ボルクラ
) (short for
Boruto Cluster
) in the
Weekly Shōnen Jump
. Winning Borukura entries have gone on to appear in both the anime and manga.
