Personality

Azami is a positive, sweet and devoted girl. She didn't believe that her grandfather had died and thus, she daily left him riceballs at his supposed gravesite.

Appearance

Azami is a young girl with light brown hair pulled into a high ponytail and shoulder-length side bangs. She also has stone grey eyes and thin eyebrows. She wears a yellow and black jacket with a high collar and red pants.
