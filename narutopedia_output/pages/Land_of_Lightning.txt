The
Land of Lightning
 (
雷の国
,
Kaminari no Kuni
) is  located on a peninsula north-east of the
Land of Fire
 and is one of the
Five Great Shinobi Countries
. Its government leader is the
Lightning Daimyō
. In the centre of the country are vast mountain ranges, whose many thunderstorms are said to give the country its name. From these mountain ranges, many rivers flow to the sea, creating a very crooked coastline that displays an impressive oceanic beauty. There are many hot springs located within the country. The country's western border with the Land of Frost has a large desert.
