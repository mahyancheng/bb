This article is about the ninjutsu used by an unnamed Anbu. For the ninjutsu used by Kujaku, see
Wind Cutter Technique
.

Using their
chakra
 or a
ninja tool
 such as a
war fan
 to manipulate or create an external source of wind, the user can create one to several blades of wind, which are capable of slicing through their target with ease.
Temari
 and a group of
tessenjutsu
 users from
Sunagakure
 also used this technique as a means of countering a powerful, incoming torrent of wind.
