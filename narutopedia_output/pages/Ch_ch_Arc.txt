The
Chōchō Arc
 (
チョウチョウ編
,
Chōchō-hen
), also known as the
Super Chōchō Butterfly Mode Arc
 (
超チョウチョウ蝶モード編
,
Chō Chōchō Chō Mōdo-hen
), is the eighth
arc
 of the
Boruto: Naruto Next Generations
 anime. It covers episodes 67 to 69. It is preceded by the
Versus Momoshiki Arc
 and followed by the
Mitsuki's Disappearance Arc
. The arc focuses on
Chōchō
 and is based on
Kishimoto
's idea, with him also providing some dialogue for it.
