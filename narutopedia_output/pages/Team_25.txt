Team 25
 is a shinobi team from
Konohagakure
, consisting of
Hōki Taketori
,
Hako Kuroi
,
Renga Kokubō
, and formerly an unknown
shinobi
.
Sai Yamanaka
 was later appointed as the team's new leader when the team's former leader was taken off duty due to disabilities. He considers the
genin
's abilities particularly suited for intelligence missions. As such, he had them often assigned to Anbu missions and trained as such.
 Following Hōki's chūnin promotion, he was appointed as team captain as Team 25 became a three-person team.
