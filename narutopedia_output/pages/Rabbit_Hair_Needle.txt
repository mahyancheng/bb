The
Rabbit Hair Needle
 is a Kekkei Mōra taijutsu used by Kaguya Ōtsutsuki.

Usage

Kaguya hardens her lengthened hair to launch many needles with enough ferocity to pierce even
Sasuke
's
Susanoo
. By using her
Byakugan
, she targets the vital points of her enemy. It's a technique that effectively seals the movements of an enemy.

Trivia

When written as "棘", "toge" means "thorn; splinter".
