Kiki
 (
キキ
) is a monkey and a trained circus animal.

Background

Kiki was partnered with
Chamū
 in a circus and together, were its best act.

Personality

Kiki cares about his owner
Hikaru Tsuki
 and his friends.

Appearance

Kiki is a brown furred monkey with a long tail and big black eyes. Kiki wears a small red vest and a small red hat. Kiki also wears a tiny yellow cape and a yellow bow-tie on its tail.

Plot Overview

Kiki is a trained circus animal belonging to the circus that
Michiru Tsuki
 purchases. He is Chamū's partner. At the end of the movie, Kiki and Chamū restrain
Ishidate
, allowing the injured
Naruto Uzumaki
 to hit him with the
Crescent Moon Rasengan
.

Trivia

Kiki, along with Chamū, Hikaru Tsuki, and Michiru Tsuki, appears in
Naruto: Path of the Ninja 2
 on a poster promoting the film.
