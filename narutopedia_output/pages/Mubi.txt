 (

) is a
genin
 from

Personality

He has been described as patient, and methodical. He like his teammates seems to really enjoy a fight, especially one that they can win easily.

Appearance

Mubi's full appearance.
Mubi without his mask.

Mubi has a low, trimmed black hair and wore a yellow jumpsuit that was stitched up the middle along with a pair of sandals. He also wore a
forehead protector
 that covered his whole head, a
rebreather
 and a sash around his face leaving only his left eye visible.

In the anime, he was seen in a kimono shirt, pants, slippers, bandages around his legs and waist and a pair of glasses with the right eye obstructed.

Abilities






Mubi using the Earth Release: Underground Projection Fish Technique.



Mubi is a fairly competent shinobi with knowledge of techniques across a wide range. He is especially proficient in
genjutsu
 and
clone techniques
 being able to use the
Mist Servant Technique
 which created clones of himself as well as the
Sly Mind Affect Technique
 both of which were used to wear out their enemies before attacking them. In the anime, he has been shown using a crossbow, and also the sword in the battle with Sasuke, which, however, was easily knocked out of his hands with the help of a kunai

Nature Transformation

He is also able to use
earth
 release techniques which allowed him to
travel
 and emerge from underground without disrupting the surrounding area. In the anime it was also shown that he is able to create
clones from water
.
