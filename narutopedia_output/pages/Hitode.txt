Hitode
 (
ヒトデ
) is in charge of protecting the shipments of gold that the
Land of the Sea
 sends to
Kirigakure
 as payment to protect its many islands.

Appearance

Hitode is a balding man with black hair and dark-coloured eyes. His sideburns connect to his moustache, beard, and eyebrows. He has large lips and wears the Land of Seas boat guard uniform. He keeps a sword strapped to his left leg.

Abilities

Hitode carries a
sword
 with him which points to Hitode having some skill in
kenjutsu
.

Part I

Kaima Capture Mission

He's seen guarding the shipments of gold in the Land of the Sea.

Trivia

"Hitode" (
ヒトデ
) means "sea star" most likely referring to his country of origin.
