Ugai
 (
ウガイ
) is a
medical-nin
 of
Konohagakure
.

Personality

Ugai has been described as both serious and methodical.

Appearance

Ugai is a young man with dark, brown hair and dark eyes. He wears the standard uniform of the hospital medical ninja which consists of a high-collared cream-coloured outfit and what appears to be a medical hat.

Abilities

Noted as an inexperienced, yet knowledgeable,
medical-nin
, Ugai has basic knowledge of medical treatments and
medical ninjutsu
, as well as good chakra control in general.

Might Guy
 find
Tsunade
 in the
Konoha Hospital
. He then looked on in shock when
Migaki
 arrives suddenly, telling Tsunade that something terrible had happened.

Trivia






Another shinobi appearing instead of Ugai in the anime.



In the anime, a different shinobi is shown in place of Ugai. This shinobi appears much older, is physically larger, wears glasses, and has a full beard and moustache.
