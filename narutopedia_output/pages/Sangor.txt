Sangorō
 (
サンゴロウ
) is a miner from the
Land of Rivers
.

Appearance

Sangoro has a short crop of black hair and thick eyebrows. He has a very short beard that is grey in colour. This beard is very close to his skin. He also wears a bandanna on his head covering his hair and it is tied in the back.

Part I

Kurosuki Family Removal Mission

Sangorō and his friends left their village after
Raiga Kurosuki
 took control of it. They were caught in a storm after leaving and eventually reached the
Land of Fire
, where they met
Naruto Uzumaki
. Sangorō returned to
Katabami Gold Mine
 along with Naruto and other
Konoha
 ninja that were sent to investigate Raiga.
