The
Curry of Life Shop
 (
命のカレー屋
,
Inochi no Karē Ya
) is a small shop located in the
Land of Rivers
 near the
Katabami Gold Mine
. Its current owner is
Sanshō
. The heir to the shop is her son,
Karashi
, who is also an Assistant Chef.
Ranmaru
 eventually becomes an Apprentice Chef. The shop's specialty is the eponymous
Curry of Life
.
