Hōsei
 (
ホウセイ
) is a
genin
 of
Konohagakure
.

Personality

Hōsei seemed to be afraid of challenges as seen when he chose to drop out of the
Chūnin Exams
 knowing full well that him doing so would also cause his teammates to be disqualified as well.

Appearance

He has short, spiky, black hair and wears a simple black attire with mesh armour underneath. Along with this he wears his blue
forehead protector
 in the traditional manner but with an extra strap going under his jaw.

Part I

Chūnin Exams

Main article:
Chūnin Exams

Hōsei enters the Chūnin Exams, but, afraid of never again being able to take the exams if he failed the tenth question during the first phase with
Ibiki Morino
, he chose to drop out, disqualifying the rest of his team in the process.

Trivia

"Hōsei" has various meanings, for example "sewing" (
縫製
), "legislation" (
法制
), "rectitude" (
方正
) or "justice administration/law and politics" (
法政
).
