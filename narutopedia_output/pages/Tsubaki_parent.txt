Appearance

Tsubaki has long brown hair, with her bangs hanging over her forehead. She also has brown eyes. She wears a long-sleeved white shirt with a black tank-top like shirt over it.

Personality

Tsubaki seems to hate Naruto and the fact that he has the Nine-Tails inside of him.
