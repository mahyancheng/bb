Background

When the country was dealing with an epidemic, the daimyō used this as an opportunity to make a fortune by restricting medical care to only the highest payers, leaving most of the citizens unable to afford it. Later, the daimyō discovered a medical-nin,
Aosa
, developing a cheaper and more effective treatment for the population, and sent his elite assassins
Kurobane
 and
Jujumaru
 to eliminate him and his students while also securing their treatment variants.

Personality

He is very amoral self-serving man, willing to sacrifice his own citizens for a profit. Likewise, so shameless, he is willing to kill innocent people who interfere with his personal gain.
