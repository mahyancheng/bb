The
Exploding Sphere
 (
爆砕玉
,
Bakusaidama
) is a ninja tool stored within Tenten's large scroll. It is a metal sphere with
kunai
 welded onto it and a hidden
explosive tag
 attached to it. It explodes upon contact, sending kunai and fragments of the sphere flying out as shrapnel.

Trivia

While unnamed in the anime and manga, it is named in
Naruto Shippūden: Ultimate Ninja 4
.
