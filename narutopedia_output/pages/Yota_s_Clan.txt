This travelling clan had the ability to
manipulate weather
, which they used for their trades.

Yota
 was especially good at manipulating the weather among his clan despite of his young age.

Overview





Yota's clan performing a ritual.



They were wanderers by trade, travelling from village to village selling weather. They also practised a ritual to help the individual in the ritual's centre to manipulate the weather.

Trivia

All members of this clan use bandages and tracks.
