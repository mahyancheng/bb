Tezutsu Mikawa
 (
三河テヅツ
,
Mikawa Tezutsu
) is a shinobi from
Kumogakure
.

Appearance

Tezutsu has fair skin, spiky brown hair, brown eyebrows, and a green teardrop marking tattoo on his left cheek. His outfit consists of a white, rope tied headband with red markings on it, a blue kimono with white trimmings, underneath it is a mesh armoured shirt with a visible red trimming on the top, white bandages on his legs, white shorts with red triangle patterns on them. Tezutsu also has a cannon strapped to his back.

Abilities

Not much is known of Tezutsu's abilities other than that he uses a cannon when on the battlefield.
