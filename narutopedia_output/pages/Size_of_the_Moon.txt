Trivia

This is the second opening in the
Naruto: Shippūden
 and overall
Naruto
 series in which none of the
Konoha 11
 appear except Naruto. The first opening was
Not Even Sudden Rain Can Defeat Me
.

In the second version, Nonō is depicted with black and red colours instead of the original colours.

Strangely, Ōnoki's outfit is in the condition it was in before Madara's
assault
 in one instance, but is damaged throughout the rest of the opening.
