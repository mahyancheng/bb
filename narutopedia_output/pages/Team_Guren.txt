This is a team formed upon
Orochimaru
's orders to capture the
Three-Tails
. They were chosen from the best of the initial twenty-one prisoners that survived a battle royal, which
Guren
 had organised; sixteen of them tried to rebel afterwards, however, Guren killed them. Guren leads the remaining five and
Yūkimaru
 on a mission to capture the beast. Their efforts are repeatedly interrupted by the shinobi from
Konohagakure
. After four of the members are killed and Guren realises that Orochimaru has little concern for her, she defects with Yūkimaru and
Gozu
.
