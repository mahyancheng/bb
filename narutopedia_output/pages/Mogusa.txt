Mogusa
 (
モグサ
) is a
medical-nin
 of
Konohagakure
.

Personality

Mogusa has been described as a cool and calm
shinobi
 who never displays impatience in any situation.

Appearance

Mogusa's most distinctive feature are the markings that cover the upper half of the left and right side of his face. He also has what appears to be large pupiless eyes and wears the standard attire of the Konoha Medical unit.

Abilities

He is a
medical ninjutsu
 expert. He assisted in
resuscitating

Neji Hyūga
 using a delicate, high-level technique. In the anime, he can also use the
Mystical Palm Technique
.
 As a
medical-nin
, Mogusa also has knowledge of medical treatments and ninjutsu, as well as good chakra control in general.

Part I

Sasuke Recovery Mission

When Neji was brought back to the village in critical condition, he replaced
Kitō
 in assisting with the Healing Resuscitation Regeneration Technique to heal him after Kitō became exhausted. Together with the other medical ninja's hard work, they were able to heal Neji's devastating wounds.

Mizuki Tracking Mission

In the anime, Mogusa along with
Mitate
,
Iyashi
,
Kusushi
, and a few other medical-nin were deployed from the hospital to save
Asuma Sarutobi
,
Kurenai Yūhi
, and the others after their encounter with
Mizuki
. They were able to give them first aid on the stop and then later relocated them to the hospital to recover.

Part II
