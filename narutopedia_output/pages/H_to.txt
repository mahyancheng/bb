Hāto
 (
ハート
) was a former student of
En no Gyōja
 and the father of
Shiranami
.

Background

He passed on the teachings from his sensei to his son, who was even more skilled than Hāto. Shiranami killed his father during the
Third Shinobi World War
 trying to obtain the
Tsuchigumo Clan kinjutsu
, which Hāto was protecting.

Appearance

Hāto had short spiked up black hair. He wore a grey outfit with a silver sleeveless haori which had grey trimmings and a leaf symbol that showed his worthiness to wield to the clan's kinjutsu, a long sleeve grey shirt inside and long pants, a black bracelet on each wrist and black shinobi sandals.

Abilities

Hotaru
 has stated that Hāto was very skilled in
ninjutsu
. He could use the
Tsuchigumo Clan

kinjutsu
, which was strong enough to destroy an entire village.

Trivia

His name is the Japanese way of pronouncing the word "heart".
