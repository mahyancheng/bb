Michizō Manaka
 (
間中道蔵
,
Manaka Michizō
) is a bandit from the
Land of Stone
.

Personality

He is easily offended, as seen when
Sazanami
 gives
Akamaru
 some milk, on Michizō's bill. He also sees no problem in stealing money from children.

Appearance

He is a big bald man, with a small black beard around his mouth. He has small black eyes. He wears a red scarf, and a green vest which is tied at the front. He also wears a belt, were he has got his
sword
. He also wears fingerless gloves.

Abilities

He is seen wielding a
sword
, so he is assumed to have some knowledge of
kenjutsu
.

Michizō is seen playing cards with some other bandits, when
Naruto Uzumaki
,
Kiba Inuzuka
 and
Hinata Hyūga
 goes into a inn to seek information about another bandit called
Gosunkugi
. When Naruto pulls out his wallet to pay for some food, Michizō grabs his arm, and attempts to steal his money. He is stopped by Sazanami, who offers to buy some milk for Akamaru on Michizō's behalf. This makes him draw his sword, but before he could swing it the sword is cut in half by Sazanami, who quickly knocks Michizō in the stomach, making him collapse. Sazanami proceeds to pull out a wanted poster on Michizō, giving the award to the bartender, for all the damages that were made. He status and whereabouts after this is unknown.
