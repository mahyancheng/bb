"
Tonton! I'm Counting on You!
" (
走れトントン!お前の鼻が頼りだってばよ
,
Hashire Tonton! Omae no Hana ga Tayori Dattebayo
) is episode 143 of the original
Naruto
 anime.

Synopsis

Shizune
 and her team encounter
Mizuki
, but all of them are defeated. When
Tonton
 comes back to warn
Tsunade
,
Naruto
 takes the initiative to find Shizune with Tonton's help.
Kakashi
 arrives and finds out the situation. He has another mission. So, he summons
Pakkun
 who will guide
Iruka
 to Naruto. Iruka eventually catches up to Naruto and Pakkun sends Tonton back to the village. Pakkun leads them and they soon find Shizune. They chase after Mizuki, when they soon fall into a trap. Iruka reveals that Shizune is not Shizune, but actually Mizuki. Mizuki attacks Iruka while Naruto deals with
Fūjin
 and
Raijin
. Naruto soon finds that his attacks are ineffective.
