" (
ナルト死す

Naruto
 anime.

Synopsis

Neji
,
Tenten
, and
Kakashi
 stare at a grave. As they head back they throw off a pursuer with a replacement, but are being shadowed by another.
Kōmei
 is about to be forced to commit seppuku, but the
Cursed Warrior
 saves him. Shortly afterwards, it is revealed that
Naruto
 disguised himself as the Cursed Warrior. Kakashi fights
Nagare
, who uses moves copied from other ninja. Naruto, Neji and Tenten discover that the true identity of the Cursed Warrior is the feudal lord himself, who seeks to kill
Mōsō
 to avenge his father.
