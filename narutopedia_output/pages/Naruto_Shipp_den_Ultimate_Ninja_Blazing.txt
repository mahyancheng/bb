Naruto Shippūden: Ultimate Ninja Blazing
 was the first instalment of the
Naruto: Ultimate Ninja
 series for mobile systems. It was developed by Namco Bandai and published by GREE.

Gameplay

Naruto Shippūden: Ultimate Ninja Blazing
 was a free to play social turn-based action RPG. The game retains many elements from
Naruto: Shinobi Collection Shippū Ranbu
, but with a different combat system.

Story

The game's story picked up story elements from the original
Naruto
 anime. It covered the series from the beginning of
Naruto
, to the defeat of
Kakuzu
. The game also covered various side content such as
Kakashi Gaiden
.

Closure

On December 8, 2020, it was announced that the game would be shutting down its servers on February 9, 2021.
