D-rank
 (
Dランク
,
Dī ranku
) is one of the six classifications. It is preceded by
E-rank
 and followed by
C-rank
. It is typically used when classifying
techniques
 intended for
ninja
 of the
genin
 level. D-rank techniques are often one of the first techniques a ninja will learn after leaving the
Academy
.

There are also D-rank
missions
, the lowest classification a mission can receive. These missions are usually assigned to genin.
Naruto Uzumaki
 describes D-rank missions as not being actual missions as they deal with tasks as simple as finding missing pets and weeding a garden. D-rank missions pay between 5,000 and 50,000
ryō
.
