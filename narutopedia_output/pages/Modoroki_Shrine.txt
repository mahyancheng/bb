The Modoroki Shrine.



The
Modoroki Shrine
 is a shrine located along the southern shore of
Nagi Island
, off the coast of the
Land of Tea
. A giant stone staircase, known as the
Stone Steps that Tear Hearts Apart
, leads to the shrine. It serves as the halfway point of the Todoroki Great Shrine Race, after
Degarashi Port
 and before the
Todoroki Great Shrine
 on
Ouza Island
, where participants retrieve one of Ryochu's Jewels to dedicate to the Todoroki Great Shine.

During the
Land of Tea Escort Mission
,
Naruto Uzumaki
 escorted
Idate Morino
 to the shrine to retrieve one of Ryochu's Jewels.
