Sentoki
 (
千時
) is one of the young
ninja monks
-in-training at the
Fire Temple
.

Personality

Sentoki cares about and worries for his comrades.

Appearance

Sentoki is bald with a scar running across the left side of his head and dark eyes. He wears the standard attire of the ninja monks.

Part II

Akatsuki Suppression Mission

Main article:
Akatsuki Suppression Mission

Sentoki was away from the Fire Temple when
Akatsuki
 members
Hidan
 and
Kakuzu
 attacked it. He returned immediately after, finding the temple in ruins and the Akatsuki members as they were just about to leave with
Chiriku
's body. He subsequently travelled to
Konoha
 to notify them of the attack and requested help. He was present when Tsunade addressed a meeting with the
Twenty Platoons
. When
Asuma Sarutobi
 asked what happened to Chiriku, Sentoki informed him of Chiriku's fate.

He is one of only three known monk survivors in the attack, along with
Bansai
 and
Zenza
.

Trivia

Sentoki's name means
"a thousand hours"
 or
