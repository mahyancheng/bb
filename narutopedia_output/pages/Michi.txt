Naruto Exhibition Official Guidebook "Michi"
 (
NARUTO -ナルト-展 公式ガイドブック「道-MICHI-」
,
Naruto-ten Kōshiki Gaidobukku "Michi"
) is the official guidebook for the
Naruto
 exhibition. It's B5 size and costs 1.300 Yen. It was released for non-visitors of the exhibition in May 2015.

The book contains an interview between
Masashi Kishimoto
 and
Eiichiro Oda
, the creator of
One Piece
. Furthermore, it shows pictures from the Tokyo venue of the
Naruto
 exhibition and many coloured images from the manga itself.
