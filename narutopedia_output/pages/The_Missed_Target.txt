" (

Naruto
 anime.

Synopsis

A
Giant Eagle
 drops countless copies of the letter of introduction into town, serving as a diversion from
Gennō
's true plan. The
genin
 group discovers that a
kunai
 target in the
Academy
 dōjō is actually the trigger for
explosive tags
 in the Academy, and that explosive tags are planted all across the village.
