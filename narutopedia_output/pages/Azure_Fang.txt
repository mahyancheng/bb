Azure Fang
 (
苍蓝之牙
) is a ninja of
Konohagakure
, and one of the playable characters in
Naruto: Ultimate Ninja Online
.

Personality

She has a curious and fun-loving personality and is compassionate as well. She is determined to heal incurable diseases in the ninja world. She enjoys cleansing short blades and bathing.

Abilities

Her techniques are based on Water Release techniques, along with use of poison and Medical Ninjutsu. Her forte lies in chakra control. She is a user of
senjutsu
, but has no specific techniques associated with it.

In Other Media

Video Games

Naruto: Ultimate Ninja Online

Azure Fang is one of the five player avatars available in the game. They work through the plot of
Naruto
 and
Naruto: Shippūden
 alongside the main characters.
