Mohawk Fish Market.



The
Mohawk Fish Market
 (
モヒカーン類魚場
) is a seafood market located in
Konohagakure
 that sells a variety of seafood including fish and octopus. The building is also used as a training ground for the beginning basics of
medical ninjutsu
, where trainees practise using the
Mystical Palm Technique
 on dead fish and octopus provided by the market. The inside contains an extensive library of books regarding medical ninjutsu.

The fish market is where
Tsunade
 trained
Sakura Haruno
, and later
Ino Yamanaka
, in the basics of medical ninjutsu.
