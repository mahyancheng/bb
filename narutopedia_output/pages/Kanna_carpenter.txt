Kanna
 (
カンナ
,
Kanna
) is a citizen of
Konohagakure
 as well as the current Master Carpenter.

Background

He is the son of a carpenter, who was the master carpenter thirty years prior the series. He supervised the rebuilding of Konohagakure, where he hired a lot of foreign help, among others,
Gennō
.

Personality

He seemed like a kind man, eager to help the
Konoha 11
 with their requests. He also seemed organised, knowing where each document he has is located. He is also very proud, stating he could never face the
Fifth Hokage
 again, since he let the blueprints be stolen.

Appearance

He has short black hair, and a black full beard, as well as small black eye brows. He also has light brown eyes and a white headband. he wears a white shirt with a purple jacket over it and a black pair of pants, which also go around his feet.

Part I

Konoha Plans Recapture Mission

He held a meting with the Konoha 11, where
Shikamaru
 receives a list on which is written all the blueprints that where stolen by Gennō. He proceeds to tell them how Gennō got to the village, and gives Shikamaru Gennō's fake letter of recommendation.  He later was surprised when Shikamaru learned that several other blueprints where also missing.

Trivia

Although Shikamaru said that his papers are blueprints, Tsunade later states that they were nothing more than letters.
