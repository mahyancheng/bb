Kyūroku Wagarashi
 (
ワガラシ九六
,
Wagarashi Kyūroku
) is the head of the
Wagarashi family
 in the
Land of Tea
, which is a clan warring with the nearby
Wasabi family
.

Personality

He is a very dishonest and manipulative man.

Appearance

He has short black hair, and small dark eyes with a small wart over the left. He also has a black moustache. His attire consist of a green and white kimono.

His clan and the
minister
 of the
daimyō of the Land of Tea
 challenged the Wasabi family to a race. Kyūroku tried to win the race unfairly, but the
daimyō
 found out what he had done and disbanded his clan for their evil deeds.

Trivia

"Kyūroku" (九六) literally means "Nine Six". It also denotes an
ozeki
-ranked sumo wrestler unable to fulfill the rank's expectations.
