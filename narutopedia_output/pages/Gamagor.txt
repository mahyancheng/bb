Gamagorō
 (
ガマ五郎
) is a giant
toad
 that resides on
Mount Myōboku
.

Personality

Gamagorō is pleasant and respectful, using honorifics when referring to his summoner, and even going as far as to pledge to go anywhere Konohamaru wanted.

Appearance

Gamagorō is a huge purple toad with a lighter shade of purple on his underside. He also has red markings around his eyes.
