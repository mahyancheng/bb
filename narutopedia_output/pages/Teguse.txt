Teguse
 (
テグセ
) is a poor man living in the
Land of Waves
.

Personality

Teguse wasn't above stealing from strangers.

Appearance

Teguse has a bald head and small black eyes. He wears a green-coloured hat and what appears to be a black unzipped trench coat. He also has a black moustache.

Part I

Prologue — Land of Waves

He tried to steal
Sakura Haruno
's purse, but she mistook him for a pervert and kicked him in the face.

Trivia

Teguse (手癖, also written as "Tekuse")  translates into "having sticky fingers" or "compulsive thievery", alluding to the character's criminal activities.

In
Naruto: Konoha Ninpōchō
, Teguse appears again after his failed attempt to steal Sakura. He fights Sakura with a knife, but isn't strong enough to defeat her and flees.
