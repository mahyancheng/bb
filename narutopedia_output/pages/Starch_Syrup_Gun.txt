Trivia

Mizuame
 is a Japanese sweetener made by converting starch to sugars. It takes the form of a clear, thick, and most of all sticky liquid. It can be literally translated as "Water Candy", emphasising its likeness to water and explaining why it is used in a Water Release technique.
