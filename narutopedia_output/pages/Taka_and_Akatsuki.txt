"
Taka and Akatsuki
" (
“鷹”と“暁”
,
"Taka" to "Akatsuki"
) is chapter 404 of the original
Naruto
 manga.

Summary

Naruto
 is called to
Tsunade
's office. There, he finds
Team 7
 and a number of
toads
 waiting for him.
Fukasaku
 informs him of
Jiraiya
's death. Elsewhere,
Taka
 has a meeting with
Akatsuki
. Sasuke states his intentions to kill the leaders of
Konoha
 responsible for the
Uchiha
's destruction but does not care about the villagers. Akatsuki volunteers to help, but only if Taka captures the
Eight-Tails
 while they handle the
Nine-Tails
. Afterwards,
Tobi
 speaks with
Zetsu
, glad to be rid of
Itachi
 at last. He admits that the losses the organisation has suffered have been severe, but that they were worth it to gain Sasuke's loyalty.
