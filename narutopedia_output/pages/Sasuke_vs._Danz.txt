"
Sasuke vs. Danzō…!!
" (
サスケVSダンゾウ…!!
,
Sasuke Bāsasu Danzō…!!
) is chapter 476 of the original
Naruto
 manga.

Summary

Kakashi
 prepares to chase after
Sakura
, knowing that she is currently no match for
Sasuke
. As
Naruto
 thinks about everything everyone has told him about Sasuke, he begins to hyperventilate and passes out. Elsewhere, Sasuke asks
Danzō
 if it is true that
Konoha
 ordered
Itachi
 to kill the
Uchiha clan
. Danzō attacks him but is caught by Sasuke's
Susanoo
. Danzō admits it, then curses Itachi for putting so much importance in Sasuke, even going so far as telling Sasuke a secret that he was supposed to die with. Sasuke crushes him in anger. Despite his apparent death, Danzō appears behind Sasuke and offers to end their conversation.
