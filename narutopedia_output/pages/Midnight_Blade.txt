Midnight Blade
(黑夜之刃) is a ninja of
Konohagakure
, and one of the playable characters in
Naruto: Ultimate Ninja Online
.

Personality

He has a cool and quiet personality and likes being alone. He enjoys counting money.

Abilities

His main forte are Lightning Release techniques and Kenjutsu. A bounty hunter that specialises in assassination skills.
