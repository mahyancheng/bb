The Jofuku Forest.



The
Jofuku Forest
 (
徐福の森
,
Jofuku no Mori
,
literally meaning:
 Gradual Good Fortune Forest), also known as the
Lost Forest
, is a location on
Mount Jofuku
 on the border of
Land of Fire
. It is known for its
Jofuku flowers
, which are used for medicinal purposes. An old lady from the nearby village said that the forest is haunted.
Furofuki
 lives in the forest inspecting the flower.
Naruto
,
Sakura
 and
Ino
 were sent to the forest to deliver a message to Furofuki from
Tsunade
, telling her to give them the Jofuku flower so
Might Guy
 could make a medicine for
Rock Lee
.
