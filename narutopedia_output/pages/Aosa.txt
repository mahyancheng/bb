Aosa
 (
アオサ
) is a
medical-nin
 of the
Land of Calm Seas
 and
Mozuku
's sensei.

Background

When the
daimyō
 restricted the medical treatment during an epidemic, seeking to financially profit off it, Aosa secretly developed a cheaper and more effective treatment for the population. However, the daimyō discovered this and sent his elite assassins to eliminate him and his fellow students. Before being forced into hiding, Aosa gave enough money to his student for them to make the journey to his close acquaintances abroad.

Personality

Aosa is a kind man who cares about the safety of his village. Aware of his daimyō's callous nature, he was willing to risk his life for the sake of others. He greatly loved his students. His compassion and efforts earned him great praise and loyalty from his students.

Appearance

Aosa is fair-skinned man of above-average height. He has sharp, blue eyes, and short, brown hair combed to the side. He wears blue robes and round, frameless glasses.

Abilities

While little is known about his abilities as a shinobi, Aosa is revealed to be a brilliant researcher, having developed on his own means to cure an entire epidemic. He is also apparently a capable teacher, having taught his students how to use medical ninjutsu and continue his work in his absence.
