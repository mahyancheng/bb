Appearance

The Lion-Turtle was a very large beast, and had the combined appearances of both a lion and a turtle. Covering its face, was a green mane of fur as well as an orange protective plate with two piercings on the sides. Its body had many distinctive red markings covering its outer shell. When merged with Ni, Ni sticks to the top of its head with her legs completely merged with the creature.

Abilities

The beast possessed the ability to breathe fire from its mouth, and shoot steam from its nose. But as mentioned by
Kiba Inuzuka
, it is very slow and cannot counter fast moves.
