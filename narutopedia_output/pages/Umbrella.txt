A
Japanese umbrella
 is a common accessory and weapon among ninja from
Amegakure
. It can be equipped with many
senbon
 that can be released from spring-loaded mechanisms situated in the ribs, which are used to perform the
Senbon Shower
. Worn on the back, the ninja can quickly open the umbrella and hurl it into the air.

In the anime,
Shura
 has an umbrella that has had several modifications made to his advantage. It could spew flames from its tip and shoot wires to bind a target. Additionally, the handle could be removed from the umbrella to reveal a hidden sword.
