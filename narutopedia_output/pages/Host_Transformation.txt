Host Transformation
 is a ninjutsu exclusive to the player avatars in
Naruto to Boruto: Shinobi Striker
. The player momentarily takes the form of one of the following
Tailed Beasts
:

Two-Tails
 (
二尾
,
Nibi
): As Matatabi, the player jumps up and dives down on the opponent, causing a fiery explosion.

Three-Tails
 (
三尾
,
Sanbi
): As Isobu, the player spins around, damaging any nearby opponent.

Six-Tails
 (
六尾
,
Rokubi
): As Saiken, the player gathers bubbles together to form a
large purple-coloured bubble
.

Seven-Tails
 (
重明
,
Nanabi
): As Chōmei, the player uses their wings to release a large gust of wind.
