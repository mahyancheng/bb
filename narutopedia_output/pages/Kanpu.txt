Kanpu
 (
カンプ
) is a caretaker at an
orphanage
 located on the outskirts of
Konohagakure
.

Background

He and
Keiri
 warned
Nonō Yakushi
 about the orphanage's financial struggles, which only became worse after
Kabuto
 was taken in. He was defensive of Kabuto when it was discovered he needed glasses and saw great potential in him. Sometime later, when
Danzō Shimura
 came to the orphanage to recruit a new member for
Root
, Kanpu became outraged when Danzō threatened to steal what little money the orphanage had if they didn't agree to his terms.

Personality

Kanpu is a very caring man as seen when he defended a young Kabuto from Keiri's verbal assault and when he became outraged when Danzō was going to take one of the orphanage's children. He is said to have poor management skills.

Appearance

Kanpu is an old man who has grey beard and moustache and black eyes. He also wears a pair of rectangular glasses and a black kimono with white trimmings and a white apron.

Trivia

"Kanpu" (
還付
) means "restoration".
