Personality

He was a somewhat impressionable man as demonstrated during the food competition. While little was shown of his personality, the daimyō showed a distaste for foods which lost its consistency and turned soft after his tasters were done checking it for poison, such as ramen. He seemed to be a happy person, but a little arrogant about his position as a daimyō.

Appearance

The daimyō wore the traditional headdress and attire of one of his station. Unique traits of his included his large dark lips and wide mouth.
