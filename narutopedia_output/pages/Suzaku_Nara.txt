Suzaku Nara
 (
奈良スザク
,
Nara Suzaku
) is a shinobi from
Konohagakure
's
Nara clan
.

Appearance

He wears his hair in a high ponytail with the ends styled into what resembles horns. He also has a small goatee, faint lines around his mouth and wears the standard uniform of a Konoha shinobi.

Abilities

Like all members of the Nara clan, he is able to manipulate shadows. He demonstrated aptitude with the
Shadow Imitation Technique
, able to bind Naruto before he was able to get any further after escaping the
Falls of Truth
.
