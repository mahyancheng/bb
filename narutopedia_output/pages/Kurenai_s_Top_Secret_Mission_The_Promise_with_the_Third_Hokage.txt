"
Kurenai's Top Secret Mission: The Promise with the Third Hokage
" (
紅の極秘任務～三代目との約束～
,

) is episode 205 of the original
Naruto
 anime.

Synopsis

As
Kurenai
 attempted to escape with
Yakumo
,
Naruto
, and
Sakura
, the members of
Team 8
 and
Might Guy
 came to their assistance and defeated the
enemy
. Kurenai and Yakumo revealed that Yakumo had been entrusted to Kurenai as her student, as, while she was unable to fight, she was a talented
genjutsu
 user. However, Kurenai had sealed away her abilities, and Yakumo revealed that she had done so under the orders of the
Third Hokage
 to do something to Yakumo.
