Hokushin
 (
ホクシン
) is a
shinobi
 from the
Land of Birds
 and a member of the
Watari Ninja
.

Appearance

Hokushin has long sky blue hair with his bangs going over the right side of his face down to his chest area. He also has big black-coloured eyes. He wears a white very thin headband covered by his hair. He wears a beige-coloured vest-like shirt that goes down to his knees. He wears a purple-coloured shirt underneath it. He also has a black lower arm sleeve on each lower arm area and a thick black belt around his waist.

Abilities

He is able to use the
Hiding with Camouflage Technique
, which could help him in gathering information about his opponent. The fact that he carries a sword suggests that he is skilled in
kenjutsu
.

Part I

Cursed Warrior Extermination Mission

He, Nagare, and other unnamed ninja were sent by Hōki to pursue
Chishima
, who easily evaded them. He sought to advance in the Watari ninja by defeating
Kakashi Hatake
. He shadowed Kakashi,
Neji Hyūga
, and
Tenten
 as they pretended to leave the village, only to find that he had been tricked when they used a
Body Replacement Technique
 upon some monkeys. During the final battle, Kakashi easily defeated him after surprising him with a Body Replacement Technique, noting the effortlessness in which Kakashi defeated him.

Trivia

When translated, Hokushin can mean "North Star".
