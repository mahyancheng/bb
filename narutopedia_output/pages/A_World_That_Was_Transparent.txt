Trivia

This opening,
Diver
, and
Assault Rock
 are the only openings to have the
Naruto: Shippūūden
 logo placed near the end.

This opening shows many fights that did not occur in the manga or anime during
Pain's assault
, such as:

Sai fighting the
Asura Path
.

Hinata, Sakura and Ino fighting Konan.

Neji, Rock Lee, Tenten and Kiba fighting the
Deva Path
.

Chōji and Kakashi fighting the
Preta Path
.

This song was used in a
movie version
 opening of the
Naruto: Shippūden
 anime.
