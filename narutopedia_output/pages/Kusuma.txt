Kusuma
 (
クスマ
) is a citizen of the
Land of Fire
 and sells masks in
Tanzaku Quarters
.

Personality

He loves festivals and children, particularly his own three.

Appearance

Kusuma has trimmed, short, grey hair and black eyes. In the manga, he wore a white suit of clothing along with a wide obi and a rope tied around his head. In the anime, he was shirtless and had a towel around his neck.

Part I

Search for Tsunade
