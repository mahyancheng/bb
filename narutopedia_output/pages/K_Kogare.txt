 (
焦コウ

Konohagakure

Personality

Kō doesn't appear to be very sure of himself as he didn't dare to take part in the promotion exam.

Appearance

Kō is a fair-skinned boy with black eyes, very thin eyebrows, and dark hair in a ponytail. He wears a cyan shirt, a turquoise cape with a large gold button, cyan pants and a pair of sandals.
