Abilities

The Sarutobi are famous for their high willpower, and exceptional strength. The clan also uses
hiden

ninjutsu
 involving the
Fire Release

nature transformation
.

Other Notable Clan Members

Konohamaru's parents served as Anbu under the direct command of the Hokage.

During the Fourth Shinobi World War, a group of Sarutobi clan members attacked the Ten-Tails by super-heating the cement-like substance which the beast had been trapped in.

The clan name "Sarutobi", as well the name of

Sasuke Uchiha

one of the clan's members
