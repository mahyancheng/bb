Trivia

Its title is a reference to
Naruto
 returning to
Konoha
.

In order to promote
Naruto Shippūden the Movie
, the broadcast versions of episodes
24
 through
27
 included scenes from the movie in both the opening and ending themes, while still retaining the original music.

In the anime, after Sasori's face was revealed, in the opening's fourth version, Sasori's real body puppet appears instead of his Hiruko puppet.

Being used for 30 episodes, this is the longest opening used for
Naruto: Shippūden
.

This is one of the longest openings ever used in Naruto media with 30 episodes, tied with
Starting and Growing
.
