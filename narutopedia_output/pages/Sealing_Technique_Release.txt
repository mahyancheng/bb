This technique is a counter-measure that can be used to undo
fūinjutsu
 that may be binding the user. By placing their hand atop the seal, it activates, nullifying the effects of the fūinjutsu casted on them.

Trivia

This technique bears the
Sarutobi clan
's symbol at its centre. As Konohamaru also noted, it is something he always has prepared due to his wary nature.
