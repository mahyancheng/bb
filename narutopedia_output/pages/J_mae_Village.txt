The
Jōmae Village
 (
錠前の里
,
Jōmae no Sato
,
literally meaning:
 Lock Village) is an anime-only shinobi village located in the
Land of Keys
. This village's
shinobi
 are experts in espionage in order to gain information for negotiations with larger, more powerful nations.
 In this village, ninja seem to be specifically trained in gaining information while giving none in return as evident by
Hanare
's
Eye Mind Reading
 technique and their
leader
's willingness to commit suicide rather than be taken hostage by enemy ninja.
