Trivia

In the last part of the ending an umbrella with a heart on top is drawn on the sand with the names; "Sasuke" and "Sakura" written under, although
Sasuke
's name is crossed.

This ending marks the debut of "Narutonic Water", which would later canonically exist in
Boruto
.
