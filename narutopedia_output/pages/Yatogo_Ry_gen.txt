Yatogo Ryūgen
 (
りゅうげんヤトゴ
,
Ryūgen Yatogo
) is a
chūnin
 of
Konohagakure
.

Background

When he heard that
Sasuke Uchiha
 killed the notorious criminal
Itachi Uchiha
, he wondered if Sasuke would receive a reward. Sasuke, who was secretly listening nearby, nearly killed Yatogo and his friends for this remark only to be stopped by
Tobi
.

Personality

He is a curious
shinobi
, and is predisposed to easily believing rumours, no matter how baseless they are.

Appearance

Yatogo has short black hair that frames either side of his face and small black eyes. He wears the typical Konoha
flak jacket
 and a bandanna-style
forehead protector
.

Trivia

Ryūgen (
流言
) means "groundless rumour" possibly referring to how he and his friends heard a rumour about Sasuke killing Itachi.
