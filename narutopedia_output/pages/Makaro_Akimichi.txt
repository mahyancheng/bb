Appearance

Like many members of the Akimichi clan, he is described as robust in appearance. He has dark brown spiky hair and dark eyes. He also has triangular markings on both sides of his cheek. He wears the customary armour of his clan, with the kanji for "food" (
食

shoku

Fourth Shinobi World War

Abilities

Makaro is mentioned to have only been promoted to chūnin before the war.
 Although the full extent of his abilities are unknown, as an Akimichi, Makaro can convert calories into
chakra
