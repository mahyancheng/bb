Anbu

Appearance

He has short shaggy brown hair and wears a white fox-like mask with two blue markings on either side of the face. He wears the standard attire of the Konoha Anbu.

Abilities

As an Anbu, he is presumably a very powerful
shinobi
. He carries a
sword
 hinting at skill in
kenjutsu

shuriken
, being able to effectively hit multiple targets without missing.

Yakumo Kurama Rescue Mission

He was ordered to protect

unknown shinobi
