"
Surviving the Cut: The Rookie 9 Together Again!
" (
第二試験突破!勢ぞろいルーキーナイン!
,
Daini Shiken Toppa! Seizoroi Rūkī Nain!
) is episode 37 of the original
Naruto
 anime.

Synopsis

Team 7
 opens its scrolls inside the tower, summoning
Iruka
 to inform them that they made it in time. He observes their growth, then allows them to enter the main room, where they meet with the other passing teams:
Team 10
,
Team 8
,
Team Guy
, the
Three Sand Siblings
,
Team Kabuto
, and
Team Dosu
. The
Third Hokage
 briefly speaks about the exams and leaves
Hayate Gekkō
 in charge.
