Tenten summons a
manriki-gusari
 from one of her
scrolls
 and uses it to restrain her opponent. When her opponent is restrained, she uses the scroll to launch a barrage of weapons at them and then finally slams them into the ground with the manriki-gusari.

Trivia

This technique was named in
Naruto: Ultimate Ninja Heroes
 and
Naruto: Ultimate Ninja Heroes 2: The Phantom Fortress
 as it was unnamed in the anime.
