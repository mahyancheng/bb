Naruto x UT
  is the eighth
Naruto
 OVA. Approximately 200,000 copies of this OVA were distributed by
Uniqlo
 to promote a line of
Naruto
-themed shirts designed by
Masashi Kishimoto
 in conjunction with
Studio Pierrot
.

Synopsis

Sakura
 cries, holding a seemingly dead
Naruto
, who has slash marks in his chest, in her arms, with
Kakashi
 beside her. A recap is shown of some of their moments together as
Team 7
: The announcement of the teams, Sasuke beating Naruto in the kunai-throwing challenge, the teams first mission (find the lost pet
Tora
) and the
Tree Climbing Practice
. Then it laps into some of Naruto and
Sasuke
's fights: the
Chūnin Exams
; Naruto fighting
Kiba Inuzuka
 and
Neji Hyūga
, Sasuke fighting
Yoroi Akadō
, Naruto's fight with
Gaara
 and
Shukaku
, Sasuke fighting
Orochimaru
 in the
Forest of Death
, Team 7's encounter with
Zabuza Momochi
 and
Haku
, Sasuke's first encounter with his brother
Itachi Uchiha
, Sasuke being captured by the
Sound Four
, Sasuke and Naruto's first fight with Chidori and Rasengan and their battle at the
Valley of the End
. They both face each other in battle again, Naruto in
Sage Mode
 using
Chakra Blades
 and Sasuke using his
Sword of Kusanagi
. They finish with a
Rasengan
 and
Chidori
. Following that, scenes of Naruto fighting
Nagato
 and Sasuke fighting Itachi are shown. After the flashback, Naruto wakes up, comforting the crying Sakura, who joyfully embraces him. Meanwhile,
Kabuto
 and
Tobi
 gaze at the aftermath of the battle, then they walk away. A wounded Sasuke smirks and shows his
Sharingan
, which changes into
Itachi
's
Mangekyō Sharingan
, and then into Sasuke's own, following the cry of an infant.

Trivia

Midnight Orchestra
, the sixteenth ending song of the
Naruto: Shippūden
 anime, is used in this OVA.

When Naruto is running towards Sasuke, his Rasengan is shown in his left hand as opposed to his right.
