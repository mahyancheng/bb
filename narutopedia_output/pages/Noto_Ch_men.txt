Noto Chōmen
 (
帳面ノオト
,
Chōmen Noto
) is the Director of the Technical Bureau of the
Cultural Institute
 from the
Land of Stairs
.

Personality

Noto likes to go to nightclubs and drink alcohol.

Appearance

Noto is described as a young man wearing an expensive suit.

Abilities

As the director of the Technical Bureau, Noto has a unique intelligence, and is able to lead a group of scientists, as well as afford to hire shinobi bodyguards, who respectfully call him "Noto-san".
