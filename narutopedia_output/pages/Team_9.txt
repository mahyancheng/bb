Team 9
 is a shinobi team from
Konohagakure
. After graduating a year before Boruto's class, this team is normally away from village as they accept missions overseas. After the invasion of
Isshiki Ōtsutsuki
, Team 9 returned to Konoha to participate in the new
Chūnin Exams
. None of them made it to the final exam.
