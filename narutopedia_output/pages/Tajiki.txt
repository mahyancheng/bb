Tajiki
 (
タジキ
) was a
shinobi
 from
Kumogakure
.

Personality

Tajiki admired the
Fourth Raikage
 and worked hard with weight training.
 He was focused during battle, and it appeared as though he was somewhat overconfident in his abilities.

Appearance

Tajiki has thick black eyebrows and small black eyes. He had a large physique and towered over many other shinobi. He wore the standard attire of a Kumogakure shinobi, including
flak jacket
 and leg guards. He also wore the forehead protector of the Alliance in a bandanna style.

Abilities

Tajiki seemed to prefer the use of
taijutsu
 in battle, taking advantage of his size and using his colossal fists as his weapons.

Part II

Fourth Shinobi World War: Confrontation

Tajiki was assigned to the
Third Division
 of the
Allied Shinobi Forces
 during the
Fourth Shinobi World War
. When the division took on the
reincarnated
 shinobi of the
Akatsuki
 army, he clashed with
Gari
 in battle. Using his colossal fists, he attempted to land a hit on the ninja, but was
blown to bits
 before he was able to.
