The user makes the water under their opponent explode, launching them into the air. They then jump above them, creating a large whirlpool around their arm that gets concentrated into a water lance, that strikes their opponent. This technique has a long attack range and tremendous piercing power that is said to be able to pierce through nearly anything.

Trivia

Though only appearing in the video game series, this technique was illustrated in the
Naruto: One Decade, One Hundred Ninja
 guidebook.
