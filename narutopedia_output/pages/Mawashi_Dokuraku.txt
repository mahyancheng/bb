Personality

While much is not known of his personality, Mawashi is renowned as the "

Appearance

Mawashi is a bespectacled man with short cropped brown hair. He wears the standard attire of the Konoha Torture and Interrogation Force, which comprises of a grey suit with a black belt and shinobi sandals.

Abilities






Mawashi assists Inoichi in extracting intel.



Not much has been seen in the way of Mawashi's abilities but he is skilled enough to aid Inoichi in
delving
