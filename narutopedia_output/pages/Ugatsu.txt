Ugatsu
 (
ウガツ
) was a
shinobi
 from
Amegakure
.

Background

As a remnant of the former
Amegakure
 loyal to
Hanzō
, he and two other Amegakure remnants snuck back into the village to try and kill
Pain
. He was presumably killed during the failed attempt.

Personality

Before Pain overthrew Hanzō, Ugatsu was a loyal shinobi of his. Ugatsu attempted to assassinate Pain out of revenge for Hanzō's death.

Appearance

Ugatsu had short spiky dark-coloured hair and dark-coloured eyes. He wore an Amegakure
forehead protector
 that came down in the back of his head covering his neck. He wore a gas mask over his mouth and a light bluish-coloured flak jacket with a white long sleeve shirt underneath it.

Abilities

Ugatsu was said to be very skilled in the art of infiltration.
