Makibishi
 are small, sharp objects that are mainly used to inhibit someone from walking in a certain direction or area, since they would injure the ninja's feet if they tried to walk over them.

Trivia

Although being rarely used in
Naruto
, they're commonly a selectable tool in video games such as

.

In the prologue novelisation,
Kakashi
's makibishi are drenched in
poison
.

Furie Kanhen employs copper makibishi, making them more susceptible to magnetic fields, and developed deadly traps based on their movements.
