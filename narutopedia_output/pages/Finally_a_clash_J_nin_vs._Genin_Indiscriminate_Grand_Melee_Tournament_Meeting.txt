 (

Synopsis

A contest is made by the
Fifth Hokage
 called
jōnin
 vs
genin
. The point is to collect crystals for points, with the higher-ranked
chūnin
 and jōnin holding crystals worth more points. The genin have blue crystals, while the chūnin and jōnin have red crystals. The video shows various fights between the genin and jōnin, with each instance ending in the jōnin unknowingly gaining or discarding their crystal.

Gaara

 and
Kotetsu Hagane

Kakashi Hatake
 vs.
Naruto Uzumaki
 and
Sasuke Uchiha

Rock Lee
 vs.
Might Guy

Shikamaru Nara
 slept the whole time.

Gaara, Kakashi and Guy won their battles. It is unconfirmed who won or lost the other battles.

Hokage

shinobi
