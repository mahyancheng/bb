Flash bombs
 (
光玉
,
hikaridama
) are used for diversions, creating a blinding light when they detonate. They may look like small spheres wrapped in paper with the character 'light' (
光
,
hikari
) or, more conventionally, as canisters.
 In the anime, it was shown that flash bombs can also be used in paper form, similar to
explosive tags
.

Trivia

Flash bombs are popular tools of the
Nara clan
, as they can use them to temporarily increase the size and range of their
shadows
.

Ryōgi
 made use of a flash bomb disguised as a crystal.
