Nadeshiko Village
.

Background

Wanting to rule over the Nadeshiko Village, Kokuyō challenged the leader, Shizuka, in an attempt to defeat her so she would be forced to marry him per the village's rules. Because of his ambition, Kokuyō ended up challenging Shizuka ninety-eight times with his puppets, but each one had been easily destroyed by the latter.

Personality

From all indications, Kokuyō was a selfish man that didn't want to marry out of love but instead to gain power. He was also shown to be a relentless individual, but is willing to bide his time, making a plan which battling against Shizuka ninety-nine times with different puppets so he could use a technique to bring her in against her will.

Appearance

Kokuyō has long brown hair that framed his angular face and dark eyes. He wears square-rimmed glasses, a light pink shirt with a purple waistcoat over it, a pair of white trousers and brown shoes.

Abilities

He is a puppeteer who utilises the
Puppet Technique
 to control his robot-like puppets. He utilises the
Hundred Puppet Soul Binding
, an elaborate technique that drove "
bolts
" into his opponents each time they destroyed his puppet. After destroying the hundredth puppet, he gained the ability to control the target in a similar manner to the
Puppet Performance: Skilful Achievement with a Human Body
 technique albeit forcefully.
