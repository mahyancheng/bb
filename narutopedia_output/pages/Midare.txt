Midare
 (
ミダレ
) was a
genin
 from
Amegakure
.

Personality

Midare was very over-confident in his and
Shigure
's abilities. He talked down to the
Three Sand Siblings
 for trying to challenge his team. Midare has been described as being both spiteful and selfish. He's cruel and uses his brain to judge situations.

Appearance

Midare was somewhat the second tallest of the three who also had, fair skin, had some type of dark black glasses or markings with separate dark blue lenses, no eyebrows, wore a pin striped light grey jumpsuit with a dark black side going down in the centre, an oxygen tank carried on the right side of his unseen pocket or foreleg, wore dark black sandals, wore light grey gloves, the same altered kasa hat that had the
Amegakure
 symbol on the front of it, and had carried a dark black sheath on his back to carry his four umbrellas on his back.

Abilities

Midare is a user of assassination techniques and is able to strike his enemies down hidden within the rainfall. With his cunning personality, Midare acts as his team's strategist.
