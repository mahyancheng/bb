Mikazuki Amaashi
 (
あまあしミカヅキ
,
Amaashi Mikazuki
) was a project developer for the
Kaneki Corporation
.

Background

Mikazuki was a project developer in a Kaneki Corporation chemical plant. Despite her cautious nature, her superiors forced her to go ahead with a dangerous project, which resulted in an explosion that killed many people with Mikazuki included. Her superiors were able to put the blame of the accident on her, destroying her reputation and avoiding being investigated themselves.

Legacy

Although Mikazuki's legacy was ruined, her brother
Samidare Amaashi
 believed in her and sought revenge by killing those responsible for framing her. Ultimately, the president and mastermind of the tragedy,
Kaneki
, was arrested and the investigation on the explosion was reopened.

Trivia

"Mikazuki" (
三日月
) means "crescent moon".
