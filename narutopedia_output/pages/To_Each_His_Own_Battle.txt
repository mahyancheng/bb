"
To Each His Own Battle
" (
それぞれの闘い
,
Sorezore no Tatakai
) is episode 121 of the original
Naruto
 anime.

Synopsis

Ukon
 separates from
Kiba
 and rejoins with
Sakon
 so that he can recover. Kiba finds
Akamaru
, and the two flee downriver, hoping to escape the brothers.
Naruto
, meanwhile, is unable to make a dent in
Kimimaro
, and his
shadow clones
 are still rapidly defeated before they can land a blow. Elsewhere,
Shikamaru
 believes to have picked up on the movements of
Tayuya
's
creatures
, though she quickly has them switch actions before Shikamaru can act.
