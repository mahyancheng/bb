Raging Thunder
 (
魔導昇雷撃
,
Reijingu Sandā
) is a sword technique utilised by
Temujin
 by drawing energy from the
Stone of Gelel
.

Overview

By focusing gelel energy onto his
sword
 and after dealing a blow with it, Temujin can create a powerful
Gelel
 barrier before him. This technique was used in an attempt to negate
Naruto
's
Rasengan
. The two attacks resulted in a massive explosion that destroyed the mountain peak that Naruto and Temujin were fighting on.

Trivia

Although the Japanese subtitle of this technique is written in kanji, it is pronounced in English, "Raging Thunder" (
ライジングサンダー
,
Reijingu Sandā
). The Japanese pronunciation of the kanji would be
Madō Shōraigeki
, which would literally translate to "Magic Rising Lightning Strike".
