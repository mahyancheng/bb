Kusabi
 (
クサビ
) is one of the many mercenaries hired by
Gatō
.

Personality

Kusabi seemed to be very violent and belligerent by nature as seen from his shouting of death threats to
Team Kakashi
 and declaring that he was going to pillage the
Land of Waves
.
 He is also very cowardly when a challenge is given.

Appearance

Kusabi had brown hair which was tied in four and had decals hanging from it. He had a red cloth wrapped around his pure white eyes with holes in it for seeing. He carried a long, pole-like weapon which had a ring in the top.

Abilities

Kusabi carried a pole-like weapon, indicating him having some skill in this weapon's use.

In the anime, he became part of a group led by
Zori

Waraji

Inari

Sasuke

Tazuna
. The criminals were fired at from crossbows and they surrendered, falling to their knees, and then they were tied up and handed over to the servants of the law.
