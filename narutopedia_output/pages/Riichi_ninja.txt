Background

Riichi and
Fugaku Uchiha
 were seen evacuating villagers to the southeast shelter during the
Nine-Tailed Demon Fox's Attack
.

Personality

Riichi is the type of person that would rather do what he wants instead of working. He would even complain about this. He normally has a happy demeanour.

Appearance

Riichi has shoulder length brown hair, with bangs. His eyes have a very distinctive sunken look that gives him the appearance of being tired. He wears the standard attire of the Konoha shinobi inclusive of
forehead protector
 and
flak jacket
.

Part I

Chūnin Exams
 and not be on guard duty.

Part II

Akatsuki Suppression Mission

Akatsuki Suppression Mission

Riichi was part of the
Nijū Shōtai

S-rank
