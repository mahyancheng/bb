Ibara
 (
イバラ
) is a citizen of
Konohagakure
 and the mother of one of the students in
Naruto Uzumaki
's class at the
Academy
.

Personality

She, like other people in the village, greatly disliked Naruto, seeing him only as the Nine-Tails itself. This is later displayed when she was glad that Naruto did not graduate along with the rest of his class.

Appearance

Ibara has long smooth dark brown hair and brown-coloured eyes. She wears a dark pinkish-coloured headband around her head and a light green shirt under an opened black vest-like shirt.
