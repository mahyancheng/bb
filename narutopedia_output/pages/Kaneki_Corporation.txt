The
Kaneki Corporation
 (
カネキコーポレーション
,
Kaneki Kooporeeshon
) in a company that rivals the
Kaminarimon Company
 in status. In order to remain competitive, its leaders pushed forward the development of a project in a chemical plant that resulted in an explosion that killed many people. The higher-ups successfully pinned the blame on the deceased project developer,
Mikazuki Amaashi
, destroying her reputation and avoiding being investigated. Years later, people involved in the accident were targeted and killed by Mikazuki's brother,
Samidare
. While he failed to kill the corporation's president, his involvement brought up new charges against the corporation, leading to the investigation being reopened.
