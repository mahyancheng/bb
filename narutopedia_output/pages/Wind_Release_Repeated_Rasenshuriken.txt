The
Wind Release: Repeated Rasenshuriken
 is a ninjutsu created and utilised by Naruto Uzumaki.

Usage

After activating
Kurama Mode
 or
Sage Mode
 (in the anime), Naruto is able to form two
Rasenshuriken
 and wield both at the same time. Their cutting power is so extreme that by using one of them as a melee weapon, Naruto was able to cut through the
Ten-Tails
' extremely durable tails with ease.
