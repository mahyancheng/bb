Naruto: Shippūden
 anime.

Synopsis

After having braved a storm, four of
Naruto
's
shadow clones
 rebel against him, take him hostage, and seize control over the ship, forcing the crew to stop on an island. Naruto's clones were going to kill him, but he wakes up and realises it was all just a dream of one of his clones. It was caused when a shadow clone was released after saving Naruto from a falling rope pulley, and its memories returned to the real Naruto. Naruto then realises the worth of his clones and releases them.

Trivia

Starting from this episode in the English dub, Matthew Mercer replaces Troy Baker as the voice of
Yamato
.
