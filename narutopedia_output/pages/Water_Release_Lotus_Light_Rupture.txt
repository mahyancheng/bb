The user creates drops of water from an outside source, which are then shaped into razor-sharp
lotus petals
. As the petals expand in size and spin around, emitting explosive energy in every direction, they are launched at the opponent. The petals can be controlled and are able to kill a man with a single petal.
Hisen
 noted it to be his strongest technique.
