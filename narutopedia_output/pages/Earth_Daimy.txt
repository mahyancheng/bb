He is the former
daimyō
 of the
Land of Earth
.

Personality

He appears to have an impatient attitude, as seen when he and the other daimyō awaited the response of the uncertain
Water Daimyō
. He prompted him to respond quickly due to the urgency of the impending
war
.

Appearance

He has a very prominent, grey beard and moustache and his eyes have dark marks underneath them, giving him somewhat a similar appearance to the
Tsuchikage
. His face is further accentuated with creases running from his eyes. He wears the standard attire of the daimyō.
