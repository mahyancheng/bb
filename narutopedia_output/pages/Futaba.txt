Futaba
 (
フタバ
) is a former
academy student
 of
Konohagakure
.

Personality

Futaba cares deeply about her family, friends and her pet dog. She wants to always protect them.

Appearance

Futaba has blonde hair that she keeps under a pink poofy hat and brown eyes. She wore an orange blouse along with a pair of light-coloured pants and sandals.
