Ryūsa
 (
リュウサ
) is a senior from
Sunagakure
. He has served his village for many years.

Appearance

Ryūsa has dark eyes, fairly thick eyebrows, and a strong jawline. He wears the standard attire of the Sunagakure Seniors.

Personality

His speech and conduct are very prudent, allowing him to act with calm judgement. His actions are always based on his deliberate judgement.

Abilities

As a member of the advisory council, he is most likely one of Sunagakure's wisest shinobi.

Part II

Kazekage Rescue Mission

Gaara

Five Kage Summit

In the anime, he is seen once again alongside the other Suna-nin seeing Gaara off to the
Kage Summit
. He tells
Temari

Kankurō
