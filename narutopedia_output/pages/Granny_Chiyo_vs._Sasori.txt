"
Granny Chiyo vs. Sasori…!!
" (
チヨバアVSサソリ…!!
,
Chiyo-Baa Bāsasu Sasori…!!
) is chapter 272 of the original
Naruto
 manga.

Summary

Sakura
 grabs
Sasori
 before he can reach
Chiyo
 and pulls him towards her. She punches him and he falls apart but,
being a puppet
, he is able to put himself back together. Seeing that he will not be easy to defeat, Chiyo takes a
puppet
's arm to replace the one she lost and brings out the
Chikamatsu Collection
. Sasori is impressed by her ability to control one puppet with each of her fingers, but demonstrates how his puppet body allows him to control
one hundred puppets
. Disappointed that the battle has dragged on for so long, Sasori prepares to finish Chiyo and Sakura.
