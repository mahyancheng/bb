Hayase
 (
ハヤセ
) is a
chūnin
 of
Konohagakure
.

Personality

Hayase was a bright individual, noted to have created the questions for the first stage of the
Chūnin Exams
.

Appearance

Hayase has black hair that spikes outward and upward and tends to squint. His attire consists of a
forehead protector
 and a grey jacket that has a hood and a pair of pants.

Chūnin Exams

Hayase along with
Ōkei
 - his academic rival
 - were inserted into the first phase of the Chūnin Exams so that other examinees could cheat off of them.
