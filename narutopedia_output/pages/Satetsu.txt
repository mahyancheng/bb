Personality

He was stunned by the Fourth's death and held great resentment towards
Orochimaru
 for falsely leading Sunagakure into the
Konoha Crush

Appearance

Satetsu is a bald man with dark eyes and a goatee. He wears the standard attire of the Sunagakure jōnin which consists of a
flak jacket
 over a black suit and sandals.

Part I

Search for Tsunade

Satetsu was one of the many ninja to scour the
Land of Wind

Fourth Kazekage
.
