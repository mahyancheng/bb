Flower Ninja Art
 (
花忍法
,
Hana Ninpō
) is a subset of
ninjutsu
 and
genjutsu
 involving secret flower-oriented techniques used by a clan in the
Land of Vegetables
. The two known users of flower ninja art are
Kikunojō
 and
Yurinojō
.

Trivia

In several games,
Ino Yamanaka
 and
Kurenai Yūhi
 make use of techniques resembling the Flower Ninja Art.
