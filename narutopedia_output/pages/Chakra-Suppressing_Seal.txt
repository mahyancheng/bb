This special seal tag was developed by Jiraiya so it can be used to suppress the
Nine-Tails
' influence over
Naruto Uzumaki
. By applying the seal to Naruto's forehead, the Nine-Tails'
chakra
 and personality recede. Naruto is left fatigued afterwards due to his chakra being suppressed.

Trivia

The kanji written on the seal is 押 (
ō
), from the verb 押さえる (
osaeru
), meaning "to suppress" or "to restrain".

When it was introduced, it could be assumed that this sealing tag could be used on any
jinchūriki
 to suppress their
tailed beast
's influence.
