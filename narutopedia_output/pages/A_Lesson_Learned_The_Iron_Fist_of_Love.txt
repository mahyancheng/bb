"
A Lesson Learned: The Iron Fist of Love!
" (
心に届け!愛の鉄拳
,
Kokoro no Todoke! Ai no Tekken
) is episode 153 of the original
Naruto
 anime.

Synopsis

Determined to try and save
Rokusuke
,
Naruto
 and the others ambushed some members of the
Kurosuki family
 and managed to rescue an old man. They then heard the funeral bell, and went to try and save Rokusuke. However,
Rock Lee
 met an old friend among the group. Lee tried to convince
Karashi
 to return to his old way of life, but to no avail. Their time together was cut short, however, as
Raiga
 was watching from the sidelines. Naruto and his team pursued Raiga into a thick fog, and found that Raiga was able to conceal himself from even the
Byakugan
.
