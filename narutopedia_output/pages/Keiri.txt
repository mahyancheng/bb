Keiri
 (
ケイリ
) is a caretaker at an
orphanage
 located on the outskirts of
Konohagakure
.

Background

She and
Kanpu
 warned
Nonō Yakushi
 about the orphanage's financial struggles, which only became worse after
Kabuto
 was taken in. She was frustrated by Kabuto's inability to tell time, unaware he needed glasses. Sometime later, when
Danzō Shimura
 came to the orphanage to recruit a new member for
Root
, Keiri threatened to report Danzō's blackmail to the
Third Hokage
.

Personality

Keiri seemed to get easily frustrated with the children at the orphanage but cared deeply for them. She also wasn't afraid to threaten a higher-up
shinobi
 like Danzō.

Appearance

Keiri has black hair and black eyes. Her outfit consists of a black gown with white apron and a white coif that covers her hair.

Trivia

"Keiri" (
経理
) means "management".
