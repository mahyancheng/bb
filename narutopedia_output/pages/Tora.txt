Tora
 (
トラ
) is the cat owned by
Madam Shijimi
, the wife of the
Fire Daimyō
.

Personality

Due to the huge amount of affection Madam Shijimi shows to her, which includes nearly squeezing the life out of the cat, Tora constantly tries to run away.

Appearance

Tora is recognised both times by the red ribbon on her right ear. She has brown fur, with a line running down her forehead with two lines (three in the anime) going across it, and amber-coloured eyes.
