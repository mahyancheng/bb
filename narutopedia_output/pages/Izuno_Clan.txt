The
Izuno Clan
 (
伊豆野一族
,
Izuno Ichizoku
) is a family of
shinobi
 in
Konohagakure
 known for their use of cat inspired ninjutsu.

Overview

A lesser known Konoha clan, only a few of its members have been promoted to
chūnin
 during adulthood. Wasabi knows of no clansmen to ever be promoted to
jōnin
 in her lifetime, while she herself is the youngest to be promoted to chūnin, and the only one to do so before adulthood.
