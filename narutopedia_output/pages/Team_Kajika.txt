Team Kajika
 is the team of antagonists from
Naruto: The Cross Roads
. It consisted of
Kajika
, the leader,
Iwana
 and
Yamame
. They were loyal to
Orochimaru
.

Plot Overview

Kajika was a
missing-nin
 from
Kirigakure
. His team was sent to face
Team Kakashi
 and test
Sasuke
's strength. The two others from his group were easily defeated by
Naruto
 and Sasuke, but their leader was much more difficult to beat. Eventually Naruto and Sasuke defeated Kajika, who later was possessed by Orochimaru. He later releases his control of Kajika after a talk with Sasuke and his body explodes, resulting in his death.

Trivia

All members of this teams' names are derived from fish.
