Summary

It is the time of the
Third Shinobi World War
.
Obito Uchiha
 is running late, for which his teammate, a young
Kakashi Hatake
, reprimands him when he eventually arrives. Kakashi, having recently become a
jōnin
, is given gifts by his team-mates.
Rin Nohara
 gives him a personalised medical-kit. Their master, the future
Fourth Hokage
, gives him a
special kunai
. Obito has forgotten to get him anything. Kakashi is to be the team leader for their mission to destroy the
Kannabi Bridge
, which will give
Konoha
 an advantage over
Iwagakure
 in the war effort. As they travel through
Kusagakure
 they discover they are being watched by an enemy ninja. Kakashi decides to deal with the ninja with a new technique: the
Chidori
.
