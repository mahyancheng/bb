Himuka Suzukaze
 (
涼風ヒムカ
,
Suzukaze Himuka
) was a
genin
 of
Konohagakure
.

Background

After graduating from the
Academy
, she was added to
Team 2
 under the leadership of
Yūki Minazuki
. Her teammates were
Yōji Aburame
, a fellow graduate, and
Itachi Uchiha
, who graduated the year before her. Although he was much younger than her, Himuka called Itachi "master" because of how skilled he was. Itachi left the team only a few months after she joined, but Himuka continued to admire him throughout his career. Years later, she was saddened by Itachi's involvement in the
Uchiha Clan Downfall
.
