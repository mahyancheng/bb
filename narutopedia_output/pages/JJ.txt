JJ
 (
ジェイ・ジェイ
,
Jei Jei

Konohagakure
.

Appearance

JJ is a boy with a sleek pompadour and blue eyes. He wears a blue jacket with a white undershirt.

Abilities

JJ is adept in
taijutsu
, being able to fight on an equal footing with
Iwabee
.
