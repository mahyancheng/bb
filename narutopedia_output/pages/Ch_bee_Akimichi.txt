Chōbee Akimichi
 (
秋道チョウベエ
,
Akimichi Chōbee
) is a member of
Konohagakure
's
Akimichi clan
.

Personality

Like his nephew and grandniece, Chōbee has a great love of potato chips. He is also shown to be a very upbeat individual, not letting much get to him.

Appearance

Like other Akimichi, Chōbee has a rotund build and distinct markings on his cheeks. He is a rather short middle-aged man with a triangular beard and a moustache that has angular corners. His hair and moustache are brown. He wears a small pair of glasses, a samurai like outfit which entails a green suit completed with armour that has the kanji for "food" (
食
,
shoku
) on it, a pair of red pants and black sandals.

Abilities

Chōbee is very nimble in battle. Against
Team 7
, he was able to seamlessly evade their combined assault. He also showed noticeable skills in his clan's
secret techniques
, able to use the
Partial Multi-Size Technique
 as a strong defence to repel
Sarada Uchiha
's
chakra enhanced punch
.

Plot Overview






Chōbee fights Boruto.



A disguised Chōbee, along with
Chōji Akimichi
, stole a box with potato chips from a store, causing him to be pursued by
Boruto Uzumaki
, who did not know who the mysterious thief was, as a mission target for Team 7. He cornered Chōbee and attacked him, but he deftly deflected his blows. Boruto was later joined by Sarada and Mitsuki, whom Chōbee was able to defeat. Boruto attacked the opponent with the
Rasengan
, at which point Chōji protected him. The issue ended amicably when the identities of the kidnappers were revealed and the box with chips was destroyed by the Rasengan that got into it.
