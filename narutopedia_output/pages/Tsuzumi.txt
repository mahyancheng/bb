Tsuzumi
 (
ツヅミ
) is a police captain from the
Land of Forests
.

Appearance

Tsuzumi has short black hair and violet eyes. He also has small circular eyebrows and a small circular moustache.

Abilities

Tsuzumi carried a
sword
, hinting at some skill in
kenjutsu
.

Part I

Gantetsu Escort Mission

Tsuzumi oversaw the delivery of
Gantetsu
 back to the Land of Forests' capital for trial because of his crimes.

Trivia

The "
Tsuzumi
" (鼓) is a Japanese drum of Chinese and Indian origin.
