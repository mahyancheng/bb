Boruto: Naruto Next Generations
 anime. It started its run from
episode 151

180

Teenage Dream

Trivia

The opening animation pays homages to both the animations of
Rhapsody of Youth
 and
Wind and Waves Satellite
.

This is the first opening animation in the franchise to not feature
Naruto Uzumaki
.

In the second version of the opening, Kawaki's and members of the
Kara
's faces except
Code
's,
Victor
's and
Deepa
's are fully shown.

In this opening, Amado is depicted wearing a Kara cloak despite not doing so anywhere in the actual series.

Being used for 30 episodes, this is currently the longest opening used for
Boruto: Naruto Next Generations
.

This is one of the longest openings ever used in Naruto media with 30 episodes, tied with
Hero's Come Back!!
.
