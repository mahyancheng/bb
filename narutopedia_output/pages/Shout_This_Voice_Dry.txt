Shout This Voice Dry
 (
この声枯らして
,
Kono Koe Karashite
), performed by
AISHA
 featuring CHENON, is the twenty-second ending on
Naruto: Shippūden
. It began with episode
269
 and ended with episode
281
. It was followed up by
MOTHER
.
