Iroha Hyūga
 (
日向イロハ
,
Hyūga Iroha
) is a
jōnin
 of
Konohagakure
's
Hyūga clan
. During the
Fourth Shinobi World War
, he was assigned to the
Kumo Barrier Team
.

Personality

Iroha has been noted to be an overly serious individual, and as such, he is not good at dealing with abnormal situations.

Appearance

Iroha has long brown hair over which wears a bandanna-style
forehead protector
. Two strands of his hair hang down on either side, framing his face. Customary of all Hyūga he has the featureless white eyes of the
Byakugan
. He wears the standard Konoha shinobi attire complete with
flak jacket
.

Abilities

Like all members of his clan, he possesses one of the three great
dōjutsu
: the Byakugan. These eyes grant him a near 360º of vision, and the capability to see through solid objects amongst other various abilities. He was able to use his dōjutsu to follow the progress of Naruto and Killer B as they escaped the
island
 and report on their progress.
