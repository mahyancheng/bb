Genzō
 (
ゲンゾウ
) is a photographer and civilian of
Konohagakure
. He's been taking pictures of newly graduated
Academy students
 for use in their ninja profiles for a long time.

Background

Years in the past, Genzō was charged with photographing
Team Minato
. He was shown being vexed at
Obito
's late arrival.

Personality

Genzō doesn't like childish things, but he still continues to do his job while having to deal with it. He also appears to be an impatient individual.

Appearance

Genzō has dark eyes with round glasses and a balding head. He has white hair jutting up on each side of his head. He wears a tan jacket with a black hood and has a white T-shirt inside.

Abilities

Genzō was noted to be the greatest photographer in Konohagakure.
