"
The Tactician's Intent
" (
策士・紅明の思惑
,
Sakushi - Kōmei no Omowaku
) is episode 163 of the original
Naruto
 anime.

Synopsis

The group talks at
Mōsō
's house. After that, they discuss that
Kōmei
, the village strategist and the son's potential rival for succession, may be behind the
Cursed Warrior
. To prove this, they try to visit the new feudal lord
Sagi
 at a lake where he holds his lost memories of his sister
Toki
. The group protects the feudal lord from assassins and pursues the Cursed Warrior to its meeting with Kōmei, whereafter it is defeated, it is revealed that it and the previous Cursed Warrior were two different individuals. The group finds evidence that indicates that Kōmei may have been behind the second Cursed Warrior's appearance, and Kōmei is arrested.
