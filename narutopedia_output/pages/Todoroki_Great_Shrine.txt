The Todoroki Great Shrine.



The
Todoroki Great Shrine
 is a shrine located on the southern point of
Ouza Island
, off the coast of the
Land of Tea
. The shrine serves as the final destination of the Todoroki Great Shrine Race, where participants dedicate one of Ryochu's Jewels at the shrine after retrieving them from the
Modoroki Shrine
 on
Nagi Island
.

During the
Land of Tea Escort Mission
,
Team 7
 escorted
Idate Morino
 to the shrine to dedicate one of Ryochu's Jewels, resulting in Idate's victory and granting the
Wasabi Family
 control over
Degarashi Port
.
