Ashitaba
 (
アシタバ
) was a character from
Jiraiya
's novel, who appeared in
Tsunade
's
Infinite Tsukuyomi
 dream.

Personality

Ashitaba was very protective of his little brother
Komichi
, willingly accompanying him to their village to retrieve his teddy bear, even while knowing about the dangers of going there.

Appearance

Ashitaba was a boy with short black hair and large black eyes. He wore a plain blue shirt with an orange belt, grey pants and dark-green sandals.

Ashitaba accompanied his brother Komichi to their village to get his teddy bear, even though they were warned by
Team Jiraiya
 earlier to stay away from the village. After finding the bear, they tried to leave but were approached by
Team Asuma
. While Ashitaba and Komichi hid in fear, a fight broke out between Team Asuma and Team Jiraiya, leading the house to break apart. Ashitaba and his brother were swallowed by the ground as a consequence.

In the underground, Ashitaba and his brother were attacked by
giant worm-like creatures
 until Team Jiraiya and Team Asuma rescued them. After having been brought to their mother, the family went back to their village. However, during the night, a
giant explosion
 destroyed the village and killed everyone in it, including Ashitaba.
