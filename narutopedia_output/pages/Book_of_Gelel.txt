The
Book of Gelel
 (
ゲレルの書
,
Gereru no Sho
) is a tome passed down from the royal family of a
distant continent
.

Contents

The book contains all secrets of the
Stone of Gelel
, including:

how to control its power.

the history of Temujin's people and the developments they attained with the aid of the Stone's power.

the story of how the power of Gelel came to be discovered and used by
Temujin
's ancestors.

It does not, however, contain information on how to destroy the stone.

History

The book followed Temujin's ancestors over the sea and later came into the possession of
Haido
 (claiming he bought it from a travelling merchant, to which
Kahiko
 doubted it was so simple) who started to use its secrets for evil purposes. The book is lost when it was left aboard Haido's airship, which was sucked into the
Space–Time Hole
.
