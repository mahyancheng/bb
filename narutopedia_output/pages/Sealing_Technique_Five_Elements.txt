Kuroma uses his
sealing tags
 to use the five
element natures
.

Fire
 (火): Kuroma shoots balls of fire at the opponent.

Earth
 (土): Kuroma shakes the area around the opponent, who could get damaged.

Water
 (水): Kuroma creates a flow of gushing water under the opponent which traps them in a sphere of water. Kuroma can perform an attack while the opponent is trapped. This technique is similar to
Water Prison Technique
, though Kuroma doesn't need to keep his arm in the sphere of water.

Wind
 (風): Kuroma unleashes a wave of wind that slashes at the opponent.

Lightning
 (雷): Kuroma fires a wave of lightning bolts at the opponent.
