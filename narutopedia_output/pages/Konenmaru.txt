Konenmaru
 was one of
Suien
's
summons
 in
Naruto: Mission: Protect The Waterfall Village! (novel)
.

Personality

Konenmaru was very loyal and obedient to Suien.

Appearance

Konenmaru had the appearance of a
catfish
 with white markings and long spiny whiskers.

Abilities

Konenmaru had the ability to use
Water Release
 to shoot torrents of water at enemies. Konenmaru could also use his whiskers to capture his opponents.
