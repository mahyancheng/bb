Appearance

Suzuran is a young, light-skinned man with long violet hair, a small part of which is tied in a ponytail at the back of his head, and dark green eyes. His outfit consists of a white tunic, over which he wears a red robe, with a yellow belt tied around his waist. Suzuran also wears a pair of white gloves, dark blue pants, and dons shinobi sandals, with his ankles being wrapped in cloth.
