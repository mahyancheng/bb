The
Flying Thunder God: Guiding Thunder
 is a space-time barrier (
時空間結界
,
jikūkan kekkai
) technique.

Usage

This barrier technique incorporates the use of the
Flying Thunder God
 formula to erect a barrier space capable of warping away anything that comes into contact with it. The subject of the technique is then directed to another location marked by the user’s formula.
 The barrier has proven to be powerful enough to warp away techniques as destructive as a
Tailed Beast Ball
.
