"
The Legendary Seven Shinobi Swordsmen!!
" (
伝説の忍刀七人衆!!
,
Densetsu no Shinobigatana Shichininshū!!
) is chapter 523 of the original
Naruto
 manga.

Summary

The
Third Division
 battles the
Seven Ninja Swordsmen of the Mist
. While searching for his
sword
,
Suigetsu
 explains why he wants to obtain the
seven swordsmen's weapons
, and explains their abilities.
Kakashi
 nearly hits
Zabuza
, but is stopped, and held by
Haku
. Zabuza prepares to cut through both Haku and Kakashi.

Trivia

Kakashi's
Lightning Cutter
 hit Haku while he held Kakashi in place, in the same manner in
chapter 30
 of the manga.
