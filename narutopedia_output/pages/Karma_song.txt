Trivia

This is the fifth opening that has the series logo placed near the end of the opening, the others being "
A World That Was Transparent
", "
Diver
", "
Assault Rock
", and "
BAKU
".

In the second version, the opening shows an animated sequence of the Konoha shinobi.

Being used for 11 episodes, this is currently the shortest opening used for
Boruto: Naruto Next Generations
, as well as any Naruto media.
