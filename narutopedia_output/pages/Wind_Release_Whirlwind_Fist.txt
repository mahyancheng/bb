A technique where the user clads their fist with
Wind Release
 to form somewhat of a typhoon that is a revolving blade of wind. Upon impact against an individual, the technique pierces their body and blows them away with a shock wave.

In
Konoha Shinden
,
Mirai Sarutobi
 is said to be this jutsu's creator, having modelled the wind's movements on
Temari
's
Sickle Weasel Technique
 and the general shape on the
Rasengan
.
