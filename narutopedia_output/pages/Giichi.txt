Giichi
 (
ギイチ
) is a construction worker and an old friend of
Tazuna
.

Background

At some point in the past, he met
Agari
 and married her. Unable to have children, Agari sees
Inari
 as if he were her own.

Personality

Giichi used to be cowardly as seen when he refused to help build the bridge anymore because of
Gatō
. Later, after he saw how brave Inari was, he overcame his cowardly self and rallied others to attack Gatō's men. His wife, Agari also had some influence over his decision to help Inari.

Appearance

Giichi is an older man with black eyes and shaggy grey hair. He wears a greenish-coloured T-shirt.

Part I

Prologue — Land of Waves

Main article:
Prologue — Land of Waves

He initially helps build the
Great Naruto Bridge
, but leaves the job for fear of retaliation by Gatō. Inari later asks for his help in freeing the
Land of Waves
 from Gatō. He refuses, but after realising how brave Inari is, he rallies the townspeople to help Inari.

Trivia
