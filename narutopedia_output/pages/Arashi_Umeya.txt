Arashi Umeya
 (
梅矢アラシ
,
Umeya Arashi
) is a shinobi from
Sunagakure
.

Appearance

Arashi is a tall young man with spiky brown hair that he keeps under a turban. His outfit consists of a long black cape over grey pants and dark brown vest, and a face mask. He also carries his weapon strapped to his back.

Abilities

Not much is known of Arashi's abilities other than that he fights using a
scythe
 that is connected to metal spikes with what appears to be electrical wires. Through those metal spikes, Arashi is able to
generate lightning
.

Trivia

Arashi was named after "Shin Umeya".

When written as 嵐, "Arashi" means "storm".
