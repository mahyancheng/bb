Tsubute Futaba
 (
ふたばツブテ
,
Futaba Tsubute
) is a shinobi from
Iwagakure
.

Appearance

Tsubute has black hair and eyes, and wears a grey hooded jumper and blue shorts. He also carries his weapon strapped to his back.

Abilities

Not much is known of Tsubute's abilities other than that he wields what appears to be a
battering ram
.
