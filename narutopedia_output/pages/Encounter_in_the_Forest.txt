"
Encounter in the Forest…!!
" (
森の中の出会い…!!
,

Viz:
 A Meeting in the Forest…!!) is chapter 21 of the original
Naruto
 manga.

Summary

Naruto
, while resting from all the training he has done, is awoken by
Haku
. Unaware that he is the masked boy he met during the fight with
Zabuza
, Naruto explains that he was training to get stronger. Haku remarks that people become truly strong when they're protecting someone precious to them, something that Naruto can agree with. They part ways and Naruto continues his training, finally managing to climb to the top of the tree.
