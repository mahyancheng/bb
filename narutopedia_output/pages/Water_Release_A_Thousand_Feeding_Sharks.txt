A much more powerful version of the
Water Release: Five Feeding Sharks
, where the user creates one thousand sharks out of water that replicates a giant wave to swarm down and attack the opponent.

Trivia

In the manga, these sharks are formed from the water. In the anime, they appear to be
summoned
 sharks.

In the Shōnen Jump publication of chapter 506, the name of this technique was pronounced
Senjikizame
. It was corrected to
Senshokukō
 in the tankōbon release.
