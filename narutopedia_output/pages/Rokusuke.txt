Rokusuke
 (
ロクスケ
) is a miner from the
Land of Rivers
.

Appearance

Rokusuke has short black hair and black eyes. He wears a grey bandanna tied backwards, and a blue yukata.

Part I

Kurosuki Family Removal Mission

He and his friends left the village after
Raiga Kurosuki
 overthrows the village's government. They get caught in a storm after leaving and eventually wind up in the
Land of Fire
, where they meet
Naruto Uzumaki
. Rokusuke returns to the
Katabami Gold Mine
 once Naruto and other
Konoha
 ninja are sent to investigate Raiga.
