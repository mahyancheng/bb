"
Crisis: The Konoha 11 Gather!
" (
大ピンチ! 木ノ葉の11人全員集合
,
Daipinchi! Konoha no Jūichinin Zen'in Shūgō
) is episode 197 of the original
Naruto
 anime.

Synopsis

Naruto
 has lunch with an old man named
Gennō
, who came to
Konoha
 as a carpenter to help rebuild the village. However, during the night, the carpenter escapes with some documents and seemingly blows himself up in a suicide bombing, warning that Konoha will soon be destroyed. The
Konoha 11
 are told to find out more about the man's activities, but the
Anbu
 brings Naruto in for questioning, saying that Gennō was the one who stole the plans and escaped.
