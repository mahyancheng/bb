Background






Babu and Inagi kills Iwato.



Iwato and
his comrades
 were on a mission to deliver a scroll of classified data while being guarded by Team Minato. While crossing the forest, they were attacked by their opponents, causing Team Minato to split up, leaving
Rin Nohara
 and
Kakashi Hatake
 with Yugakure shinobi. They take them to the bridge, but
Shinbu
 was injured and fell off the cliff. Switching sides,
Inagi
 and
Babu
 revealed that they were traitors and mortally wounded Iwato by attacking him from behind. After a brief fight with Kakashi, during which Babu was injured, the traitors escaped. Iwato revealed that he knew about the traitors among their group, but he didn't know who they were. Therefore, they had two scrolls to deceive the traitor. The second scroll was held by Shinbu, followed by Babu, Inagi and the rest of their allies. Iwato died, and
Obito Uchiha
 began the pursuit of the traitors, joined by his team-mates.

Personality

Iwato was a brave and responsible man who guarded the scroll entrusted to him until his death. He was shocked by the betrayal of his comrades, and pleased that it was
Team Minato
 who was guarding them.

Appearance

Iwato was a man of average height with short, messy brown hair and black eyes. He wore a grey turtleneck and pants, and a purple flak jacket with one chest pocket on the left side, and one stomach pocket on the right side. In addition, a bandanna with a forehead protector was tied around his head, and a brown bag hangs from a belt on the back.

Abilities

Iwato did not display any martial skill, but he seemed to be a responsible and reliable person, as he was entrusted with one of the important scrolls that was being hunted by a large number of people.
