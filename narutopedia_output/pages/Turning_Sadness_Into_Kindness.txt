Turning Sadness Into Kindness
 (
悲しみをやさしさに
,
Kanashimi wo Yasashisa ni
), performed by
Little by Little
, was the third opening for the Japanese version of
Part I
 of the
Naruto
 series replacing
Far Away
. It was not used in the English dub and instead, was replaced by
GO!!!
. It was used from episodes
54
 to
77
, when it was succeeded by
GO!!!
 in the Japanese version as well.
