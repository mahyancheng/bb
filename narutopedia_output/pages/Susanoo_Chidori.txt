An application of Chidori through a Complete Body — Susanoo, whereby Sasuke produces a massive Chidori with its primary left hand.

Trivia

While unnamed in the manga, this technique was named in
Naruto Shippūden: Ultimate Ninja Storm 4
. In it, Sasuke also channels Chidori through a Susanoo arm he manifests to act as his own missing forearm.
