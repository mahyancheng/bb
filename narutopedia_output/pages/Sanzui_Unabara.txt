 (
海原サンズイ

daimyō

Land of Water
.

Personality

Sanzui cares deeply for his country, having spent a lot of time helping develop new power stations. He believes in making things better for the future, pointing out to his son Awaji he would inherit it all one day. He is also vengeful, as he demanded that Kirigakure severely punish the Funato clan for his son's death.

Appearance

Sanzui is a thin old man with a wrinkled face, short grey hair, a thin moustache, and a small goatee. He wears a black jacket with gold epaulettes on the shoulders and gold trim, pants with gold on the bottom of the legs, and shiny boots.
