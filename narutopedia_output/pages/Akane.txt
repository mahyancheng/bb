Akane
 (
アカネ
) was a bully who stole
Pochi
 from
Inari
.

Background

After he lost interest in Pochi, Akane threw him into the sea. When Inari hesitated to go in and save him, Akane pushed him in too. Although Inari started to drown, Akane and his friends left. After
Kaiza
 saved Inari, he gave Akane a stern talking to. He appears to not own Pochi anymore.

Personality

Akane was at first really mean and always bullied Inari but later on, in the anime, he came to realise that he was wrong and he apologised and became friends with him. Akane is also very cowardly as seen when he and his friends were kidnapped.

Appearance

Akane has short brown hair and small dark-coloured eyes.
