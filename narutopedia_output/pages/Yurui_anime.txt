Appearance

Yurui was a dark-skinned man with dark eyes, shoulder-length dull-purple hair, thin dull-purple eyebrows two sets of loop earrings in each earlobe and a labret piercing as well. He wore the standard attire of the Kumo-nin.

Abilities

Although his abilities were never shown, being a leader of a team implies that he was quite capable. According to
Nurui
, he was a great strategist and also skilled in laying traps, as he prepared a clever trap using the preexisting environment and
Earth Release
 with the help of Nurui and
Kayui
.
