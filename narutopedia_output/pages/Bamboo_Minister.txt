大臣
,
Daijin
) of the
Land of Bamboo
. He is also the former guardian of
Kae Yukiwari

Background

The minister had been gathering fifty orphaned children and trained them to become his personal assassins. After many of them died,
Hana Kaka
 was the one of the survivors and selected as his assassin. Ten years after the
Fourth Shinobi World War
, he ordered her to infiltrate
Konoha
 and become an Academy teacher.

Personality

The minister is a scheming man who wishes Kae's younger stepbrother to ascend the throne instead of her, even willing to have her assassinated to ensure it. He is also cruel and ruthless as he had many orphans raised into his personal army of assassins, not caring if most of them died during the training.

Appearance

The minister is a bald man with a dark short beard and two gold teeth.
