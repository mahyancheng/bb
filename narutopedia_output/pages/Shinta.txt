Background

He refused to let

Personality

He is a seemingly self-centred boy who immediately says what he is thinking, regardless of whether or not it will hurt someone's feelings.

Appearance

He had short, dark brown hair and dark eyes and had two rectangular markings on his cheeks. He wore a green pageboy-style cap, grey shirt and a pair of dark-coloured pants.
