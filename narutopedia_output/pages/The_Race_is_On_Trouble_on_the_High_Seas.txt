Naruto
 anime.

Synopsis

Team 7
 follows
Idate
 to another port which makes a better starting point of the boating part of the race, due to the winds and the current. En route, they are attacked by
Team Oboro
 from the
Chūnin Exams
, who have been hired by the other clan. Team 7 defends Idate and tell him to swim for the shore, while they follow later. He does so, but is confronted by
Aoi
, leader of the Ame ninja.
