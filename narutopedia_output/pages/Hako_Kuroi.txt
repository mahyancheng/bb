Personality

Hako is a very straight-forward person with a melancholic demeanour. She is very free and open with her opinions and likewise can be very blunt. Along with this direct nature, she is extremely perceptive, able to notice the smallest of details from a quick observation. From this, she can quickly discern the nature of person and their life to make correct conclusions on situations at hand. She seems to be very attached to her rabbit plushie, carrying it around regularly and decorating it with her Konoha forehead protector. Despite her normally indifferent nature, like many of the girls in her class, she can swoon over boys she is attracted to.

Appearance

Hako has dark blue hair and pink eyes that are often obstructed by her bangs. Her outfit consists of a black dress with a cape, as well as gloves and a ribbon on her head.

Abilities

Alongside her team-mates, despite her young age, Hako excels in information gathering missions, often being assigned Anbu-class missions.

Main article:
Byakuya Gang Arc

With the continued thefts performed by the

Hako celebrates Sai joining Team 25.



Later, while their
jōnin

Afterwards, at the suggestion of Sukea, Hōki apologised for his excessive commands, leading Renga and Hako to also apologise for their mistakes. Later, Team 25 was official reassigned to the command of
