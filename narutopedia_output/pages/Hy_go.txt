Kusagakure

Background

Hyōgo was once a famed shinobi from Kusagakure. Eventually, he abandoned his village to start his own crime syndicate. He took great effort to keep his activities hidden from the rest of the world, rarely showing his face in public.

Personality

Hyōgo is a very composed and indifferent man, rarely getting worked up. He is shown to be cautious, believing in keeping to himself so as to not draw attention. He is very shameless, taking over an entire village by force to better hide his personal actictivies. He is also very perceptive, able to see through lies by analysing the situation.

Appearance

Hyōgo is a middle-aged man of fair complexion. He has short grey hair and a well-groomed beared. He also wears sunglasses.

Abilities

While weak in battle, Hyōgo's efforts earned him recognition in the
Bingo Books
. He is well-known for his talents in espionage and intelligence.
