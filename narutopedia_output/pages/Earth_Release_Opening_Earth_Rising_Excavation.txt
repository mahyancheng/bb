With this technique, the user can bring up on the surface something that is deep underground. The targets are launched in air with great strength and speed, the result of which has the appearance and nearly size of an erupting volcano. It can force a large number of
soldiers
 hidden underground to resurface all at once.

While in the manga the technique has only been performed by two users together, the anime shows that a single user can perform a smaller scaled version which bring forth multiple earth pillars.
