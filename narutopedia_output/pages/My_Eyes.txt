"
My Eyes…!!
" (
オレの眼…!!
,
Ore no Me…!!
) is chapter 393 of the original
Naruto
 manga.

Summary

With
Orochimaru
 gone, Sasuke is out of options.
Itachi
 approaches Sasuke to take his
eyes
, and Sasuke makes futile attempts to keep him away. As Itachi draws nearer his
Susanoo
 begins to disappear and he starts to cough up blood. This does not prevent him from cornering Sasuke. He reaches for Sasuke's eyes and mutters something, but only makes it as far as poking Sasuke's forehead before collapsing.
