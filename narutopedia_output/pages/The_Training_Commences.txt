Summary

To improve their
chakra
 control, and thus the effectiveness of their
techniques
,
Kakashi
 teaches
Team 7
 how to climb trees.
Naruto
 fails completely,
Sasuke
 can only make it halfway up, and
Sakura
 is able to make it to the top on her first try. After repeated failures, Naruto asks Sakura for tips. Meanwhile,
Zabuza
 recovers from his fight with Kakashi.
Gatō
 approaches him intending to kill him for his failure, but is stopped by
Haku
, who breaks Gatō's arm in punishment for even considering touching Zabuza. Zabuza assures Gatō that he will finish the job in a week's time.
