 from

Background

Hisame left her village behind to help
Suien
 seek the
Hero Water
 in
Takigakure
.

Appearance






Hisames full appearance.



Hisame is a young, quite tall, and slim kunoichi with fair skin, short light brown unkempt spiky hair, azure blue earrings, red lipstick, pupil-less black eye irises, short thin light brown eyebrows and wears a white hairband over her head, holding the hair out of the way from her eyes. She wore a pink tube top that has a dark red linen along with a open, light blue shirt, navy blue pants and ninja sandals. She wore her shuriken holster on her right leg.

Abilities

She was noted to be one of the smartest kunoichi in Amegakure. Also, she could use
Water Release: Water Whip
 to make a whip out of water which she could use to bind her opponent. She could also use
Lightning Release
, capable of channelling lightning chakra through her water whip to immobilise enemies. In the novelisation, Hisame could
create weapons out of water
 for both short and long-range combat.

She assisted

Sasuke Uchiha
 while
Naruto Uzumaki
 pursued
Shibuki
. Hisame was defeated when Shibuki drank the Hero Water to fight back against Hisame and her fellow attackers. She was revealed to be alive in the novel and was captured by
Kakashi Hatake
 in the end.
