Team Ro
 is an elite
Anbu
 unit of
Konohagakure
 that serves directly under the
Hokage
.

Known Missions

Eliminate shinobi from Kirigakure

Assigned to:

Kakashi Hatake
,
Tenzō

Outcome:
 Success

Team Ro was once tasked to kill hostile shinobi from
Kirigakure
, who were all eliminated during their escape.

Eliminate all hostiles

Assigned to:

Kakashi Hatake
,
Itachi Uchiha

Outcome:
 Success

Team Ro members, Kakashi and Itachi, were sent to watch over the exchange of information between a team from Konohagakure and the
Prajñā Group
 of the
Land of Woods
. In the likely event of betrayal, they were to eliminate all opposition which actually happened, as the Prajñā Group indeed attempted to betray Konohagakure.

Eliminate the saboteurs from Kusagakure

Assigned to:
 Kakashi Hatake, Itachi Uchiha, Tenzō and two others

Outcome:
 Success

One time, five shinobi from
Kusagakure
 tried to sabotage a street by burying
explosive tags
. Team Ro was tasked to kill them on the spot without leaving any traces that could burden Konohagakure.

Investigate barrier transgression

Assigned to:
 Kakashi Hatake

Outcome:
 Partial Success

After several instances of an
intruder
 bypassing the Konoha
barrier system
 around the
Uchiha clan
 district, Kakashi was tasked with investigating. Unable to uncover the perpetrator, Kakashi reported his findings to the
Third Hokage
.

Intercept Coup d'État

Assigned to:
 Presumed all

Outcome:
 Failure

Team Ro accompanied the Third Hokage to the Uchiha clan district in order to pacify the Uchiha and thus averting civil war. Their efforts however turn out to be irrelevant, due to the
slaughter
 of the majority of the clan by Itachi.

Treat Sasuke Uchiha

Assigned to:

Yūgao Uzuki

Outcome:
 Success

The sole survivor of the Uchiha Clan Downfall,
Sasuke Uchiha
, was found by Yūgao Uzuki, a member of Team Ro who was tasked by Hiruzen Sarutobi to ensure Sasuke's survival by bringing him to the
Medic Corps
.

Dispose of the corpses

Assigned to:
 Presumed all

Outcome:
 Success

Directly after the slaughter of the Uchiha clan, Team Ro and the other present Anbu units were tasked to take away the corpses which was a tremendous mission even for Anbu standards.
