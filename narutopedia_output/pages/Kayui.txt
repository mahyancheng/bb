Personality

Kayui is a relatively calm and level-headed individual as seen from when he calmly questioned what they should do when they were being pursued by Ameyuri. He later attempted to keep the peace between his comrades when a conflict arose.

Appearance

Kayui is a tall, dark-skinned shinobi of muscular build with shoulder-length slicked-back blond hair and purple, pupiless eyes. He wears the standard attire of the Kumogakure shinobi, and wears his shirt zipped down midway.

Abilities

He is skilled in laying traps as he helped his comrades Yurui and Nurui creating a clever trap, using the pre-existing environment and
Earth Release
. He is also very durable, able to survive a
Lightning Release
 attack by Ameyuri Ringo.
