Trivia

The episode has a number of differences from
Boruto: Naruto the Movie
 including:

Urashiki gives information about the tailed beasts and Earth's inhabitants to Momoshiki and Kinshiki.

Urashiki appears alongside Momoshiki and Kinshiki while the two were extracting Gyūki from Killer B.

Shukaku warns Gaara that it lost its connection to Gyūki's jinchūriki.
