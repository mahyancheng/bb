Himeyuri
 (
ヒメユリ
) was a wealthy businesswoman and the president of the Himeyuri Company. She was a patron at the
Coliseum
.

Personality

Himeyuri indulged in a wealthy lifestyle and had a short temper, scolding the
shinobi
 she contracted after they lose a fight.

Appearance

Himeyuri was a fair-skinned woman with a rotund build. She had long blonde hair held back with a red hairband and turquoise-coloured eyes. She wore red lipstick and light purple eye shadow. She wore a large pink dress with white gloves and purple earrings.
