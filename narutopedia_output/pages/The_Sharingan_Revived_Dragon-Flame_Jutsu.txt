"
The Sharingan Revived: Dragon-Flame Jutsu!
" (
蘇れ写輪眼!必殺・火遁龍火の術!
,
Yomigaere Sharingan! Hissatsu: Katon Ryūka no Jutsu!
) is episode 30 of the original
Naruto
 anime.

Synopsis

Sasuke
 and
Shiore
 engage in combat. After a long and difficult battle, Sasuke seemingly ends the battle with his
Fire Release: Dragon Fire Technique
 to incinerate Shiore. However, the
shinobi
 survives with his face partially melted off, revealing
another face
 underneath. The shinobi reveals himself to be
Orochimaru
 and then
extending
 his neck, bites Sasuke and leaves behind a
cursed seal
; wishing Sasuke luck in surviving, he leaves.
Anko
 finds Orochimaru and attacks him, intent on killing her former sensei for his crimes, even at the potential cost of her own life, although she fails and Orochimaru escapes.

Trivia

This is the first
Naruto
 episode to be distributed and animated by
Aniplex
 instead of
VIZ Media
.
