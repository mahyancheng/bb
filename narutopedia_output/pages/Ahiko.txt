Ahiko
 (
アヒコ
) was a prisoner at
Orochimaru
's
Northern Hideout
.

Appearance

He received a
cursed seal
 in the course of Orochimaru's experiments. When active, the seal gives him two large flap-like protrusions on both sides of his head, imitating that of the frill necked lizard also gaining some visible features of said lizard. He also had long light purple hair and he wore the typical attire of one of Orochimaru's prisoners which consisted of a white T-shirt and a pair of pants.

Abilities

He is a ferocious fighter because of his violent temper and was able to enter the second state of the cursed seal.
