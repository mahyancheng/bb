Personality

Okyō seems to be a very happy and fun person as she is seen to always smile.

Appearance

Okyō has long, smooth light-brown hair that hangs over her face a bit and wears a greenish-coloured swimsuit. She is fair-skinned and has a plain looking face. She also has large, light-coloured eyes and was seen to wear a thin shade of red lipstick.
