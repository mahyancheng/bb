The
Steam Ninja Scrolls Arc
 (
湯煙忍法帖編
,
Yukemuri Ninpōchō-hen
) is the twelfth
arc
 of the
Boruto: Naruto Next Generations
 anime. It covers episodes 106 to 111. It is preceded by
Jūgo Arc
 and followed by
Konohamaru's Love Arc
. This arc is an anime adaptation of the
Konoha Shinden: Steam Ninja Scrolls
 novel, although it takes place at a later point in the storyline. The same novel later received a
manga adaptation
.
