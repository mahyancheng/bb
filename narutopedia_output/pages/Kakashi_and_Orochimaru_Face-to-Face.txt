"
Kakashi and Orochimaru: Face-to-Face!
" (
一触即発!!カカシVS大蛇丸
,
Isshokusokuhatsu!! Kakashi tai Orochimaru
) is episode 40 of the original
Naruto
 anime.

Synopsis

Kakashi
 threatened to kill
Orochimaru
, a threat that Orochimaru took lightly. Despite this, Orochimaru chose to walk away. As he left, Kakashi admitted to himself that he could not kill Orochimaru on his own. Meanwhile,
Shino
 and
Zaku
 continued their match; although Zaku revealed that he was in fact able to use both of his arms again, Shino ultimately won his match through the deceptive use of his destructive bugs, leaving
Kankurō
 and
Misumi Tsurugi
 to fight next.

Trivia

In the manga, Zaku's arms were blown off while in the anime, they weren't.
