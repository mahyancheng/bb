Trivia

Vajra is a legendary
ritualistic tool
 that symbolised the properties of a diamond and a thunderbolt. It was also the weapon of
Indra
. In East Asian culture, it is often synonymous with diamond/adamantine, and is said in Japanese as "kongō" (金剛).
