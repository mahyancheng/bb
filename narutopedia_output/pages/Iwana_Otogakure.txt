Personality

Like most petty villains Iwana thought that he was very powerful when in actuality he wasn't. He was very loyal to Orochimaru. Iwana also had a perverted side, getting a nosebleed from watching Naruto's
Harem Technique
, and he was also short-tempered, as he vowed to kill Naruto for it.

Appearance

He wore a green-coloured shark-like top complete with a hood which resembled a shark's head, with an appendage on the back that resembled a dorsal fin and dark pants. He also had black-coloured eyes and two purple markings under each eye. He carried a giant shuriken on his back.

Iwana could use
Hiding with Camouflage Technique

chakra

weapons
