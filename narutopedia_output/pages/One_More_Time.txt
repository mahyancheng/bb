" (

Naruto
 manga.

Summary

Naruto
 wakes up and learns that
Kakashi
 has gone to stop
Sakura
. He tries to follow, but is stopped by
Yamato
. Naruto accepts that he cannot go and asks to rest some more, but only takes the opportunity to sneak out. Elsewhere, Sakura manages to knock out
Sai
 and her other escorts. Sai is able to inform Kakashi of her success before passing out. Meanwhile,
Tobi
 advises Sasuke not to go to
Konoha
 since his eyes are on the verge of blindness. He adds that it would be a good idea to finish off
Karin
. Tobi leaves and Sasuke prepares to kill Karin. He is interrupted by Sakura, who asks to join forces with him.
