Murasame
 (
ムラサメ
) is a
missing-nin
 from
Amegakure
.

Background

Murasame left his village behind to help
Suien
 seek the
Hero Water
 in
Takigakure
.

Appearance

Murasame is a muscular, tough-looking shinobi. He wears a black, sleeveless shirt, grey pants, ninja sandals, and brown-coloured arm warmers. He has about chin length grey hair which is spiked at the top. He also has a teal line marking that goes over his nose, somewhat resemblant of
Iruka
's scar.

Abilities

Murasame was more skilled than
Hisame
 and
Kirisame
 in the fact that when Suien is not around he was left in charge of the team.

Ninjutsu

Murasame could use
Water Release: Water Whip
 to make a whip out of water that he could use to bind his opponents. He is also skilled at genjutsu, which he uses to create hazy afterimages of himself to confuse his enemies.

Taijutsu

Murasame utilises a high-speed taijutsu, which minimises the opponent's ability to react to his attacks.

Other Skills

Murasame also carries a specially made wire that, when used to bind a person, can slice through their body to cause a great deal of damage if the person tries to break free with the
Rope Escape Technique
.

Plot Overview

He assisted
Hisame
 in taking down
Sasuke Uchiha
 while
Naruto Uzumaki
 pursued
Shibuki
. Murasame was defeated when Shibuki drank the Hero Water to fight back against Murasame and his fellow attackers with a Water Dragon technique. He was revealed to be alive in the novel and was captured by
Kakashi
 in the end.

Trivia
