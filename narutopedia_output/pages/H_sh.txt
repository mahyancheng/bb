Hōshō
 (
ホウショウ
) is a
medical-nin
 from
Konohagakure
.

Personality

He was noted to be a kind-hearted person who desires nothing more than peace for Konohagakure.

Appearance

He wears the standard high collared, full body uniform of the Konoha medical ninja. He also has black hair and big black eyes.

Abilities

As a medical-nin, Hōshō presumably has a high level of intellect, is well-versed in
medical ninjutsu
, and has excellent
chakra control
 to perform medical procedures.

Part I

Sasuke Recovery Mission

Hōshō was assigned to one of the two medical units that was deployed to provide backup for the
Sasuke Recovery Team
, and was disappointed to hear that they failed to retrieve
Sasuke
. He then informed
Kakashi
 of the status of the other members of the team while they travelled back to Konoha.
