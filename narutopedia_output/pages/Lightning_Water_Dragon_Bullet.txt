Usage

After kneading chakra in her stomach and converting it into water, Mei expels it in the shape of a dragon. Taking advantage of the highly conductive nature of water, A then imbues the water dragon with lightning-natured chakra. When this technique makes contact with a target, it numbs their body, thereby dulling their movements and leaving them susceptible to other attacks.
