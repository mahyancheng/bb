A "
fighting style
" is the characteristic ability upon which most or even all of a
shinobi
's
jutsu
 are based. In the case of
taijutsu
 users, their fighting style dictates their stance in combat and how they strike their opponents; examples include the
Strong Fist
 and
Gentle Fist
. In the case of
ninjutsu
 users, their fighting style is a cornerstone to all their tactics and by which they are most easily identified; examples include
Sai
's
Super Beast Imitating Drawing
 and
Konan
's
Dance of the Shikigami
. Ninjutsu users' fighting styles  may alternatively be defined by what tool or resource they most commonly employ rather than a single ability; examples include
Gaara
's
sand
 or the
Inuzuka clan
's
ninken
 partners.
