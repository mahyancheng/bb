Sakuya
 (
サクヤ
) was a noble of the
Land of Silence
.

Personality

Sakuya was a vain woman, very obsessed with how she looked. To which, once her youth faded, she ordered for all reflective surfaces be removed from her palace, just so she couldn't see her diminished form. So desperate to regain her former looks, she went to great lengths to acquire some of
Hashirama Senju
's cells due to her incorrect belief that it can be used for rejuvenation. She could also be cautious, having a dedicated team to project a
barrier
 over her
residence
 at all times.

Appearance

Sakuya was an old woman with long white hair. Her face is covered with wrinkles, she had a thick layer of red coloured lipstick and purple eyeshadow. She was reputed to have been exceptionally beautiful in her youth.
