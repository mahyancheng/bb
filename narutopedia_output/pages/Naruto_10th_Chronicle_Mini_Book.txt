The
Naruto 10th Chronicle Mini Book
 (
NARUTO -ナルト- 10th クロニクル・ミニブック
,
Naruto 10th Kuronikuru Mini Bukku
) is a supplementary book included with an issue of
Weekly Shonen Jump
 to celebrate the manga's tenth anniversary. There are pages depicting select scenes of the manga, each followed by a summary of the arc the scene appeared in.

Contents

Sasuke saves Naruto from Haku
 during the
Battle of the Land of Waves
 (pp. 4-5)

Naruto releases the Nine-Tails while fighting Neji
 during the
Chūnin Exams
 (pp. 6-9)

Naruto releases the Nine-Tails while fighting Gaara
 during the
Konoha Crush
 (pp. 10-11)

Naruto saves Tsunade from Kabuto
 during the
Search for Tsunade
 (pp. 12-13)

Naruto releases the Nine-Tails while fighting Sasuke
 during the
Sasuke Recovery Mission
 (pp. 14-17)

Naruto finds Deidara with Gaara's body
 during the
Kazekage Rescue Mission
 (pp. 18-19)

Naruto sees Sasuke again for the first time
 during the
Sasuke Reunion
 (pp. 20-23)

Naruto readies his Rasenshuriken
 during the
New Way of Study
 (pp. 24-25)

Sasuke meets Itachi face-to-face
 during the
Fated Battle Between Brothers
 (pp. 26-27)

Naruto vows to defeat Pain
 during
Pain's Assault
 (pp. 28-29)
