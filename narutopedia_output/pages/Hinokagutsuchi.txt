Hinokagutsuchi
 is a Mangekyō Sharingan dōjutsu unique to Indra Ōtsutsuki's left eye. It strongly resembles
Amaterasu
 in both form and function, allowing him to produce black flames at the focal point of his vision. Little else is known about the technique, though the flames faltered before
Asura Ōtsutsuki
's
Amenomihashira
.

Influence

As with
Sasuke
's
Blaze Release: Kagutsuchi
, the name of this technique is derived from
Kagutsuchi
, the
Shinto
 God of Fire.

Trivia

While unnamed in the anime, this technique was named in
Naruto: Shinobi Collection Shippū Ranbu
.
