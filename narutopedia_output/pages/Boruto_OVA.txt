This animated short is the OVA for
Boruto: Naruto Next Generations
. It premiered at Jump Special Anime Festa in November 2016, and was officially released alongside
Naruto Shippuden: Ultimate Ninja Storm Legacy
 and in the anime's first English DVD/Blu-ray set.
