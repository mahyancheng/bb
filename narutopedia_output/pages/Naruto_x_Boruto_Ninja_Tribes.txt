Naruto x Boruto: Ninja Tribes
 (
NARUTO X BORUTO 忍者TRIBES
,
Naruto X Boruto Ninja Toribesu
) is a free-to-play action strategy RPG published by
Namco Bandai Games
. It was released globally on June 2019. From December 16, 2021, the game is closed and will not be updated more, but it leaves players the opportunity to continue playing what they have.

The game begins with the events of the first episode of
Naruto: Shippūden
 and goes down to the events of
Boruto: Naruto Next Generations
. Shippūden's events are presented as memories of the
Seventh Hokage
.
