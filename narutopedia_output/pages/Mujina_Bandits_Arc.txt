The
Mujina Bandits Arc
 (
貉強盗団編
,
Mujina Gōtōdan-hen
) is an
arc
 of
Boruto: Naruto Next Generations
. It spans from volumes
3
 to
4
 and covers chapters 11 to 15
arc
 of the
Boruto
 manga and episodes 141 to 151 of the
Boruto
 anime. In the manga, it is preceded by the
Versus Momoshiki Arc
 and is followed by the
Ao Arc
. In the anime, it is preceded by the
Time Slip Arc
 and is followed by the
Kara Actuation Arc
.
