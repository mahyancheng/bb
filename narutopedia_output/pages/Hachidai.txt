Hachidai
 (
ハチダイ
) is a miner from the
Land of Rivers
.

Appearance

Hachidai is a bald headed man with black eyes. He has a black beard and wears a simple outfit with a blue-coloured cloth tied around his shoulders and a white twisted cloth wrapped around his head tied on the right side.

Part I

Kurosuki Family Removal Mission

He and his friends leave the village after
Raiga Kurosuki
 overthrows the village's government. They get caught in a storm after leaving and eventually wind up in the
Land of Fire
, where they meet
Naruto Uzumaki
. Hachidai returns to
Katabami Gold Mine
 once Naruto and other
Konoha
 ninja are sent to investigate Raiga.

Trivia

Hachidai means "great eight".
