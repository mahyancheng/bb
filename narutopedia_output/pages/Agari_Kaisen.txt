Agari Kaisen
 (
海鮮アガリ
,
Kaisen Agari
) is the head of the
Kedōin Clan
 and is an old friend of
Tsunade
.

Personality

Agari is a very joyous man as well as a great actor though according to Tsunade, he has flaws.

Appearance

Agari has shoulder-length smooth black hair and almond-shaped grey-coloured eyes. He also has a very thin moustache.

Abilities

Agari is able to use the
Face Copying Technique
 which is an advanced version of the
Transformation Technique
.

,

, and

 a mission to find a treasure chest for Agari in which if they fail, Tsunade will send them back to the

Konohagakure
". But after Agari and his clan members left, Naruto, Kiba, and Hinata managed to free themselves, caught up with Agari, and defeated him and his clan members. In the end, the mission was revealed to be a test for Naruto and the others as part of their last chance mission. Agari allows Tsunade to let them pass. He also left Tsunade a bill before leaving for his home.
