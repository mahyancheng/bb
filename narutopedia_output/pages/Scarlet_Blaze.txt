Scarlet Blaze
 (
红莲之瞳
) is a ninja of
Konohagakure
, and one of the playable characters in
Naruto: Ultimate Ninja Online
.

Personality

He enjoys looking in the mirror and shows interest in the various forms of ninjutsu in the world. A hardworking and pragmatic person with a striking appearance. Calm and collected temperament, Scarlet Blaze is adept at analysing opponents' weaknesses.

Abilities

Prefers using
Fire Release
 techniques and
genjutsu
. He is calm in battle and his forte lies in the analysis of the opponent's weaknesses. A key characteristic of Scarlet Blaze is being able to identify flaws in the enemies'
ninjutsu
 and, in turn, strengthen his own while weakening theirs.

In Other Media

Video Games

Naruto: Ultimate Ninja Online

Scarlet Blaze is one of the five player avatars available in the game. They work through the plot of
Naruto
 and
Naruto: Shippūden
 alongside the main characters.
