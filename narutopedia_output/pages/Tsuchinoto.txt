Background

To prevent

Hokage

Personality

Tsuchinoto would always listen and do what Danzō wanted him to do.

Appearance

Tsuchinoto has long, greyish-brown hair that he wears in a high ponytail. He wears a standard Anbu attire, and his mask has dark green markings.

As an Anbu, Tsuchinoto is a presumably a powerful shinobi. He wields a
sword
, hinting he's skilled in
kenjutsu
