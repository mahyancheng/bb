Appearance

Tomaru sports short, black hair with a distinctive protrusion at the back. His black eyes are complemented by two dark markings underneath. Attire-wise, he opts for a short-sleeved, light-purple kimono shirt with pleats at the bottom. Around his waist, he wears a purple obi paired with black pants, and sandals adorn his feet.
