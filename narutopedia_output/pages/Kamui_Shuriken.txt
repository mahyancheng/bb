Kamui Shuriken
 is a technique utilised by Kakashi Hatake through his Susanoo.

Usage

After manifesting the
Complete Body — Susanoo
, Kakashi is able to create several, large projectiles shaped like
Obito Uchiha
's
Mangekyō Sharingan
 which he imbues with
Kamui
. The shuriken are then launched by Susanoo — rapidly spinning towards the intended target before cutting and instantly warping them into
Kamui's dimension
 on contact.

Trivia

In
Naruto Shippūden: Ultimate Ninja Storm 4
, Kakashi is also able to throw miniature versions of these shuriken without manifesting his Susanoo.
