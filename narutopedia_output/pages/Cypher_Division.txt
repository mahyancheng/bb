 (

Kirigakure
's secret intelligence squad.
Kisame Hoshigaki
 was assigned by
Fuguki Suikazan
 to be the squad's bodyguard, as well as protecting the codes they were carrying. When the group was cornered by
Ibiki Morino
, a master interrogator, Kisame remembered that
his superior
 had stressed that the codes were far more important than the lives of any member of the group, and resolved to slaughter all of his comrades, to prevent the codes from falling into the wrong hands.
