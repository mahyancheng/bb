The
Naruto Project
 (
-NARUTO PROJECT-
), also known short as
Naru Pro
 (
ナルプロ
,
Naru Puro
), is a special project to celebrate 15 years of the
Naruto
 series. It's also titled
Naruto's New Era Opening Project
 (
NARUTO -ナルト- 新時代開幕プロジェクト
,
Naruto: Shinjidai Kaimaku Purojekuto
).

List of Projects

The following releases and events are part of the Naruto Project:

2014

The databook
Jin no Sho
, which was released on November 4th.

The tenth movie
The Last: Naruto the Movie
, which began airing on December 6th.

The moviebook
Retsu no Sho
, which was given out to viewers of the movie.

The Super Big Yosegaki Project (
超巨大よせがきプロジェクト
,
Chōkyodai Yosegaki Purojekuto
), a
Naruto
 exhibition in the Shibuya park on December 6th and 7th.

2015

Live Spectacle Naruto
, a stage play that started on March 21st.

Naruto Hiden
, six short novels that are released during the year.

Kakashi Hiden: Lightning in the Icy Sky
, which was released on February 4th.

Shikamaru Hiden: A Cloud Drifting in Silent Darkness‎
, which was released on March 4th.

Sakura Hiden: Thoughts of Love, Riding Upon a Spring Breeze
, which was released on April 3rd.

Konoha Hiden: The Perfect Day for a Wedding
, which was released on May 1st.

Gaara Hiden: A Sandstorm Mirage
, which was released on June 4th.

Akatsuki Hiden: Evil Flowers in Full Bloom
, which was released on July 3rd.

The
Shūeisha Jump Remix
, a re-release of the
Naruto
 manga in bigger volumes.

The Naruto Exhibition (
NARUTO展
,
Naruto-ten
), which opened on April 25th.

Naruto Shinden: Fū no Sho
 and
Naruto Shinden: Rai no Sho
, two booklets given out to the visitors of the exhibition.

The sequel mini-series
Naruto Gaiden: The Seventh Hokage and the Scarlet Spring
, which was serialised from April 27th to July 6th.

An Opening Day Countdown Event for the eleventh movie on August 7th.

The eleventh movie
Boruto: Naruto the Movie
, which began airing on August 7th.

The moviebook
Zai no Sho
, which was given out to viewers of the movie.
