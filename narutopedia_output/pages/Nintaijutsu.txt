Nintaijutsu
 (
忍体術
,
literally meaning:
 Ninja Body Techniques)
 is a term used to describe the
fighting style
 that incorporates the use of
ninjutsu
 and
taijutsu
. This fighting style is most prominent in
Kumogakure
,
 with the
Third
 and
Fourth Raikage
 being its foremost users. By surrounding themselves in their
Lightning Release Chakra Mode
, the two's strength, durability, speed, and reflexes are augmented to an exceptional degree.
