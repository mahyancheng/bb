This technique creates water that spirals in the user's hand which fires at a high speed towards the enemy. In the anime, it was also shown to be able to be conjured from the surrounding area in the form of a torrential wave.
 It can also be combined with the
Wind Release: Rasengan
 to create the
Typhoon Water Vortex Technique
.

Trivia

In
Naruto Shippūden: Ultimate Ninja Heroes 3
, Yamato uses this technique in a similar manner as the
Rasengan
.

In
Naruto Shippūden: Kizuna Drive
, Yamato uses it as an offensive shield.
