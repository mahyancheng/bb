Background

Asami had been hospitalised due to an unspecified illness. The day before she died, she and Tatsumi have a trivial argument, after which, Tatsumi refused to speak to her for the rest of the day. By the following day, her condition had worsened, and Asami died before she and Tatsumi get the chance to reconcile.

Appearance






Tatsumi's photograph of Asami.



Asami had fair skin, clear blue eyes with no visible pupils, and straight, light brown hair. She wears her hair down with bangs that reach her eyes. She had strong resemblance to Tatsumi's.
