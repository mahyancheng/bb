When
Kimimaro
 enters the second level of his
cursed seal
, his spine grows longer to give him a tail-like appendage. For this technique, Kimimaro removes that spine to use as a weapon.
Cartilage
 between the individual vertebrae makes the spine flexible for use as a
whip
, and the cartilage can be stretched further to increase the whip's range.
 The vertebrae are also modified to have spikes, making it easier for them to hook into targets. When this spine whip is wrapped around a target, it holds them in place, making it easier to kill them with
Dance of the Clematis: Flower
.
