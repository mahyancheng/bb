The
Three Senka Brothers
 (
撰歌三兄弟
,
Senka Sankyōdai
) are a set of triplets that make up a
Kirigakure

genin
 team.

History

Having qualified for the
Chūnin Exams
, the brothers participated in the
Five Great Shinobi Countries
 sponsored exams in
Konohagakure
. There, the team managed to pass the first round of the exams by not falling into a pit of ink. Three days later, for the second round of the exams, the team competed in a game of capture the flag against
Team Konohamaru
. After one of its members protected their flag using
genjutsu
, the three confronted
Boruto Uzumaki
, who
defeated
 them, leading to them being disqualified.
