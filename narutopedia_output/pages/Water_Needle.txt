Using the chakra control granted by the
Gentle Fist
,
Hinata
 concentrates her chakra until water vortexes are created around her. Those vortexes shoot water blasts which turn to needle-like water. By using
Byakugan
, this technique can be used with great precision to hit very small targets such as
bees
.

Trivia

Though unnamed in the anime, this technique was named in
Naruto: Path of the Ninja 2
.
