Variants

The black flames can form a sword to be wielded by Susanoo, which is able to stab and slice through individuals or defences, incinerating whatever remains.

Magatama-shaped projectiles
 can be unleashed from the orb in volleys, knocking back and subsequently incinerating their targets.

This technique can produce arrows to be utilised with Susanoo's bow.
