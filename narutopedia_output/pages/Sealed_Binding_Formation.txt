The
Sealed Binding Formation
 (
封縛法陣
,
Fūbaku Hōjin
) is a capture technique (
捕縛術
,
hobaku jutsu
).

Overview

An array of
sealing tags
 are placed around an area that can be activated with a
hand seal
 and binds their target. Since the target is unable to move, another individual will then be able to attack them with no problem. These tags can be deactivated by the user.
