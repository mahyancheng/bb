The term "
absolute defence
" (
絶対防御
,
zettai bōgyo
) refers to any defensive technique that provides protection for the user to the point that is considered near impenetrable. Techniques that are considered to be such include:

Eight Trigrams Palms Revolving Heaven
, the Hyūga clan's secret technique.

Magnet Release: Lightning God Self
, one of Kajūra's techniques.

Protecting Eight Trigrams Sixty-Four Palms
, a unique technique created by Hinata Hyūga.

Shield of Sand
, Gaara's automatic defence.

Ultimately Hard Absolute Defence: Shield of Shukaku
, Gaara's ultimate defensive technique.

Susanoo
, a humanoid being composed of the user's chakra, manifested by their Mangekyō Sharingan.

Summoning: Rashōmon
, a massive metal gate capable of intercepting nearly any attack.

Summoning: Triple Rashōmon
, three massive metal gates capable of intercepting almost any attack.

Summoning: Quintuple Rashōmon
, five massive metal gates capable of intercepting almost any attack.
