The
Crescent Moon Rasengan
 is a more powerful version of the
Rasengan
 used by Naruto in
Naruto the Movie: Guardians of the Crescent Moon Kingdom
.

Usage

By combining the rays of a crescent moon with a Rasengan, the size of the Rasengan is increased, which glows a bright white, light-purple colour, and forms a white crescent moon with a star inside of it.
