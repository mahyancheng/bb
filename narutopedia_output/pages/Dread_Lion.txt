The
Dread Lion
 (
ドレッドライオン
,
Doreddoraion
) is a giant lion whose pawprint was collected by
Itachi
 and
Sasuke Uchiha
 for their
Paw Encyclopaedia
.

Background

Some time in the past when Itachi and Sasuke were children, they partook in a game where they searched for cats to fill their Paw Encyclopaedia. When confronted by the brothers, the lion broke down and began, what seemed to be, pleading for his life.

Personality

It seems to be very cowardly.

Appearance

The Dread Lion is a huge, tan-coloured lion whose brown mane is styled into
dreadlocks
. His paw print is huge, showing he is much bigger than an ordinary lion. He also has a brown nose.

Trivia

This character may be based on the
Cowardly Lion
 from
The Wizard of Oz
 as he resembles the character as portrayed in the 1939 movie version. The Cowardly Lion at first appeared fierce, but then broke down into a cowardly, whimpering figure when confronted.
