The
Land of Haze
 (
霞の国
,
Kasumi no Kuni
) is a country where the
Haze Quadruplets
 originate from. From the
Land of Silence
, the country can be reached in a day by foot, if the long route is taken. The shinobi specialise in making a
lethal poison
 that kills in an instant. According to
Omoi
, shinobi from this land once used their poison to kill a higher up from
Kumogakure
.

Trivia

The
Moya Triad
, with "Moya" meaning "Haze", who bore haze-like symbols on their headbands, specialised in a type of poison that causes the victim to laugh, hiccup or burp uncontrollably to death. Whether they originated from this country is unknown.
