Tsuzumi Sarugaku
 (
申楽ツヅミ
,
Sarugaku Tsuzumi
) is a
chūnin
 of
Konohagakure
.

Background

Not much is known about Tsuzumi's past. The anime revealed that he entered the
Academy
 at the same time as
Kakashi Hatake
 and others.

Personality

Tsuzumi has a happy demeanour and is usually seen smiling. He enjoys hearing and watching the going-ons in the Chūnin Exams.

Appearance

Tsuzumi has spiky brown hair and small black eyes. He wears the standard Konoha shinobi outfit complete with
flak jacket
 and
forehead protector
.

Part I

Chūnin Exams

Main article:
Chūnin Exams

During the final rounds of the
Chūnin Exams
, Tsuzumi was one of the ninja guarding the entrance to the arena. When
Lee
 and
Guy
 arrive, he welcomes them and then informs them of
Neji
's defeat by
Naruto
.

Part II

Pain's Assault

Main article:
Pain's Assault

In the anime, Tsuzumi was with
Riichi
 and several other guards outside the village wall when
Pain
 attacked them. After Pain killed Riichi and the others, Tsuzumi attempted to flee to inform
Tsunade
 of the incident. Before he could leave, however, he was
pulled in
 by Pain and impaled with a
chakra rod
, dying in the process. He was later revived by
Nagato
's
Outer Path — Samsara of Heavenly Life Technique
.

Trivia

The "
Tsuzumi
" (鼓) is a Japanese drum of Chinese and Indian origin.

Tsuzumi's last name is written with the kanji 申楽, which is an alternative way to write down the name of a form of theatre popular in Japan during the 11th to 14th centuries called "
Sarugaku
" (猿楽).

A shinobi resembling Tsuzumi, alongside Riichi and
Fugaku Uchiha
, were seen evacuating villagers to the southeast shelter during the Nine-Tails attack on Konoha.
