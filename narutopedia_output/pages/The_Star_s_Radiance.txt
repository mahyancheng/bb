"
The Star's Radiance
" (
星は輝きを増して
,
Hoshi wa Kagayaki o Mashite
) is episode 183 of the original
Naruto
 anime.

Synopsis

After
Akahoshi
 tries to execute
Sumaru
,
Hokuto
 and the rest of the children stand up to protect their village, revealing the poisoning caused by the star. After learning the truth, the adults turn on Akahoshi as well. After Akahoshi nearly kills
Mizura
, he is given no second chances. Not being one to lose, Akahoshi fuses his body with the Star, causing his chakra to go out of control.
Naruto
, guided by
Natsuhi
's spirit, destroys the star and defeats Akahoshi. Although
Tsunade
 initially seems upset over Naruto failing the mission, she concedes that the mission was cancelled due to being ordered by Akahoshi himself, while
Shizune
 announce the recovery of the children from their poisoning.
