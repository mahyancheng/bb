Naruto x Boruto: Ninja Voltage
, known as
Naruto x Boruto: Ninja Borutage
 (
NARUTO X BORUTO 忍者BORUTAGE
,
Naruto X Boruto Ninja Borutēji
) in Japan, is a free-to-play action strategy RPG published by Bandai Namco Entertainment. It was released globally on November 22, 2017. The game ended its service on December 9, 2024.

Gameplay

Players can play solo to build forts and place traps to protect their fort against invaders. They can also raid the forts of other players in Attack Missions. In an event called Team Battle, players team up with other players worldwide and use teamwork to overcome enemies. Once a month, players can team up in Surprise Attack Missions where they will take on Tailed Beasts to win rewards. Most of the techniques that are used are carried over from the
Ultimate Ninja Storm
 series.
