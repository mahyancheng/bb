"
Genjutsu or Reality?
" (
幻術か現実か　五感を制するもの
,
Genjutsu ka Genjitsu ka, Gokan o Seisuru Mono
) is episode 206 of the original
Naruto
 anime.

Synopsis

Naruto
,
Sakura
,
Kurenai
,
Yakumo
 and
Team 8
 return to
Konoha
, which appears to have been destroyed in their absence. They learn from the head of the
Kurama clan
 that they are trapped in a
genjutsu
 that has enveloped the entire town, and Naruto, Sakura, and Team 8 manage to escape with the help of
Unkai
. They then enter the mansion to find Kurenai and Yakumo.
