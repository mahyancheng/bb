Personality

Hikaru is a calm person and he seems to normally forget things that he was supposed to mention to others a lot.
 Hikaru is also referred to as being a show-off and is often blamed for being showy. Despite this, he is a hard worker.

Appearance

Hikaru has brown spiky hair, a small goatee, and dark eyes. Hikaru wears the standard Kumogakure shinobi outfit inclusive of
flak jacket
 and
forehead protector
, with a black haori draped over his shoulder which has wave-like patterns on the edge. He also has his right eye closed constantly. Whether or not this is due to an injury or simply a habit is unknown.
