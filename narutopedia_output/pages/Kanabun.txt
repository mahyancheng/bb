Personality

Kanabun is a person who, even in a small turmoil that took place in a small town, will not miss the chance of obtaining benefits and sent his people to take bets on the fight between
Tsukado
 and
Katazu
. He also showed an aggressive side, refusing to acknowledge a draw in battle and directing his men to kill those for whom he lost money. After a small fight, Kanabun and his men fled in fear, promising revenge.

Appearance

Kanabun has short spiky grey hair and small black eyes. He wears a purple kimono with a belt over which he wears a robe that has a green top and bottom, separated by a red wavy pattern, and a dirty yellow in the middle. The border of the sleeves of the robe is also red. Apart from this, Kanabun wears brown pants and a pair of sandals.
