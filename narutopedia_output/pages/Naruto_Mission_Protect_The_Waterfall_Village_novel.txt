Trivia

The whole light novel book has an entirely different story, compared to the anime OVA adaption. Examples are:

Team Suien (including Suien himself) was revealed to have a large variety of different moves rather than
the single one they used

in the anime OVA adaption
.

Senji
, an extra character who supports, helps and protects Shibuki and his villagers.

Four summoning animals were being used in the light novel adaptation,
three

giant

fishes
 and
Pakkun
.

Senji fighting against Suien instead of Shibuki.

Suien's
three

Amegakure

partners
 are revealed to be alive (and captured by Kakashi) at the end of the light novel adaptation.

Team Suien having faced off against Kakashi until they had been interrupted by Konenmaru.

Kakashi's
flak jacket
 was beige in colour.

Himatsu
 and
Shizuku
's mother was attacked later rather than earlier in the story.

Murasame
's black sleeveless shirt was ripped in the light novel adaption, but did not happen in the anime's OVA adaption.

Shibuki fights against Suien at the near end of the light novel adaption using summoning animals to fight his own.

Suien inflates with overwhelming chakra from overusing the Hero Water, and deflates like a balloon, flying into the air, and falls down the waterfall.

Kakashi meets Suien, who is weakened from overuse of the Hero Water.
