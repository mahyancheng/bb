Gandō
 (
ガンドウ
) was a member of the
Shirogane clan
, who appeared in
Naruto: Uzumaki Chronicles 2
.

Personality

Gandō wanted his clan to be completely revived and he would gladly kill himself if it meant getting closer to his goal.

Appearance

Gandō was the tall and muscular man with black braided hair tied up into a ponytail. He had red marks on the eyes. As with the rest of the Shirogane Three, he wore a purple onmyoji–like outfit that has the Shirogane clan symbol at the front, holding it together was a dark red sash wrapped up around his
onmyōji
–like outfit, two scrolls carried on his lower back, one red at the bottom and one blue at the top, and covering his thighs and wore fade purple pants tucked inside bandages on his lower legs with shinobi sandals and even a dark blue tassel attached to his left forearm.

Abilities

Gandō, like other members of his clan, was proficient in the use of puppets.
