The Great Naruto Bridge.



The
Great Naruto Bridge
 (
なると大橋
,
Naruto Ōhashi
) is a long and wide bridge that was built and completed in
Prologue — Land of Waves
 by
Tazuna
. He built the bridge to connect the
Land of Waves
 to the
Land of Fire
, thus freeing his country from
Gatō
's trade monopoly. After
Team 7
 helped him complete the bridge by fending off Gatō's assassins, he named the bridge after
Naruto Uzumaki
 for he had played a major part of the reason that the bridge was completed.

Trivia

The bridge is frequently a selectable battle stage in Part I video games. It also appears in Part II video games such as the
Ultimate Ninja Storm
 series.

There is an actual bridge named the
Great Naruto Bridge
 (
大鳴門橋
,
Ōnaruto-kyō
). It is located between the cities of
Naruto
 and
Minamiawaji
 and crosses the
Naruto strait
, famous for its
whirlpools
 (Uzumaki (
渦巻き
) is a Japanese word for whirlpool).
