The
Yotsuki Clan
 (
夜月一族
,
Yotsuki Ichizoku
) is a clan from
Kumogakure
. During the
First Raikage
's reign, they were given land in the
Valley of Clouds and Lightning
 after swearing their loyalty to the village.

In
Taka
's attempt to capture
Killer B
, they confront
Kiyoi Yotsuki
. When he proved unwilling to sell out his allies,
Sasuke
 used
genjutsu
 to make him reveal Killer B's location and identifying features. According to Kiyoi, the Yotsuki will never willingly sell out their comrades.

Trivia

"Yotsuki" (
夜月
) means "night moon".
