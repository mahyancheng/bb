Aoneko
 (
アオネコ
) is a recruitable character in
Naruto: Ultimate Ninja Online
.

Appearance

Aoneko is a human-sized blue and white cat with a yellow vest and a necklace consisting of multiple green beads. He carries a card with a green circle on it.

Plot Overview

Aoneko is the proprietor of the Cat Quiz and is notably affiliated with
Konohagakure
.

Trivia

"Aoneko" means "blue cat" or "green cat".
