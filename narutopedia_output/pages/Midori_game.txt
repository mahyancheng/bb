Personality

Midori has been a huge fan of
Naruto Uzumaki
 ever since she saw his match at the
Chūnin Exams
. Ever since she became a genin, she has been wanting to one day go on a mission with Naruto.

Plot Overview

She first appears when Naruto meets
Sakura Haruno
 and Midori, who live next door to each other, where Midori wants to observe Naruto and Sakura in a sparring match as she did not have a whole lot of experience. She appears again to observe Naruto when he helps
Chōji Akimichi
 and
Ino Yamanaka
 train.

Her final appearance comes in place where Sakura couldn't find Midori as she's vanished and requests Naruto for help.
Kiba Inuzuka
 comes along to aid Naruto in search of Midori. Naruto catches up with Midori and she tells Naruto that she and her family will be leaving Konoha due to her father taking on a long-term mission which requires her to move out for a while. Both Naruto and Midori promises that she'll come back a stronger ninja to aid Naruto on missions in the future.
