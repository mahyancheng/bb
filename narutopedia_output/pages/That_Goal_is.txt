Summary

Fearful that
the ninja
 will kill them all,
Sasuke
 offers to give the ninja their scroll in exchange for their lives.
Naruto
 attacks Sasuke and takes the scroll from him, believing he is an impostor since the real Sasuke would never give up so easily. Even if he is the real Sasuke, Naruto points out that there's no guarantee the ninja would spare them. The ninja agrees,
summoning
 a
snake
 to attack Naruto. As Naruto is batted around Sasuke pleas for Naruto to give up. Naruto, using the strength of the
Nine-Tailed Demon Fox
, quickly defeats the snake as it turns its attention towards Sasuke. Naruto mockingly asks if Sasuke has been hurt.
