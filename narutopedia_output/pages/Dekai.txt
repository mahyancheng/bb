Background

Dekai was put into Team 6, consisting of future well known figures,
Jiraiya
 and
Minato Namikaze
. During the
Third Shinobi World War
, Dekai and his team confronted the
Iwagakure

jinchūriki
,
Rōshi
 and
Han
. Thanks to Minato, the team was able to safely
teleport
 away from their
Tailed Beast Balls
. After the successful escape, Dekai had thought he had gone to the afterlife. Later, after the team returned to Konoha, Dekai informed Minato that the team needed to report their mission to the
Third Hokage
.

Personality

Dekai doesn't seem to like being late, as he asks Minato not to delay their team with a report to the
Hokage
.

Appearance

Dekai is a large, broad-shouldered guy with dark skin, black eyes, and short blond hair. He wears the standard Konoha shinobi outfit, which includes a green vest and forehead band. His arms are protected by special armour.

As a child, he wore a dark kimono with yellow trim.

Trivia






Dekai's appearance as a child with Team 6 in the anime.



In his first appearance in the manga, Dekai was depicted with white hair and light coloured skin. In the anime, he had dark hair and dark skin. In his second appearance in the manga one-shot,
Naruto Gaiden: Whirlwind in the Vortex
