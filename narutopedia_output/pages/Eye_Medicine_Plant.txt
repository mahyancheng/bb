The
Eye Medicine Plant
 (
目薬草
,
Megusurisō
) is a rare medicinal herb that can be found in the
Valley of Judgement
 and is noted to be great for eye-strain. According to
Kakashi
,
Orochimaru
 was rumoured to have an interest in the Eye Medicine Plant, as it would further progress his research in
dōjutsu
.
