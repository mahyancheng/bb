BAKU
, performed by
Ikimonogakari
, is the eighth opening of
Boruto: Naruto Next Generations
. It ran from
episode 181
 to
205
. It replaced
Starting and Growing
 and was replaced by
Reckless
 in
episode 206
.

Trivia

Like the
previous opening
, this opening animation pays homages to some animations of Blue Bird,
Sign
, and
LINE
.

This is the fourth opening to feature footage from the anime, the others being "
Wind and Waves Satellite
", "
Silhouette
" and "LINE".

This is the fourth opening that has the series logo placed near the end of the opening, the others being "
A World That Was Transparent
", "
Diver
", and "
Assault Rock
".

The
jutsu
 that Boruto and Kawaki use against Jigen in the opening animation would later be added in
Naruto x Boruto: Ninja Voltage
.
