This is a giant drum, specifically an
ōdaiko
, easily more than
Tsunade
's height in diameter, used to control
Konohagakure
's
Mechanical Eight Trigrams Formation
 technique. When used, the Ōdaiko is kept on a horizontal stand on top of the Hokage Tower. The user bangs on the ōdaiko to activate the technique in order to trap the enemy in a maze of walls. Each subsequent beat will activate or deactivate more walls.
