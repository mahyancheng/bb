Mitate
 (
ミタテ
) is a
medical-nin
 of
Konohagakure
.

Appearance

He wears the standard high collared, full body uniform of the Konoha medical ninja. He also has dark-coloured eyes.

Abilities

As a
medical-nin
, Mitate has basic knowledge of medical treatments and ninjutsu, as well as good chakra control in general. His experience allows him to immediately recognise a fatal injury.
 In the anime, he also showed the ability to use
genjutsu
 to
restore forgotten memories
.
