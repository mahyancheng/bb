Mode
 (
モード
,
Mōdo
) could refer to one of heightened states used by many shinobi.

Baryon Mode
, the final form of Kurama's chakra used by Naruto Uzumaki.

Butterfly Mode
, a heightened transformation unique to the Akimichi clan.

Tailed Beast Mode
, a heightened state of the jinchūriki.

Lava Release Chakra Mode
,  a heightened state of Rōshi after manifesting one of Son Gokū's tails.

Lightning Release Chakra Mode
, a heightened state used by the Third and Fourth Raikage.

Nine-Tails Chakra Mode
, a heightened transformation unique to jinchūriki with Kurama's chakra sealed within them.

Sage Mode
, a heightened transformation using natural energy along with a shinobi's normal chakra to drastically empower all of their abilities.

Six Paths Sage Mode
, a heightened state of Sage Mode, which drastically empowers the user's abilities to a far greater extent by utilising the Six Paths Sage Chakra.

Slithering Snake Mode
, a heightened state used by Orochimaru.

Tenseigan Chakra Mode
, a heightened transformation unique to those who possess the Tenseigan.

List of Non-Canon Modes

Medical Mode
, a heightened state of a medical-nin. (Game only)

Sannin Mode
, a heightened transformation of the Sannin. (Game only)

Tsukuyomi Mode
, a heightened state of Tsukuyomi used by Itachi Uchiha. (Game only)
