Jakō Ryūdōin
 (
龍堂院邪光
,
Ryūdōin Jakō
) is one of the
Three Ryūdōin Brothers
, alongside
Rokkaku
 and
Genshō
, who were seeking revenge against
Might Guy
 for their father's humiliation and death.

Appearance

Jakō has small black eyes with a dark outline. He wears a purple cloth that covers his entire body and carries a giant scroll that he uses to activate his technique. He also wears white bandages to cover up most of his face.

Abilities

Jakō is able to use the
Wooden Puppet Manipulation Technique
, which allows him to manipulate several wooden figures to attack enemies when they come too close. He can also break apart these wooden figures to create a temple to trap enemies.

Part I

Third Great Beast Arc

He and his brothers seek revenge on
Might Guy
 for giving their father major injuries that eventually caused his death.  After decieving Guy, he and his brothers trap Guy in a wooden temple that they manipulate with their chakra to try to kill him. Afterwards, he and his brothers used wooden figures to fight
Neji
,
Tenten
,
Naruto
 and
Lee
 who show up to try and stop them. Despite his and his brothers' actions, their plan ultimately failed, but not before having an of Guy and how their father respected him and wanted them to fight him too fairly.
