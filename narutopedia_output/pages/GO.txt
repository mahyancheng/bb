GO!!!
, performed by
FLOW
, is the fourth opening for
Part I
 of the
Naruto
 series. The song ran from episode
78
 to
103
 after it replaced
Turning Sadness Into Kindness
 in the Japanese version and
Far Away
 in the English version. It was then replaced by
Rhapsody of Youth
 in episode
104
.
