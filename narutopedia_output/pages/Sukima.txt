Appearance

Sukima has long brown hair that hangs over to the right side of his face. He wears a green, high-collared shirt and matching pants along with sandals and bandages strapped to his left leg.

Trivia

"Sukima" (
隙間
) means "gap" or "crevice", where wind, water or light might come through. It can also mean "unpreparedness" or "carelessness", which could allude to him being the first to be caught cheating five times.
