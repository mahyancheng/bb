Team Tobirama
 (
扉間班
,
Tobirama-han
) was a team led by the future Second
Hokage
,
Tobirama Senju
. It was comprised of himself,
Hiruzen Sarutobi
,
Homura Mitokado
, and
Koharu Utatane
. Very little is known of the team, but on occasion during the
First Shinobi World War
, they teamed up with
Danzō Shimura
,
Kagami Uchiha
 and
Torifu Akimichi
 to carry out a mission as the Second Hokage's elite
Escort Unit
.
