Hinoki
 (
ヒノキ
) is the
village head
 of the Valley of Great Evil and the grandfather of
Nazuna
 in
Naruto: Path of the Ninja 2
.

Appearance

Hinoki is an elderly man with creases under his eyes and prominent cheekbones. He had white hair similar to cat ears, pointy eyebrows that reached to his chin, a long goatee and a short moustache. He wears a black kimono, with a red haori covering his kimono, a comb on his hair and red traditional Japanese geta.

Trivia

Hinoki's name is derived from the cypress tree native to Japan, the
Chamaecyparis obtusa
.
