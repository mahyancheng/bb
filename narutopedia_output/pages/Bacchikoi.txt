Bacchikoi!!!
 (
バッチコイ!!!
,
literally meaning:
 Bring it on!!!), performed by Dev Parade, was the eighth ending of
Naruto: Shippūden
. The ending replaced
Long Kiss Goodbye
 and was shown from episode
91
 to
102
 and it was replaced by
Deep Breath
 on episode
103
.
