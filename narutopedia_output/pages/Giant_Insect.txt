This giant insect is the personal summon of
Shino Aburame
.

Appearance

It has a red hide, yellow midsection, and black head large enough for the summoner to sit atop.

Abilities

It is a powerful flyer, able to handle full submergence into water and easily return to the sky while carrying three passengers.

New Era

Academy Arc

When
Boruto Uzumaki
 and
Mitsuki
 began drowning after saving Shino from his
possession
, Shino summoned this creature. It swiftly emerged from the water with its passengers and delivered them safely to the ground.

Trivia

It bears a strong resemblance to the
new species of insect
 that Shino dreamed of discovering while under the
Infinite Tsukuyomi
.
