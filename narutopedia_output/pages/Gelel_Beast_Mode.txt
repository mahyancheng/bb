Using more of the energy of the
Stone of Gelel
, the user takes on a monstrous transformation, further enhancing their strength and abilities. These enchancements also enables those who were not trained in combat to fight experienced opponents with ease.

Forms

The transformation is different for each user, providing a variety of different abilities:

Fugai's werewolf transformation; allows her to produce powerful soundwaves.
Kamira's bat transformation; enables her to produce illusions and smokescreens.
Ranke's ape transformation; further enhances her strength and manipulation of plasma.
Haido's transformation; grants him a variety of abilities, among them being superhuman strength, speed and enhanced healing.
