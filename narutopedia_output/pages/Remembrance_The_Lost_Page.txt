"
Remembrance: The Lost Page
" (
記憶　失われた頁
,
Kioku Ushinawareta Pēji
) is episode 169 of the original
Naruto
 anime.

Synopsis

Anko Mitarashi
 is dispatched to the
Land of the Sea
 with
Naruto
,
Shino
, and
Ino
 to investigate the nature of the "
Kaima
" monster. Upon arriving, Anko recalls her time with
Orochimaru
 in this area, and her
cursed seal
 begins to resonate and cause her pain. At the docks, Naruto sees
Yoroi Akadō
 and
Misumi Tsurugi
 accosting a
young girl
. He chases them away, but does not recognise them. When the group sets off to one of the islands, Yoroi and Misumi attack, and Naruto nearly drowns until he is saved by the girl he saw earlier.
