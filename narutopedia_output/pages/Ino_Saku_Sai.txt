Ino–Saku–Sai
 is a highly specialised team from
Konoha
, formed by the
Sixth Hokage
, under
Ino Yamanaka
's self-appointed leadership.

Known Missions

Investigate the Security Incidents

Assigned to:
 Sai

Rank:
 Unknown

Status:
 Success

After a few minor security incidents involving Konoha higher-ups takes place right before a budget meeting where
Anbu
's budget is to be cut in favour of other projects, Sixth
Hokage
,
Kakashi Hatake
 quickly realises they are inside jobs involving an Anbu executive. With this, Kakashi tasks Sai with investigating who in Anbu is behind them.

Investigate Sasuke Incidents

Assigned to:
 Ino Yamanaka, Sakura Haruno

Rank:
 Unknown

Status:
 Success

With reports of
Sasuke Uchiha
 terrorising Hidden Villages, Kakashi assigns Sakura and Ino to look into said incidents to determine if he is behind said events on their request.

Investigate
Kido Tsumiki

Assigned to:
 Ino Yamanaka, Sakura Haruno, Sai

Rank:
 Unknown

Status:
 Success

With numerous incidents of Sasuke not matching up to his actual location and reports of Kido collecting Sasuke's DNA, Kakashi has Sakura, Ino, and Sai team-up to investigate Kido as all signs are pointing to him.

Rescue Sakura

Assigned to:
 Ino Yamanaka, Sai

Rank:
 Unknown

Status:
 Success

Realising Sakura was missing, Ino and Sai immediately surmise that Kido kidnapped Sakura in hopes of drawing Sasuke out. With no other aid, Kakashi assigned Ino and Sai to locate Sakura.

Arrest Kido and
Magire

Assigned to:
 Ino Yamanaka, Sakura Haruno, and Sai with assistance from
Naruto Uzumaki
 and
Hinata Hyūga

Rank:
 Unknown

Status:
 Success

With Kido's numerous criminal acts including kidnapping, domestic terrorism, and international terrorism brought to light, Kakashi assigns the Ino-Saku-Sai (who are in the process of rescuing Sakura) to arrest them with aid from other Konoha shinobi.

Trivia

The team's name is a play-off of Ino's ancestral team name, the
Ino–Shika–Chō
.
