Muta with one of his shōkaichū.



The
shōkaichū
 (
消壊虫
,
literally meaning:
 Neutralising Destruction Insect)
 are one of several species of insect used by members of the
Aburame clan
. They resemble
worms
. They can dig into the ground and locate chakra signatures that they then report to their respective Aburame.

Muta Aburame
 was known to
store
 these particular insects within his body.
