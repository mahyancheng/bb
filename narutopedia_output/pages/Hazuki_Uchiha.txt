Hazuki Uchiha
 (
うちはハヅキ
,
Uchiha Hazuki
) was a member of
Konohagakure
's
Uchiha clan
.

Background

About two years younger than
Fugaku
, Hazuki left the clan when she married her husband - a non-Uchiha - and with him had a daughter,
Izumi
. After her husband died defending their daughter during the
Nine-Tailed Demon Fox's Attack
, she returned to the Uchiha's compound with Izumi. She was the second to die during the
Uchiha Clan Downfall
, killed by
Itachi Uchiha
 shortly after Izumi.
