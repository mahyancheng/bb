Trivia

This song has the least amount of lyrics of all the
Naruto
 songs.

"Tsuyosa" (
強さ
) means power.

This opening was also used in France for the broadcast of the series on the public channel France 3. The series was renamed for the occasion "Phénomène Naruto".

When
Naruto
 started airing on Toonami again in 2024, this opening was no longer used in favor of a shortened version of ROCKS.
