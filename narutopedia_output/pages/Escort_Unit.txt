The
Escort Unit
 (
護衛部隊
,
Goei Butai
) was an elite team under the leadership of the Second
Hokage
—
Tobirama Senju
. Along with the members of
Team Tobirama
:
Hiruzen Sarutobi
,
Koharu Utatane
 and
Homura Mitokado
, the unit included
Danzō Shimura
,
Kagami Uchiha
, and
Torifu Akimichi
.

On one occasion during the
First Shinobi World War
, while being pursued by a
team
 of twenty
Kumogakure
 ninja, Tobirama sacrificed his life to give the others a chance to escape. Before leaving, however, he appointed Hiruzen as the Third Hokage.
