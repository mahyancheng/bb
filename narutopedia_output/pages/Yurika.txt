Yurika
 (
ユリカ
) is a
Konohagakure

kunoichi
 and a member of the
Konoha Cryptology Team
.

Appearance

She has short, straight brown hair and eyes of the same colour. She wears a shade of pink lipstick along with a short dress with the kanji for "number" (
号
,
gō
), which itself comes from the word "cypher" (
暗号
,
angō
) on the back of it, a white belt around her waist and a pair of low-heeled sandals.

Abilities

Yurika is a highly skilled decoder and has been called the best in Konohagakure.

Part II

Kazekage Rescue Mission

After being called to the
Konoha Aviary
 by
Natori
, she decodes the message from
Sunagakure
 that reports of
Gaara
's kidnapping by
Akatsuki
. In the manga, Yurika told
Tsunade
 herself about Gaara's kidnapping, while in the anime, she handed her the message to read instead.

Trivia

Her name is one of many ways to write
"eureka"
, an exclamation indicating sudden discovery, in Japanese.
