This is the mode in which Mecha-Naruto takes the form of a vehicle. While in this mode, its head could produce multiple blades which can then easily slice through large trees. It could also launch these blades as a shuriken. In this mode, it could also produce various weapons, such as
large mallets
 and drills to combat its opponents.

Trivia

Though unnamed in the anime, this technique was named in
Naruto Mobile
.
