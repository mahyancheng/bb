Sumashi
 (
スマシ
) was a
tokubetsu jōnin
 of
Iwagakure
.

Background

He and his group attacked
Team Minato
 during the
Third Great Shinobi War
 after they broke into
Iwagakure
 territory. Congratulating the young
shinobi
 for achieving this feat, he then attacked Kakashi after he and his teammate,
Rin Nohara
 had managed to escape the landscape that had killed their team member,
Obito Uchiha
. Sumashi was; however, presumably killed after
Minato Namikaze

teleported
 to his team's side after being alerted that the
kunai
 he gave Kakashi was thrown.

Personality

Sumashi has been described as being confident.

Appearance

Sumashi had a short crop of black hair, small dark eyes, and thick eyebrows. He wore the standard attire of the Iwagakure shinobi complete with a
forehead protector
 which he wore like a bandanna,
flak jacket
, and the single left-sleeved shirt with a lapel running down the right side of his waist and sandals.

Abilities

Though little was known of his skills, he was proficient with
Earth Release
 techniques witnessed by his use of the
Earth Release: Tearing Earth Turning Palm
 in an attempt to bury the members of Team Minato alive.

Trivia

Sumashi is the only introduced tokubetsu jōnin in the series who is not from
Konohagakure
.
