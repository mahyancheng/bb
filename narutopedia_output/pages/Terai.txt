Background

As a member of Root, Terai was likely trained to lose emotions in order to better accomplish missions. As Root members each uses a codename, it is unknown what Terai's real name is, if he even has one. Even though the organisation has been officially disbanded, Terai remains loyal to its head,
Danzō Shimura
.

Personality

Terai is a very loyal shinobi that follows any and all orders given to him by Danzō.

Appearance

Terai wears a light-coloured hooded jacket and an Anbu mask on his face. The mask has three eyebrow-like markings above each eye hole and a red cat-like nose. He also wears a pair of black gloves.

Fū Yamanaka

Torune

 the former being considered the strongest member of Root.
