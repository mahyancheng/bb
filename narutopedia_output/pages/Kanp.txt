Kanpō
 (
カンポウ
) is a
medical-nin
 of
Konohagakure
.

Appearance

He wears the standard high-collared, full body uniform of the Konoha medical ninja. His uniform has also the
Medic Corps
 sign on the left side of his chest.

Abilities

As a medical-nin, Kanpō presumably has a high level of intellect, is well-versed in
medical ninjutsu
, and has excellent
chakra control
 to perform medical procedures.

Neji Hyūga
 after his match with
Naruto Uzumaki
 in the final part of the
Chūnin Exams
. He was surprised when
Hiashi Hyūga
 showed up to speak with Neji, and exited the room leaving the two alone at his request.

.
