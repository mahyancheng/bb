This technique creates a stream of fire that can be manipulated into several forms (so far, rings of fire that run on the ground or a circle of fire flying through the air) before striking the target. It can also be used in combination with
Wind Release: Divine Wind
 to create a tornado of flames, or with
Wind Release: Divine Mountain Wind
 to release a massive inferno, the latter which is made even more powerful when used in an enclosed space created by
Earth Release: Earth Corridor
.
