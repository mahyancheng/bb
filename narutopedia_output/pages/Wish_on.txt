Wish on
, performed by "
Longman
", is the eleventh ending of the
Boruto: Naruto Next Generations
 anime. It started on episode
127
, replacing
The Incomplete Lights
, and ended on episode
138
, replaced by
Fireworks
 after that.

Trivia

This ending theme is fully based on a Japanese card game,
Hanafuda
. Coincidentally, at the end of this ending reveals three cards of
Team 10
 excluding Moegi.
