Chōseki
 (
チョウセキ
) is a former shinobi who settled down in
Tanzaku Quarters
 with his partner,
Gantetsu
.

Background

Chōseki left his profession as a shinobi, eventually settled down in Tanzaku Quarters to seek a more luxurious life, with his fellow fallen ninja Gantetsu. In the anime, Chōseki became a yakuza.

Personality

He does whatever he wants and picks fights with everyone, relying on
Gantetsu
 to protect him.

Appearance

Chōseki is a middle-aged man with thick and burly features,  he also has a bald head and dark-coloured eyes. He wore a black suit with a white dress shirt underneath it. He wore a black tie around his neck on his dress shirt which he kept tucked into his suit.

Part I

Search for Tsunade

While staying in Tanzaku Quarters, he and Gantetsu happened upon
Naruto Uzumaki
 and
Jiraiya
 due to Naruto dirtying Gantetsu's clothes and refusing to pay for it. However, they were swiftly defeated by Jiraiya's
Rasengan
 when they attempted to intimidate Naruto.
