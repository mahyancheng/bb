Kamakiri Ikeno
 (
池乃カマキリ
,
Ikeno Kamakiri
) is a shinobi from
Kirigakure
.

Appearance

Kamakiri is a young man with long, white hair, with a small ponytail tied at the end, and bandages that cover the lower half of his face. His outfit consists of a purple kimono, with waves at the end of its long sleeves, and a red belt, tied around his waist. Kamakiri carries his weapons strapped on his back.

Abilities

Not much is known of Kamakiri's abilities other than that he fought using two
kama
 that resembled regular kunai in size, just more twisted in terms of shape.
