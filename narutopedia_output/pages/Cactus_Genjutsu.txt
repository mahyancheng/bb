Using a nearby
cactus
' pollen, the user places anyone who comes in contact with it under a strong genjutsu that is hardly noticeable due to the technique's inconspicuous execution. In this genjutsu, the victim has the impression to cross an area over and over again while in reality, it does not move at all.

The technique can be countered by marking an area within the genjutsu, so that the never-ending circle can be noticed. Once noticed, the technique can be easily dispelled.
