This tool, similar to the
Shinobi Gauntlet
, does not require chakra from the wielder, and instead uses stored
Fire Release
 ninjutsu within the tool as ammunition. The rotating barrels release fireballs rapidly, causing heavy widespread damage.

Trivia

While not named in the manga and anime, it was named in
Naruto to Boruto: Shinobi Striker
.

The name of this tool appears to feature wordplay. The kanji for it give a pronunciation similar to type of gun it's modelled after, with the first kanji having a similar phoneme, referencing its ammunition.
