"
Last Chance…!!
" (
ラストチャンス…!!
,
Rasuto Chansu…!!
) is chapter 60 of the original
Naruto
 manga.

Summary

Four days after the second phase of the
Chūnin Exams
 began,
Team 7
 has recovered from their battles with
Orochimaru
 and
Team Dosu
. With the phase almost over and the team still in need of a second
scroll
,
Naruto
 considers opening the scroll they already have so that they can forge a second. He is stopped by
Kabuto
, who, having taken part in the exams several times before, warns that anyone that opens the scroll before the second phase is over is rendered unconscious. Naruto thanks him for intervening. Having been separated from his
team
, Kabuto begins to leave to go and find them when
Sasuke
 asks to fight him.

Trivia

The results for the first of the
Naruto Character Popularity Polls
 were included with this chapter.
