Kneading his chakra and then converting it to fire, the user creates a multitude of fireballs at the same time which then rain down on the target causing severe, widespread damage around the field. These fireballs can easily destroy
Earth Release
-created shelters and even overpower
Water Release
 attacks.

Trivia

This technique was first given a name in
Naruto: Ultimate Ninja 3
. In the game, Sasuke jumps in the air and shoots three giant bullets of fire that come down like giant meteors, then erupt into a geyser of flames. This name was much later used in the anime.
