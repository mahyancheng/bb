Otokaze
 (
オトカゼ
) is a
jōnin
 of
Sunagakure
.

Appearance

He has spiky brown hair and his eyes take to squinting. He wears the standard attire of the Suna-nin inclusive of
flak jacket
 and
forehead protector
.

Part I

Search for Tsunade

Otokaze was the first to find the
Fourth Kazekage
's body after the failed
Konoha Crush
. He alerted his fellow shinobi that had been scouring the
Land of Wind
 in search of the Kazekage using his whistle.

Trivia

"Otokaze"
 can mean "sounded wind".

Oto means "sound" and Kaze means "wind" which means his name could be a reference to how Otogakure and Sunagakure teamed up to take down Konoha.

He collects whistles, which he uses to gather his allies in case of emergency.
