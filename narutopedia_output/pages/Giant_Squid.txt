Appearance

The giant squid had big black pupils and the outside of its eyes were a dark yellow colour. The rest of its body resembled a giant squid and its body was a pinkish colour.

Abilities

The giant squid could use its long huge tentacles to grab, hold and restrain its opponents. It was capable of restraining highly skilled shinobi with ease.
