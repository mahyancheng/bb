Komaza
 (
コマザ
) is a
genin
 from
Sunagakure
.

Personality

Komaza was angry when he got caught cheating five times during the Chūnin Exams testing and he angrily questioned the chūnin that were tasked with watching the genin take their test.

Appearance

Komaza has short, spiky brown hair and dark eyes. He wears a light-green shirt with the sleeves rolled up, brown pants and a bracelet of sorts around his left hand.
