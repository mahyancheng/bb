This lizard was a messenger from
Kumogakure
.

Appearance

It was small and looked like a normal lizard. It had light-blue skin and yellow eyes. It wore a vest to carry scrolls.

Abilities

It had displayed the ability to
control its chakra and walk on water
, similar to real-life
basilisk lizards
.

Part II

Pain's Assault

It was used by
J
 to deliver messages to his fellow Kumogakure ninja trailing team
Taka
. Before they were able to accomplish this, however, it was stabbed through the back by
Sasuke
 before dispatching J, ceasing transmission and prompting the trailing team to retrieve him.
