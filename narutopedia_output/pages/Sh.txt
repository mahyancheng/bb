Shū
 (
シュウ
) belongs to the owner of a motel where
Naruto
 and
Jiraiya
 stay during their search for
Tsunade
.

Appearance

Shū is a small cat with white fur and light-coloured eyes. He has a brown tail and a circular-shaped brown fur marking right above his right eye.

Part I

Search for Tsunade

When Naruto sees him playing with a water balloon, he gets the idea to use two hands to create the spinning motion for the
Rasengan
.

Trivia

Masashi Kishimoto
 may have named this cat after his own cat, Shu.
