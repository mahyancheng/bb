This fighting style relies on
Jirōbō
's brute strength to carry out showy and violent physical attacks. By taking careful control of his weight and making complicated body movements, Jirōbō is able to use his entire body to break and crush targets. Jirōbō's naturally "monstrous" strength is drastically increased when he activates his
cursed seal
, to the point that, in its second level, he is able to throw
Chōji Akimichi
's
Super Multi-Size Technique
.
