Chiyomatsu
 (
チヨマツ
) is a
samurai
 of the
Land of Iron
.

Personality

Chiyomatsu holds some admiration for shinobi.

Appearance

Chiyomatsu wears the standard samurai attire consisting of a helmet and face mask which completely obscures the face and grey samurai armour. He also carries two sets of swords with him strapped to his leg armour.

Abilities

As a samurai, Chiyomatsu wields a
sword
 and is skilled in its use, preferring to catch his enemies off guard with a single strike from above.

Part II

Five Kage Summit






Chiyomatsu falls for Sasuke's trap.



During the
Five Kage Summit
, Chiyomatsu and several other samurai are mobilised to look for the infiltrating
Sasuke Uchiha
. During the search, he leaves his group to investigate behind a pillar. Seeing a silhouette, Chiyomatsu rushes forward to strike but merely discovered a cloak, which was a lure to draw him away from his group. While still being bewildered and believing the team to have already left, Chiyomatsu is knocked out from behind by Sasuke's surprise attack.
