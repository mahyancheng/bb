The user generates one or multiple lion-like
sand
 creatures to attack or subdue a target. They appear to be easily dispersed if struck hard enough.

Trivia

The name of this technique refers to
Shisa
, a traditional Ryukyuan decoration, usually in pairs that resemble a cross between a lion and a dog in
Okinawan mythology
.
