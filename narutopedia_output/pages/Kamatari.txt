Kamatari
 (
カマタリ
) is the personal summon of
Temari
.

Appearance

Kamatari is a giant, white-furred weasel with a dark green eye-patch covering his left eye. He carries a large sickle that is almost as long as himself.

Abilities

He was summoned using
Temari
's
Giant Folding Fan
 as a medium through the
Summoning: Quick Beheading Dance
, after which he slices down everything in his path with enough force to decimate an entire forest. After he starts moving, he can't be seen by the unaided human eye. He was summoned holding a giant
Kama
 with other smaller sickles around him and something that resembles a Chinese lantern with the kanji for scythe (
鎌
,
kama
) on it. In the manga, Kamatari gave a battle cry before attacking.
