Zaji
 (
ザジ
,
Zaji
) is a gambler from the
Land of Fire
.

Background

During one of
Tsunade
's visits to their den, Zaji and his cohorts immediately recognised her as the Legendary Sucker and could hardly contain their euphoria. When she had gambled away all her money, he and the others thank her emphatically as she left.

Personality

Zaji enjoys gambling a great deal, especially when he wins. Upon seeing the Legendary Sucker walk into his gambling den, he could hardly contain himself at the prospect of making a small fortune off her.

Appearance

Zaji has a short crop of brown hair, dark eyes and a round nose. He wears a simple, black, loose-fitting kimono with white rimmings.
