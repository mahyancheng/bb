Trivia

This opening bears several similarities to the second opening,
Distance
, including Naruto and Sasuke walking towards each other in a blue background.

After Tsunade awakens from her comatose like state, she replaces Danzō as Hokage in the second version.

This song was also used in a
movie version
 opening of
Naruto: Shippūden
 anime.
