Yajirobee
 (
ヤジロベエ
) is a
chūnin
 of
Konohagakure
.

Personality

Yajirobee displayed outward contempt for
Naruto Uzumaki
 in lieu of the
Nine-Tailed Fox
 as many did in those days; voicing his desire for Naruto to be captured and punished for stealing the
Scroll of Seals
 from the
Hokage Residence
.

Appearance

His attire consists of a helmet, oversized glasses and the older version of the Konoha
flak jacket
 without the scroll pouches. Underneath this he wears a high-collared outfit. He also wears the village's
forehead protector
 underneath his helmet.

Abilities

Yajirobee was shown carrying a
tantō
 on his back, which alludes to him being a practitioner of
kenjutsu
.

Yajirobee was one of the many ninja who gathered at the start of the series to track down Naruto Uzumaki when he stole the Scroll of Seals from the
Hokage
's residence. He noted that they needed to find the young man before he accidentally released the power of the Nine-Tailed Demon Fox.

Trivia

"Yajirobee" (
弥次郎兵衛
) is the name of a traditional Japanese toy, which is, in its function, similar to a
seesaw
.
