Personality

She is a kindly woman, so much so that her eldest daughter, Hinata, aspired to be kind like her, while also strong like her father.

Appearance

She possesses fair skin and long, dark-purple hair slightly past her waist and side bangs framing her face down to her shoulders. She was seen wearing a purple, long-sleeved kimono. Overall, her eldest daughter, Hinata, bears a striking resemblance to her.
