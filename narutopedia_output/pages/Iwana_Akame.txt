Iwana Akame
 (
赤目イワナ

chūnin
 of
Konohagakure

Background

In the past, Iwana participated in a foreign espionage mission, where he was attacked and lost his left eye. In the anime, he and
Bekkō
 were seen alongside
Daikoku Funeno
 at the
Academy
, watching
Itachi Uchiha
 get attacked by bullies.

Personality

Iwana loathes
Naruto Uzumaki
 and thinks that he should be captured and punished for stealing the
Scroll of Seals
. In the anime, he showed himself as a teacher who worried about the students, who immediately rushed to separate the fighting.

Appearance

Iwana's attire is that of the standard Konoha shinobi complete with a
flak jacket
 and a
forehead protector
 which he wears like a bandanna. He also wears an eye-patch over his left eye.
