Naruto Retsuden novels.



Naruto Retsuden
 (
NARUTO -ナルト- 烈伝
,
literally meaning:
 Naruto Intense Story) is a
light novel
 series which was released from June to October 2019.
 Unlike previous series of novels, the three
Naruto Retsuden
 novels are all written by
Jun Esaka
, causing plot details to carryover between novels far more heavily.
