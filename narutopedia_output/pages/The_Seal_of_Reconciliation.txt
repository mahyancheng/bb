"
The Seal of Reconciliation
" (
和解の印
,
Wakai no In
,

 Unison Sign) is chapter 699 of the original
Naruto
 manga.

Summary

Naruto
 and
Sasuke
 both lie defeated on what appears to be the statues' hands forming a seal of reconciliation. Meanwhile,
Sakura
 and
Kakashi
 arrive at the
Valley of the End
 to witness Naruto and Sasuke both lying next to each other. Sakura begins to heal both Naruto and Sasuke. Then Sasuke apologises to a teary-eyed Sakura for what he has done to her. As the duo are both rejuvenated, they then release everyone from the
Infinite Tsukuyomi
.

Samui
 and
Atsui
 are also freed from the
Benihisago
 respectively, thanks to
Tenten
 and
Darui
. The
tailed beasts
 from Sasuke's
Chibaku Tensei
 are also freed. At
Konohagakure
, a memorial service is held for
Neji
.
Tsunade
 has also stepped down at some point, with Kakashi as her successor. For his role in ending the war and the Infinite Tsukuyomi, Kakashi, as
Hokage
, officially pardons Sasuke for his past crimes. Sasuke then bids farewell to Sakura and leaves Konoha. As Sasuke departs, he meets Naruto who hands him over his
forehead protector
 which he discarded during their battle in the Valley years ago.
