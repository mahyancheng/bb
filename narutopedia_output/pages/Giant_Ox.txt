This giant
ox
 was a summon of
Nagato
.

Appearance






The Giant Ox's appearance.



With an appearance similar to a normal ox, this summon has a number of
black receivers
 stuck on its head and legs, along with
Rinnegan
, implying it was being controlled by Pain. Its entire body was also covered in brown fur and it has black horns. It also has a small tail.

Abilities

This summon, by its sheer size, possessed a great deal of brute strength. Also, due to its Rinnegan eyes, it possessed a field of shared vision with
Nagato
.

Part II

Tale of Jiraiya the Gallant

It was first summoned during Pain's fight with
Jiraiya
, where it charged at Jiraiya, forcing him into some sewers. Once Jiraiya entered
Sage Mode
, however, he easily repelled it and sent it flying while its left horn was snapped off. After this Nagato desummoned it.

Pain's Assault

It was summoned again during Pain's attack on Konoha, where it was badly damaged by
Naruto
's
Sage Art: Big Ball Rasengan
 and subsequently killed by
Gamahiro
.
