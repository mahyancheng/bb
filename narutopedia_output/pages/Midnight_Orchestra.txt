Midnight Orchestra
 (
真夜中のオーケストラ
,
Mayonaka no Ōkesutora
), performed by
Aqua Timez
, is the sixteenth ending of
Naruto: Shippūden
. It replaced
U Can Do It!
. It also appeared in an
OVA
 which was given to the first 5000 people who bought a
Naruto
 shirt. It started in episode
193
 and ended in episode
205
, where it was replaced with
Freedom
.
