Terubō
 (
テルボウ
) was one of the orphans at the
Konoha Orphanage
.

Background

Terubō was seen with the other kids at the Konoha orphanage. He was present when
Nonō Yakushi
 was thinking of a name for
Kabuto
.

Personality

Terubō wasn't impressed by Nonō Yakushi's idea to name Kabuto after a helmet. He also likes clothing that resembles paper dolls.

Appearance

Terubō has long brown hair and his bangs frame his face. He also has dark-coloured eyes.

Trivia

Masashi Kishimoto
 named Terubō based on paper dolls.
