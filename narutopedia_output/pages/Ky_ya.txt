 (
キョウヤ
) is an accountant of
Kakuzu
, a member of the
Akatsuki

Personality

Kyōya seems to be a very obedient person and always listens to what his boss says.

Appearance

He has short, spiky light-brown hair and dark eyes. A scar runs across his forehead and another runs downwards to his cheek. He wears a simple green suit along with a pair of shoes.
