Ōtora
 (
大虎
) was a drunk villager who would always wander around
Sunagakure
 with a bottle in his hand.

Background

When he stumbled into
Gaara
, he began to insult him, only to soon realise who he was speaking to. When Gaara saw the fear in his eyes, he unintentionally killed Ōtora.

Personality

Ōtora appeared to be a rude person, who would readily insult others even over minor offences. He seemed to pay very little attention to things, like who he is yelling at. He was also a heavy drinker.

Appearance

Ōtora had short black hair and black-coloured eyes. He wore a beige-coloured hat on his head with parts of his hair only showing sticking out of the back. He also wore a purple-collared shirt.

Trivia

"Ōtora" means "big tiger" or "heavy drinker".
