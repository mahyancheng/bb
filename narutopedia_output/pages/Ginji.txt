Ginji
 (
ギンジ
) is the banker of
Kakuzu
, a member of the
Akatsuki
.

Background

As a banker, he managed Kakuzu's extensive wealth that was obtained through collecting the
bounties
 on ninja.

Appearance

Ginji has a crop of short, dull, black hair and dark eyes. He wears a simple blue suit along with a pair of shoes. He's also seen with a cigar in his mouth.
