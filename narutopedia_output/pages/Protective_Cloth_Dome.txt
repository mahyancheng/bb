Maki uses her cloth for defensive means by wrapping it around her and any nearby allies, she can form a protective barrier. The cloth can be made around a nearby object such as a rock, to better hold down the fortification. If the cloth is made wet by a
Water Release
 technique, it allows the dome to defend against powerful
heat-based attacks
.
