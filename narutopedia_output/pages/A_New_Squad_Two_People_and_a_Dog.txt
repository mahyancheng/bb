"
A New Squad! Two People and a Dog?!
" (
新生三人一組　二人と一匹!
,
Shin'sei Surīman Seru Futari to Ippiki!
) is episode 144 of the original
Naruto
 anime.

Synopsis

While
Naruto
 has trouble with the
Legendary Stupid Brothers
,
Iruka
 has his share of difficulties with
Mizuki
. Iruka struggles to try to get the old Mizuki back, but Mizuki explains the only reason he was ever nice to Iruka was because he wanted to be the
Hokage
's favourite too. He truly wanted Iruka to be miserable and be an outcast. Iruka and Mizuki then prepare to fight again. Meanwhile, after many
shadow clone
 attacks, Naruto finally has a chance to use
Rasengan
. The brothers accidentally destroy their lunch and make Naruto's attack hit his shadow clones. As they prepare to kill him,
Shikamaru
,
Chōji
, and
Ino
 come to the rescue.
