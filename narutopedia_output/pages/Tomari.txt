Background

In his youth, Tomari used to play with
Abiru
,
Tsuchino
, and
Kashike
 around the village. One time, while Tomari was playing football with his friends, they were visited by
Gaara
 who accidentally attacked them while they were trying to run away from him. However, Tomari and his horrified friends were saved by
Yashamaru
.
 Still during his time at the academy, Tomari and his friends threw rocks at
Shira
 since he couldn't use
ninjutsu
 and
genjutsu
.
 As time passed, he lost his fear towards Gaara.

Personality

Tomari was said to have a slow demeanour, but he was nevertheless quick to sense danger. Though he initially feared Gaara, he lost his fear over the years.
 In the anime, he is shown to discriminate others for their lack of skills.

Appearance

Tomari has black, spiky hair and dark-coloured eyes. He wore an insulated jacket, short pants and a pair of fingerless gloves.
