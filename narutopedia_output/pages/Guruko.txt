Guruko
 (
グルコ
) is a
ninken
 and a personal summon of
Kakashi Hatake
.

Background






Guruko and the other ninken are summoned to search Orochimaru.



In the anime, after they made a contract with Kakashi, Guruko and the other ninken aided him to search the abducted
Rin Nohara
 by the
Kirigakure
 shinobi and acted as a diversion in order to allow them to flee.
 They also helped searching for the fleeing
Orochimaru
.

Appearance

He has tan fur, a white snout, long ears that have a dark brown colour and noticeably more pronounced whiskers than the other ninken. He wears the standard outfit of Kakashi's ninken, which consists of a blue vest with the
heno-heno-moheji
 seal and a Konoha
forehead protector
 on his forehead.

Abilities






Guruko and his pack attacking Kiba.



Guruko possesses heightened senses with which he can use to track his target with ease. He has also demonstrated the ability to track his target underground through the use of the
Summoning: Earth Release: Tracking Fang Technique
 as well as hold a target down, allowing Kakashi to attack them freely. He also has has exceptional teamwork with the rest of his pack, able to develop and effectively use several offensive and defensive formations in battle which make use of the manoeuvrability and powerful limbs.
