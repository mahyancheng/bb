Shooting Star ~Shooting Star~
 (
流れ星 ~Shooting Star~
,
Nagareboshi ~Shooting Star~
), performed by
HOME MADE Kazoku
 (
HOME MADE 家族
,
"Home Made Family"
), was the first ending in the Japanese version of the
Naruto: Shippūden
 anime. It ran from Shippūden episodes
1
-
18
 and was replaced by
The Way ~ to you all
.
