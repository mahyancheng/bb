Gamatama
 (
ガマたま
) is a tadpole from
Mount Myōboku
.

Background

Due to not having the combat ability to yet form a contract with
shinobi
, Gamatama is entrusted as a name-registration clerk on Mount Myōboku.

Appearance

Gamatama has a very small body. He has black irides with yellow sclerae. Gamatama also has pink skin covering the upper half of his body while his underside is white. As a
tadpole
, he still has a tail.

Part II

Pain's Assault

Main article:
Pain's Assault

Gamatama was seen watching over the list of inhabitants of Mount Myōboku. When he noticed that
Kōsuke
 was no longer on the list of inhabitants, he surmised that he was deceased and proceeds to inform
Fukasaku
 about his demise.
