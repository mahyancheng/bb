Kurobachi
 (
クロバチ
) was a
shinobi
 from
Iwagakure
's
Kamizuru clan
.

Background

The Kamizuru clan fell from grace due to conflict with the
Aburame clan
 of
Konohagakure
 in the past. Like the Aburame clan, the Kamizuru focused on the utilisation of bugs in their techniques. They sought the legendary
bikōchū
 in the hopes it would help in the revitalisation of their clan. Kurobachi's grandfather, the
First Tsuchikage
, then hid away his forbidden secrets so they couldn't be used against the clan.

Personality

Kurobachi, like his brother and sister, hated the Aburame clan.

Appearance

Kurobachi had black eyes and black hair that connected into a beard and a moustache. He wore grey zip up pancho over a long black zip up covering that had beige-coloured outlining. He wore a pair of black baggy pants and red open-toed sandals.

Abilities

He has shown to be physically strong and could also use the
Hiding with Camouflage Technique
, which made him highly suitable for scouting missions.

Kamizuru Clan Techniques

As a member of the Kamizuru clan, Kurobachi's fighting style resolved around the use of bees. He could create a
clone out of wax
 and also use his bees to
shoot poisonous stingers
 at the target.
