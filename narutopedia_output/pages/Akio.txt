Akio
 (
アキオ
) is the younger brother of
Todoroki
.

Appearance

Akio has short brown hair and black-coloured eyes. Part of his bangs hang over a red-coloured thick headband that he wears around his head.

Abilities

Akio could handle a
spear
 in battle and has also a great sense of orientation, able to find his way through the
Forest of Bewilderment
 easily. He also has skill in
kenjutsu
, being able to cleanly slice a large tree branch in two with little effort.

Part I

Gantetsu Escort Mission

Todoroki believed him to have died, but he is actually alive, and was the leader of a group of orphans in the
Land of Forests
. He was kidnapped by the
Shinobazu
, but was saved from their hideout by
Naruto
, Todoroki, and
Gantetsu
. Instead of returning with his brother, he chooses to follow Gantetsu to help him protect the orphans. Todoroki gives him his best wishes, and gives him the gift he was supposed to give him when they were younger.
