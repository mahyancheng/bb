Fukurokumaru
 is a summon animal and playable character in
Naruto: Ultimate Ninja Online
.

Appearance

Fukurokumaru is a human-sized white cat with a red collar and a golden bell with a similar bell on her tail. She carries a large golden coin with her.

Abilities

Fukurokumaru's skills largely revolve around money and prosperity, raining coins on enemies to cause damage.

Plot Overview

Fukurokumaru is the proprietor of the Rich Field, allowing players to obtain coins and level up items.

Trivia

Despite "maru" being a common suffix to male names in Japanese, Fukurokumaru is female.

Fukurokumaru's design is largely inspired by
maneki-neko
 (
招き猫
,
literally meaning:
 beckoning cat), a common symbol of prosperity in Asian cultures.

The 福 on her forehead means "blessing", "fortune", "luck", or "wealth".
