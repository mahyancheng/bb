 faction
Root

Background

To prevent
Hiruzen
 from being reinstated as
Hokage
,
Danzō
 gave Tsuchinoe and nine other
Root
 members a mission to assassinate him. Disguised as a foreign
kunoichi
, she fought the
Anbu
 accompanying her target, who turned out to be a disguised
Kakashi
, resulting in the mission's failure.

Personality

Tsuchinoe would always listen and do what Danzō wanted her to do.

Appearance

Tsuchinoe has shoulder length purple hair. She wears a standard Anbu attire, an her mask is bear-like, with red markings, with a scar pattern going from the left eye hole towards the cheek.

Abilities

As an Anbu, Tsuchinoe is presumably a powerful kunoichi. She wields a
sword
 during combat.
