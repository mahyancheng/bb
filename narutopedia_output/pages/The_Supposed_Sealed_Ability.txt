"
The Supposed Sealed Ability
" (
封じられたはずの能力
,
Fūjiraretahazu no Nōryoku
) is episode 207 of the original
Naruto
 anime.

Synopsis

Naruto
 and the members of
Team 8
 head into
Yakumo
's house to find her with
Kurenai
, threatening to erase her former sensei from existence. It is revealed that the malicious entity known as the
Ido
 dwelled within Yakumo's subconscious, and was the reason why her abilities were sealed away. Yakumo ultimately overcomes the Ido and realises that Kurenai cared about her. Kurenai then returns to an overjoyed Team 8.
