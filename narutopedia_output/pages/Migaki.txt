Personality

Migaki has been described as a sincere individual.

Appearance

Migaki has black hair and small black eyes. He wears the standard white outfit of the hospital medical-nin.

Abilities

As a medical-nin, Migaki presumably has a high level of intellect, is well-versed in
medical ninjutsu

chakra control
 to perform medical procedures.
