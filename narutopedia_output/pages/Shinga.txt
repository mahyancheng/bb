Shinga
 (
真牙
) was the main antagonist in
Naruto Shippūden: Ultimate Ninja Heroes 3
. He was a
missing-nin
 from
Konohagakure
.

Background

Shinga once worked as a scientist under the command of the
Third Hokage
. With the help of his two subordinates, he created a new technique of untold power, the
Jibakugan
. Only Shinga knew how to control this technique, causing him to gain a lust for power. He created the demon,
Shinki
, which could drain chakra from the land. Hearing of Shinga's new discovery and feeling that the technique was morally wrong to use on others, the Third Hokage banned the technique and dispatched
Itachi Uchiha
 to arrest Shinga, but he was able to escape. The
Anbu
 assumed that Shinga died in a mudslide.

Appearance

Shinga had long grey hair that is very spiky. It spiked up on the front top part of his head. He wore bandages around both of his arms. He had black markings all over his face and a red gem in the centre of his forehead.

Personailty

Shinga was overconfident and power hungry. His lust for power was so strong that he would do almost anything to obtain more.

Abilities

Shinga was a brilliant scientist, able to create a new technique called the
Jibakugan
. Using this technique, he could bring out the victim's negative or hateful emotions within their subconscious, and use them to his advantage. He commented that the Jibakugan was "stronger than hypnosis" and irresistible, but
Naruto Uzumaki
 was able to resist it due to the
Nine-Tailed Demon Fox
. When merged with the Shinki, Shinga gains a slew of other powers, including the ability to steal chakra from others.
