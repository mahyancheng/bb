Suzume
 (
スズメ
) is a
chūnin
 from
Konohagakure
. She primarily serves as a teacher at the
Academy
.

Background

When
Ino
 and
Sakura
 were in the
Academy
, Suzume taught their
kunoichi
 class, wherein they learned how to pass themselves off as normal women when undercover.

Personality

Though not much has been seen of her personality, Suzume has been described as a crybaby and a
germaphobe
.

Appearance

Suzume has long, curly, black hair and wears oval glasses along with makeup, wearing red lipstick. Her outfit consists of a simple pink kimono with mesh armour underneath and a pair of baggy red pants with flip-flop-like sandals worn with socks.
