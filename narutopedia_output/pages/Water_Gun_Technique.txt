The
Water Gun Technique
 is a technique used by the
Hōzuki clan
.

Usage

The user makes a
finger gun
 with their hand and, through the Hydrification Technique, compresses a drop of water in their index finger, firing it with tremendous force and speed similar to a gunshot — enough to easily pierce through a
sand clone
.

Sasuke Uchiha
 with this technique after he was freed. However, the technique was not named until it was used by the
