Summary

Ibiki
 explains that, in addition to intelligence-gathering skills, this written test, specifically the tenth question, was designed to test participants' willingness to face the unknown. Since all present chose to risk their futures to answer the tenth question, they have what it takes to become
chūnin
. After he congratulates them, Ibiki allows
Anko Mitarashi
, the proctor for the second phase, to guide the remaining
genin
 to the
Forest of Death
.
