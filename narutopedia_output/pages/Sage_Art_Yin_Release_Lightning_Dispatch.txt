The
Sage Art: Yin Release Lightning Dispatch
 is a ninjutsu used by
Madara Uchiha
.

Usage

By using
Yin Release
 and combining it with natural energy, Madara releases myriads of white (purple in the anime) tangible lightning bolts from the palms of each of his hands to capture the target.

Trivia






The technique is an homage to the movie poster in this interview between Kishimoto and Arad.



In the manga, the appearance of the technique and Madara's pose is an homage to a poster for
The Amazing Spider-Man 2
 featuring the villain
Electro
, which appeared on the previous page of
Weekly Shonen Jump 2014 No.22-23
 as part of an interview with Kishimoto and the film's producer,
Avi Arad
.
