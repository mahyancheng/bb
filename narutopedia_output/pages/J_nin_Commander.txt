Shikaku Nara, a Jōnin Commander of Konohagakure.



The
Jōnin Commander
 (
上忍班長
,
Jōnin Hanchō
) is a position in the shinobi system of
Konohagakure
. As a representative of the regular shinobi forces, they are a member of the
council
, giving them a say in important matters such as choosing a new
Hokage
. They are the highest-ranking normal shinobi in the village, but still officially a
Jōnin
 themselves. The most recent person known to hold the title of Jōnin Commander was
Shikaku Nara
.

Trivia

In
Kumogakure
, there is a similar position called
Head Ninja
.
