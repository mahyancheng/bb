Kusushi
 (
クスシ
) is a
medical-nin
 from
Konohagakure
.

Appearance

He wears the standard high collared, full body uniform of the Konoha medical ninja and a pair of light-coloured glasses. He also has black eyes.

Abilities

As a medical-nin, Kusushi presumably has a high level of intellect, is well-versed in
medical ninjutsu
, and has excellent
chakra control
 to perform medical procedures, such as the
Mystical Palm Technique
 in the anime.

Part I

Sasuke Recovery Mission

He, along with a team of medical ninja are sent as medical support to the
Sasuke Recovery Team
. He meets
Kakashi Hatake
 while he is returning to the village with
Naruto
 and informs them of the condition of the other team members.

Mizuki Tracking Mission

In the anime, Kusushi, along with
Mitate
,
Iyashi
,
Mogusa
, and a few other medical-nin were deployed from the hospital to save
Asuma Sarutobi
,
Kurenai Yūhi
, and the others after their encounter with
Mizuki
. They were able to give them first aid on the stop and then later relocated them to the hospital to recover.

Trivia

"Kusushi" (
薬師
) is an archaic term for "doctor".
