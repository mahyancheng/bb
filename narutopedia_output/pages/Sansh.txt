Sanshō
 (
山椒
) is the owner of the
Curry of Life Shop
.

Background

Little is known about Sanshō, only that she once saved
Rock Lee
's life by making an odd-looking and very spicy curry. Lee seems to enjoy the curry very much.

Personality

Sanshō is a very kind and helpful person. She can be serious at times and will go to great lengths to help good people.

Appearance

Sanshō is an elderly woman with white hair and small black eyes. Two large strands of her hair hang down below each side of her face. She wears a beige-coloured hat and a large pair of glasses.
