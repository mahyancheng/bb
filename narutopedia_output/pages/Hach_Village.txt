The
Hachō Village
 (
ハチョウ村
,
Hachō-mura
) is a village lying on the outskirts of the border of the
Land of Fire
. They are on very friendly terms with the
Tonika Village
. When the village was attacked by
Kabuto Yakushi
 in his attempt to gain access to
The Hole
, the villagers that were able to survive all fled to this village and the Hachō Village later sent officers to help guard the ruined village.

Disonasu
, the
village head
 also went to the Tonika Village to aid in the village's rehabilitation.
