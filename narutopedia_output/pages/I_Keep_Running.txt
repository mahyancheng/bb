I Keep Running
 (
僕は走り続ける
,
Boku wa Hashiri Tsuzukeru
), performed by MELOFLOAT, is the third ending of
Boruto: Naruto Next Generations
 anime, following
Sayonara Moon Town
. It ran from
episode 27
 up until
episode 39
 before being replaced by
Telegraph Soul
.
