This is among the more basic techniques taught in
Iwagakure
. By placing the palm of their hand on the ground, the user breaks up and shifts the local
earth
. The better the user's
chakra
 control, the greater the amount of earth that will be broken up. Despite its relative simplicity, this technique is effective enough to remain a viable option in combat, especially when performed by multiple users at the same time.
