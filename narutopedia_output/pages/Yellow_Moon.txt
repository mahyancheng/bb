Yellow Moon
, performed by
Akeboshi
 (
明星
), is the thirteenth ending in the Japanese version of
Part I
 of the
Naruto
 series, replacing
Parade
. It ran from episode
179
 to
191
. It was replaced by
Pinocchio
.
