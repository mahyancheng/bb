Kohada
 (
コハダ
) is a citizen of
Konohagakure
 who works at her father's sushi-store. She helps keep the store clean.

Personality

Kohada is described as being well-liked by regulars at her father's store. She has also been described as being a hard worker.

Appearance

She has brown hair and light-coloured eyes.

Part I

Search for Tsunade

After the
Third Hokage
's funeral,
Jiraiya
 spied on Kohada and some other girls who were bathing in a public bath house.

Trivia

Kohada (
小鰭
) is a
Japanese gizzard shad
 that is a common ingredient for
sushi
. This is a reference to her place of employment.
