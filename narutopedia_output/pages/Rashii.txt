Appearance

Rashii is an old man with a long, grey, hair, bushy moustache and beard. He has white featureless eyes with dark rims underneath and thick eyebrows. Due to his old age, he also has visible cheekbones and wrinkles. He wears a white bandanna-styled
forehead protector
 and the standard
flak jacket
 of Kumogakure.

Abilities

Being an old shinobi, Rashii has both extensive knowledge and ability with ninjutsu.
