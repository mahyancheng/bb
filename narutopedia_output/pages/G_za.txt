Gōza
 (
ゴウザ
) is a Senior from
Sunagakure
.

Personality

He has been described as a rational, level-headed, and calm individual.

Appearance

Gōza has dark eyes and brown hair which is obscured by the ghutrah he wears. He has a full beard and wears the standard attire of the Sunagakure Senior which consists of a dark kimono under a light-coloured haori and a poncho.

Abilities

As a member of the
advisory council
, he is most likely one of Sunagakure's wisest shinobi.

Part II

Kazekage Rescue Mission

During Gaara's abduction, his foremost concern was the dignity of the village. He was part of the council of Seniors that discussed the state of affairs after the abduction of the
Fifth Kazekage
 by
Akatsuki
. With Gaara gone, he feared for the stability of the village and was afraid the village would be disgraced if the other countries found out the Kazekage was kidnapped by their own
missing-nin
,
Sasori
.

Five Kage Summit

In the anime, after the
Three Sand Siblings
 had returned to the village after the
Kage Summit
 conference was over, he is seen in a meeting with them along with the other seniors discussing what happened at the Summit and the future actions of Sunagakure.
