They are quadruplet brothers hailing from the
Land of Haze
 who specialise in
Haze-Style

kinjutsu
. Orphaned at a young age, they were raised in an orphanage where they appreciated having a home at all to call their own, and such grew up to become very committed to serving their country. They have a rule that each abides to of always sharing everything equally amongst each other.

They were tasked in retrieving cells of the First
Hokage
,
Hashirama Senju
