The
Water Release: Water Bullet Technique
 is a highly versatile
Water Release
 technique with many variations. After kneading
chakra
 in their stomach, the user expels a large quantity of water in the form of a powerful torrent aimed towards their intended target.

This technique can also be used collaboratively to increase its range and effect.
