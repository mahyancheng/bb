Kumo spectators

Personality

He seems to be a very cruel and ruthless individual as he had no qualms kidnapping a child and then threatening to behead her.

Appearance

He was a very stout man with brown eyes, a bald head and a large nose. He wore the standard attire of the Kumo-nin at that time with the exception of his clothes and
forehead protector
 cloth being a bit ragged, as well as his sleeves being very long.

Abilities

He is a rather strong man, as he withstood Hinata's frontal attack without any problems, quickly recovering from her. He is also very fast, instantly getting behind Neji and attacking him with a kunai. According to the latter, his opponent is at a level higher than chūnin.
