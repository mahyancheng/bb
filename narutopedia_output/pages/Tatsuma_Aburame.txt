Tatsuma is regularly seen sporting the same style as the rest of his clan, consisting of dark sunglasses. His most distinguishing feature is the blue marking on his right cheek. He also wears the standard Konohagakure

Tatsuma's skills in infiltration techniques were well reputed. He set a high standard for successive generations of Root.

While his abilities were never displayed in the series, as a member of the Aburame clan it stands to reason that he uses insect-based
