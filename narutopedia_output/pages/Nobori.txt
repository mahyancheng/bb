 (
ノボリ

academy student
 of
Konohagakure
.

Personality

Nobori cares deeply about his friends and wants to always protect them.

Appearance

Nobori has short light brown hair and black-coloured eyes. He wore an orange shirt under a brownish-coloured jacket, a pair of blue pants and sandals. He wears a black belt with a silver-coloured belt buckle. His brownish-coloured jacket is held closed by twelve intertwined black strings in the front. The black strings hold the jacket closed in an "X" style.
