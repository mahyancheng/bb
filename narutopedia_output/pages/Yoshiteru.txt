Yoshiteru
 (
ヨシテル
) is a
samurai
 of the
Land of Iron
.

Personality

Yoshiteru has been described as being reliable.

Appearance

Yoshiteru wears the standard samurai attire. To show his rank, his helmet is equipped with horns.

Abilities






Yoshiteru channelling chakra through his swords.



As a high-ranking officer of the samurai, Yoshiteru is presumably an experienced fighter and proficient in
kenjutsu
, preferring the two-sword style. To boost the reach and the cutting ability of his
swords
, he can
channel

chakra
 through them. Furthermore, he can use said chakra for a
long-range attack
.

Part II

Five Kage Summit






Yoshiteru attacks Sasuke.



Under orders from
Urakaku
, Yoshiteru leads a squad to seek out and capture the infiltrating
Sasuke Uchiha
. After the latter revealed himself, Yoshiteru angrily launches an
attack
, which is repelled by Sasuke's
sword
. Ignoring Sasuke's warnings, Yoshiteru rushes towards him to bring him down but is instead quickly cut down himself.

Trivia
