This device is used by Shizune to launch
poison
-coated
senbon
 at her opponent with deadly accuracy. It is comprised of a five-part segmented device with barrels on top where the senbon are loaded and soaked inside the barrels when the
strings
 are pulled. Wires on the back of these facilitate the launching of the weapons. It is usually kept concealed underneath the sleeve of her kimono on her right arm.
