) is a
shinobi
, in contract with the Makibi Corporation, that fought in the

Appearance

Raimei is a young, light-skinned individual with spiky brown hair. He wears a white robe, with a violet sash tied around his waist, cloth that is wrapped around both of his wrists and ankles, that also substitutes as an undershirt, a pair of dark blue pants and shinobi sandals.

Abilities

Raimei is able to perform
Lightning Release
 techniques, one of which engulfs his entire body in electricity.
