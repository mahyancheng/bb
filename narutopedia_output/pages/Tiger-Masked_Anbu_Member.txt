He is an
Anbu
 from
Konohagakure
.

Background

In the anime, he,
Kakashi
 and four other Anbu were tasked with protecting the
Third Hokage
 while he left the village to meet with the
Fire Daimyō
. En route, his group was attacked by disguised
Root
 members with the intention of killing Hiruzen. During the attack, he fought
Kanoto
 and
Kanoe
 for some time. As their group gained the upper hand, the disguised root members retreated after their failed assassination, but managed to separate the group from Hiruzen.

Appearance

He has long brown hair that he keeps held in a pony-tail and wears a white tiger-like Anbu mask with nine line markings on it. He wears the standard attire of an Anbu of Konoha.

Abilities

As an Anbu, he is presumably a very powerful
shinobi
. He carries a
sword
 hinting at skill in
kenjutsu
. In the anime, it was shown he had some skill with shuriken and was able to hold his own while fighting against two Root members simultaneously.
