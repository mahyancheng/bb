The
Water Attack Raid Unit
 is a sub-division of the
Allied Shinobi Forces
. It is mainly made up of
shinobi
 from
Kirigakure
.

History

During the
Fourth Shinobi World War
, a group of twenty-four shinobi from this unit were sent to the
Seventh Front
. There, it was invaded by two thousand
White Zetsu clones
 where the entire detachment was wiped out.
