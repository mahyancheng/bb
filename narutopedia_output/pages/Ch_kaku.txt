Background






Chūkaku unintentionally reincarnated by Kabuto.



A ninja monk and scholar, Chūkaku taught Chiriku sometime before his death and was eventually interred at the cemetery in the Fire Temple.
Kabuto
 would later
reincarnate
 him by accident while looking for Chiriku.

Personality

Being very calm and seemingly enlightened man, Chūkaku opposed violence, but like many of the other reincarnated
shinobi
, resigned himself to his fate. He claimed to have been quite ignorant in life, he was first shown meditating most of the time while watching others in battle.

Appearance

Chūkaku was a short, thin man with a bald head, a long and thin grey beard, large protruding ears, he also had dark eyes and possessed several wrinkles on the forehead, around the eyes and nose. When reincarnated he had a grey sclera, a common feature among reincarnated individuals, and broad features. He wore the typical white and dark grey garb of ninja monks.

Abilities

While claiming not to be as powerful as his student, Chūkaku instead possessed knowledge on sealing techniques' formulas. He also displayed an ability where he could use a set of
prayer beads
 to nearly
undo the seal
 on the
Third Raikage
 before being ambushed and sealed.
