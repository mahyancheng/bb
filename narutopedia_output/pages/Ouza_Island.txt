Ouza Island.



Ouza Island
 is a large island located east of the
Land of Tea
 and south of the
Land of Water
. It's connected to
Nagi Island
 to the north by a suspension bridge. Located on the southern point of the island is the
Todoroki Great Shrine
, which serves as the final destination for the Todoroki Great Shrine Race, after the
Modoroki Shrine
 on Nagi Island.

During the
Land of Tea Escort Mission
,
Team 7
 escorted
Idate Morino
 to the island to dedicate one of Ryochu's Jewels at the Todoroki Great Shrine, resulting in Idate's victory and granting the
Wasabi Family
 control over
Degarashi Port
.
