Naruto: One Decade, One Hundred Ninja
 (
NARUTO -ナルト- 十年百忍
,
Naruto: Jūnen Hyakunin
) is a supplementary guidebook released to celebrate the manga's tenth anniversary. It's composed of different character artworks from the anime, presented alongside brief profile information on its subjects.
