Sadoru
 (
サドル
) is a kunoichi of
Konohagakure
 who serves as an instructor at the
Academy
.

Background

According to
Iruka Umino
, Sadoru recently became an instructor at the Academy.

Personality

Sadoru is a kind and loyal shinobi getting worked up when her students were vandalising a statue of the
Third Hokage
. Sadoru holds Iruka in high regards and was impressed with his ability to trick
Tsukune
 to admitting to the vandalism.

Appearance

Sadoru has long black hair that she keeps held in a top knot bun with multiple strands popping out around her head. Three from the forehead and one near each ear. She wears a short sleeved Konohagakure flak jacket. Sadoru also has blue eyes and wears red lipstick.
