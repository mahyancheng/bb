The user creates a wave of sound by crying that can incapacitate anyone who hears it. The frequency of the sound waves can be used to trigger
explosive tags
.

Later, after undergoing chakra control training, Namida is able to focus the technique into a single direction, making it safer to use for her allies, while also greatly increasing its power and reach. This increased focus; however, takes a toll on Namida, leaving her unable to perform it repeatedly.
 With additional training, Namida later became able to use this variation more regularly, at least 3 times in a single day.
