"
Yakumo's Sealed Power
" (
狙われた八雲　封印された能力
,
Nerawareta Yakumo, Fūinsareta Nōryoku
) is episode 204 of the original
Naruto
 anime.

Synopsis

Naruto
 sneaks into
Yakumo
's mansion in the night, and has a dream that
Kurenai
 threatened to seal Yakumo's abilities. He then discovers that this is the reason why she left the team, but Kurenai refuses to disclose any more. As Kurenai goes to see Yakumo, several
Kurama clan
 members attack.
