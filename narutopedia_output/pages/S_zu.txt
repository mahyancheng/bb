Sōzu
 (
ソウズ
) is a gambler from
Tanzaku Quarters
 in the
Land of Fire
.

Background

His den is one of the many visited by
Tsunade
 off of whom he made a fortune. He normally proceeds to spend it all around Tanzaku Quarters.
 On another of her visits he and his cohorts could barely contain themselves and were very grateful for the money that Tsunade had lost to them.

Personality

It would appear that Sōzu likes gambling greatly and even more so when he wins. He was overjoyed that the Legendary Sucker had entered his gambling den that he and
his

cohorts
 could barely contain themselves.

Appearance

Sōzu has trimmed black hair, a goatee, dark eyes and wears a simple, black, loose-fitting kimono with white trimmings.

Part I

Search for Tsunade

During
Jiraiya
's search for Tsunade, he overheard him asking her whereabouts and told him after the Sannin had bought him a round of drinks.

In Other Media

Video Games

Sōzu appears in
Naruto Mobile
 as a constant rival for the player in the Tanzaku location. He fights with cards, throwing them at the opponent.

Trivia

Written as "索子", Sōzu is the Japanese term for mahjong bamboo tiles.
