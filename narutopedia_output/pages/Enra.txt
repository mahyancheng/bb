Enra
 (
猿羅
) is a personal summon of
Konohamaru Sarutobi
.

Personality

Enra has a somewhat immature attitude, complaining to Konohamaru about the situation and talking a lot. He shows strong team-work and loyalty towards Konohamaru.

Appearance

Enra is a small white monkey with black markings around his eyes. He wears a yellow, sleeveless kimono-shirt with white fur trimmings and mesh armour underneath, secured with a red belt and black wristbands. He also wears a black bandanna with a
Konoha

forehead protector
 on it and has a tuft of hair styled in a ponytail sticking out from behind it.

Abilities

Enra is very quick and agile, able to skillfully evade enemy attacks. He can transform into a
staff
. Upon transforming, Enra can be wielded by his summoner, becoming a perfect team for attacks. He can also perform
fūinjutsu
 in unison with his summoner.

New Era

Konohamaru's Love Arc

Konohamaru's Love Arc

When Konohamaru was forced to battle the memory-stealing demon
Sōma
, Konohamaru summoned Enra to aid him. Enra quickly transformed into a staff. Together, with the aid of
Boruto Uzumaki
 applying special
sealing tags
, Enra and Konohamaru attempted to seal away the demon with the
Adamantine Seal: Monkey Yang Suppression
. The demon, however, proved too strong as the duo quickly tired and were repelled, forcing Enra to disperse away.
