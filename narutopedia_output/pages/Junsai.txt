Junsai
 was a
shinobi
 of
Kirigakure
.

Background

Ten years prior to the formation of the
Allied Shinobi Forces
, Junsai was part of
Ganryū
's
team
 that was tasked with retrieving a scroll along with a team from
Iwagakure
 taken by
Kumogakure
 shinobi. After retrieving the document, Iwagakure betrayed Ganryū's team and killed Junsai and
Suiren
. This incident which came to be known as the
Tragedy of Yosuga Pass
 marked the beginning of hostilities between Kirigakure and Iwagakure.

Personality

From all indications, Junsai was a calm person. He also seemed to have a very pleasant disposition.

Appearance

He had long wavy brown hair that he held in a ponytail with a black hair holder and dark eyes and a strong jawline. He wore the standard attire of the Kirigakure-nin albeit his outfit had short-sleeves, along with bandages around his wrists and a jade-green bangle that was worn by his teammates. On the bandages of his right hand, he had two black bracelets wrapped around them.
