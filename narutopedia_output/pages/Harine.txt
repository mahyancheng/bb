Harine
 (
ハリネ
) was one of the orphans at the
Konoha Orphanage
.

Background

Harine was seen with the other children at the Konoha orphanage. He was present when
Nonō Yakushi
 was thinking of a name for
Kabuto
.

Personality

Harine wasn't impressed by Nonō Yakushi's idea to name Kabuto after a helmet. Harine is proud of how thick his hair is, so thick that one cannot comb it.

Appearance

Harine has tall, thick spiky blue hair resembeling a hedgehog. He has black-coloured eyes. He wore a grey shirt with three blue coloured stripes.

Trivia

Masashi Kishimoto
 named Harine after a hedgehog, referring to his hair.
