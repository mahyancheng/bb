Appearance

The bird-like creature is a very large beast. It was large enough for San to be able to stand on top its head, and was even then dwarfed in comparison. It had a very large wingspan which had distinctive markings that combined near the middle. It also had two jet-like pistons near the end of its body, with two tails attached. It has an orange face, green feathers and white skin.

Abilities

It showed the ability to shoot
explosive feathers
 from its body with almost pin-point accuracy. The creature also has jet-like modifications that allow it to have short bursts of speed.
