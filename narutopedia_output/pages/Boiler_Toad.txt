Appearance

The Boiler Toad is a giant toad with wrinkles around its eyes. It seems to be wearing a full body armour of beige colour, with its forelegs covered in harnesses, while its hind legs wearing what seems to be dark blue leggings. The armour has two siphons on the back.

Abilities

Being of giant proportions, the Boiler Toad can take advantage of its heaviness to crush a target to death by being summoned from above.
