Hikaku Uchiha
 (
うちはヒカク
,
Uchiha Hikaku
) was a member of the
Uchiha clan
 before the founding of
Konohagakure
.

Appearance

He had shoulder-length brown hair that framed his face and the rest of which he tied into a high ponytail. He wore a high-collared purple shirt that had a chain of sorts draped around the right shoulder.

Abilities

Despite his young age, he was one of the clan's most powerful shinobi and was also endowed with great leadership skills.
 He possessed the clan's
kekkei genkai
, the
Sharingan
.

Trivia

Hikaku
" can mean "
comparison
" (

), "
leather
" (
皮革
), or "
secret tower
" (
秘閣
).

In the anime, he is the only pre-Konoha Uchiha whose design is not changed from his original manga appearance.
