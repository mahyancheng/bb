The Adventures of Naruto!…Oh, and Everyone Else Too! / One on One: Every Genin For Themselves!
 is a one-hour recap special shown only in North America. This special is made up of footage from the episodes of the
Chūnin Exams
 arc.

Synopsis

In the first part of the recap special,
Team 7
 talk about their battles in the
Forest of Death
, in the second part,
Might Guy
 and
Kakashi Hatake
 discuss about the matches of the Chūnin Exams, and in the final part,
Naruto Uzumaki
 tells
Sasuke Uchiha
 and
Sakura Haruno
 of his training with
Ebisu
 and his meeting with
Jiraiya
.
