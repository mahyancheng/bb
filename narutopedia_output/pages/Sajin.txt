Sajin
 (
砂仁
) was a
chūnin
 from
Sunagakure
.

Background

Sajin had only just become a Chūnin before being sent to attack
Konohagakure
 and thus, had little mission experience.

Appearance

He had short brown hair and wore the standard attire of a Suna-nin which included
flak jacket
,
forehead protector
, a shuriken holster strapped to his right thigh along with shinobi sandals.

Part I

Konoha Crush

During the
Konoha Crush
 he was travelling with a team attacking Konoha. He later encountered
Shikaku Nara
 and was killed by his
Shadow Imitation Technique
 and
Shadow–Neck Binding Technique
 combo attack.

Trivia

"Sa" (
砂
) means "sand" and "jin" (
仁
) means "compassion". Written as 砂塵, "sajin" means "sand cloud".
