Summary

Haku
, dead, is holding
Kakashi
 in place.
Naruto
, having gone looking for Haku after his sudden disappearance, finds him in this position just as
Zabuza
 is about to slice through his body to get to Kakashi. Kakashi avoids the attack and protects Haku's body before staging a counter assault.
Sakura
, seeing that Naruto is okay, asks about
Sasuke
. When Naruto doesn't respond, she suspects the worst. Because she cannot leave
Tazuna
's side, she asks that he accompany her to Sasuke's body.
