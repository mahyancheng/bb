This technique destroys the equilibrium that allows a cave to remain standing. By removing the supporting spot, such as a single rock, even the most solid cave becomes fragile and ultimately collapses. A favourite tactic of
Iwagakure
-nin is to lure opponents into a cave and to then perform this technique. Stones gradually fall upon the opponents until finally a cave-in is caused, wiping them out.
