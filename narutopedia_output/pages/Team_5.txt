Team 5
 is a
shinobi
 team from
Konohagakure
, which is led by
Udon Ise
. Its members consist of:
Metal Lee
,
Denki Kaminarimon
 and
Iwabee Yuino
. The team participated in the
Chūnin Exams
, but were eliminated in the second round by
Team Shinki
. Following Denki's chūnin promotion in the following exam, he was appointed as team captain as Team 5 became a three-person team.

Known Missions

Farming

Rank:
 D-rank

Status:
 Success

For Team 5's first mission, they were assigned to help a villager with their farming.

Gather intel and locate the Byakuya Gang

Rank:
 Unknown

Status:
 Suspended

With the continued thefts performed by the
Byakuya Gang
 throughout Konohagakure, Team 5 was assigned alongside the other genin teams to help stop the self-proclaimed noble thieves. However, the genin were not permitted to engage the Byakuya Gang without aid from a chūnin or higher-ranked shinobi. Due to Iwabee's disobedience of the latter order, which resulted in Metal being injured, the team itself was suspended from the investigation.

Rank:
 Unknown

Status:
 Success

After the
Seventh Hokage
 lifted the team's suspension, Team 5 was assigned to participating in a Genin documentary to increase the dwindling
Academy
 enrolments.

Delivery of artefacts

Rank:
 Unknown

Status:
 Failure

Team 5 was assigned to deliver an expensive china plate, though Metal's anxiety caused him to drop it the moment it was handed to him. Later, the team was to deliver a Ming vase, and Metal once again lost his nerve and nearly dropped it.

Capture the panda

Rank:
 D-rank

Status:
 Success

Team 5 was assigned to capture the panda that was running loose. As part of an exam to become genin,
Kawaki
 was assigned to work with Team 5 to complete a D-rank mission. While Team 5 managed to complete the task, Kawaki had fallen asleep during the mission.

Deliver and supervise autonomous puppets

Rank:
 B-rank

Status:
 Unknown

Team 5 was assigned to deliver newly developed
autonomous puppets
 by

 to Dotō Island in the

 to help with mining. Their mission would be interrupted when the
Funato clan
 later invaded the island, forcing the team and the civilians to evacuate with assistance of Team 7,

,
Buntan Kurosuki
,

 and
