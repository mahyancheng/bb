Mozuku
 (
モズク
,
Mozuku
) is presumably the leader of the gang of the
Mokuzu Island
.

Personality

Mozuku is greedy and ruthless, willing to destroy countless ships with people on board in order to get valuables. He was initially confident when he sailed for treasure from the
Konohagakure
 ship, but became cowardly when he learned that the passengers survived and were ready for revenge.

Appearance

Mozuku has long brown hair that is smooth on the top of his head, but sticks out like spikes on the sides, and has small dark eyes. He wears glasses on his forehead, part of the strap is black and the rest is green. Mozuku wears a simple yellow short sleeve T-shirt and grey pants.

Abilities

Mozuku is an experienced mechanic, as he was able to develop a whole system located under water, helping to destroy ships for profit. He is a good leader, as a large number of people who are part of the
Mokuzu Gang
 obey him.
