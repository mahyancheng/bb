Bansai
 (
板斎
) is an elder
ninja monk
 in the
Fire Temple
 as well as the Head Monk of the temple.

Background

At some point, Bansai became a ninja monk and later retired.

Personality

Bansai is a very kind person who cares about his comrades deeply as seen when he said a prayer for
Asuma Sarutobi
 before he set out for battle.

Appearance

Bansai is an elderly man with a long light grey beard. He also wears the typical ninja monk uniform.

During the attack on the Fire Temple, Bansai had been away from the temple for some time. When Asuma Sarutobi and his team went to the temple to gather information on the
Akatsuki
 attack, Bansai had returned after hearing of the incident. As the team was about to leave, Bansai asked to say a prayer for Asuma and his team's well-being.
