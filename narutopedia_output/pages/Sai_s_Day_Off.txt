Synopsis

Given the day off,
Sai
 spends it strolling the
village
 and drawing. He initially encounters three children that he at first finds it difficult to interact with but later realises that two of them remind him of his teammates
Naruto
 and
Sakura
. He has several flashbacks of their time together and the bonds they share. In the end he uses the doors of a destroyed house and paints a mural of
Team 7
, along with
Sasuke
, holding hands.
