"
Miscalculation…!!
" (
誤算…!!
,
Gosan…!!
) is chapter 270 of the original
Naruto
 manga.

Summary

Temari
 and
Kankurō
 depart from
Sunagakure
, intending to finally join the effort to rescue
Gaara
.
Naruto
 and
Kakashi
 meanwhile are still struggling to get Gaara's body back from
Deidara
. Elsewhere,
Sasori
 grows tired of
Sakura
's continued success with dodging the
Third Kazekage
's attacks, so covers the area in
Iron Sand
. She is injured and collapses, the Iron Sand having been soaked in Sasori's
poison
. The Third moves in to finish her off but Sakura gets up at the last minute and destroys the
puppet
. Sakura reveals to
Chiyo
 that she gave herself the antidote she created earlier, and is now immune to the poison for the next three minutes. She gives the last vial of antidote to Chiyo and encourages her to end the battle before they need to use it too.

Trivia

The front cover page of this chapter may come from the
second Naruto movie
.

The Suna shinobi wearing glasses who appears alongside
Maki
 in this chapter is used as a character model for NPC characters in
Naruto Shippūden: Ultimate Ninja Storm 4
.
