Used primarily by
Suna
-nin, this cloth is a tool used to immobilise an opponent. It can also be used as a medium to
seal
 an opponent by placing a
sealing tag
 on it. The shinobi are also able to freely manipulate the cloth. In the anime, the cloth was used as a means to
conceal an escape
, as well as defend against attacks. While drenched in
water
, it proved an efficient tool against
Scorch Release
 ninjutsu.
