Trivia

This opening,
A World That Was Transparent
, and
Diver
 are the only
Naruto
 openings to have the opening logo placed near the end of the opening.

In its full version, it's currently one of the shortest openings, only 2:26 minutes long.

This opening appears on the movie version of
Road to Ninja: Naruto the Movie
.
