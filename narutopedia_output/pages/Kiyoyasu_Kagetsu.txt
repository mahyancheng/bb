Kiyoyasu Kagetsu
 is a citizen from the
Land of Fire
 and a member of the
Kagetsu Family
.

Personality

Kiyoyasu seemed very confident in his own abilities to make people laugh.

Appearance

Kiyoyasu is a tall man, with a short crop of black hair and black-coloured eyes. During the "funeral" of
Tōbei Kagetsu
, he wears a black suit with a white shirt and black tie underneath.

Part I

Funeral Proxy Mission

Main article:
Funeral Proxy Mission

He is one of the family members attending Tōbei's funeral. After some failed attempts from his other relatives to make
Naruto Uzumaki
 laugh, it's his turn to try. He walks up to the memorial of Tōbei, where he sneezes which results in a flower coming out of his nose, he then proceeds to sniff in and make it come out of the other nostril.
