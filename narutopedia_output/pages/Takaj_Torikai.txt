Takajō Torikai
 (
鳥飼タカジョウ
,
Torikai Takajō
) is a
tokubetsu jōnin
 who works at the
Konoha Aviary
.

Appearance

Takajō has shoulder-length blond hair over which he wears a bandanna-style
forehead protector
. He wears the standard attire of a
Konoha
-shinobi without the
flak jacket
. He also has small black eyes.

Part II

Five Kage Summit

Main article:
Five Kage Summit

In the aftermath of
Pain's assault
 on Konoha, he and his teammates collect and distribute communications to and from the village. When Takajō finds out about
Danzō Shimura
's selection as the next
Hokage
, he is very saddened.

Trivia

Takajō's last name means "bird breeder", while his first name, when written as 鷹匠, means "falconer".
