Suguro
 (
スグロ
) is a thief that was part of
Jako's gang
.

Personality

He is a rather cruel person who respects
Jako
 and follows his orders.

Appearance

Suguro has short, somewhat spiky, dull, black hair and wore a green, v-neck shirt, emblazoned with his group's symbol, a simple obi around his waist, brown pants and sandals. He also carried his manriki-gusari strapped to his sides.

Abilities

Surguro has shown himself to be extremely proficient in the use of manriki-gusari, even wielding two at once with great dexterity. He seemed to prefer to employ a mid to long-ranged fighting style with the use of his manriki-gusari.
