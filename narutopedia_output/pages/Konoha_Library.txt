Konoha Library
 (
木ノ葉図書館
,
Konoha Toshokan
,
literally meaning:
 Tree Leaf Library) is a public library in
Konohagakure
. It is centrally located and is accessible by all members of the village. It contains books referring to many subjects ranging from advanced medical textbooks to self help books about social interaction.
