Plot

Tan Dijun tries to recruit Yunxiao, recognizing his extraordinary talent, but Yunxiao refuses and attempts to flee.

A fierce chase ensues, with Tan Dijun using his domain power and mist technique to trap Yunxiao. However, Yunxiao’s skills allow him to break free temporarily. Tan Dijun is shocked by Yunxiao’s capabilities but remains determined to capture him.

Seeking refuge, Yunxiao finds a group of familiar faces, but his arrival also exposes them to danger. Tan Dijun reveals his identity as the rightful heir to the Big Dipper Sect, returning to reclaim his position and eliminate traitors. The situation intensifies as Yunxiao and his reluctant allies must now deal with a relentless and powerful enemy.

Frustrated, Tan Dijun escalates his attacks, yet Yunxiao cleverly evades, utilizing teleportation and a newly refined ancient Heaven's Eye technique. Tan Dijun eventually realizes Yunxiao is not an easy opponent and demands to know his true identity. Yunxiao, ever playful, dodges the question and instead proposes they cooperate to uncover a long-lost treasure. Tan Dijun remains skeptical, but the discovery of an ancient map in Ping Yingyi’s possession shifts the group's focus to their common goal.

Ping Yingyi, then show treasure map made from the skin of a ninth-tier fire-type demon beast. Initially skeptical, Tan Dijun tests the map’s authenticity but fails to reveal its secrets. Yunxiao, an alchemist with a powerful flame, successfully burns away the beast skin, revealing a hidden titanium platinum paper containing the real treasure map.

Tan Dijun is shocked to recognize the marked location a forbidden "dead land" near Southfire City, known for its dangerous yet precious Li Fire Golden Crystals. He had once discovered a rare Li Fire Golden Source there in his youth but was deceived into trading it for a meager reward.

Despite Yunxiao’s eagerness to seek the treasure immediately, Tan Dijun insists on delaying the expedition until after the upcoming sect tournament. His priority is to expose Zhang Linghua’s crimes before all the sect’s forces and personally kill him. He reveals that Zhang Linghua, despite claiming to have reached Martial Supreme status, is only a Half-Martial Supreme who relied on external resources to advance. With this knowledge, Tan Dijun is confident in his ability to defeat his enemy and reclaim his rightful position before setting off on the treasure hunt.

He try recruits Yunxiao, with his teleportation abilities, to launch the initial attack using his powerful flame. Tan believes that Yunxiao's attack will either seriously injure Zhang or at least create an opening for his pre-arranged ambush involving six of the sect's elders who secretly oppose Zhang.

Yunxiao, knowing the extreme risk, demands five billion superior-grade primordial stones as payment. Tan reluctantly agrees but secretly plans to let Yunxiao suffer Zhang's retaliation, hoping it will either kill or severely injure him, making him easier to eliminate afterward.

Zhang Linghua moves to suppress Yunxiao, severely injuring him even destroying his demonic armor, which corrodes his organs. However, Yunxiao retaliates, dealing serious damage to Linghua. Seizing the moment, Tan Dijun reveals himself and calls upon his loyal elders to finish off Linghua.

Thinking he has succeeded, Tan Dijun kills what appears to be Linghua only to discover it was the vice leader, who willingly sacrificed himself. The real Zhang Linghua, who had been in disguise from the start, reveals himself, now having reached the realm of a One-Star Martial Sovereign.

Instead of retaliating, Linghua offers Tan Dijun the position of vice leader and pardons the other rebels, choosing unity over conflict. He understands that internal strife would only weaken the sect against external threats. Realizing he has no choice, Tan Dijun accepts.

Meanwhile, Yunxiao, heavily injured, knows he cannot stay. Using his lightning transformation technique, he escapes. Even Zhang Linghua, now a Martial Sovereign, fails to catch him, leaving the entire sect in shock as Yunxiao vanishes without a trace.

After the failed assassination attempt, Yunxiao becomes the most wanted man in Nanhuo City. With Mo's help, he remains hidden while recovering from his injuries.
