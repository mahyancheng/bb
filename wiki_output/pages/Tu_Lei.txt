Tu Lei, an Enforcer and Elder from the Thousand Crane Pavilion, was one of the top authorities representing the three major forces in the South Domain, along with
Fang De
 and
Huang Xiaolong
.

Plot

During the battle, Tu Lei was present when
Luo Qingyun
 used a powerful mental attack through the mesmerizing sound of the Spirit Heaven Ring to affect
Li Yunxiao
. He witnessed the struggle Li Yunxiao faced in countering the mental attack due to his lower cultivation level. Tu Lei was amused by Luo Qingyun's confidence in his mental attacks and watched as the battle intensified.

As the fight between Li Yunxiao and Luo Qingyun escalated, Tu Lei was among the enforcers who set up a barrier to contain the powerful attacks unleashed by both fighters. He observed the intense clashes, including the use of profound techniques and powerful treasures by Li Yunxiao and the demonification process activated by Luo Qingyun using a mysterious pill.

Later, He was saved Luo Qingyun, by surrendering on his behalf, Li Yunxiao emerged victorious with over a thousand crystal stones. As the remaining 132 crystal stones couldn't be found due to the damaged array, the enforcers decided to end the competition and activate the teleportation array to send everyone back.

Tu Lei was also present when Li Yunxiao announced his plan to auction entry quotas to the Veiled Desolation in Flame Martial City. Although this decision astonished some participants.
