Appearance

A white-bearded old man with an extraordinary appearance, with his eyes closed and his expression lazy.

Background

Zhang Linghua, the Martial Sovereign of the Nine Heavens realm, is the esteemed leader of the Big Dipper Sect. He ascended to the Martial Supreme stage only four to five years ago.

History

Zhang Linghua betrayed his sect by killing his own master and seizing the position of sect master of the Big Dipper Sect. In his greed for a ninth-tier medicinal pill, he also launched a sneak attack on
Tan Dijun
, gravely injuring him.
