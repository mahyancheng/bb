Appearance

A woman in a Palace dress, her long hair was tied into a bun with a few scattered golden flowers. Her whole body exuded a noble aura.

Trivia

She joined Shenxiao Palace out of anger and jealousy, feeling that her father had never praised her and that she could never measure up to her sister. Wanting to escape Hongyue City and sever ties with her father, she chose to leave.

Her father had been killed by Wei Qing, and she hated the people of the Holy region to the bone.

She was born in Red Moon City.
