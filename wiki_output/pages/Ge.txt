SPOILER ALERT!

Ge, was one of the Great Monsters  from ten thousands years ago, His friends are very important to him.

Past

In order to comprehend the rules of the
Ten Directions Realm
, he and his close ones sealed themselves in an array for tens of thousands of years, which resulted in a massive drop of their cultivation from a Peak Martial Sovereign (estimated even Divine Transcendence for himself and his friends and Peak of Perfection for Shang) down to the Martial Emperor Realm.

Plot

Demon Origin Plains

In the midst of an intense battle above the Demon Plain, a fierce and bloody confrontation unfolds between a character named
Yi Qing
 and an opponent named
Fu
, who wields a powerful 9th Rank Battle-Axe. Despite Yi Qing's efforts, he is overpowered by Fu's strength, and their battle becomes increasingly dire.
Shang
 and his companions observe the fight.

During the battle, Shang reveals his true identity to a figure named
Little Qing
, who is also known as the Eternal Evergreen Tree. Shang explains his possession of the
Supreme Palace Armillary Sphere
, a treasure that has extended his life for millennia. He seeks Little Qing's assistance in restoring the Artifact's power using the Eternal Evergreen Tree's energy. However, Little Qing resists Shang's plea, leading to a tense exchange between them.

As the confrontation intensifies, Little Qing transforms into lightning and traps Shang and his companions. Shang utilizes the power of the Supreme Palace Armillary Sphere to create a celestial barrier, attempting to contain Little Qing.

Ge sacrifices himself to block Little Qing's devastating
Void Jade Azure Violet Palace Brahma World Annihilation Divine Thunder
 attack. Ge's sacrifice stabilizes the situation momentarily, preventing the annihilation of their group. The act of selflessness and bravery has a profound impact on his old friends and Shang.

Power

The four great demons were not strong but the weapons in their hands had been 100% refined tens of thousands of years ago. The power of a ninth tier profound armament was something that even a martial sovereign would not dare to underestimate.
