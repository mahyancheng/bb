Plot

Leng Xingbo, the Lord of the Emotion Severing Mountain, on the foot of a mountain. Leng Xingbo was a formidable expert at the Eight Disolates Realm, a Martial Supreme. Confronted Li Yunxiao on Qionghua Island. He appeared without any concealment and displayed his Martial Supreme's domain, creating a formidable barrier that divided the earth into two halves.

Li Yunxiao recognized Leng Xingbo's domain and showed respect towards the powerful figure. Leng Xingbo revealed that he was there to reclaim his treasured sword, the Snow & Frost Bright Sword, which symbolized his Emotion Severing Mountain. However, he found the sword's spiritual aura had died, leading to confusion and sorrow in his heart.

Li Yunxiao explained that the sword had found its best destination and died without regrets, conveying emotions despite being an unfeeling sword. This revelation caused a breakthrough in Leng Xingbo's cultivation, as he broke through a long-standing bottleneck.

Feeling grateful to Li Yunxiao for helping him understand the message from the sword, Leng Xingbo decided to reward him by betrothing his daughter,
Hong Ling
, to him. Li Yunxiao, however, Li Yunxiao, feeling overwhelmed, proposed holding an auction in three days in Flame Martial City, where Leng Xingbo could bring Hong Ling to find a suitable son-in-law.

In the end, Leng Xingbo agreed to send Hong Ling to participate in Li Yunxiao's upcoming auction in three days. Afterward, Leng Xingbo disappeared into the distance, and Li Yunxiao continued his journey towards Flame Martial City.
