Plot

SPOILER ALERT!

In order to help
Luo Yunshang
 successfully refine the
Rainbow Stone
, to complete her physique to the extreme Yang Divine Body, she travels with her to the Western Region. In the Western Regions Ancient Martial Empire they enter the "
Land of No Return
", a special training ground used by nearby forces.
 While she guards Yunshang, Yunxiao appears in the Land of No Return. Hongyan doesn't recognize him, but
Li Yunxiao
 expresses his willingness to help Yunshang. Hongyan tells him the Seal on Five Cloud Mountain has to be broken, since it interferes with Yunshangs refinement. Five Cloud Mountain is a place were the Blood Monster Race was sealed by Heavenly Night Palace Ancestor Gu Qingqing. After Yunxiao leaves for the seal she realizes his real identity. During the refinement of the Rainbow Stone Gu Qingqing's soul appears and possesses Yunshangs body, she then proceeds to seal Hongyan under Five Cloud Mountain. Meanwhile a great battle around Five Cloud Mountain broke out between Holy Regions Law Enforcers, Li Yunxiao's companions, Shangs Monster Faction, Huangs Monster Faction and the Blood Monsters Faction who managed to escape. During the battle Yunxiao manages to convince
Gu Qingqing
, who is possessing Yunshangs body, to release Hongyan, who then joins in the battle against the Monster Race.
