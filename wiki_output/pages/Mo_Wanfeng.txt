History

When Mo Xiaochuan repeatedly trespasses into the clan’s forbidden area, facing severe punishment, Mo Wanfeng attempts to take responsibility by offering his own cultivation as atonement. However, the ancestral elder intervenes, preventing his sacrifice. The reeson he deeply devoted to Mo Xiaochuan is due to his gratitude toward Xiaochuan’s late father, Mo Douchen, who once saved his life.
