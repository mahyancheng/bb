The former City Lord of Crimson Crescent City. Disappeared in the past for unknown reasons.

Appearance

A old man in black robe with muddy eyes, he had a head full of silver hair and his face was full of chiseled marks left by time.

Ning Kewei was a cautious and strategic individual, always mindful of his position in the grand scheme of power.
