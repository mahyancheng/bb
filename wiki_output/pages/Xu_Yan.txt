Xu Yan is the head of the Yuan Division.

Appearance

This man had a beard and white hair.

Personality

Xu Yan is a fair and rational person who judges people based on his own experiences rather than reputation. After meeting Li Yunxiao at the burial site, he formed a positive impression of him, believing that both Li Yunxiao were not evil. Unlike many in the Holy Region, he is unafraid to voice his opinion, even in front of higher authorities, showing his integrity and courage.
