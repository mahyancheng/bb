When Yunxiao travelled to the Outer Space, he met the Starlight Soul Body of Yuan Gaohan. The former abducted him in his
Divine Realm Tablet

Upon reaching the Peak of 9th tier with his soul, Yuan Gaohan planned to merge his body in
Wufatian

Devouring Soul Sect

With Li Yunxiao's party guarding him, he focused on his refinement. If the Starlight Soul Body reached its peak, the fusion of his two souls could elevate him to the pinnacle of magic, potentially stepping into the realm of a Magic God. Later he decided to further stay in the Divine Realm Tablet to learn from the Ancient Master

Technique

The Purple Heart Origin Pill Art is a divine technique from the Holy Region, designed to strengthen the dantian’s sea of Qi, making it as solid as an impregnable fortress. It serves as the foundation for all techniques, enhancing a martial artist’s ability to endure immense energy. By mastering this art, one's dantian becomes over a hundred times stronger, eliminating the risk of energy overload and allowing unrestricted absorption of vital Qi. As a result, cultivation speed increases significantly. Yuan teach this to to help
Li Yunxiao
  to help him break the restrictive spell placed on him by
Yu Shengfeng
, However, in the end, he came to regret his decision.
