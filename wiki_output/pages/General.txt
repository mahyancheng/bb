Appearance

The General was a tall and sturdy man. The man’s entire figure was covered in a gray robe, beneath the grey rob was clad in a golden armor that was engraved with arrays. His face was calm and his eyes were intelligent. with six golden beads between his fingers, each of which was engraved with a small seal character, which read: soldier, elephant, horse, chariot, cannon, and soldier.

Background

The general was the most mysterious person in the myriad Stars Valley. He followed the myriad Stars Valley’s sect master, Tian Xingzi, like a shadow and had never been separated. Even the people of the myriad Stars Valley never knew of the general’s identity.

History

Back then, Tian Xingzi brought this very General to wager against Teng Guang in a game of strategy. But even with such a formidable creation, he still lost

Power

The Chessboard General and his Six Puppet Knights were formidable, each representing a key piece in a deadly game of strategy and power. These puppets were not mere machines; they possessed intelligence, agility, and an eerie semblance of life. Their deep-set eyes flickered with eerie light as they moved with mechanical precision.

Wind-Chasing Cannon (Pao) –  A broad-shouldered, short-built puppet carrying a five-foot-long cylindrical cannon on his shoulder. He is impulsive, aggressive, and eager for battle, always the first to engage the enemy. His attacks are explosive and devastating, capable of unleashing destructive energy with immense force.

Blood-Eyed Knight (Shi Wushuang) – Slender and quick, with a head shaped like a crescent moon and red Bloodthirsty Demon Eyes that glow ominously. His gaze can see through his opponents, detecting their strength and weaknesses. Emotional and attached to his comrades, he has a strong sense of loyalty but is prone to reckless actions when enraged.

Sky-Devouring Elephant (Xiang) – A massive, heavily-armored brute with overwhelming strength. His enormous golden sickle could cleave through the battlefield like a storm.

Phantom Horse (Ma Feiying) – A master of spatial manipulation, able to teleport instantaneously. His ability to divide his body into multiple clones made him elusive and nearly untouchable.

Chariot (Yue Che) – The fastest among the puppets, specializing in overwhelming speed and maneuverability. His strikes are precise and relentless, making it difficult for opponents to counter.

Soldier (Ma Feifei) – A standard frontline warrior, disciplined and steadfast. Though seemingly unremarkable, his movements and battle instincts make him a reliable fighter.

Trivia

The General was created as a perfect vessel for Tian Xingzi's descent. Unlike ordinary possession techniques, which have limitations, the General was specially refined to ensure a high degree of compatibility, allowing Tian Xingzi to exert his full power when taking over the body.

Since the General and the other puppets are not truly living beings, they are essentially immortal. Even if they are killed or destroyed, Tian Xingzi can simply create new ones,
