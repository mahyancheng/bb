She is the superior of
Shen Li
 and the overseer of the
Light Song Forest
's
Empyrean One Pavilion
. She first appeared in the Demon Origin Plains, chasing after
Tang Jie
, the Young Master of the
Four Extremes Sect
.

Appearance

The beautiful woman in the palace dress, she was thirty-four years old. Although she was no longer young, her charm was still there. Her smile had a charming charm.

Personality

She was a person who cared about her face the most.

Plot

Demon Origin Plains

Qing Luo confronts
Li Yunxiao
 and his companions in an 8th Rank Underworld Warship representing the
Tianyi Pavilion
. Suspicion arises about Li Yunxiao leaking information about
Noah's Ark
. Li Yunxiao strategically responds, implying he joined the
Rookie Mercenary Group
 for opportunities. The conversation escalates, and
Shen Li
 suspect the identities of the individuals accompanying Li Yunxiao.

Li Yunxiao warns Qing Luo about hasty accusations. The conversation shifts to the Skeleton Mercenary Group's whereabouts. Li Yunxiao audaciously devours Martial Intent Dao Fruits, shocking Qing Luo. She suppresses her anger, needing his help to locate the group. Shen Li warns about Li Yunxiao's tactics.

As the conversation continues, Li Yunxiao's knowledge creates mystery around him. He shifts focus to the Nine Evil Yellow Spring, implying a reward for aiding the Tianyi Pavilion. Qing Luo reluctantly agrees to Li Yunxiao's conditions for assistance in finding the Skeleton Mercenary Group. Li Yunxiao's manipulation and connection to
Li Huachi
 raise questions. They discuss strategy for navigating the Demon Plain and the potential involvement of Li Huachi. Qing Luo extends an olive branch, agreeing to provide materials.

Later, a mysterious and powerful youth dressed in green is discovered within a sphere of lightning energy in the Empty Space by Qing Luo and her group. The mysterious youth absorbs lightning and gazes at Li Yunxiao, uttering the name "Little Wu." His enigmatic behavior leaves Qing Luo and her group baffled and intrigued, adding complexity to the unfolding events in the Demon Plain.







Not long after, Yunxiao and the Empyrean One Pavilion forces come across  the wreckage of a battleship and numerous corpses from the
Mando Chamber of Commerce
, a battle was taking place not far from the scene.
Shang
 and his companions fight
Little Qing
 in the void. Unable to avoid conflict, a fierce battle erupts between both sides, resulting in heavy casualties. Eventually, both groups retreat, unwilling to continue the fight in their weakened state.








With many injured and their strength depleted, the Empyrean One Pavillion decides to withdraw from Demon Land, deeming it too dangerous. However, on their way back,
Shen Li
, Qing Luo along with the Empyrean forces are ambushed by
Zhang Shaotian
 and completely annihilated.
