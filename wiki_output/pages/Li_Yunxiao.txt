Appearance

Personality

Li Yunxiao
 has a personality that seems arrogant at first sight, and he does not know his limits under the heavens, but this personality is due to the fact that he was at the Peak of the Martial World, so even after his reincarnation he could not change it, in addition to him being wise, which makes him appear bold and cunning, he will also take what he wants in all possible ways.  Despite that, he still cares a lot about his friends and family, and if someone hurts them, he will not rest until this person is gone.

Background

One of the
Conferred Martial Sovereigns
, and the top third strongest in Heaven and Earth Power Chart, Gu Feiyang, passed away in the Tiandang Mountains and was reincarnated 15 years later as Li Yunxiao in the
State of Tianshui
, embarking on a heaven-defying journey against the innumerable geniuses of the time.
