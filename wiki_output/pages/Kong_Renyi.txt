Plot

After the
Three Wises
 incident, the scholars either retired, returned home, or went mad. Business along the Qinhuai River's flower boats also declined sharply.

Furious, Kong Renyi vomited blood and developed a high fever. Despite this, he had himself carried to the court, summoning all the imperial preceptors to demand an explanation from Li Chunyang. Terrified, Li Chunyang fled at the sight of them. Hearing they were still waiting at court, refusing to leave until he appeared, he had no choice but to call Yunxiao for help.

He led imperial preceptors in an attempt to slander and block Li Chunyang and Yunxiao from meeting the emperor. However, Yunxiao skillfully turned the law against him, Overcome with rage and humiliation, he suffered a fatal breakdown, vomiting blood and collapsing on the spot.
