General Information

An extremely heavy
Xuan Artifact
. It's refined from mutated earth elements. Due to its high weight it's extremely slow when used in attacks, hence Yunxiao uses it in combination with his
Moon Eyes
, catching the enemy off-guard through illusions. It crushes almost everything in its path and is also used as a shield against strong attacks. Upon obtaining the Tusita Heaven Peak it is three-colored, its perfected form is said to be seven-colored.

History

 Obtained 3 colored Tusita Heaven Peak.

 Refined to 4 colors with mutated earth element from
World God Monument
.

 Refined to 5 colors with filthy Earth Element of the Netherworld.

 Refined to 6 colors with Five Cloud Mountains Mutated Earth Element.

 Refined to 7 colors with Strange and Traceless Land with
Nanqiu Yu
.
