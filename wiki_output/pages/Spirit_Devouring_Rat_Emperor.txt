Four Seasons Immortal Mountain

Inside the Immortal Mountain, Little Qing and the the ancient Rat Empress discuss the dire situation. Little Qing explains that the revelation of the true formation's core will activate the Immortal Mountain's power, causing the four seasons to rotate and crush all life within the formation, including themselves. To buy time for Young Emperor Hao Lian's breakthrough, Little Qing plans to stall and strengthen himself. He hopes that once he becomes more powerful, he can break open a sealed space below his main body and retrieve Noah's Ark.
