Appearance

A bearded man wearing a tiger-like monster skin coat.

Background

Shou Wei is an experienced martial artist. Before coming to South Fire City, he was only at the peak of the martial king level. After staying here for more than ten years, he has already broken through to the six-star martial master level. Moreover, he has been stuck at this level for three years, and he is afraid that he will have to stop here for the rest of his life. He also plans to mine for a few more years, and then find a small sect to become an elder and live out his retirement in peace, and train his own descendants.
