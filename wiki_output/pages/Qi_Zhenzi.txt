The old man’s name was Qi Zhenzi, he was the deputy palace master of the Supreme Palace in the Firecrow Empire and He accidentally discovered clue of the treasure in Mount Phoenix from ancient books and records in the Supreme Palace.

Plot

After
Xingyang Huo
 massacred all the members of the
Supreme Palace
, including their lord
Ma Tianhe
, Qi Zhenzi,
Hu Yanming
 and
Yi Xiaoshan
 returned, only to find the palace empty with no one in sight. To their surprise, Li Yunxiao had arrived before them, carrying out his task of absorbing all the Qi Vein to fill the World God Monument.

Yunxiao then issued a challenge if they lost, they must submit to him. With
Duan Yue
 overseeing the Fight and preventing any escape, Yunxiao and Qi Zhenzi exchanged several moves. In the end, Yunxiao crushed Qi Zhenzi to death using the
World God Monument
. Shocked by their comrade’s fate, the remaining two had no choice but to pledge their loyalty to Yunxiao.

Trivia
