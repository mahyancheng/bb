Lu Daozi is a powerful and renowned expert Martial Sovereign in the Western Regions, and he is known as the master of the Tide-listening Pavilion.

Plot

Mount Meru

He becomes involved in the events at the "Mount Meru's Soaring Heaven Palace" when he senses the Divine Prayer Technique being used by
Xu Qing
.

During the confrontation with
Di Jia
, Lu Daozi attempts to suppress the Black Demon Shadow unleashed by Di Jia's powerful demonic techniques. He shows great expertise and strategic thinking, leading the group in their efforts to overcome Di Jia.

Lu Daozi's powers are instrumental in aiding Li Yunxiao and his companions in their escape from the inner hall. He creates a hole through his powerful attack, allowing them to flee from Di Jia's overwhelming strength.
