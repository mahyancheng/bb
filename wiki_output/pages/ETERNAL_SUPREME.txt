Previously one of the ten greatest Martial Emperor, Gu Feiyang somehow died near the mountains of Heaven Wave.

Fifteen years later, Gu Feiyang crossed through the cycle of rebirth. He was back! But this time, he was in the body of Li Yunxiao. Since then, Li Yunxiao started a treacherous journey. The world is in turmoil once more!

昔日十大武帝之一的顾飞扬，不知何故陨落在天波山脉附近。

十五年后，顾飞扬轮回重生，归来了！但这一次，他重生在李云霄的身上。自此，李云霄踏上了一条充满险阻的征途。天下再起风云!.

Reception

The webtoon has received praise for its exceptional art and classic cultivation themes, while others criticize its pacing and excessive side events that distract from the main plot. Fans of the genre may enjoy its storytelling, but some find it frustrating due to frequent interruptions in the protagonist’s journey.
