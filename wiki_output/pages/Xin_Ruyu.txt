After The Golden Crow Arena

He, along with his mentor,
Yue Jiulin
, intercepts
Li Yunxiao
 and
Duan Yue
 as they leave Shangyang City. Xin Ruyu tries to intimidate Li Yunxiao into submitting to him, offering him a chance to become his servant and spare his life. Li Yunxiao refuses and attacks Xin Ruyu, displaying impressive speed and power. However, when Yue Jiulin intervenes, Li Yunxiao realizes that he and Duan Yue might not be able to defeat them in a direct confrontation.

Just as the situation becomes tense,
Baili Gongjin
, arrives with a group of men on a majestic Tiger-Shaped War Chariot. He questions Yue Jiulin's intentions for intercepting the Association's alchemist, Li Yunxiao. The situation becomes complicated, leaving Yue Jiulin in a situation he hadn't anticipated.

Li Yunxiao calls out Xin Ruyu for his arrogance and disrespect towards alchemists, revealing that the Alchemist Association has issued a pursuit order against him. This revelation surprises Yue Jiulin, who realizes the gravity of the situation.

As the confrontation intensifies, Baili Gongjin offers Xin Ruyu a way out, advising him to apologize to Li Yunxiao to resolve the matter peacefully. Xin Ruyu becomes angry, but eventually swallows his pride and leaves the scene with Yue Jiulin, heading towards Shangyang City.
