defeated many skilled fighters

one of the greatest geniuses the Western Region had seen in a thousand years

Cheng Haomiao
, the leader of the
Five Heroes of the Western Region
. However, unlike other disciplined warriors, Cai Linhai had an
inflated sense of self-importance
 and often
