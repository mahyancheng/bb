Appearance

A man walking in slowly, wearing golden purple cloud clogs, a green robe with hundreds of butterflies, and his face was sallow, which looked a little weird.

Plot

Faced with Li Yunxiao, who was determined to rescue his captured comrades, Jiu Guang knew he couldn’t fight him alone. He devised a strategy, using his subordinates as cannon fodder while attempting to escape and summon reinforcements, including the sect master. However, as the battle unfolded, he realized too late that Li Yunxiao's power was beyond his expectations. Before he could react, Li Yunxiao had already unleashed his ultimate move.

In his final moments, Jiu Guang was consumed by desperation and fear. He begged for mercy, but his pleas were ignored. In a last-ditch effort, he summoned a massive blue palm print to defend himself, but Li Yunxiao’s sword light cut through it effortlessly—slicing Jiu Guang’s body in half
