General Information

Producing a clone with ones Soul Power and the Starlight Soul Refining Technique. The Clone consists purely of Soul Power and can absorb the Starlight to increase the Soul Power / Alchemy Realm. After fusing back into the main body ones soul strength gets permanently increased and also receives all the information the clone has collected.

If the clone gets killed, the main body receives a backlash, the severity depends on the strength of the clone. The stronger the clone the greater the backlash after its destruction, which may lead to permanent damage to ones soul. In case of destruction the collected information gets transmitted to the main body.
