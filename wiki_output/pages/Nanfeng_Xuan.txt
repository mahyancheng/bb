Appearance

A petite woman.

Personality

Nanfeng Xuan is a lively and carefree woman with a playful personality. She often speaks in a lighthearted and humorous manner, even in serious situations. Talking about age was her bottom line.

Background

As the head of the Holy region’s Law Enforcement Division, Nanfeng Xuan’s strength was publicly acknowledged to be the strongest. She was also basically the candidate for the next Governor. Although she usually looked like a little girl, this old woman was already a hundred years old.
