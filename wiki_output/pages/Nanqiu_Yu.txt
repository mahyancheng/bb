Leader of the
Natural Moat Order
 Occult Sect, Nanquiu is one of the greatest experts in the World and one of the only Prime Voids in the current Era.

Appearance

an old man in a brocade robe, He had a kind face. His eyes were slightly sunken, and his face seemed to have experienced the vicissitudes of life.

Plot

Nanqiu Yu suddenly appeared with
Lu Jianbo
 and saved Huangfu Bi at a critical moment during his battle with Li Yunxiao. His reason for saving Huangfu Bi was not just out of loyalty but also for the opening of the Eternal World in Langya Grotto-Heaven.
Huangfu Bi
’s rare mastery of the soul path and the guidance of the ancient Golden Whisperer made him crucial for breaking the realm’s barriers. Nanqiu Yu knew that without him, their chances of unlocking the Eternal World’s secrets would diminish. Another reason was to prevent
Myriad Star Valley
 and the
Devouring Soul Sect
 from further slaughtering each other.
