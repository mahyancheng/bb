He is the eldest son of the Zhou Great Family, and one of the most talented persons in the younger generation in the Southern Region. He cultivated the Forgotten Love Heavenly Book, which is the heavenly scripture of the Emotion-Severing Mountain.

General Information

He is always compared with the
Prince of the Empire
, and they say that he may have more talent than him. The rumors multiplied even more when he, with the help of two elders from the Emotion-Severing Mountain, broke through from a Five-Star Martial Lord to a Three-Star Martial King in 2–4 Months.

Plot

Declaration of Accession

When he arrives in
Tianshui Kingdom
, he assumes authority, attempting to dictate the appointment of the crown prince, favoring
Qin Yang
. However, when
Qin Zheng
 defies him and names Qin Yue as the rightful heir, Zhou Yushan is enraged, openly threatening the newly appointed crown prince’s life.

Zhou Yushan showed no respect for Qin Zheng, the ruler of Tianshui, acting as if his word was absolute. When Yunxiao mocked him for his arrogance, tensions flared, and a battle broke out between Zhou Yushan and Yunxiao's party. After exchanging a few blows, Yunxiao, unwilling to escalate the situation further, agreed to a life and death duel in three months.

Confident of his victory, Zhou Yushan openly declared that if he won, he would take Luo Yunshang as his woman. To further ensure his control over the duel, he to abduct
Qin Ruxue
, planning to use her as leverage against Yunxiao, intending to make her his twenty-third concubine . Enraged, Yunxiao intervened, unleashing his power in an attempt to stop him. However,
Leng Hongling
, Zhou Yushan’s fiancée, suddenly stepped in, blocking Yunxiao and forcing him back. With that, Zhou Yushan vanished beyond the horizon, taking Qin Ruxue with him.

The Golden Crow Arena

In the duel,
Li Yunxiao
 displays incredible agility and physical strength, surprising everyone with his Heavenly Tyrant Body Tempering Technique. He neutralizes Zhou Yushan's powerful strike and counterattacks with his own sword technique, asserting his dominance in the battle. Li Yunxiao also delivers a humiliating slap to Zhou Yushan's face, further impressing the spectators with his prowess.

Zhou Yushan activates the Fifth Layer of the Forgotten Love Heavenly Book, wielding the Snow & Frost Bright Sword, a powerful Sixth-Tier Xuan Artifact. He releases moonlight that threatens to crush Li Yunxiao, but Li Yunxiao remains calm and appreciates the Snow & Frost Bright Sword's craftsmanship. He skillfully controls the Tiger Tooth Sword, which is eventually shattered by the Wugou Frost. Despite the significant power difference, Li Yunxiao showcases remarkable skill and control, handling the dangerous situation with ease, leaving everyone astonished by his abilities and calm demeanor.

Later, Li Yunxiao effortlessly neutralizes Zhou Yushan's powerful strike using his "One-Finger Flower Pinch" technique. He then subdues Zhou Yushan, defeating him without killing him, and challenges anyone who dares to provoke him in the future. However,
Jiang Wuji
, a Two-Star Martial Grandmaster, interferes and tries to attack Li Yunxiao. Li Yunxiao uses a soul attack to make Jiang Wuji kneel before him, astonishing everyone present. Later, Yunxiao killed Jiang Wuji and Zhou Yushan with The
World God Monument
.
