Lord Lan is one of the eight demon lords in the demon world.

Plot

Battle of Crimson Crescent City

Due to the weakness of the dimensional crack near
Crimson Crescent City
, his real body couldn't pass through, forcing him to use his clones to infiltrate the human world. However, the lack of demonic energy and the world's suppression prevented him from fully manifesting. Only by returning to the Demon World or completely demonizing the city could he restore his true form.

Power

Possessing the ability to split himself into a hundred powerful clones, each at the God Realm level. while his true body exists in the Void Extreme Realm.
