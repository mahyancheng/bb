Moon Pupil

Yue Tong (Moon Pupil)

The moon pupil is part of the so-called
Pupil Race
.

Gu Feiyang originally obtained the moon pupil on
Mystical Faerie Realm
. Right after optaining it, Gu Feiyang became incapable of fully controlling it, causing him to became berserk in his emotions and actions.

Time later Gu Feiyang found a secret technique within the
Great Library
, allowing him to finally gain full control over the moon pupil

Trivia

Gu Feiyang was considered to have the strongest soul attack power thanks to him owning the moon pupil.

Once in full control, the moon pupil gifted Gu Feiyang powerful soul abilities that ultimately allow him to claim the 3rd place on the Heaven and Earth ranking and become a Titled Martial Sovereign.
