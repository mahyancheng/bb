The Twelve Swords of Lingyun are an elite assassin organization known for their ruthlessness and ambition. They have risen to prominence after the downfall of the Eight Elephants of Death, positioning themselves as the most feared killer group in the world. Each member is a highly skilled martial artist, with several among them reaching the Nine-Star Martial Sovereign level. They are currently in Xinyan City, preparing to assassinate
Ling Baiyi
, one of the
Conferred Martial Sovereigns
, and aiming to establish themselves as the top assassin organization.
