The Mutant Wind element raw material closest to the Origin of Wind. The Immemorial Astral Wind is formed by the evolution and transformation of chaotic matter that arises through the convergence of countless forces of space, joined to a Wind Element product.

It is an incredibly wild and devastating raw material, it can be used to create True Spirits, refine High-Grade Xuan Artifacts, and temper the body. It is absurdly aggressive and can be found in the Starry Sky, Ancestral Land, and the Thirty-Three Heavens.

Li Yunxiao
 was able to achieve great mastery in the use of the Immemorial Astral Wind after creating a Xuan Artifact with the infusion of Immemorial Astral Wind and the fragments of the Eye of Immemorial Heaven.
