Appearance

The old man had a head full of silver hair and was wearing a navy blue shirt with a crescent-colored ribbon tied around his waist. His eyes were clear and he looked leisurely and contented.

Background

Bai Lun is a mysterious and little-known figure in the Tianwu Realm, yet his lineage has played a crucial role in shaping major events for hundreds of thousands of years. Unlike prominent sect leaders or martial emperors, Bai Lun operates from the shadows, his influence extending far beyond what most people realize.

His calculations and foresight have guided the course of history, and even the Duanmu family, known for their powerful divination abilities, inherited part of his legacy. In fact, the
Duanmu Family
 divination arts are said to be merely a fragment of Bai Lun’s profound knowledge.

Even more astonishing, the
Eternal World
, one of the most mysterious and sought after places in the Tianwu Realm, was refined by another master at Bai Lun’s suggestion.

Plot

Heaven of Langya

After Yunxiao was severely injured in his battle against the two mighty experts, Zhan Yu and Zhan Bo, who attempted to kidnap Fe Ni, Bai Lun Jieyi appeared just in time to save him from the brink of death.
Not only did he stabilize Yunxiao's injuries, but he also guided Fe Ni, helping her break through her cultivation. Recognizing Yunxiao's destiny as the Master of the World God Monument, Bai Lun Jieyi used his remaining lifespan to impart crucial guidance.
In his final act, he performed a powerful technique, sacrificing what little time he had left to provide Yunxiao with the time and space coordinates that held the key to the war between the two worlds. His actions ensured that Yunxiao could continue his path toward his ultimate fate, carrying the weight of the world's destiny on his shoulders.

As Bai Lun Jieyi neared the end of her life, she remained a key figure in preserving the Taichu lineage and protecting its secrets. Despite being weak, she held on, waiting for three important visitors Duanmu Youyu, Yin Yu, and Feng Yaoli.

First, she entrusted Duanmu Youyu and Duanmu Cang with the Taichu inheritance, passing down knowledge, treasures, and the Jiuyao Xing Staff, ensuring the lineage's survival.

Then, Yin Yu, once trapped for years, arrived demanding the location of an unknown power. However, Bai Lun Jieyi had erased her own memory, making it impossible for him to extract the information. Enraged, Yin Yu prepared to forcibly read her mind, but before he could act, Feng Yaoli appeared.

Feng Yaoli, also seeking the Taichu secrets, confronted Yin Yu, sparking a conflict. Meanwhile, Wei Qing and his group, who had initially allied with Yin Yu, abandoned him, leaving him vulnerable.

Even in her final moments, Bai Lun Jieyi remained composed, ensuring the Taichu legacy was safely passed down while her enemies fought among themselves. Her role was pivotal in preserving the ancient knowledge that would shape the future of the Tianwu Realm.

Power

The Taichu Zhen Jue(Taichu Devine Art) The Taichu Zhen Jue was an ancient divine technique created to control time, allowing its wielder to manipulate its flow and create time domains. However, over generations, the Taichu lineage misused it to peer into fate, bringing them into conflict with the will of heaven. The Taichu Zhen Jue Divided into nine levels, Bai Lun Jieyi, one of the most accomplished practitioners, had reached only the eighth.

Trivia

Bai Lun Jieyi extended his life far beyond normal limits by consuming the Three Life Fruit and sealing himself in the Realm of Eternal Life. However, his fate was ultimately sealed when he attempted to deduce a forbidden place, drawing the punishment of heaven, which directly severed his remaining lifespan.
