Background

Mr. Yang was originally just a clone of Mo, born from his blood essence. However, over time, while the real body trapped in a seal, he developed his own consciousness. For years, he secretly devoured the blood essence of other monster races using his innate ability, growing stronger.

He then hid within Qianyue Mansion for years, even learning powerful martial arts that had been passed down for thousands of years techniques that no one else had mastered "
the ten directions divine skill
,
Grand secular world".
 During this time, he also helped guard the Five Cloud Mountain to prevent the seal from breaking.

Plot

Five Cloud Mountain

In the midst of the chaos at Qianyue Mansion Palace, Mr. Yang stole the seal from the sect master and attempted to escape. However, Lu Yunxiao, who had been watching from the void, intercepted him. Yunxiao defeated Mr. Yang and nearly killed him before he managed to escape.

After the seal of Five Cloud Mountain was broken and the war between the two races began, another clone of Mo, severely injured by Yunxiao, confronted Mr. Yang, intending to reclaim his stolen power.

With no other choice, Yang risked his life to perform the Ten Directions Divine Skill: Grand Secular World, using the Five Cloud Mountain Formation he learn from the wandering mortal world scroll was actually a secret technique to power the formation Five Cloud Mountain to restrain the Mo clone. He launched a desperate attack, but the Mo clone, drawing power from his original body, wielded the profound bone-scale double blades.

With a single strike, the clone easily tore through space, turning the tide of battle. The power of the blade cut through the void, splitting Yang in half and defeating him.
