Yi Hongming is a powerful but ruthless member of the Long family, known for his violent and uncontrollable bloodline. He aligns himself with Wei Qing, aiming to become the next head of the Long family after Long Qianmiao’s death. Believing in sacrifices for power, he fully supports the blood purification experiment to refine true spirit bloodlines. However, he lacks the leader’s token, which is held by Fei Ni, and relies on Wei Qing to help him seize control.

Appearance

An old man with a curly beard.

Power

Yi Hongming possesses the bloodline of Zhuyan, a powerful ancient beast resembling a red-furred ape with a white head, massive feet, and a golden "Yan" character on its chest.
