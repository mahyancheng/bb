Plot

Light Song Forest

Zou Chen, proposes cooperation among the warriors to search for clues related to a rumored treasure. He offers his Time-fixing Plate, a unique artifact, to aid in the search. Despite skepticism about his reputation and intentions, Zou Chen's artifact reveals a lead pointing to the east, igniting hope among the gathered warriors.
Kang Wu
, associated with the Thunder Fire Palace, adds skepticism, implying the treasure's uncertainty and potential involvement of various factions.

The warriors share a gruesome tale of the Skull Mercenary Group's actions, intensifying their resolve. Zou Chen and Kang Wu engage in banter, reflecting discord between them. Zou Chen's lead prompts determination to pursue the Skeleton Mercenary Group. The warriors, led by Zou Chen and Kang Wu, set off on a mission to confront the perpetrators.

Demon Origin Plains

At the site, warriors from across the Demon Plain gather. Zou Chen and Kang Wu discuss the emergence of a formidable power and its link to Noah's Ark. Zou Chen expresses intent to withdraw due to the power's magnitude, while Kang Wu remains resolute. Tensions rise as a Martial Supreme offers a choice to continue or withdraw. The crowd divides, reflecting the uncertain atmosphere.
