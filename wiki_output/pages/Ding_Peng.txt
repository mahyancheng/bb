Background

Ding Peng was young master of the
Tianyun Merchant Guild
 He is supposed son of
Ding Shan
, the president of Tianyuan Chamber of Commerce. However, it is later revealed that he is not actually Ding Shan’s biological son. Ding Shan adopted him due to his unique Yin-Yang constitution, which made him an ideal vessel for a crucial alchemical process.

Ding Peng is loyal to Ding Shan and obediently follows his orders, unaware that he has been raised as nothing more than a tool. When the truth is revealed, he is left in shock and disbelief, struggling to accept that his entire life has been manipulated for the sake of his adoptive father’s ambitions.
