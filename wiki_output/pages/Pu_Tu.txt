Pu Tu is a unique being born from the leaked demonic energy of Demon Lord Pu’s seal, though not his direct incarnation. He along with
Pu Qi

Background

The place where Pu Tu resides is a thirty-three-layered sealed space, an area formed by the powerful world-sealing formation that was originally designed to trap Demon Lord Pu. Over time, the seal began to evolve, creating multiple layers filled with different creatures, with the deeper levels being home to the strongest ones. Pu Tu himself reside at 11th level. he ruled the 11th level within after
Pu Luo
 been exile to 32 Layer.

Plot

Pu Tu brought along
Pu Qi
to confront
Pu Luo
 for leading an outsider into the deeper levels. As both sides clashed, Yunxiao displayed his strength, unleashing a powerful purple thunder that easily defeated Pu Qi’s subordinates, leaving everyone in shock.

Witnessing Yunxiao’s overwhelming power, Pu Qi suddenly turned his anger toward Pu Tu. In a desperate attempt to save himself, Without hesitation, Pu Qi struck Pu Tu with a single devastating blow, shattering his armor and piercing his heart, killing him instantly.

After Pu Tu’s death, Pu Qi attempted to shift the blame onto him, but Yunxiao remained indifferent. Without wasting any words, he swept through Pu Qi and the others with his destructive purple thunder, annihilating them all.
