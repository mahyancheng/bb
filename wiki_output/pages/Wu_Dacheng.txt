Plot

Dacheng impersonated Li Yunxiao in an attempt to marry the two daughters of
Yongxiang city
 City Lord. However, during the grand celebration, the real Yunxiao appeared and exposed his deception. Enraged, Yunxiao mercilessly slapped Dacheng until his face swelled beyond recognition, turning purplish-red like a pig’s head.

After enduring enough of a beating, Dacheng finally confessed his true identity. With no further use for him, Yunxiao handed him over to City Lord Song Guang, who, furious at the deception, subjected him to relentless torture—an ordeal that likely led to his demise.
