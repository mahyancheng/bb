Martial arts competitions Held in
Luofu Island
 by hidden families to get to know each other, increase their prestige, and understand the situation of other sects.

History

Martial arts competitions weren’t very important. They were just a way for the hidden families to get to know each other, increase their prestige, and understand the situation of other sects. They could be considered a more important form of communication, but they were just communication and didn’t have much of a relationship of interest. But it’s different this time. It’s directly related to two major matters, the quota to enter the heaven of Langya and the distribution of the
Ancient Treasure Trove
.

Rule

the martial arts competition must be young disciples of each sect, and their
bone age
 is limited to
thirty years old
 and below, each sect will have at least one quota. There will be a total of 12 places in the martial arts competition. Whether it’s going to the eternal world or the secret treasure of the true dragon Next, it will be distributed according to the martial arts ranking. for the first place, will get thirty quotas as a reward for the the heaven of Langya.

Trivia

Since
Jing Qi
 cultivated the Corpse Fiend Technique, which refines the body into a corpse fiend, his bone age remains fixed at the year he first practiced this divine ability. Although he is much older, his bone age is technically 23 years old, allowing him to qualify as a participant in the tournament.
