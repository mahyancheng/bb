Plot

Demon Origin Plains

Zheng Yunfeng, along side with
Chen Chuanjiu
 and
Ouyang Qi
, observes
Young Emperor Hao Lian
 in the Demon Plain. Recognizing Hao Lian's deep connection with nature, Chen, and his companions plan a surprise attack. Chen initiates a powerful martial technique, but Hao Lian remains unperturbed, displaying a calm demeanor. Instead of defending himself, Hao Lian approaches a Demon Snake and retrieves a half-eaten Five-Elements Spirit Devouring Rat from its mouth, showing compassion for the creatures.

In a subsequent encounter, Young Emperor Hao Lian protects the Demon Snake and Spirit Rat from harm using a green battle armor, which shatters under Chen Chuanjiu and his companions' combined attack. Believing Hao Lian to be injured or dead, they enter a passage he opens. However, Hao Lian has hidden inside a pit, and when Chen and Zheng Yunfeng enter the passage, he surprises them by reemerging, killing Ouyang Qi, and leaving a warning.

Chen Chuanjiu and Zheng Yunfeng face attacks from a swarm of Five Elements Spirit Devouring Rats summoned by Hao Lian. After defeating the rats, they discover Hao Lian has escaped again. Tracking the thickening Spiritual Qi, they attempt to pursue him.

Meanwhile, a lavishly dressed man contemplates whether to save Young Emperor Hao Lian using a flower-petal counting method. Ultimately, he decides against it, leaving a trail of intriguing thoughts as he departs.

Illusionary Realm of the Water Moon Flower Mirror

Amidst the illusionary landscape,
Young Emperor Hao Lian
 and his pursuers, Chen Chuanjiu and
Zheng Yunfeng

Despite realizing the illusion, Hao Lian is intrigued by its authenticity and presses on, while Chen Chuanjiu uses his formidable power to break through.

Young Emperor Hao Lian, while trying to comprehend the Power of Rules within a unique space, encounters
Zheng Yunfeng

Chen Chuanjiu

The confrontation results in a cataclysmic clash of powers that shatters the space, creates a devouring black hole, and ultimately triggers the activation of the Immortal Mountain's power ahead of schedule. As the Immortal Mountain's power activates, the entire Four Seasons Boundary, which is connected to
Xiao Qing
