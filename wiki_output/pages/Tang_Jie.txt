This revelation adds to Zhang Shaotian's determination to capture Tang Jie and take advantage of his connection to the Vermilion Bird Saber.

Shen Feng shares his findings about the Immortal Mountain's strange environment and the disappearance of Mister Yu and his group. They discover a peculiar cliff that absorbs divine sense and essence upon contact, revealing a hidden space resembling a giant chessboard.
