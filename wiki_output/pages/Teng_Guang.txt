Appearance

A striking young man in a Guangling brocade robe, with long black hair flowing in the wind and jade-like eyes, warm and clear. His presence was mesmerizing, as if the world revolved around him, making him the undeniable protagonist of the moment.

Background

This creator of the incomplete Go game Teng Guang, and is called the 'Game of Heaven and Earth'. It contains his ultimate insight into the Dao of Arrays. Anyone who knows how to solve it can inherit his eight hundred scrolls of the Dao of Arrays and become a Master of Arrays. The reason he created the Go Game, Because he wanted to take in disciples, but none of those who went to him was gifted enough, which is why he had come out with this idea. Anyone who can fully understand the mystery in the game can become his disciple and inherit his Dao of Arrays.

Plot

Teng Guang was obsessed with pursuing the Heavenly Dao. To achieve this, he created 800 Dao Array Diagrams, trapping countless experts as test subjects for his grand experiment. His ultimate goal was to witness the evolution of a world, a rare opportunity he saw as a divine blessing. In his final pursuit of enlightenment, he was even willing to sacrifice himself, merging into the Great Void to attain the Great Dao.

In the final clash against
Shang
 and the
Ghost King
, both unleashed their ultimate techniques and wielded their profound weapons. However, Teng Guang drew upon the entire illusion's power, draining the martial artists trapped in the 800 Dao Array in an instant. Their strength converged into him, as his Dao Spirit absorbed the First Realm’s power, channeling it directly into his body, The sky shattered, and the earth crumbled, plunging everything into chaos. A terrifying force erupted in all directions, engulfing everyone in a whirlwind of destruction, as if the world itself was ending. The impact triggered a massive explosion, obliterating everything within a thousand miles, His 800-array formation collapsed, sending warriors flying—the weaker ones perishing instantly. At the battlefield’s center, a bottomless chasm split the land, marking the devastation. At this moment, the great illusionary land no longer existed. All the martial artists who were trapped inside and were lucky enough to survive when the illusionary land shattered had all come out.

Despite overwhelming his enemies, Teng Guang paid the price. His body withered, his life force drained, and his Dao Foundation shattered beyond repair. Knowing he wouldn’t survive, he ripped the Dao Fruit from his chest, forcing it into Yunxiao’s hands, urging him to leave. Yunxiao hesitated, knowing he lacked the strength to protect it, but Teng Guang, filled with despair and unwillingness, had already made his final decision.

As the Dao Fruit drifted away, The people around had long disappeared, all of them crazily snatching the Dao fruit fragments. Even beiming Nan and Fei Ni had disappeared in a flash, Teng Guang’s gaze darkened—his lifelong pursuit crumbling before him, leaving behind only emptiness and regret.

Teng Guang, gravely injured and coughing blood, could only bitterly laugh at his fate. Despite his pain, he urged Yunxiao to seize the Dao Fruit Fragment, believing that with Yunxiao’s talent, even a fragment could lead him to the God Realm’s Great Dao. However, Yunxiao refused, instead offering heavenly materials and earthly treasures to heal Teng Guang. Among them was the Nine-Five Supreme Pill, shocking Teng Guang, who questioned its origin. Yet, despite taking the pill, he remained disheartened, knowing his Dao Foundation was destroyed, leaving him with no hope of further advancement.

Yunxiao insisted that as long as one is alive, there is always a chance. With a sigh, Teng Guang reluctantly accepted, beginning his recovery—but his lifeless eyes revealed that his spirit had already crumbled.
