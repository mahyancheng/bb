Personality

Zhuo Qingfan has a carefree and mischievous personality, often appearing relaxed and unbothered even in the most intense situations. He carries a lighthearted demeanor, frequently making jokes and teasing others, which contrasts sharply with the serious and high-stakes battles around him. However, beneath his playful exterior lies a keen intellect and sharp instincts. He enjoys provoking reactions from others, as seen when he taunts
Wan Yiqian
 with a casual offer of tea after ruining his carefully laid plans. His sense of humor can sometimes be infuriating to those who take themselves too seriously, but it also highlights his confidence and ability to navigate dangerous encounters with ease. He doesn't seem to care much about authority or rigid formalities, preferring to do things his own way.

Plot

After news broke that one of the fragments of a great demon was sealed in the Boneyard, experts from the Sacred Region were tasked with sending information about the true face of the Dragon Seal. After certain conflicts, Zhuo Qingfan was notified of the situation to the point where the information sending formation was set up, and given the mission to subdue the great demon, Zhuo Qingfan, who was participating in a chess match with a friend, traversed the Void in an instant to arrive at the scene and save the envoys of the Sacred Region.

After developing his attack on the chess pieces after thinking of a counterattack against his friend during the game, Zhuo Qingfan returns for a few minutes to attribute his move, which results in the death of three people during the period of time. After being reminded by his Friend of his mission, Zhuo Qingfan returns to battle, demonstrating his playful personality, Drawing his Halberd and fervently confronting the great demon Di Ye, Zhuo Qingfan makes use of the True Dragon Cradle and the Dragon Breath contained within it to suppress and collapse the personal World of the Battlefield of the Ancient Gods, a Saint Artifact that was in Di Ye's hands at the time of the battle. This resulted in the environment being deformed, as well as the Boneyard's huge Spiritual Energy decline. And after suppressing Di Ye and forcing him to a critical point, Zhuo Qingfan loses sight of Di Ye and leaves.
