Appearance

A man in red, with thick eyebrows and big eyes, and a stiff face.

Personality

Despite his unmatched strength and prestigious background, Long Qianmo is a cautious and strategic individual. He carefully weighs every decision, always choosing the best possible outcome—not just for himself but for his family as well. He never lets emotions cloud his judgment, ensuring that his actions serve a greater purpose. Though he often appears cold and harsh, he deeply cares for
Fei Ni
, his most beloved disciple, and silently protects her in his own way.

Background

He was the previous sect leader of  Heavenly draconic family . He was among the top powerhouses among seven super sect . He was with Wei Qing plan to transform Spirit bloodline people of his sect to attain avatism and to form pure 10th grade dragon blood so become powerful.

Dragon Field is a domain created by Long Qianmo, an innate divine ability of the Dragon Race that forms its own independent world. By absorbing the power of the Nirvana Body of the Celestial Phoenix, he strengthened this domain, elevating its power to new heights. Unlike ordinary domains, Dragon Field is a manifestation of the True Dragon’s bloodline, which no longer exists in the Heavenly Martial Realm but resides in the legendary Dragon Realm. Though the power of the Dragon Realm far surpasses anything in the present world, Long Qianmo’s ability to create Dragon Field proves his extraordinary mastery over his bloodline.
