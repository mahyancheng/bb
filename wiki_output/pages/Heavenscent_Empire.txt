The Heavenscent Empire is the strongest among the three empires in the southern region of the Tianwu Continent. The most powerful organization of the empire isthe Thousand Crane Pavilion and its most prosperous city is the Qinghai Town.

Qinghai Town

Qinghai Town is a gathering place for more than half of the cultivators in the empire. Although it is called a small town, it is larger than ordinary cities, with a population of at least several tens of millions. It is renowned for its prosperity, ranking at the forefront within the country.
