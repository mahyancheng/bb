History

Ye Qingyu is the King of the Eastern Region and the leader of Xuanli Island, a floating island established within the Realm of Eternal Life.

As a key figure in the Demon Sealing War, Ye Qingyu deeply understood the significance of the Demonic Tribulation. To gather and strengthen the forces of the Tianwu Realm, he established Xuanli Island, accumulating its essence power and recruiting warriors willing to take part in the battle. The requirements to join were simple: swear allegiance to Xuanli Island and commit to fighting in the Demonic Tribulation, in exchange for access to its vast resources.

With his talent and leadership, Ye Qingyu is expected to be one of the leading figures in the coming Demonic Tribulation.
