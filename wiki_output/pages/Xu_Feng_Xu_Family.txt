Xu Feng was one of the participants in the
Mount Meru
 expedition. He came from the renowned
Xu Family
, known for their mastery of formations. The Xu Family's head was a distinguished disciple of the titled Martial Sovereign,
Teng Guang
. Xu Feng spent hours deciphering the array formation protecting the main palace door. However, just as he succeeded in unlocking it, he was instantly killed by the powerful dragon energy that leaked from within.
