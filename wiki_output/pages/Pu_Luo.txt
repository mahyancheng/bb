Background

Pu Luo is a mysterious being born from the overflowing demonic energy of Demon Lord Pu, He is not a direct incarnation of the Demon Lord but rather a unique existence shaped by the energy that leaked from the powerful seal trapping Demon Lord Pu. Over countless years, this energy gave birth to various demonic creatures, all possessing a mixture of demonic and monstrous traits.

The place where Pu Luo resides is a thirty-three-layered sealed space, an area formed by the powerful world-sealing formation that was originally designed to trap Demon Lord Pu. Over time, the seal began to evolve, creating multiple layers filled with different creatures, with the deeper levels being home to the strongest ones. Pu Luo himself before being exiled to the 32nd level, he ruled the 11th level within this mysterious prison.

Trivia

Because Pu Luo are the direct descendants of Lord Pu, he possesses purer magic energy lead him has a golden blood.
