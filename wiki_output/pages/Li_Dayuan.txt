Li Dayuan
 was a miner who lived next door to Meng Wu and Meng Bai. From time to time, he would check on the siblings, making sure they were safe and doing well. Because of his kindness and concern, Meng Wu and Meng Bai came to see him as a trusted uncle figure in their lives.

Personality

Despite his rough mining background, he is straightforward and honest.
