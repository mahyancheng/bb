The Tiandang Mountains are the place where the sky is buried, an extremely dangerous place even for peak Cultivators of the Heavenly Martial Realm. An unparalleled proof regarding this is the fact that Gu Feiyang (
Li Yunxiao
), one of the top three in the Heaven and Earth ranking competition perished in the Tiandang Mountains, making his death a clear indication that this place is dangerous to any existence in the Heavenly Martial Realm.
