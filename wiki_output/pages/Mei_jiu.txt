Mei Jiu is the renowned and extremely well-known (especially among merchants and traveling Cultivators) “Ninth Aunt” or “Aunt Jiu”, the current leader of the
Mei Family
, the clan of appraisers who wield an incredibly accurate Analysis skill that allows them to perfectly score the quality and Grade of an Object, Artifact, Treasure, or Technique.

The Mei Family enjoys high recognition among organizations as a whole, and Aunt Jiu in particular is often called upon to conduct high-level auctions or exchanges. demonstrating his improvement with the analysis skill by understanding even the quality of materials at the peak of the Ninth Grade delivered by
Li Yunxiao
.

She initially has a difficult relationship with
Mei Dong'er
 and Li Yunxiao, but after some events, they become good friends, and at Li Yunxiao's request, she takes care of Mei Dong'er, treating her as a precious piece of the Mei Family, to the point that in just a few short years (about two or three years), Mei Dong'er reaches the High-Level Martial Supreme Realm with the help of the Mei Family.
