Yi Xiaoshan was a member of the
Supreme Six
, a renowned group from the Supreme Palace
Duskflame Empire
. He was the man who wounded
Xiao Qingwang
 and
Chen Dashen
 in the
Ranking Battle
 five years ago.

Background

He chose to stay in Baizhan, driven by a sense of duty and gratitude toward the emperor who had once shown him great kindness. For the past decade, he had tirelessly worked to elevate Baizhan to an upper-rank vassal state, but his efforts had yet to bear fruit.

With the five-year qualifying match approaching, Yi Xiaoshan saw it as his final opportunity to achieve his goal. He resolved to return to the Supreme Palace after the match, leaving behind worldly affairs to dedicate himself solely to martial arts.

Plot

Supreme Palace

Xingyang Huo

Yunxiao then challenged Qi Zhenzi, declaring that if he lost, they must submit to him. With
Duan Yue
