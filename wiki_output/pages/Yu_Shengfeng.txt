Plot

Yu Shengfeng suddenly appeared at the Fengling city, blocking Huanmie and Xiao Xiao from escaping into the void. With a swift strike His light wheel cut through the void, relentlessly pursuing its target and severed of Xiao Xiao’s arms.

Sensing an unprecedented crisis, Huanmie and Xiao Xiao immediately joined forces, unleashing the strongest techniques of their lives. However, Yu Shengfeng calmly formed a hand seal, embedding several incantation seals into his sword. A true dragon emerged from its blade, splitting into tens of thousands of swords that rained down from the sky. The phantom swords and the tyrannical blade tore through the sky, converging like rivers into the sea, engulfing everything in their path.

Overwhelmed, Huanmie and Xiao Xiao were left bloodied and in utter despair. In the next moment, Yu Shengfeng struck again, reducing their bodies to lifeless mud. Without hesitation, he turned his attack on Bo Yuqing, Li Yunxiao, and Qiumujie. Tens of thousands of dragon-shaped sword shadows surged through the sky like a mighty river, distorting the heavens.

With just a few moves, Yu Shengfeng had crushed four peak experts. Even Yunxiao, whose strength rivaled theirs, stood among them, facing the overwhelming might of his assault.
