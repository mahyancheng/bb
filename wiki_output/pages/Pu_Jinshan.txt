First meets
Li Yunxiao
 before the opening of the
Mystical Faerie realm
 and asks Yunxiao to refine a rank 9 Xuan artifact for him. A little bit after leaving the realm he meets Yunxiao again with another alchemist, and they try to poison him to get his treasures. Ultimately it falls through and Yunxiao traps him in the World God Monument.

After becoming more docile, Yunxiao takes him out occasionally to look at formations and help him.

After establishing the
Heavenly Martial Alliance
, he becomes the head of the city defenses in
Martial Flame city
