Plot

The moment Yunxiao arrived at 4 Layer, a powerful formation was triggered, suppressing their divine senses and drastically increasing the gravitational force amplifying it by a hundred thousand times. Yunxiao quickly realized that Pu Shu, along with the other overlords, had deliberately led him into this ambush.

Yunnxiao, affected by the intense gravity, unleashed his Tusita Peak. Its weight increased more than tenfold, overwhelming the spatial rules and causing the fourth layer to collapse instantly. The destruction triggered a chain reaction, forcing the third and fifth layers to merge, creating a distinct boundary between the differing densities of demonic Qi.

Pu Li and the other overlords, shocked by the sheer power of the Tusita Peak, Pu Shu hastily offered an alliance to escape the Sealed Land. However, enraged by their earlier betrayal, Yunxiao declared that if they could withstand a single move from him, he might reconsider their offer.
