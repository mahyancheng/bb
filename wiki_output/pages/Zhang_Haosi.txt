Zhang Haosi is a elder within the Long family, aligned with
Yi Hongming
 in his political maneuvering.

Appearance

an old man in gray.

Plot

During Fei Ni's attack on Yi Hongming, Zhang Haosi intervenes to block her divine fire with a protective barrier, demonstrating his considerable martial prowess. However, he quickly finds himself overwhelmed, struggling to maintain his defense as the divine fire drains his energy. Despite his composed outward demeanor, he is visibly shaken by Fei Ni's strength.
