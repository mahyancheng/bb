Appearance

The old man had a sallow face and no expression on his face. His linen clothes were torn and his hair was disheveled. He looked slovenly. But he has the air of someone who has been in a high position for a long time, which is extraordinary and compelling.

Plot

Heaven of Langya

Jing Baimu confronted Li Yunxiao, demanding he hand over a magic pill in exchange for his life, However, Yunxiao refused then The confrontation escalated when Jing Baimu showed lecherous intent towards Qu Hongyan and the other women, enraging Yunxiao.

After a fierce battle, Yunxiao and Qu Hongyan used the Ten Thousand Swords Diagram to defeat and severely injure him. Yunxiao shattered Jing Baimu's magic sword, searched his soul, and reduced him to a fool. He then seized Jing Baimu's treasures, including the rare Bei Guang Fa Tian Pill, which aids in breaking through to the divine realm.
