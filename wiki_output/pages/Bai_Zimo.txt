Bai Zimo is  a disciple participating in the
Big Dipper Sect
 tournament.

Appearance

He was a big fat that carried a big hammer and walked up to the ring in a funny way, which caused a burst of laughter.

Plot

In his First fight Bai Zimo, attempted a surprise attack on Cheng Yu by launching his massive hammer. However, Cheng Yu effortlessly countered with a single punch, crushing the weapon and sending it hurtling back. With a final strike, he amplified the impact, launching Bai Zimo
hundreds of meters into the valley
, ending the fight in an instant.
