Plot

Light Song Forest

He tried to kill
Hai Lin
 and their caravan during their way to the Light Song Forest, and failed in two attempts. So he tried to use a secret technique to summon a High-Rank Demonic Beast. But Li Yunxiao successfully traps the beast using his profound artifact, the Heavenly Spirit Ring, and the caravan absorbs the star power to cultivate and make breakthroughs.

Upon entering the Light Song Forest, Li Yunxiao confronts Gu Yuesheng and Situ Heng, Li Yunxiao displays his strength as a fifth-rank Alchemist and engages in a battle of spiritual attacks with Gu Yuesheng. Despite Gu Yuesheng's powerful attack using the
Immemorial Heaven Eye
 and the Pupil Technique — Moon Gap of Gu Feiyang, Li Yunxiao manages to counter it with the
Imperial Bell
, an ancient artifact, and overwhelms him.

During the battle,
Duan Yue
, who was previously thought to be dead, appears to assist Li Yunxiao. Duan Yue reveals himself as a Martial Emperor,
Situ Heng
 recognizes Duan Yue and tries to manipulate him into attacking the rookie mercenary group, but he failed.
