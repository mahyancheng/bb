He is a member of the Mo Great Family, and he is the top young talent of the family.

Plot

He attends the banquet, where
Li Yunxiao
 challenges
Zhou Yushan
 to a duel. At the banquet,
Wang Chen
 makes a significant bet of five million high-grade elemental stones on Li Yunxiao's victory, and Mo Ziqian places a bet of two million high-grade elemental stones on Zhou Yushan's victory. Mo Ziqian becomes agitated and worried about losing his bet when Li Yunxiao reveals his powerful Martial Ancestor level demonic beast, Thundercloud Roaring Sky Beast. Later, he also joins
Cheng Xiaolan
 in expressing sarcasm and mockery towards Zhou Yushan after his death in the duel against Li Yunxiao.
