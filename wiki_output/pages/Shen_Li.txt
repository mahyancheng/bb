Plot

Demon Plains Noah Ark

Shen Li arrives in Demon Land with her superior
Qing Luo
 and an eighth-tier Netherworld Warships from the
Empyrean One Pavillion
, serving as a guide for Qing Luo. After hearing news of  Noah’s Ship, she encounters Li Yunxiao, who is observing the aftermath of a battle between a mysterious group and
Xuan Bing
 from the Feather Frost Sect.  Qing Luo proposes an alliance with Yunxiao to capture
Tang Jie
 alive, as he possesses the Yang Key, and to assist in the search for Noah’s Ark.

Later, Yunxiao and the Empyrean One Pavilion forces come across
Shang
 and his companions battling
Little Qing
 in the void. Unable to avoid conflict, a fierce battle erupts between both sides, resulting in heavy casualties. Eventually, both groups retreat, unwilling to continue the fight in their weakened state.







With many injured and their strength depleted, the
Empyrean One Pavillion
 decides to withdraw from Demon Land, deeming it too dangerous. However, on their way back, Shen Li,
Qing Luo
 along with the Empyrean forces are ambushed by
Zhang Shaotian
 and completely annihilated.
