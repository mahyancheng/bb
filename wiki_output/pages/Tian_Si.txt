Plot

One of the Sanctum’s rulers, Lord Hei Yuhu joined forces with Nangong Buhai, the leader of the Nangong family, and several other top experts to launch an assault on Yue Tong. The Sacred Zone’s attack on the Moon Pupil resulted in heavy casualties, with countless deaths and injuries. Even Lord Hei Yuhu suffered severe wounds in the battle. Despite their combined efforts, Yue Tong also sustained serious injuries. However, after this battle, the Moon Pupil mysteriously vanished from the sight of all major forces, leaving no trace behind.
