Description

It can be undone Automatically and the method is very simple the individual who is sealed just have to castrate himself. It is an ancient seal Discovered by Gu Feiyang, he than changed the seal a little, leaving the victims with the ability to break the seal on their own. Their is one more method that require caster blood to be undone.
