History

In order to comprehend the rules of the
Ten Directions Realm
, Shang and his close companions sealed themselves within an array for tens of thousands of years. As a result, their cultivation plummeted—from Shang’s Peak of Perfection and his companions’ possible Divine Transcendence levels—down to the Martial Emperor Realm.
To sustain himself and his companions, Shang relied on the Supreme Palace Armillary Sphere to maintain their lives for millennia. However, the artifact had been pushed to its limits and was now on the verge of collapse.
