Plot

Though forced to submit by

 and becoming his spy, he felt an unexpected sense of relief even a hint of joy. Ever since
Tang Qing
 took over Red Moon City, the Elders had been cast aside. Their former prestige had vanished, and resources were scarce. Frustration had weighed on them for too long. Now, submitting to Nie Shengfeng felt far more dignified than serving Tang Qing. The oppression that had plagued them for so long seemed to lift, replaced by a renewed sense of purpose.
