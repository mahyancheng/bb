Plot

Land Of No Return

Qu Hongyan is trapped in the land of  No Return, struggling to save Luo Yunshang, who is being consumed by the Rainbow Stone and threatened by Gu Qingqing's lingering consciousness. When Yunxiao and his group arrive to help, Qu Hongyan reveals that the only way to save Yunshang is to break the seal on Five Cloud Mountain. However, doing so would release deadly monsters and plunge the Tianwu Continent into chaos.

Li Yunxiao, upon learning this, decides to prioritize saving Yunshang, even if it means risking the continent. His cold resolve shocks Qu Hongyan, who senses a powerful, familiar aura from him but doesn't realize his true identity.
