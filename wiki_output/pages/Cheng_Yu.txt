Plot

Mei Dong'er and Cheng Yu face off in an Tournament battle, with Cheng Yu believing his raw strength can overpower any technique. Mei Dong'er, calm and composed, channels the water elements around her and counters his powerful
Dao Qing Finger
 with a graceful yet overwhelming palm technique. Just when it seems she has the upper hand, Cheng Yu consumes a
Demon Transformation Pill
, turning into a mighty
Earth Bear
, drastically increasing his power.

As he launches a devastating
Earth Bear Fist
, Mei Dong'er appears exhausted and unable to continue. Just as defeat seems inevitable,
Li Yunxiao
 suddenly intervenes, effortlessly blocking Cheng Yu’s attack with his mysterious
Demonic Sky Armor
. His unexpected appearance shocks the audience, including powerful elders and rival sect members.

Undeterred, Cheng Yu refuses to accept defeat and strikes with all his might. However, Yunxiao counters with a simple but terrifyingly powerful punch, shattering Cheng Yu’s arm and breaking his demonic transformation. The overwhelming force of Yunxiao's attack sends Cheng Yu flying into the valley below, his meridians shattered, leaving his cultivation permanently damaged.{{r}543}}
