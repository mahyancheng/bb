Appearance

A woman with a graceful figure wore a flowing purple-blue dress adorned with embroidered cloud patterns trailing behind her. She had a fresh, refined appearance and an extraordinary temperament.

History

30.000 years ago, she led as the sect master of the Heavenly Night Palace. Her legendary status stems from the sealing of numerous monster race clans within her profound artifact, the Five Cloud Mountain. Notably, the lineage of the then-Monster Emperor, the Ash Demon Clan, were among those sealed, marking the beginning of the Monster clan's decline.
