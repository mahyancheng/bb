General Information

Hidden Families are Sects who are living secluded from the world. Usually they inhabit some artificial islands in the
Four Seas
 and are difficult to find. Both, in the human and sea race exist those hidden familys. The most powerful ones are comparable to the
7 major forces
, while the smaller ones can contend against some of the larger sects on the mainland.

Structure

Two factions have been formed by the hidden families. With the Heaven Alliances goal to conquer the mainland (
Tianwu Continent
), while the Earth Alliance forms the opposing faction.

Heaven Alliance (Tianmeng)

An alliance of some hidden families, with the goal of dominating the continent and occupying the resources of various sacred mountains and rivers. In recent years, overseas resources have been severely depleted, and many hidden sects have long been ready to move.
