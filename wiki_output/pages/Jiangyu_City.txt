General Information

Jiangyu City,  a key stronghold on the Western Region's border, was a place of constant mist and lifeless air. The harsh environment made survival difficult, draining vitality from both ordinary people and warriors alike. To counter this, a brilliant senior from the past created the Great Five Elements Transformation Formation beneath the city. For two hours each day, this formation transformed the deathly grey aura into the life-giving essence of the North Water Primordial Tree, sustaining the city's inhabitants and allowing Jiangyu City to endure through the ages.

The Five Elements naturally reinforce and counteract each other, making their transformations within the formation unsurprising. However, maintaining this balance requires a vast amount of energy. explained that the city’s extremely high taxes were the primary source of funding to sustain the array. The energy consumption for each transformation was astronomical, far beyond what ordinary people could comprehend.

History

Beneath this land lies the rumored Netherworld Kingdom, its eerie aura seeping upward and reshaping the world above. The true cause of this transformation is the earth vein itself, which has undergone an elemental mutation over countless years, altering the environment on a massive scale.
