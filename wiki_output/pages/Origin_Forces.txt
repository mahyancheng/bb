The natural forces that represent the will of a World are special properties and authorities that follow the will of Heaven and Earth. Born with the Celestial Dao of Heaven and Earth that they represent, they can elevate a Cultivator to the highest level of their World's parameters.

Heavenly Martial Realm**

{Purple Zifu Mansion Brahma World-Destroying Divine Thunder}: only Supreme Techniques of the Lightning Attribute or those who carry the Natural Thunder Body can summon Divine Thunder, It is the supreme oppressive force of the Heavenly Martial Realm, being used by
Qing Zhu
 and
Li Yunxiao
 throughout the series. It is said that the
Sky Scorpion Sect
 Thunder Transformation Technique held the ability to summon Purple Divine Thunder, standing out as the greatest Thunder Attribute Technique, in the last Hundred Thousand Years.

{Heavenly Eye}: is the vision of Heaven itself, a sliver of its essence was permeated into Amithaba, the Golden Lotus of Fa Hua, gaining the ability to suppress any entity beyond this Realm and summon the Currents of Heaven.

{Chains of Heaven}: celestial artifacts shaped by Heaven and Earth, can bind and suppress any force beyond the Laws of the Celestial Martial Realm or that does not originate from this World.

Demon Realm**

{Ice Demon Heart Flame}: a force that gathers the Yin and Yang of Heaven and Earth, capable of damaging Saint Artifacts, can be cast by Second-Grade Demon Lords, Demon Commanders or entities beyond these parameters that condensed a Body with a Raw Demon Stone from the Demon Realm.

{Emblem of Realm}: after a World is destroyed, it can be condensed by a
Lord of a Thousand Worlds
, giving shape to a Realm Emblem that carries the Laws, Rules and Forces of this fallen Realm. The Ancient Demon Realm was destroyed and condensed by the
Madman
, forming a Saint Artifact beyond the Thousand Worlds Realm.
