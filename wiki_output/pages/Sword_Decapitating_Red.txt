General Information

Yunxiao refined it from a
Origin Neon Rainbow Stone
, which are the raw stone from the Demon World untainted by Demon Qi. Infusing it with Demon Qi allows it to produce the
Ice Fiend Heart Flame
, which can burn everything in existence. Yunxiao named it after
Xiao Hong
, who was called "Little Red", with the intention to take her life with the sword.
