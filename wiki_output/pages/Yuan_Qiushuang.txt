Background

Yuan Qiushuang is a disciple of the
Big Dipper Sect
, known for her jealousy, scheming nature, and cruelty. She holds a deep grudge against her junior sister,
Mei Dong'er
, who is more talented, beautiful, and well-liked than her. Yuan Qiushuang sees Mei Dong'er as a rival and goes to great lengths to suppress and humiliate her whenever possible.

Power

She is a
six-star Martial Grandmaster
, possessing powerful frosty cold Qi, which allows her to weaken and slow down her opponents by freezing their meridians. She is also skilled with a
short sword.
