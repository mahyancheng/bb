Plot

Xiao Hong and Yu Shengjie clashed in a battle of fire and water. Confident in his abilities, Yu Shengjie unleashed a water dragon to extinguish Xiao Hong’s flames, but when their attacks collided, a massive explosion erupted, engulfing them both. As the blinding light faded, Xiao Hong stood exhausted but victorious, while Yu Shengjie had been completely erased from existence. The battle ended with Xiao Hong’s decisive win and the shocking annihilation of her opponent.
