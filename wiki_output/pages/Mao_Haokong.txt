Plot

He secretly colluding with the Holy Region's dark agents to break the seal of the Five Cloud Mountain. His ultimate goal is to release the powerful demon sealed within, seeking to reshape the power structure of the Tianwu Continent to his advantage.

In his plot, Mao Haokong manipulates events, framing the sect leader, Bai Sheng, for colluding with demons and attempting to seize control of the Prefecture. Using the authority of the dark agents, Mao Haokong pressures Bai Sheng to hand over the key to the Five Cloud Mountain seal. However, Bai Sheng resists, demanding an official decree from the Holy Region.
