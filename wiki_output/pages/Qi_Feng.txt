He is the Master of the Heaven-Gathering Sect, one of the three great experts of the
Duskflame Empire
. He, along with
Leng Xingbo
 of the Emotion Severing Mountain and
Wang Songhe
 of the Heavenly Jewel Sect, were the symbols of the Duskflame Empire's martial path.

Plot

Martial Flame Crisis

He confronted
Li Yunxiao
, who was refining the Rank Nine Mysterious Artifacts in the
World God Monument
. Qi Feng wanted Li Yunxiao to accompany him to the Sanctuary to explain certain matters, including the destruction of the Constellation Sect's branch in
Qinghai Town
. Li Yunxiao refused and a fierce battle ensued. Despite Li Yunxiao's efforts, he was outmatched by the Martial Supreme Qi Feng, who used powerful martial arts and even summoned a Fleeting Life Seal. However, Li Yunxiao's ancient bell, a Ninth Rank magical artifact, shattered the Fleeting Life Seal, impressing Qi Feng with his strength and luck. Qi Feng eventually chose to leave without further pursuing the matter.
