Mu Family Secret Technique

The Mu family’s Three Great Puppet Arts comparable to divine skills :

Casting Beans into Soldiers
 (Ranked Third), This technique allows the user to transform small objects (such as beans) into powerful puppet soldiers that can fight in battle. Elder Mu Yijun is highly skilled in this art, as his talent is well-suited for it. Though it is ranked third among the Mu family’s puppet arts, it is still considered a divine skill.

Six Ding Six Jia (Ranked Second),
 This technique involves controlling twelve powerful puppets to form the Twelve Divine Beings Killing Formation. It is inspired by one of the Three Ancient Fearsome Formations, the Twelve Capital Heaven Divine Wraith Formation, a formation with terrifying destructive power. Despite its incredible strength, it is extremely difficult to master.

The Mu Clan’s Greatest Puppet Art (Ranked First), This technique is the Mu family’s ultimate secret, passed down only to the clan leaders of each generation.

Family Puppet

The mobile mechanical puppet, The Mu Family’s Mobile Machine is a highly advanced mechanical puppet designed for large-scale mobility and teleportation. It represents the pinnacle of the Mu family's puppetry technology and demonstrates their mastery in combining formation arrays with mechanical constructs.

The puppet is large enough to be used as a movable fortress, capable of expanding and extending mechanical limbs at will. Unlike traditional teleportation formations, which are fixed to the ground, this mobile puppet has a teleportation array inscribed on its body. It can locate and access hidden spatial tunnels, allowing it to teleport directly to different locations without the need for external teleportation gates.
