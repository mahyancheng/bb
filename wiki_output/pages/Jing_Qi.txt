Appearance

His face was as white as snow and he was surprisingly young. He was wearing a long black robe and looked calm and majestic.

Personality

Jing Qi was a calculating and ruthless individual, always weighing the risks before taking action. While he possessed great strength, he preferred strategy over brute force, ensuring he never engaged in a battle he wasn’t confident of winning.

Plot

With the sudden appearance of
the Giant True Devil
 that had taken over Li Yunxiao's body, an earth-shattering sword strike was unleashed, splitting the sky and sea for thousands of miles. The devastating attack annihilated the Sea Devil Monkey and countless Corpse Fiend Sect powerhouses. The sheer force of the strike caused the Unmoving Forest to collapse, and the island-carrying turtle began sinking, despite not being directly hit. Witnessing this overwhelming power, Jing Qi hesitated—he knew that stopping the demon was futile. Left with no choice, he abandoned the fight, allowing Li Yunxiao to depart unchallenged, leaving behind only his name.

Martial arts competitions

Despite the competition's strict age limit of 30, Jing Qi was allowed to participate due to his unique body condition. For the first time, he revealed his puppet, Gan Qi, to the public, stepping onto the battlefield to challenge Li Yunxiao for the Number Plate Zero.

As the final round of the top 20, Yunxiao wielded his Tusita Heavenly Peak, launching a powerful attack against Gan Qi. The puppet struggled to resist but was ultimately overwhelmed. With a thunderous impact, it was sent flying, shattering the Jade Coffin on impact. Both of Gan Qi’s arms were completely broken, a sight never before as the Fiendish God Zombie was believed to be indestructible. Yet, with a single blow, Yunxiao had done what no one had before.

Jing Qi was deeply shaken. In a futile attempt to claim a meaningless iron plate, he had suffered a huge loss, shattering his coffin and severely damaging his prized puppet. Suppressing his anger, he let out a cold snort and withdrew, abandoning the fight against Yunxiao.
