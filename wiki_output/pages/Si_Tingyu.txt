Appearance

Her face was dignified and delicate, and her long hair was tied into a bun. Her figure was in a trance, was peaceful and quiet.

Personality

Si Tingyu
 was a woman of
few words and unwavering composure
. Cold, calculating, and detached, she rarely displayed emotion, making her decisions based purely on logic rather than sentiment. She held a
statue-like presence
, often observing rather than engaging unless necessary.

Plot

Five Cloud Mountain

During the fierce battle at Five Cloud Mountain, Si Tengyu and Cangwu Kong fought bravely against
Shang
, using the Wheel of Reincarnation left
Ghost King
 that she refined. However, they were ultimately overpowered and forced to retreat.

The situation worsened when Shang,
Mo
,
Huang
, and thousands of monster reinforcements joined forces, turning the tide of battle. The human forces were pushed back, retreating to their battleships, only to be surrounded and relentlessly bombarded by the monster army. One by one, their formations fell, and even the mighty Five Star Formation was broken.

Meanwhile, Si Tengyu, surrounded and severely injured, was on the verge of death. At this critical moment,
Wei Wuya
 arrived with forty-nine
Ghost Asura
, managing to temporarily repel the monster forces.

However, the situation remained dire.
Gongyang Zhengqi
 appeared, revealing that he had been watching all along and had sealed the Five Cloud Mountain, trapping any forces from escaping. Upon learning the truth, Si Tengyu, devastated and heartbroken, shattered her own meridians, choosing death over despair.
