Appearance

Meng Bai having a child-Appearance, with his yellow-ish Bowl-cut hair and Blue eyes.

His appearance changes throughout the story, but the last time since he was abducted, he would wear anything was a light teal and black pattern of clothes. Seemingly having no sort of pattern but random lines and curves. That fits his blue eyes and happy nature.

Personality

Meng Bai is a kind-hearted and obedient boy who deeply respects and cherishes his older sister,
Meng Wu.
 Despite his young age, he is mature and responsible, fully aware of their family's financial struggles. Rather than burdening his sister, he takes initiative to help, working hard to support her in any way he can.
