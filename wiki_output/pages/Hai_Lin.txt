Plot

Light Song Forest

He leads a merchant group in the Demon Plain and encounters Li Yunxiao and his companions after their teleportation array deviates from its intended destination.

Initially wary of them, the group became more receptive upon learning that Li Yunxiao was an Alchemist.
Commander Zuo

During their journey together, Li Yunxiao examined Hai Lin's condition and discovered that his illness was related to the bloodline power of a Desolate Kun Bull, an ancient earth-type demonic beast. Li Yunxiao suggested several potential cures, including hunting powerful demonic beasts or finding a Five Elements Devouring Spirit Rat. The caravan faced numerous challenges and dangers along the way, including an overwhelming attack by a massive horde of Five Elements Spirit Devouring Rats.

Gu Yuesheng
