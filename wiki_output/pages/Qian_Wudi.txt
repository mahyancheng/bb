Plot

Mount Meru

Qian Wudi was one of the individuals present in the Xumi Temple's inner hall during the confrontation with
Di Jia
, the Black Demon Shadow. When Di Jia attacked, he, along with the others, tried to fight back using their martial techniques and profound armaments, but they were easily defeated by the immense power of the Black Demon Shadow.

During the battle, Qian Wudi revealed that he possessed an eighth-level ancient spiritual talisman, a rare and powerful treasure that could enhance his attacks to the level of a Martial Supreme expert. However, even with the aid of the talisman, his attack was unable to overcome Di Jia's Black Demon Qi, and his weapon was destroyed with ease.

Later, when
Xu Qing
 used the Divine Prayer Technique, the Martial Sovereign
Lu Daozi
 descends to confront Di Jia. Qian Wudi witnessed the struggle between the two powerful forces. After the battle, when the inner hall was on the verge of collapse and the defenses of the Xumi Temple opened, Qian Wudi managed to escape the scene along with Lu Daozi,
Cao Yaxing
, Li Yunxiao and Luo Qingyun.

Demon Origin Plains

At a crucial moment between
Shen Feng
 and
Li Yunxiao
, Qian Wudi, appeared on an artifact and engaged in a verbal exchange with Li Yunxiao. Tensions escalated as the situation became more complex.
Qing Luo
 of the
Tianyi Pavilion
 offered rewards for defeating the enemies, while Qian Wudi offered Li Yunxiao a deal involving a valuable Heavenly Soul Herb.

After Li Yunxiao and Qian Wudi's intense negotiation, Qian Wudi agrees to trade on Heavenly Soul Herb, 1.2 billion mid-grade Primordial Stones, and additional resources for a mysterious item Li Yunxiao possesses. This item turns out to be the Seven Constellations Martial Will Fruit, a potent treasure that Qian Wudi desires to boost his power. The exchange takes place amidst tense confrontations between various factions. Shen Feng, tries to attack Li Yunxiao, but
Zhang Hanzhe
 intervenes, leading to a standoff.

Ultimately, the trade is made, with Li Yunxiao giving Qian Wudi the Seven Constellations Martial Will Fruit in exchange for Heavenly Soul Herb and primordial stones.
Tang Jie
 and his group from the
Four Extremes Sect
 leave the scene, while Qian Wudi and his forces secure the area around Li Yunxiao to ensure his safety as he absorbs the powerful Heavenly Soul Herb. The situation remains tense, with factions watching each other closely, and Qian Wudi retreats to cultivate in seclusion, leaving his subordinates to guard the area.
