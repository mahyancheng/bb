Background

The Long Family of Skyridge is an ancient and powerful clan residing on Tianling Mountain, the tallest peak in the Tianwu Realm, standing at 180,000 feet high. Their heritage is deeply tied to the True Spirit Bloodline, a rare and powerful lineage that grants those who awaken it immense martial prowess.

The Long Family is divided into two main sects:

Inner Sect: Comprised exclusively of individuals who have successfully awakened their True Spirit Bloodline. These elite members wield extraordinary power and are considered the true inheritors of the family's strength.

Outer Sect: Made up of descendants of the True Spirit Bloodline who have yet to awaken their lineage. They are required to train diligently, as only those who reach the Ninth Heaven Martial Emperor level through their own efforts can qualify to join the Inner Sect. Because The probability of awakening the True Spirit Bloodline is incredibly rare—only one in ten thousand. Despite this low chance, the Long Family boasts over ten thousand Inner Sect disciples.
