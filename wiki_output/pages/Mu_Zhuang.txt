The leader of the Hidden Mu Family, he is an understanding man who does not judge others quickly based on what they show on the surface. He proves to be a wise expert and a sovereign authority.

Appearance

A man in a black and gold brocade robe, looked like he was only forty years old, but he exuded an air of authority without being angry. The aura of a person who had been in a position of power for a long time was endless. His gaze was extremely penetrating, almost as if it could see through people inside and out.
