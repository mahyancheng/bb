History

The
Myriad Treasures Store
 is one of the oldest and most powerful merchant organizations in the world,
founded tens of thousands of years ago
. Its rise to power was not purely based on business acumen but was greatly aided by a
mysterious ancient treasure trove
 discovered by its founder. This treasure contained immense wealth, rare artifacts, and possibly even divine-level inheritances, which allowed the store to rapidly expand and eventually become
the number one Chamber of Commerce
 in the world.

Over time,
Myriad Treasures Store
 became the dominant force within the
Merchant Union
, controlling vast trade routes, precious resources, and rare cultivation techniques.
