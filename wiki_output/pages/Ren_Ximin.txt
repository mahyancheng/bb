Ren Ximin was the master of the Spiritual Flower Wonderland, an independent space within Lanxue Holy City. where on of the
Demon Emperor
 body was sealed.

Personality

Ren Ximin was a cunning, ambitious, and who values his autonomy above all else. While he is technically affiliated with the Holy Land, he refuses to be bound by authority and only follows orders when they align with his own interests.

He was highly knowledgeable, patient, and willing to play the long game, as shown by his decades-long efforts to extract demonic energy. However, his greed blinded him, making him reckless in his pursuit of power.

Despite his intelligence, he underestimated the true nature of the Demon Lord’s will, believing he could control what he absorbed. When faced with pain and defeat, he quickly broke down, revealing a cowardly side—begging for mercy and ultimately surrendering to Dijia.

Plot

Seeking greater power, Ren Ximin discovered a way to pry open the seal of the Demon Lord, allowing him to absorb demonic energy over decades. However, the seal would repair itself, making it increasingly difficult for him to extract more energy. One day, he encountered
Di Jia
, a being filled with immense demonic essence. Believing it was an opportunity to advance his cultivation, Ren Ximin attempted to refine and absorb Dijia, unaware that he was dealing with a Demon Lord's clone. Instead of gaining power, he fell into Dijia’s trap, As a result, not only did Dijia not die, but the demonic energy that had accumulated in his body for decades was devoured, and a demon seed was planted in him that could directly corrode his body

Now overwhelmed by unbearable pain and corruption, Ren Ximin had no choice but to surrender to Dijia. The Demon Lord planted a demon seed in his spiritual consciousness, completely enslaving him. Once a respected cultivator, Ren Ximin was reduced to a demon servant, losing his free will and humanity.
