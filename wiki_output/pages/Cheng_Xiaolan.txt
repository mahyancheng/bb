She is a member of the Cheng Great Family and the top young talent of the family.

Plot

The Golden Crow Arena

She attends the banquet, where
Li Yunxiao
 and
Zhou Yushan
 are about to have a duel. Unlike
Wang Chen
 and
Mo Ziqian
, Cheng Xiaolan refrains from placing any bets on the duel. She is cautious in her actions and prefers not to pass judgment hastily. Cheng Xiaolan does not participate in the betting activity, but observes the events unfold, expressing her admiration for Li Yunxiao's unexpected strength. She is also among the group that mocks and shows no sympathy for Zhou Yushan's death and
Zhou Yangbiao
's punishment and demise at the end of the duel.
