Zhan Chen first appeared when he encountered Yunxiao and Pan Yi shortly after entering the Sky Shattering Realm in search of the Yellow Springs Hellfire. He proposed an alliance with them, but later, he and Yunxiao were intercepted by Ben Tingfeng, leading to a battle. Overwhelmed, Zhan Chen attempted to escape, but he was stopped by
Ye Nantian
, who killed him to prevent any news about the Yellow Springs Hellfire River from leaking out.

Appearance

The black-robed man had a thin face, a slightly pointed jaw, a pale face, and his eyes were almost sunken. he looked so skinny as if he had not seen the sun for many years. He was most likely a person who had escaped from the eternal world.

Background

He came from the realm of immortality and almost opened the door to the earthly realm, but unfortunately he failed in the end.
