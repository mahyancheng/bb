Plot

When he first met Li Yunxiao in Red Moon City, he recognized Yunxiao’s exceptional talent but believed he had an even chance of defeating him. However, for someone as proud as Mu Fenghua, a mere 50-50 chance was unacceptable—it was a humiliation.

After returning to the Mu family, he threw himself into brutal, self-imposed cultivation, determined to crush Li Yunxiao effortlessly the next time they crossed paths. He believed that through this rigorous training, he had elevated himself to the point where he would have a 90% chance of victory in their inevitable rematch.
