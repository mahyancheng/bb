Narcissus is the daughter of the ocean Emperor, the princess of the four Seas.

Appearance

The woman was in a white dress elegant and refined. Her skin was as white as snow, She had eyebrows like emerald feathers, waist-length hair, and she exuded a rare aura of purity. Her facial features were exquisite and soul-stirring. Her red eyes were like water, but they carried a faint coldness.

Personality

Narcissus is a very naive and simple-minded girl and the daughter of the
Ocean Emperor
, the ruler of the
Four Seas
. Her naivety often brings her and the people around her in trouble.

Power

the royal families of the four Seas once had a contract with the ancestors of the deep-sea Giant beasts. They were willing to listen to the royal family’s orders in times of crisis. Can summoning many deep-sea monsters at once.

The Lotus Platform is a supreme-grade profound weapon, possessing a trace of the Eye of Heaven’s power. It can only be fully wielded by its true owner. When activated with the Heavenly Dao Divine Art, it triggered the Chains of Law, causing a heavenly instrument phenomenon. This immense power drew countless experts to Red Moon City, all seeking to uncover its secrets.
