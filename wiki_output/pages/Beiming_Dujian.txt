Personality

Bei Ming Du Jian has a paranoid personality, he is not a bad person.

Background

Bei Ming Du Jian was once a top expert of the Beiming family, cultivating to the late Heaven Control Realm alongside his brother, Bei Ming Zhan Guan. In their pursuit of greater power and immortality, the two brothers parted ways
Beiming Zhanguan
 entered the Realm of Eternal Life, while Du Jian ventured into the cracks between worlds and eventually arrived in the Demon Realm. There, he survived for over 60,000 years, adapting to its laws and gaining immense power, transforming into a high-level Demon King.

His demonic powers fully adapted to both the Demon and Tianwu Realms. Unlike other Demon Kings who were suppressed in the Tianwu Realm, Du Jian could unleash his true power.

Plot

When he finally returned to the Tianwu Realm, he sought the Xuan Ming Stone from outer space, believing it was the key to breaking through to the Creation Realm, However, Li Yunxiao, fearing that the stone could fall into the hands of demons and lead to disaster, demanded it for safekeeping. Du Jian, however, refused, believing his strength sufficient to safeguard it. This led to a fierce conflict between them.
