Personality

Che You is confident, sharp-tongued, and a bit arrogant, always carrying a carefree attitude. He enjoys teasing others but has a strong sense of pride and refuses to be taken advantage of. Despite his blunt words, he is loyal and reliable, stepping in to help when needed. His immense talent allows him to grow rapidly in strength, making him both a formidable fighter and an unpredictable ally.
