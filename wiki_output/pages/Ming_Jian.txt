Ming Jian, were enigmatic figures whose origins remained a mystery. Their insights and predictions had shaped many major events in recent years, earning them a reputation as
wise men of unparalleled foresight
. Despite their fame, no one knew where they came from or how they acquired their prophetic abilities. Some speculated that they were connected to the
Duanmu family
, a legendary lineage known for its
power of prophecy
, but no evidence ever confirmed this claim.

Appearance

Ming Jian wore a
billowing green robe.

Plot

As they attempted to flee, Ling Baiyi, furious that they had ruined his plans, struck with his sword, unleashing a deadly purple light. The attack was so swift and precise that they remained standing in the air, seemingly unharmed, until moments later. Their mirror artifact shattered, Blood then erupted from their waists, revealing that they had been sliced clean in half.
