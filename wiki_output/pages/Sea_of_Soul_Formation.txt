General Information

Headquarters of the Alchemist Association. The Sea of Soul Formation is suspended above the
Tianwu Continent
 in the void, only reachable through teleportation arrays in a few locations or by tearing the space (Martial Sovereigns). Other than that, there are no ways to reach it.

There are two big factions: Pursuers of the alchemy path and the martial path.

The Sea of Soul Formation consists of two large areas, the Inner Sea and the Outer Sea.
