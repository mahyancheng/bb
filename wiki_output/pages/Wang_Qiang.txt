Plot

Mount Meru

Initially, He betrayed his fellow companions during a fight with a powerful stone beast. He abandoned them to save his own life, seeking safety on Li Yunxiao's Cyan Wolf Chariot. Later, Wang Qiang tried to rush from Yunxiao's chariot since the 7-Rank stone beast approaching them, when Li Yunxiao saw through his plan, Wang Qiang panicked and offered to share a valuable treasure map in exchange for sparing his life. Li Yunxiao accepted the map.

However, Li Yunxiao, being aware of his treachery, refused to take him with them when they used a short-distance teleportation formation to escape from the stone beast. Instead, Li Yunxiao extorted one billion mid-grade Yuan Stones from Wang Qiang, making him sign an IOU for the amount before leaving him behind to face the stone beast alone.

While escaping, Yunxiao and the others come across a bunch of people who fight for a seven constellation
Dao Fruit
. Luo Yunshang manages to snatch it with the help of the little green demon.

Before people can get agitated about it, the Stone Beast manages to catch up. Sitting in the same boat, the people join forces to take it down. After destroying the Stone Beast's physical body, its "heart" remains, which allows it to recover its body again. Before that happens, Yunxiao manages to subdue it.

After that, Wang Qiang, takes Luo Yunshang hostage in an attempt to get the Dao Fruit. However, before Yunxiao can make a move, a passage opens and swallows all people present.
