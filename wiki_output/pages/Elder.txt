Appearance

He was wrapped in linen clothes and had a face as clear as Jade.

Plot

He challenge Mo Xiaochuan, believing his Emperor Qi and martial arts mastery would easily overpower him. However, Mo Xiaochuan’s profound swordsmanship, wielding the dual-force Qingfeng Sword, overwhelmed Elder Qiu's techniques. Despite unleashing his powerful Thousand Hands Thousand Leaves Fist, Elder Qiu found his domain shattered by Mo Xiaochuan’s Heaven-Destroying Devil-Rinsing Sword. Unable to resist the sheer force, he was consumed by the sword’s might, leaving no trace behind. His arrogance and underestimation of Mo Xiaochuan led to his swift and total annihilation.
