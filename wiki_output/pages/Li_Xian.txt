Li Xian, a skilled member of the Li Family’s branch, served under Li Baifeng. Aware of the family’s power struggle, he warned Yunxiao that attending the palace feast would draw Li Yi’s hostility. He advised caution, believing it wasn’t the right time for conflict.

Plot

Tasked with delivering a warning to Li Yunxiao, he attempted to discreetly lead him to a secluded place but was instead caught by Yunxiao.
