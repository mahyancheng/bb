Cang Liqun is a two-star Martial Lord and a high-ranking general in the Golden Lion Army that lead at least hundreds of thousands of soldiers. Cang Liqun led the
Golden Lion Army
 to
Yangpu
 under the pretense of tracking down those who had robbed the
City Lord Mansion
. However, his true intentions  to eliminate Yunxiao and his allies with the help of
Li Yi
 and his party.

Plot

Despite his overwhelming numbers, Cang Liqun found himself in trouble when Yunxiao and his allies Heaven Core launched a desperate counterattack. A terrifying
dark purple finger
 manifested, killing Yu Yuanwen, one of his strongest warriors, in an instant. This display of power shattered the morale of his army. Soon after,
Chen Dashen
 reinforcements arrived, further tilting the battle in Yunxiao’s favor. as the Golden Lion Army, already demoralized. Ji Meng, under Yunxiao’s orders, brutally beat Cang Liqun to the point of severe injury, Realizing he was at their mercy, Cang Liqun desperately pleaded for his life, even offering his loyalty in exchange for survival. However, Yunxiao coldly dismissed him, declaring that Martial Lords were insignificant in his eyes and that Cang Liqun’s fate was already sealed.

Ultimately, Yunxiao ordered his execution. Ji Meng delivered the killing blow, slitting Cang Liqun’s throat and ending his life on the battlefield.
