General Information

The North Region is home to the most powerful forces in the Heavenly Martial Arts Realm, largely due to its rich spiritual energy. Unlike the remote South Region, the North is abundant with spiritual mountains and rivers, making it a highly competitive area. This concentration of power is reflected in Songyue City, the headquarters of the Merchant Union, which is also located in the North.
